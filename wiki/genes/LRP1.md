# LRP1

## Overview

LRP1 (Low-Density Lipoprotein Receptor-Related Protein 1) encodes a large endocytic receptor involved in lipid metabolism, cell signaling, and cellular entry of various ligands. Recurrent somatic mutations in LRP1 have been observed in anaplastic thyroid carcinoma (ATC).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Recurrent mutations previously described in anaplastic thyroid carcinoma (ATC); identified in a multi-site consortium WES/WGS study of 137 ATCs [PMID:38412093](../papers/38412093.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Anaplastic thyroid carcinoma (ATC) — recurrent somatic mutations; specific functional impact not characterized in this corpus [PMID:38412093](../papers/38412093.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Not specifically reported in the corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic link reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- The functional consequence of LRP1 mutations in ATC (tumor suppressor vs. passenger) requires experimental validation.
