# ALDOC

## Overview

ALDOC (aldolase C) is a glycolytic enzyme expressed predominantly in astrocytes and Bergmann glia in the normal brain. In oligodendroglioma it marks the differentiated astro-like tumor cell compartment, serving as a lineage-of-origin indicator within the developmental hierarchy inferred from single-cell transcriptomics.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ALDOC (together with [APOE](../genes/APOE.md), [SOX9](../genes/SOX9.md), and [GFAP](../genes/GFAP.md)) marks the differentiated astro-like compartment in grade II IDH-mutant 1p/19q-codeleted oligodendroglioma; identified from scRNA-seq of 4,347 cells across 6 tumors [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [ODG](../cancer_types/ODG.md) — ALDOC expression defines the astro-like differentiated sub-population in oligodendroglioma; these cells are largely non-cycling and distinct from the proliferative stem/progenitor compartment [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- ALDOC co-expresses with [APOE](../genes/APOE.md), [SOX9](../genes/SOX9.md), and [GFAP](../genes/GFAP.md) in astro-like tumor cells; mutually exclusive with the stem/progenitor program ([ASCL1](../genes/ASCL1.md), [CHD7](../genes/CHD7.md), [CCND2](../genes/CCND2.md)) and the oligo-like program ([OLIG1](../genes/OLIG1.md), [OLIG2](../genes/OLIG2.md)) [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- Not directly therapeutically targeted in the corpus; the astro-like ALDOC-high cells are largely non-cycling and thus unlikely to fuel long-term tumor growth [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Open questions

- Whether the astro-like program in oligodendroglioma reflects a genuine normal astrocytic identity or an aberrant transdifferentiation is not resolved in the corpus.
