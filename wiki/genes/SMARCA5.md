# SMARCA5

## Overview

SMARCA5 (SWI/SNF Related, Matrix Associated, Actin Dependent Regulator of Chromatin, Subfamily A, Member 5) encodes SNF2H, an ATP-dependent chromatin remodelling factor and catalytic subunit of the ISWI chromatin remodelling complexes. It is involved in replication, repair, and transcriptional regulation. In cancer genomics, SMARCA5 has been identified as a chromatin remodelling gene affected by somatic mutation in adenoid cystic carcinoma of the breast (breast AdCC).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Somatic mutation identified in breast AdCC (adenoid cystic carcinoma of the breast); listed among non-passenger cancer-related gene mutations alongside BRAF, FBXW7, SF3B1, FGFR2, RASA1, PTPN11, and MTOR in WES of 12 tumours (0.27 non-silent mutations/Mb) [PMID:26095796](../papers/26095796.md)
