# HMOX1

## Overview

HMOX1 (heme oxygenase 1) is a stress-response enzyme that catabolizes heme and is induced by oxidative stress, including UV radiation. In normal skin biology, its expression marks a high-mutation-burden melanocyte subpopulation with UV-damage signatures.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- HMOX1 is a lead transcriptomic marker of the HighMut melanocyte subpopulation (high UV mutation burden, enriched in interfollicular epidermis) in a single-cell multi-omic atlas of 297 melanocytes from 31 donors; co-expressed with [ABCC2](../genes/ABCC2.md), [MC1R](../genes/MC1R.md), [HERC2](../genes/HERC2.md), [LIPA](../genes/LIPA.md), and [HLA-DPA1](../genes/HLA-DPA1.md) [PMID:39975212](../papers/39975212.md).
- HighMut cells expressing HMOX1 carry predominantly UV-attributable SBS7 mutational signatures; LowMut cells lacking this program carry clock-like SBS1/SBS5 signatures [PMID:39975212](../papers/39975212.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- No cancer-type associations reported in the corpus. Findings are from non-lesional skin of a melanoma patient; no melanoma driver claim is made [PMID:39975212](../papers/39975212.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- HMOX1 co-occurs with [HERC2](../genes/HERC2.md), [HLA-DPA1](../genes/HLA-DPA1.md), [MC1R](../genes/MC1R.md), and [ABCC2](../genes/ABCC2.md) in the HighMut pigmentation/UV-damage program; anti-correlates with the LowMut neural-crest program ([HACD1](../genes/HACD1.md), [VCAN](../genes/VCAN.md), [TAGLN](../genes/TAGLN.md)) [PMID:39975212](../papers/39975212.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- Not reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Whether HMOX1 upregulation in UV-damaged melanocytes is protective (cytoprotective heme catabolism) or a marker of malignant potential is not addressed in this study [PMID:39975212](../papers/39975212.md).
