# toripalimab

## Overview

Toripalimab is an anti-PD-1 monoclonal antibody targeting [PDCD1](../genes/PDCD1.md). It is recommended by NCCN guidelines (with penpulimab) as part of first-line combination regimens with platinum-based chemotherapy for recurrent/metastatic nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Toripalimab (anti-PD-1) + platinum/gemcitabine is NCCN-recommended first-line standard for R/M NPC alongside penpulimab; ICI recommended as salvage if not used initially [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
- Anti-LAG-3 LBL-007 + toripalimab in R/M NPC (NCT05102006, n=30): ORR 33.3% in ICI-naïve (n=12) and 11.8% in ICI-refractory (n=17); median PFS 10.8 months; median DOR 15 months [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
- A2AR antagonist ILB-2109 + toripalimab under investigation in phase Ib/IIa for R/M NPC [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
