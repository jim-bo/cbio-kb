# GNLY

## Overview

GNLY encodes granulysin, a cytolytic effector released by CD8 T cells and NK cells.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Effector cytotoxic component of the 20-gene Immunologic Constant of Rejection (ICR) signature used to stratify colon cancer immune subtypes [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) — expressed as part of the prognostic ICR module in the AC-ICAM cohort [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [PRF1](../genes/PRF1.md), [GZMA](../genes/GZMA.md), [GZMB](../genes/GZMB.md), [GZMH](../genes/GZMH.md) in the ICR effector arm [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- Contributes to the ICR score, which outperformed CMS and MSI for prognosis and may have predictive utility for immunotherapy response [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
