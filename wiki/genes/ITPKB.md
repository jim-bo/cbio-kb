# ITPKB

## Overview

ITPKB encodes inositol-trisphosphate 3-kinase B. It is a recurrent driver in classic Hodgkin lymphoma HRS cells.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Mutated in 28% of cHL cases; tended to carry more than one nonsynonymous mutation per case [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- Elevated noncoding:coding mutation ratio consistent with AID off-target activity [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [CHL](../cancer_types/CHL.md) — recurrent driver in FACS-purified HRS cells across WGS+WES cohort (n=61) [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Grouped with [BCL7A](../genes/BCL7A.md), [SOCS1](../genes/SOCS1.md), [TMSB4X](../genes/TMSB4X.md), and [IL4R](../genes/IL4R.md) as genes with elevated noncoding:coding mutation ratios [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- None reported.

## Open questions

- Functional consequence of multi-hit and noncoding ITPKB lesions in cHL remains to be characterized [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
