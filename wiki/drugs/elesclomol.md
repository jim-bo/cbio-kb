# elesclomol

## Overview

Elesclomol is an investigational small molecule that acts as a copper ionophore and inducer of oxidative stress, leading to mitochondrial dysfunction and apoptosis in cancer cells. It has been explored in hematologic malignancies and solid tumors, and has shown preferential activity in certain genetically-defined [AML](../cancer_types/AML.md) subgroups.

## Evidence in the corpus

- In the Beat AML program ex vivo screen of 409 specimens from 363 AML patients against a 122-compound panel, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md)-mutant AML cases showed broad drug resistance but a trend toward elesclomol sensitivity, identifying elesclomol as a candidate repurposing agent for this poor-prognosis genotype [PMID:30333627](../papers/30333627.md).
