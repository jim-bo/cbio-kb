# Uterine Sarcoma/Mesenchymal (USARC)

## Overview

USARC is the OncoTree code for Uterine Sarcoma/Mesenchymal tumors, a heterogeneous group of rare mesenchymal malignancies arising from the uterus. In the TCGA [SARC](../cancer_types/SARC.md) cohort (PMID:29100075), the code is used in wiki links to represent Undifferentiated Pleomorphic Sarcoma ([UPS](../cancer_types/UPS.md)), a high-grade soft tissue sarcoma histology studied alongside leiomyosarcoma, dedifferentiated liposarcoma, myxofibrosarcoma, synovial sarcoma, and [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md). The canonical OncoTree code for non-uterine undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma / malignant fibrous histiocytoma is [MFH](../cancer_types/MFH.md).

## Cohorts in the corpus

- TCGA [SARC](../cancer_types/SARC.md) ([sarc_tcga_pub](../datasets/sarc_tcga_pub.md)) — 44 [UPS](../cancer_types/UPS.md) cases within the 206-sarcoma cohort profiled with WES/WGS, RNA-seq, DNA methylation, SNP6, and RPPA [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- UPS (n=44 in TCGA SARC) and [MFS](../cancer_types/MFS.md) are molecularly indistinguishable across genomic platforms; myxoid-stroma gene expression is the primary separator between histologies [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
- Pan-sarcoma SMGs [TP53](../genes/TP53.md), [ATRX](../genes/ATRX.md), and [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) are recurrently altered in UPS; deep [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) deletions in 16%, [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) deep deletions in 16%, [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deep deletions in 20% of UPS [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
- Recurrent SCNAs in UPS/MFS combined: [CCNE1](../genes/CCNE1.md) high-level amplification (10%), [VGLL3](../genes/VGLL3.md) amplification (11%), [YAP1](../genes/YAP1.md) amplification (3%); VGLL3/YAP1 Hippo-pathway target signature is strongly expressed (p=1e-24), implicating the Hippo pathway as a driver [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
- Immune microenvironment: UPS and [MFS](../cancer_types/MFS.md) had the highest macrophage scores and favorable NK-cell/dendritic-cell signatures; NK-cell infiltration was the only immune signature correlated with disease-specific survival across multiple sarcoma histologies [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
- Undifferentiated sarcoma was among the TRK fusion-positive tumor types in the MSK prospective cohort (n=26,312); TRK fusion-positive sarcomas as a group ([SARCNOS](../cancer_types/SARCNOS.md) n=9 plus USARC) were markedly depleted of co-occurring canonical MAPK-pathway drivers (1.5% vs 31.4% in TRK-negative tumors, P<0.001) [PMID:31871300](../papers/31871300.md)
- Prospective MSK-IMPACT sequencing of 107 uterine sarcoma patients (dataset: [usarc_msk_2020](../datasets/usarc_msk_2020.md)) covering uLMS (n=80), high-grade non-LMS (n=22), LG-ESS (n=4), and STUMP (n=2); actionable alterations in 45% (48/107); high-grade non-LMS had significantly worse survival than uLMS (median 3.4 vs 6.3 years; HR 0.48, p=0.03); WGD in 50.5% of high-grade tumors [PMID:32299819](../papers/32299819.md)
- In a 7,494-sarcoma cohort, uterine sarcoma/mesenchymal tumors were part of the 81% soft tissue sarcoma component; 31.7% of all sarcomas harbored ≥1 OncoKB actionable alteration [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).

## Subtypes

- UPS and [MFS](../cancer_types/MFS.md) form a single molecular spectrum by multi-platform analysis; the distinction between them is primarily based on myxoid stroma content rather than genomic features [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
- Hippo-pathway-altered subset (VGLL3/YAP1 amplified) within UPS/MFS may represent a clinically relevant molecular subtype [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- The SARC028 trial showed 40% response to a PD-1 inhibitor in UPS; the TCGA SARC analysis proposes extending checkpoint-inhibitor trials to MFS given its molecular indistinguishability from UPS [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
- Hippo-pathway inhibitors are nominated based on VGLL3/YAP1 amplification in a UPS/MFS subset [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
- [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md) — proposed for UPS/MFS based on immune microenvironment profiling and the SARC028 trial results [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md).
