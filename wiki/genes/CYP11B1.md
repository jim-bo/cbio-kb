# CYP11B1

## Overview

CYP11B1 (Cytochrome P450 Family 11 Subfamily B Member 1, also known as 11-beta-hydroxylase) is a steroidogenic enzyme in the adrenal cortex that catalyzes the final step in cortisol biosynthesis. Its expression is a marker of adrenocortical differentiation and is used to distinguish adrenocortical components in neuroendocrine tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CYP11B1 overexpressed in the cortical admixture PCC/PGL subtype alongside [CYP21A2](../genes/CYP21A2.md) and [STAR](../genes/STAR.md); these adrenal cortex markers define the cortical admixture molecular subtype in 173-patient TCGA PCC/PGL cohort [PMID:28162975](../papers/28162975.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[PHC](../cancer_types/PHC.md) / [PGNG](../cancer_types/PGNG.md)**: CYP11B1 expression is highest in the cortical admixture PCC/PGL subtype, which also carries rare germline [MAX](../genes/MAX.md) mutations (p<0.032); expression-based subtype marker [PMID:28162975](../papers/28162975.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- CYP11B1 overexpression co-occurs with CYP21A2 and STAR overexpression in the cortical admixture subtype; germline MAX mutations are enriched in this subtype [PMID:28162975](../papers/28162975.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- CYP11B1 is a subtype marker rather than a direct therapeutic target in PCC/PGL in this study.

## Open questions

- Whether the cortical admixture subtype's adrenocortical marker expression reflects multifocal disease, tissue-sampling artifact, or a distinct biological state requires further investigation [PMID:28162975](../papers/28162975.md)
