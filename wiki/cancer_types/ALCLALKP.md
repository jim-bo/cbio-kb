# Anaplastic Large-Cell Lymphoma ALK Positive (ALCLALKP)

## Overview

ALK-positive Anaplastic Large-Cell Lymphoma, a subtype of [ALCL](./ALCL.md) within Mature T and NK Neoplasms.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [mtnn_msk_2022](../datasets/mtnn_msk_2022.md): [ALK](../genes/ALK.md)+ [ALCL](../cancer_types/ALCL.md) comprised 5% (approximately n=6) of the MSK nodal [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) cohort [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- Small absolute numbers limit subgroup inference; analyzed within the [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) cohort where [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) alterations carried prognostic weight [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Subtypes

- See parent [ALCL](./ALCL.md).
