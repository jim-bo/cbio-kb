# YWHAZ

## Overview

YWHAZ encodes 14-3-3 zeta/delta, a member of the 14-3-3 family of regulatory proteins that mediate signal transduction by binding phosphoserine-containing proteins. In the expanded TCGA bladder cancer analysis, YWHAZ was identified as a focal copy-number amplification target by GISTIC 2.0 analysis.
