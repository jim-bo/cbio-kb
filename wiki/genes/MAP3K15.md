# MAP3K15

## Overview

MAP3K15 (Mitogen-Activated Protein Kinase Kinase Kinase 15) encodes a serine/threonine kinase in the MAPK signaling cascade. In cancer genomics, inactivating mutations in MAP3K15 have been observed in esophageal squamous cell carcinoma (ESCC).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- MAP3K15 harbors inactivating mutations in ESCC, contributing to MAPK pathway dysregulation [PMID:24686850](../papers/24686850.md)
