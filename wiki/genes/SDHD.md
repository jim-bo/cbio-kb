# SDHD

## Overview

SDHD (Succinate Dehydrogenase Complex Subunit D) encodes a subunit of the mitochondrial complex II (succinate dehydrogenase). Germline mutations in SDHD predispose to hereditary paraganglioma-pheochromocytoma syndrome and kidney cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SDHD referenced as a known germline kidney-cancer predisposition gene; contrasted against somatic nccRCC mutation profiles in the UTSW comprehensive study of non-clear cell renal carcinoma [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md)
