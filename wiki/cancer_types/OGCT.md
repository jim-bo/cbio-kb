# Ovarian Germ Cell Tumor (OGCT)

## Overview

Ovarian Germ Cell Tumor (parent OVARY).

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [makeanimpact_ccr_2023](../datasets/makeanimpact_ccr_2023.md): 83 female germ cell tumor patients had MSK-IMPACT (67 ovarian primaries); 59 had WES recapture on 62 tumor/normal pairs [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- Oncogenic mutations in [KIT](../genes/KIT.md), [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md), and [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) in a minority of female GCTs; mostly clonal by WES [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- Mean TMB was low (2.86; range 0–42.1); two ovarian GCTs with squamous transformation were TMB-H (28.1 and 42.1 mut/Mb) [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- Whole genome doubling observed in ≥13 patients [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- 17% (n=10) of female GCTs had a near-haploid genomic profile, mutually exclusive with 12p gain — a phenotype rarely observed in other cancer types [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- 51% of arm-level deletions in female GCTs contained a reciprocal amplification (reciprocal LOH), vs 4% in TCGA-OV [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).

## Subtypes

- Near-haploid genomic subtype (17%) distinct from the 12p-gain subtype [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- One TMB-H ovarian GCT with squamous transformation (42.1 mut/Mb) achieved complete response to [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md), ongoing at 34 months [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- Two PIK3CA-mutant ovarian GCT patients had no durable response to [alpelisib](../drugs/alpelisib.md), in contrast to a PIK3CA-mutant histiocytosis patient who did respond — suggesting lineage-specific conditioning of targeted therapy response [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- One ERBB2-amplified female GCT patient treated with [trastuzumab](../drugs/trastuzumab.md) had no durable response [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
