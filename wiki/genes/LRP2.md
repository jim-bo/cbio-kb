# LRP2

## Overview

LRP2 (Low Density Lipoprotein Receptor-Related Protein 2, also known as Megalin) encodes a large endocytic receptor involved in receptor-mediated endocytosis. In HCC, LRP2 is involved in the first recurrent gene fusion reported in that cancer type.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- LRP2 is the 3' partner in the ABCB11–LRP2 gene fusion, reported as the first recurrent gene fusion in HCC [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)
