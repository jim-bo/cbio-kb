# PDZD2

## Overview

PDZD2 (PDZ domain-containing protein 2) encodes a multi-PDZ domain scaffold protein. It has been identified as an alteration in acral melanoma genomic profiling studies.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Listed among genes with alterations in the acral melanoma (ALM) integrated genomic analysis cohort (34 patients; WGS + RNA-seq + SNP array) [PMID:28373299](../papers/28373299.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

## Therapeutic relevance

- No targeted therapy reported for PDZD2 alterations in this corpus [PMID:28373299](../papers/28373299.md).

## Open questions
