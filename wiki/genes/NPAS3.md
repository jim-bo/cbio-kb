# NPAS3

## Overview

NPAS3 (Neuronal PAS Domain Protein 3) is a basic helix-loop-helix/PAS domain transcription factor expressed predominantly in the brain. It maps to 14q13 and has been implicated in neuropsychiatric disorders. In glioblastoma, NPAS3 has been identified as a recurrent single-gene focal deletion target, suggesting a tumor-suppressor function in [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Single-gene focal deletion target in GBM; identified as a focal deletion locus in the TCGA multi-platform GBM analysis (n=543) [PMID:24120142](../papers/24120142.md)
