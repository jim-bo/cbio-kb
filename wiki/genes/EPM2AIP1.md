# EPM2AIP1

## Overview

EPM2AIP1 (EPM2A interacting protein 1) encodes a protein that interacts with laforin, the product of the EPM2A gene implicated in Lafora progressive myoclonus epilepsy. In cancer, EPM2AIP1 is notable for sharing a bidirectional promoter with [MLH1](../genes/MLH1.md) on chromosome 3p22.2; promoter methylation silencing MLH1 in microsatellite-instable (MSI) tumors co-silences EPM2AIP1.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Downregulated at mRNA and protein in MSI endometrial carcinoma tumors via shared promoter methylation with MLH1 [PMID:32059776](../papers/32059776.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Endometrial carcinoma (MSI subtype): co-silenced with MLH1 by promoter methylation [PMID:32059776](../papers/32059776.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-downregulated with MLH1, [PMS1](../genes/PMS1.md), and [PMS2](../genes/PMS2.md) in MSI endometrial carcinoma [PMID:32059776](../papers/32059776.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic agents reported in this corpus.

## Open questions

- The functional consequence of EPM2AIP1 loss in MSI cancer (beyond its co-silencing with MLH1) is not established.
