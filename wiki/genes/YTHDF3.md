# YTHDF3

## Overview

YTHDF3 (YTH N6-Methyladenosine RNA Binding Protein F3) is an m6A RNA methylation reader that regulates RNA metabolism. In salivary gland adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC), YTHDF3 has been identified as an intra-chromosomal fusion partner with [MYBL1](../genes/MYBL1.md), with the fusion deleting the C-terminal negative regulatory domain of [MYBL1](../genes/MYBL1.md) and driving its overexpression.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Intra-chromosomal fusion partner with MYBL1 in 2/102 ACC tumors; the MYBL1-YTHDF3 fusion deletes the C-terminal negative regulatory domain of MYBL1 and drives MYBL1 overexpression [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md)**: MYBL1-YTHDF3 intra-chromosomal fusion in 2/102 salivary adenoid cystic carcinomas, part of the spectrum of MYBL1 rearrangements in t(6;9)-negative ACC [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- MYBL1-YTHDF3 fusions are part of the MYBL1-rearranged ACC subclass (~14%), mutually exclusive with MYB-NFIB fusions (~53%) and the fusion-negative subgroup (~33%) [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)
