# ANKRD1

## Overview

ANKRD1 (Ankyrin Repeat Domain 1) encodes a transcriptional regulator and mechanosensing protein. It is a canonical downstream target of the YAP1/WWTR1 (YAP/TAZ) pathway and is functionally relevant in castration-resistant prostate cancer subtypes driven by Hippo pathway dysregulation.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ANKRD1 identified as a canonical YAP/TAZ target gene downregulated upon YAP1/WWTR1 double knockdown in CRPC-SCL (squamous-like) prostate cancer models [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Castration-resistant prostate cancer (CRPC-SCL): ANKRD1 expression dependent on YAP1/WWTR1 activity in the squamous-like CRPC subtype [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-regulated with CCN1 (CYR61), AXL, CCN2 (CTGF), AJUBA, CCND1 as YAP/TAZ target genes in CRPC-SCL [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- YAP1/WWTR1 double knockdown suppresses CRPC-SCL cell growth; ANKRD1 downregulation tracks with this growth suppression [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)
