# Astrocytoma (AST)

## Overview

Diffuse astrocytoma, a diffuse glioma subtype (parent [DIFG](DIFG.md)). In this corpus, refers to 1p19q-intact IDH-mutant astrocytomas.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [difg_msk_2023](../datasets/difg_msk_2023.md) — 59 (46.1%) 1p19q intact IDH-mutant astrocytomas in the MSKCC active-surveillance cohort [PMID:37910594](../papers/37910594.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations in 94% and [ATRX](../genes/ATRX.md) mutations in 77% of 1p19q intact IDH-mt astrocytomas in the MSK-IMPACT subcohort [PMID:37910594](../papers/37910594.md).
- [PIK3R1](../genes/PIK3R1.md) and [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md) mutations (with TP53 and broad CNV load) used to define "molecular grade-intermediate" in 1p19q intact tumors [PMID:37910594](../papers/37910594.md).
- CDKN2A/B homozygous deletion (molecular grade-high, n=4) drove markedly faster TVGR (19.17% vs 9.54% per 6 months, P<0.0001) [PMID:37910594](../papers/37910594.md).

## Subtypes

- Molecular grade (low / intermediate / high) based on MYCN/CDK4/PDGFRA amplification and CDKN2A/B deletion status [PMID:37910594](../papers/37910594.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- T2-FLAIR mismatch sign present in 38.9% of 1p19q intact astrocytomas and in 100% of molecular grade-high tumors; proposed as a candidate imaging biomarker [PMID:37910594](../papers/37910594.md).
