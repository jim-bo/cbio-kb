# Osteosarcoma (OS)

## Overview

Osteosarcoma is a primary malignant bone tumor classified under BONE in the OncoTree hierarchy. It can arise sporadically or as a radiation-associated sarcoma.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [sarcoma_msk_2023](../datasets/sarcoma_msk_2023.md): 12 RT-osteosarcoma patients and 76 sporadic osteosarcoma controls, sequenced on [MSK-IMPACT](../methods/msk-impact-panel.md) [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) LOF mutations/deletions in 42% of RT-OS vs 14% sporadic OS [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md)/[CDKN2B](../genes/CDKN2B.md) deletions in 33% of RT-OS [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- [PTEN](../genes/PTEN.md) truncating mutations/deletions in 17% of RT-OS vs 14% sporadic OS [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) amplification in 17% of RT-OS vs 7% sporadic OS [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- [PTPRT](../genes/PTPRT.md) missense mutations in 17% of RT-OS vs 7% sporadic OS [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- [FGA](../genes/FGA.md) of 32% in RT-OS [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- Translocations were more frequent in RT-OS (and RT-MPNST) compared to other RT-sarcoma histotypes [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- PIPseq cohort identified [TSC1](../genes/TSC1.md) splice-site c.2503-1G>C (mTOR-inhibitor target), [RAD51C](../genes/RAD51C.md) F8L (PARP-inhibitor target), PDGFRA/KDR overexpression (MTKI treated), MYC/MCL1/CCNE1 overexpression (BET- and CDK4/6 targets), [CUL4A](../genes/CUL4A.md) overexpression (NAE-inhibitor target), [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) splice-site c.1216-3A>G (germline, increased second-cancer risk), and [TNNT2](../genes/TNNT2.md) R141Q (ACMG secondary, dilated cardiomyopathy) in osteosarcoma patients [PMID:28007021](../papers/28007021.md)
- Germline WES of 372 pediatric cancer patients (Düsseldorf) included osteosarcoma cases; [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) LP/PV carriers (Li-Fraumeni syndrome) presented with osteosarcoma; three of six TP53 carriers (50%) developed SMNs including one osteosarcoma patient [PMID:29489754](../papers/29489754.md)
- OS (osteosarcoma) was a major 3D model test bed; 143B/MG63/MNNG-HOS/SAOS-2/U2OS/SJSA1 spheroids rarely exceeded 200–1,000 µm despite mean patient tumor length of 9.14 cm in patients ≤14 years (n=140); patient-derived tumoroids on 3D bioprinted decellularized scaffolds were proposed as a drug-screening platform for individualized therapy. [PMID:30643250](../papers/30643250.md)
- Osteosarcoma PDX models in PPTC cohort (n=261): TP53 altered in 82%; classifier-predicted TP53 pathway inactivation in 32/33 RNA-seq models; high breakpoint density (chromothripsis) in 97% (33/34); 30% with localized chromothripsis (≤4 chromosomes), 70% global (≥5 chromosomes); no significant TMB difference between diagnosis and relapse [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- Osteosarcoma (OS) was a major sarcoma histology in MAPPYACTS (sarcomas 37% of 787 patients); CDKN2A/CDKN2B deletions (157 events across sarcomas) were matched to CDK4/6 inhibitors (105 recommendations) [PMID:35292802](../papers/35292802.md)

## Subtypes

- **Radiation-associated osteosarcoma (RT-OS):** Higher [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) alteration rate (42% vs 14%) and enrichment for [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) and [PTPRT](../genes/PTPRT.md) alterations compared to sporadic OS. Median latency from radiation: 9.0 years [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- **Sporadic osteosarcoma:** Lower [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) alteration frequency (14%) and similar [PTEN](../genes/PTEN.md) rates [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- Genomic profiling supports histotype-specific treatment; RT-OS shows distinct alterations ([FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) amplification, [PTPRT](../genes/PTPRT.md) mutations) that may represent therapeutic targets [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
