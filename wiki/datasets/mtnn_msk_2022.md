# Mature T and NK Neoplasms (MSK, Blood Adv 2023)

## Overview

Retrospective MSK cohort of adults with nodal peripheral T-cell lymphoma treated with curative-intent CHOP-based chemotherapy, profiled with MSK-IMPACT to identify somatic predictors of outcome [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Composition

- 132 adults with nodal [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) (PTCL-NOS, [AITL](../cancer_types/AITL.md), PTCL-TFH, [ALK](../genes/ALK.md)± [ALCL](../cancer_types/ALCL.md)) plus [EATL](../cancer_types/EATL.md) and [MEITL](../cancer_types/MEITL.md), treated at MSK 2015–2020 [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- Histology mix: [AITL](../cancer_types/AITL.md) 47%, PTCL-NOS 27%, [ALK](../genes/ALK.md)− [ALCL](../cancer_types/ALCL.md) 11%, PTCL-TFH 7%, [ALK](../genes/ALK.md)+ [ALCL](../cancer_types/ALCL.md) 5%, [MEITL](../cancer_types/MEITL.md) 3%; nested cohorts for complete clinical data (n=87) and prospective analysis (n=72) [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Assays / panels (linked)

- [MSK-IMPACT](../methods/msk-impact-panel.md) across [IMPACT341](../methods/IMPACT341.md) / [IMPACT410](../methods/IMPACT410.md) / [IMPACT468](../methods/IMPACT468.md) versions depending on era [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md) — Johnson et al., [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations identify high-risk events for [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) treated with CHOP-based chemotherapy.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- Most frequently mutated genes: [TET2](../genes/TET2.md) 52%, [RHOA](../genes/RHOA.md) 30%, [DNMT3A](../genes/DNMT3A.md) 19%, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) 16%, [IDH2](../genes/IDH2.md) 11%; in [AITL](../cancer_types/AITL.md), 88% of [RHOA](../genes/RHOA.md) mutations were canonical G17V [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations (HR 3.1; 95% CI 1.4–6.8; P=.005) and TP53/17p deletions (HR 4.1; 95% CI 1.1–15.0; P=.03) were the only somatic aberrancies correlating with inferior PFS; median PFS 4.5 vs 10.5 months (TP53-mut vs TP53-wt) [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions (n=9) correlated with inferior [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) (HR 12.1; 95% CI 2.8–52.0; P<.001); 3/4 [MEITL](../cancer_types/MEITL.md) cases carried [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) aberrancies [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
