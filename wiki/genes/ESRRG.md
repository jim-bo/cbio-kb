# ESRRG

## Overview

ESRRG (Estrogen Related Receptor Gamma) is a nuclear receptor transcription factor. In cancer genomics, ESRRG has been identified as a noncanonical [MYB](../genes/MYB.md) fusion partner in sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma, and separately as the recipient in a novel ESRRG::[DNM3](../genes/DNM3.md) fusion in the same tumor type.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel ESRRGex3::DNM3ex14 fusion detected in 1 maxillary sinus metatypical adenoid cystic carcinoma infiltrating the maxilla and arising in respiratory epithelial adenomatoid hamartoma; not previously described in adenoid cystic carcinoma [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)
- ESRRG also identified as a noncanonical MYB fusion partner in sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma; diagnostic workup with FISH recommended when canonical MYB::[NFIB](../genes/NFIB.md) fusion is absent [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma: novel ESRRG::DNM3 fusion in a single metatypical case; ESRRG rearrangement detected by FISH [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- ESRRG::DNM3 and noncanonical MYB fusions co-characterize a metatypical histological subset of sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported in corpus; pairing RNA-seq panels with FISH for ESRRG is recommended for diagnostic workup of metatypical sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Open questions

- ESRRG::DNM3 was observed in only 1 case; generalizability of the metatypical-histology association requires larger cohorts [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)
