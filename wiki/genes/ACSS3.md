# ACSS3

## Overview

ACSS3 (Acyl-CoA Short-Chain Synthetase Family Member 3) encodes an enzyme in short-chain fatty acid metabolism. In hepatocellular carcinoma, ACSS3 expression is regulated downstream of CTNNB1 (beta-catenin) mutations, suggesting a role in the metabolic reprogramming characteristic of Wnt-driven HCC.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ACSS3 protein concordantly upregulated at mRNA and protein in CTNNB1-mutant HCCs in a Chinese HCC proteogenomics cohort (n=159) [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC): ACSS3 upregulation associated with CTNNB1-mutant subtype; concordant mRNA and protein elevation [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-elevated with GLUL and AMACR in CTNNB1-mutant HCC [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)
