# USP22

## Overview

USP22 (Ubiquitin Specific Peptidase 22) is a deubiquitinase and component of the SAGA histone acetyltransferase complex involved in transcriptional regulation. In lung cancer, USP22 has emerged as a novel amplification target identified by the TCGA Pan-Lung comprehensive genomic characterization study across 1,144 [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) cases.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a novel amplification target in lung adenocarcinoma ([LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md)) or squamous cell carcinoma ([LUSC](../cancer_types/LUSC.md)) in the TCGA Pan-Lung comprehensive genomic characterization of 1,144 cases [PMID:27158780](../papers/27158780.md).
