# POC5

## Overview

POC5 encodes a centriole assembly protein. In cancer genomics it is notable as a fusion partner in POC5-PRKD1, a novel fusion identified in polymorphous low-grade adenocarcinoma (PLGA) of the tongue.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- POC5-PRKD1 novel fusion identified in polymorphous low-grade adenocarcinoma of the tongue in the MET500 pan-cancer metastatic cohort (n=500) [PMID:28783718](../papers/28783718.md)
