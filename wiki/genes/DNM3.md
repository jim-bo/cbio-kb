# DNM3

## Overview

DNM3 (Dynamin 3) encodes a GTPase involved in membrane tubulation and endocytosis. In sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma (AdCC), DNM3 was identified as a novel fusion partner of [ESRRG](../genes/ESRRG.md) (Estrogen Related Receptor Gamma), producing a chimeric transcript not previously described in AdCC.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel ESRRGex3::DNM3ex14 fusion identified in 1 maxillary sinus metatypical AdCC infiltrating the maxilla and arising in respiratory epithelial adenomatoid hamartoma; the fusion is in-frame and not previously described in AdCC or any other cancer context [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Adenoid cystic carcinoma of the sinonasal tract: novel fusion partner in ESRRG::DNM3 chimera (1/88 cases); associated with metatypical histology arising in an unusual hamartomatous background [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- The ESRRG::DNM3 fusion is distinct from the canonical [MYB](../genes/MYB.md)::[NFIB](../genes/NFIB.md) and [MYBL1](../genes/MYBL1.md)::NFIB fusions; occurs in a metatypical AdCC without canonical MYB or MYBL1 rearrangement [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No targeted therapy data. The functional consequence of the ESRRG::DNM3 fusion is unknown; it is unclear whether it activates ESRRG transcriptional targets or disrupts DNM3 endocytic function [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Open questions

- Single case observation limits functional and clinical characterization; whether ESRRG::DNM3 drives oncogenesis through ESRRG transcriptional activation (analogous to MYB::NFIB) or another mechanism is unresolved [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)
