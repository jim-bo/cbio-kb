# Sarcoma, NOS (SARCNOS)

## Overview

Sarcoma, NOS (not otherwise specified) is a diagnostic category for sarcomas that cannot be further classified into a specific histologic subtype, classified under Soft Tissue Sarcoma in OncoTree (parent: SOFT_TISSUE). This category encompasses undifferentiated and unclassifiable spindle-cell, round-cell, or pleomorphic sarcomas.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [sarcoma_ucla_2024](../datasets/sarcoma_ucla_2024.md): represented in the 24-subtype UCLA sarcoma PDTO biobank. SARC0117, a metastatic undifferentiated spindle cell sarcoma (USS), is classified here and carried a [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md) hotspot mutation (reported as "H1074L", canonical H1047L). (194 specimens from 126 patients, Feb 2018–May 2022). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md): hotspot mutation (H1047L, rendered as "H1074L" in the manuscript) in SARC0117 (metastatic USS/SARCNOS) drove broad PI3K/mTOR sensitivity ([alpelisib](../drugs/alpelisib.md), apitolisib, copanlisib, BGT226, vistusertib). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
- MET500 metastatic cohort: soft-tissue sarcoma (NOS) was the third most common lineage (42/500, 8.4%); a novel GREB1-NR4A3 activating fusion was identified in uterine leiomyosarcoma and a CIC-CITED1 fusion in undifferentiated high-grade sarcoma; NF1 was the most commonly fused tumor suppressor (n=18) across the cohort. [PMID:28783718](../papers/28783718.md)
- TCGA Sarcoma working group profiled 206 adult soft tissue sarcomas across 6 histologies (LMS, DDLPS, UPS, MFS, SS, MPNST); pan-sarcoma mutation burden was low (mean 1.06 mut/Mb) with only 3 pan-cohort SMGs (TP53, ATRX, RB1), and complex karyotypes were SCNA-driven [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md)

## Subtypes

- Undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma (UPS; overlaps with [MFH](../cancer_types/MFH.md))
- Undifferentiated spindle cell sarcoma (USS)
- Undifferentiated round cell sarcoma (when not meeting criteria for Ewing or other defined entities)

## Therapeutic landscape

- [alpelisib](../drugs/alpelisib.md): SARC0117 (metastatic USS/SARCNOS with [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md) H1047L) was among the strongest PI3K/mTOR responders in the UCLA PDTO screen; broad sensitivity across [alpelisib](../drugs/alpelisib.md), apitolisib, copanlisib, BGT226, vistusertib. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
