# ADAMTS13

## Overview

ADAMTS13 (A Disintegrin And Metalloproteinase with Thrombospondin Motifs 13) encodes a von Willebrand factor-cleaving metalloprotease best known for its role in thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura. In the cancer genomics corpus, ADAMTS13 has been identified as a somatically mutated gene in the axon-guidance pathway in adenoid cystic carcinoma ([ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md)), contributing to the hypothesis that disruption of extracellular matrix and axon-guidance signalling underpins the perineural invasion phenotype of this cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Single-tumour somatic mutation in the axon-guidance pathway identified in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACYC); the axon-guidance pathway was disrupted in 56% of tumours in the cohort [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **ACYC (Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma):** ADAMTS13 was among a set of axon-guidance genes somatically mutated in ACYC [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-mutated with other axon-guidance pathway genes ([ADAM2](../genes/ADAM2.md), [ADAMTS5](../genes/ADAMTS5.md), [PLXNA1](../genes/PLXNA1.md), [SLIT1](../genes/SLIT1.md), [SRGAP2](../genes/SRGAP2.md), [SRGAP3](../genes/SRGAP3.md), [SEMA3G](../genes/SEMA3G.md)) in ACYC [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting established. Axon-guidance pathway inhibition is proposed as a candidate therapeutic strategy in ACYC [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Open questions

- The functional significance of ADAMTS13 mutation in ACYC perineural invasion is not established and requires further investigation [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)
