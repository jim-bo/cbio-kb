# IGF2BP3

## Overview

IGF2BP3 (Insulin Like Growth Factor 2 mRNA Binding Protein 3) is an RNA-binding protein that stabilizes target mRNAs, including [IGF2](../genes/IGF2.md) and [MYC](../genes/MYC.md). It functions as a cancer-testis (CT) antigen, with expression normally restricted to germline and placental tissue but aberrantly reactivated in multiple cancers. Its immunogenicity — demonstrated by in vitro and in vivo CTL induction in esophageal cancer — and recurrent expression across colorectal tumors position it as a candidate immunotherapy target particularly for MSS (microsatellite-stable) tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Recurrent cancer-testis (CT) antigen expressed in 51% of [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) tumor-NAT pairs (n=110) in CPTAC proteogenomics; has demonstrated in vitro/in vivo CTL induction in esophageal cancer; proposed as an MSI-status-independent immunotherapy target for MSS colon tumors that are poor candidates for checkpoint blockade [PMID:31031003](../papers/31031003.md)
- IGF2BP3 is among chromoplexy-mediated fusion/enhancer-hijacking events in thyroid adenocarcinoma in the PCAWG pan-cancer WGS cohort [PMID:32025007](../papers/32025007.md)
- IGF2BP3 is a cancer-testis antigen observed in >10% of endometrial tumors and is highly upregulated in serous EC; identified as a known immunogenic peptide source [PMID:32059776](../papers/32059776.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **COAD (Colon Adenocarcinoma):** CT antigen expressed in 51% of tumor-NAT pairs; candidate immunotherapy target for MSS COAD [PMID:31031003](../papers/31031003.md)
- **[ESCA](../cancer_types/ESCA.md) (Esophageal Carcinoma):** Demonstrated CTL induction in vitro and in vivo, supporting immunogenicity [PMID:31031003](../papers/31031003.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Candidate neoantigen/CT-antigen vaccine target for MSS colorectal cancer, where checkpoint blockade and neoantigen vaccines are less effective; CTL responses documented in esophageal cancer models [PMID:31031003](../papers/31031003.md)

## Open questions

- Whether IGF2BP3-directed CTL responses observed in esophageal cancer translate to colorectal or other cancer types requires prospective validation.
