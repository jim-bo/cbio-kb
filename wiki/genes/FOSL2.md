# FOSL2

## Overview

FOSL2 ([FOS](../genes/FOS.md) Like 2, AP-1 Transcription Factor Subunit; also known as FRA-2) encodes a leucine zipper protein that heterodimerizes with [JUN](../genes/JUN.md) proteins to form AP-1 transcription factor complexes. As part of the AP-1 family, FOSL2 is implicated in the CRPC-SCL (stem cell-like) molecular subtype of metastatic castration-resistant prostate cancer, where AP-1 transcription factors collectively drive lineage plasticity.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- AP-1 family member including FOSL2 identified as part of the TF network enriched in CRPC-SCL accessible chromatin peaks; CRPC-SCL is the second-most-common CRPC subtype (~28%) among 366 patients across SU2C and WCM cohorts, associated with shorter time on next-generation ARSI [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [PRAD](../cancer_types/PRAD.md) / [PRSCC](../cancer_types/PRSCC.md): AP-1 family (including FOSL2) upregulated in CRPC-SCL subtype identified by integrative ATAC-seq/RNA-seq profiling [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [FOSL1](../genes/FOSL1.md), [FOS](../genes/FOS.md), [FOSB](../genes/FOSB.md), [JUN](../genes/JUN.md), [JUNB](../genes/JUNB.md), [JUND](../genes/JUND.md) as part of the broader AP-1 family TF network in CRPC-SCL [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- T-5224 (c-Fos/AP-1 DNA-binding inhibitor) and [verteporfin](../drugs/verteporfin.md) selectively reduce CRPC-SCL cell growth, nominating AP-1 members including FOSL2 as candidate targets [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Open questions

- Individual contribution of FOSL2 vs other AP-1 members to CRPC-SCL chromatin reprogramming is not individually quantified [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)
