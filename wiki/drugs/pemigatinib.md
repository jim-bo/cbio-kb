# pemigatinib

## Overview

Pemigatinib is a selective, reversible inhibitor of FGFR1/2/3, developed for [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) fusion/rearrangement-positive intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma (iCCA). It targets the [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) kinase and received FDA accelerated approval for previously treated [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) fusion/rearrangement-positive iCCA based on the FIGHT-202 phase II trial.

## Evidence in the corpus

- [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) fusion/rearrangement-positive iCCA: FIGHT-202 phase II ORR 35.5%, mPFS 6.9 mo, mOS 21.1 mo; positioned as second-line targeted therapy after [gemcitabine](../drugs/gemcitabine.md) + [cisplatin](../drugs/cisplatin.md) failure in cholangiocarcinoma (IHCH/EHCH/CHOL); secondary on-target [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) kinase-domain resistance mutations (N550, V565) emerge in ~60% of patients on reversible inhibitors [PMID:25526346](../papers/25526346.md)

## Resistance mechanisms

- Secondary [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) kinase-domain mutations at N550 and V565 emerge in ~60% of patients on reversible FGFR inhibitors including pemigatinib; covalent inhibitor [futibatinib](../drugs/futibatinib.md) retains activity against these variants [PMID:25526346](../papers/25526346.md)
- [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md)/[CDKN2B](../genes/CDKN2B.md) deletion emerges as an acquired-resistance mechanism in [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) fusion-positive iCCA after FGFR inhibitor therapy [PMID:25526346](../papers/25526346.md)
- Wild-type [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md) bypass signaling contributes to FGFR-inhibitor resistance; co-inhibition of [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md) enhances FGFR-inhibitor response in preclinical models [PMID:25526346](../papers/25526346.md)
