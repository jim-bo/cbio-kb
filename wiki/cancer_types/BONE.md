# Bone (BONE)

## Overview

BONE is the OncoTree umbrella category for primary cancers arising in bone tissue, encompassing osteosarcoma, chondrosarcoma, chordoma, giant cell tumor of bone, Ewing sarcoma, and other bone-primary malignancies. In cBioPortal cohorts this tag is sometimes used as a cohort-level grouping for all bone sarcomas.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [sarcoma_msk_2022](../datasets/sarcoma_msk_2022.md): 1,105 bone sarcomas (14.7% of 7,494) profiled by FoundationOne Heme across 44 histologies [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).
- [sarcoma_mskcc_2022](../datasets/sarcoma_mskcc_2022.md): bone and soft tissue sarcomas combined (2,138 total) profiled by MSK-IMPACT across 45 OncoTree entities [PMID:35705560](../papers/35705560.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- In a 7,494-sarcoma cohort, bone sarcomas (14.7%) showed distinct genomic profiles from soft tissue sarcomas; p53 pathway and Rb pathway alterations were recurrent across bone histotypes; WGD in ~50% of osteosarcomas; 4q12 amplicon (KDR/PDGFRA/KIT) in osteosarcoma (10.7%) [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).
- In a 2,138-sarcoma MSK-IMPACT cohort, [TERT](../genes/TERT.md) amplification was a hallmark of intimal sarcoma (44%); MDM2/CDK4 co-amplification defined WDLS/DDLS; [STAG2](../genes/STAG2.md) mutations present in 10% of Ewing sarcomas; IDH1/2 in 27% of chondrosarcomas [PMID:35705560](../papers/35705560.md).

## Subtypes

- [Osteosarcoma (OS)](../cancer_types/OS.md), [Chondrosarcoma (CHS)](../cancer_types/CHS.md), [Chordoma (CHDM)](../cancer_types/CHDM.md), [Ewing Sarcoma (ES)](../cancer_types/ES.md), [Dedifferentiated Chondrosarcoma (DDCHS)](../cancer_types/DDCHS.md), [Giant Cell Tumor of Bone (GCTB)](../cancer_types/GCTB.md), [Intimal Sarcoma (INTS)](../cancer_types/INTS.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- CDK4/MDM2 amplification in WDLS/DDLS → [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) inhibitors (Level 3A) [PMID:35705560](../papers/35705560.md).
- IDH1/2 mutations in chondrosarcoma → IDH-inhibitors (Level 3B) [PMID:35705560](../papers/35705560.md).
