# MERTK

## Overview

MERTK (MER proto-oncogene, tyrosine kinase) is a receptor tyrosine kinase of the TAM family (TYRO3, [AXL](../genes/AXL.md), MERTK). It plays roles in phagocytosis, immune regulation, and cell survival signaling. In oncology, MERTK has been observed as a fusion partner in pan-cancer RNA-seq studies, forming recurrent kinase fusions with partners such as [TMEM87B](../genes/TMEM87B.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Recurrent singleton-pattern kinase fusion (TMEM87B–MERTK) identified in pan-cancer RNA-seq fusion analysis across 9,624 TCGA samples; flagged as a gene without a wiki entity at time of publication. [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md)
