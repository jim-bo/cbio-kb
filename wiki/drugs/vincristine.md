# vincristine

## Overview

Vinca alkaloid microtubule inhibitor; the "O" (Oncovin) in CHOP.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Component of CHOP/CHOEP/EPOCH/BV-CH(E)P regimens in 132 MSK nodal [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) patients; [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation identified patients with inferior PFS (HR 3.1, P=.005) on vincristine-containing induction [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Resistance mechanisms

- [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations and 17p deletions associate with inferior PFS on CHOP-based induction [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

- [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md), [AITL](../cancer_types/AITL.md), [ALCL](../cancer_types/ALCL.md)
