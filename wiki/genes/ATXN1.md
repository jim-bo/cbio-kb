# ATXN1

## Overview

ATXN1 (Ataxin-1) encodes a polyglutamine-containing protein involved in transcriptional regulation; germline CAG repeat expansions cause spinocerebellar ataxia type 1. In cancer genomics, ATXN1 was identified by MutSigCV analysis as a significantly mutated gene in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma, though its functional role in PDA biology remains to be validated.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- MutSigCV-significant mutation in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (PDA); functional role in PDA biology awaits validation [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)
