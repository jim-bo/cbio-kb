# becotatug vedotin

## Overview

Becotatug vedotin (MRG003) is an antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) targeting [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md), delivering a cytotoxic payload to EGFR-overexpressing tumor cells. It has shown activity in R/M nasopharyngeal carcinoma (NPC) in patients who had received prior platinum chemotherapy and anti-PD-1/PD-L1 therapy.

## Evidence in the corpus

- In R/M NPC post platinum + anti-PD-1 (NCT05126719, n=61): ORR 39.3% (dose level 1) / 55.2% (dose level 2); separate randomized study reported ORR 30.2% vs 11.2% for standard chemotherapy; grade ≥3 TRAE 45.3% [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
