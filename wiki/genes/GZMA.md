# GZMA

## Overview

GZMA encodes granzyme A, a serine protease released by cytotoxic lymphocytes.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Effector cytotoxic component of the 20-gene ICR signature used in the AC-ICAM colon cancer atlas [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) — ICR component; ICR-high tumors had improved [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) (HR 0.54, P=0.0095) [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [GNLY](../genes/GNLY.md), [PRF1](../genes/PRF1.md), [GZMB](../genes/GZMB.md), [GZMH](../genes/GZMH.md) [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
