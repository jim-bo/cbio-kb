# MUC13

## Overview

MUC13 is a transmembrane mucin glycoprotein. In pancreatic cancer, it is upregulated at the protein level relative to normal tissue and has been identified as a candidate early-detection biomarker enriched even in early-stage tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Mucin glycoprotein upregulated in [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) vs normal tissue; enriched even in early-stage tumors and among the proteins nominated as candidate serum/pancreatic-juice early-detection biomarkers via proteogenomic profiling [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md):** MUC13 protein overexpression observed in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma relative to normal adjacent tissue; proposed as an early-detection marker [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-overexpressed with [MUC1](../genes/MUC1.md) and [MUC5AC](../genes/MUC5AC.md) in [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) tumors vs normal tissue; all three mucin glycoproteins enriched in early-stage disease [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting in the corpus. MUC13 overexpression enriched in early-stage tumors makes it a candidate liquid-biopsy early-detection marker; functional validation against serum and pancreatic-juice specimens is required [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md).

## Open questions

- MUC13 has not been validated as a standalone serum biomarker in prospective cohorts; cited findings are from a discovery-phase proteogenomic landscape study [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md).
