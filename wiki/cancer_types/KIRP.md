# Kidney Renal Papillary Cell Carcinoma / KIRP (TCGA)

## Overview

KIRP is the TCGA cohort identifier for kidney renal papillary cell carcinoma. The closest OncoTree equivalent is [PRCC](PRCC.md) (Papillary Renal Cell Carcinoma). This page covers findings specifically attributed to the TCGA KIRP cohort in the corpus.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- TCGA KIRP cohort: included as one of 33 cancer types in the fusion landscape study; subset of the PanCancer Atlas ([kirp_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018](../datasets/kirp_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018.md)).

## Recurrent alterations

- Pan-cancer fusion study (9,624 TCGA samples) found KIRP has a median of 0 fusions per sample; 6% to 28% (highest in KIRP) of kinase-partner fusions were over-expression outliers, suggesting promoter-swap mechanisms are relatively frequent in papillary [RCC](../cancer_types/RCC.md) [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md).

## Subtypes

- Papillary RCC (type 1 and type 2) is molecularly distinct from clear cell and chromophobe RCC; KIRP (TCGA) corresponds approximately to OncoTree [PRCC](../cancer_types/PRCC.md).
