# IBI-310

## Overview

IBI-310 is an investigational anti-CTLA-4 monoclonal antibody targeting [CTLA4](../genes/CTLA4.md). It is evaluated in combination with sintilimab (anti-PD-1) for nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)) and other solid tumors.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Anti-CTLA-4 IBI-310 in combination with [sintilimab](../drugs/sintilimab.md) is listed as an investigational strategy targeting compensatory CTLA-4 checkpoint upregulation in PD-1/PD-L1-refractory NPC [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
