# TMEM87B

## Overview

TMEM87B is a transmembrane protein-coding gene identified as the 5' fusion partner for [MERTK](../genes/MERTK.md) in pan-cancer fusion analyses. Its biological function is not well characterized. TMEM87B-MERTK fusions represent recurrent singleton-pattern kinase fusions identified in the TCGA pan-cancer RNA-seq fusion landscape study.
