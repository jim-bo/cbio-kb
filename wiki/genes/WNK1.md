# WNK1

## Overview

WNK1 (with-no-lysine kinase 1) is a serine-threonine kinase involved in ion transport regulation and cell signaling. In the TCGA pan-cancer fusion landscape, WNK1 is involved in recurrent WNK-family gene fusions. ERC1-WNK1 has been independently reported in a Chinese esophageal squamous cell carcinoma ([ESCC](../cancer_types/ESCC.md)) cohort, and WNK-family fusions are associated with elevated WNK mRNA without copy-number amplification.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified in WNK-family fusions across 23 samples in the TCGA pan-cancer RNA-seq landscape; WNK fusions are associated with higher WNK mRNA without copy-number amplification. ERC1-WNK1 was independently validated in a Chinese ESCC cohort [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md)
