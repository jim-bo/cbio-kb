# BAT6026

## Overview

BAT6026 is an agonist monoclonal antibody targeting OX40 ([TNFRSF4](../genes/TNFRSF4.md)), a T-cell co-stimulatory receptor. It is designed to enhance anti-tumor T-cell responses. BAT6026 was in phase I investigation in solid tumors including NPC; no significant NPC-specific activity has been reported to date.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Cited among investigational OX40 agonists in phase I for NPC; no significant NPC activity reported [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
