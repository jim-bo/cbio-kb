# tifcemalimab

## Overview

Tifcemalimab (JS004) is an investigational anti-BTLA monoclonal antibody targeting [BTLA](../genes/BTLA.md) (B and T lymphocyte attenuator), an inhibitory receptor implicated in T-cell exhaustion. It is evaluated in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Tifcemalimab (JS004) anti-BTLA antibody under clinical investigation for R/M NPC; BTLA is implicated in T-cell exhaustion in NPC alongside CTLA-4, LAG-3, and TIM-3 as compensatory checkpoint mechanisms following PD-1/PD-L1 therapy [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
