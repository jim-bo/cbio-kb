# NKX3-1

## Overview

NKX3-1 is a homeobox transcription factor. In the corpus it appears as a gene whose alterations associate with local intracranial progression of [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) brain metastases.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Alterations enriched in local intracranial progressors after craniotomy + RT for [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) brain metastases (16% vs 3% in non-progressors; p=0.044) [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Lung adenocarcinoma ([LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md)) — associated with local intracranial progression [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Local-progression group also enriched for [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) loss [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic link reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Mechanism linking NKX3-1 alteration to local CNS recurrence is untested.
