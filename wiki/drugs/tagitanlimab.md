# tagitanlimab

## Overview

Tagitanlimab is an investigational anti-PD-L1 monoclonal antibody targeting [CD274](../genes/CD274.md). It is evaluated in combination with chemotherapy for recurrent/metastatic nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Phase III tagitanlimab + gemcitabine/cisplatin in R/M NPC (NCT05294172, n=358): PFS HR 0.47; ORR 81.7%; median DOR 11.7 months; grade ≥3 TRAE 3.9% [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
