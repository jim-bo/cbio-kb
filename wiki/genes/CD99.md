# CD99

## Overview

CD99 is a cell-surface glycoprotein that is constitutively highly expressed in Ewing sarcoma and is used as a diagnostic marker for the disease. Its expression is independent of the EWS::FLI1 fusion oncogene.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Constitutive Ewing sarcoma marker; expression unaffected by EWS::FLI1 introduction in heMSCs (cell-of-origin model) [PMID:25186949](../papers/25186949.md)
