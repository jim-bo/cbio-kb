# PATZ1

## Overview

PATZ1 (POZ/BTB and AT hook containing zinc finger 1) encodes a transcription factor that can act as a fusion partner for [EWSR1](../genes/EWSR1.md) in undifferentiated small round cell sarcomas. EWSR1::PATZ1 fusions define a subtype within the WHO 2020 "round cell sarcoma with EWSR1-non-ETS fusions" category.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Discussed as a characterised EWSR1-non-ETS fusion partner in the WHO 2020 "round cell sarcoma with EWSR1-non-ETS fusions" category, alongside EWSR1::[BEND2](../genes/BEND2.md), EWSR1::[NFATC2](../genes/NFATC2.md), and EWSR1::[POU2AF3](../genes/POU2AF3.md); mentioned in the differential diagnostic context of a bladder EWSR1::BEND2 sarcoma case [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).
- Located on 22q11.21-q11.23; deleted in 77% of SWNTS-SWN cases as part of a coordinated NF2/SMARCB1 neighborhood deletion with coordinated expression loss; proposed as an additional contributor to schwannomatosis pathogenesis [PMID:33025139](../papers/33025139.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Undifferentiated small round cell sarcoma ([SARC](../cancer_types/SARC.md)): EWSR1::PATZ1 fusions are a WHO-recognized subtype distinct from classical Ewing sarcoma [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- EWSR1::PATZ1 is mutually exclusive with canonical EWSR1::[FLI1](../genes/FLI1.md) Ewing sarcoma fusions; part of the EWSR1-non-ETS group [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No specific targeted therapy reported; Ewing-sarcoma-like chemotherapy protocols are used in the absence of dedicated trials for EWSR1-non-ETS fusion sarcomas [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).

## Open questions

- Whether EWSR1::PATZ1 and related EWSR1-non-ETS fusions (BEND2, NFATC2, POU2AF3) should be classified as distinct entities or merged based on site/morphology remains an open WHO classification question [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).
