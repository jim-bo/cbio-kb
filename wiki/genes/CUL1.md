# CUL1

## Overview

CUL1 (Cullin 1) is the scaffold protein of the SCF (SKP1-CUL1-F-box) E3 ubiquitin ligase complex. Copy number loss of SCF complex genes including CUL1 induces chromosomal instability (CIN) in colorectal cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Invariable core member of the SCF E3 ubiquitin ligase complex; copy number loss of SCF genes ([SKP1](../genes/SKP1.md), CUL1, [RBX1](../genes/RBX1.md)) induces CIN in CRC [PMID:36334560](../papers/36334560.md)
- CUL1-BRAF fusion identified in an MSS metastatic colorectal cancer case as an activating BRAF rearrangement; represents one of two BRAF fusion events (alongside AGAP3-BRAF) in a cohort of 1,134 colorectal adenocarcinomas [PMID:29316426](../papers/29316426.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- CRC: SCF complex gene copy number loss associated with CIN [PMID:36334560](../papers/36334560.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Copy number loss co-occurs with SKP1 and RBX1 loss in CRC [PMID:36334560](../papers/36334560.md)
