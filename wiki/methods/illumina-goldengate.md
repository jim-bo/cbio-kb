# Illumina GoldenGate

## Overview

An assay platform used for high-throughput analysis of DNA methylation or SNP genotyping.

## Used by

- Used for DNA methylation profiling across 1,505 CpG sites in 206 glioblastoma cases [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).

## Notes

- Identified *[MGMT](../genes/MGMT.md)* promoter methylation as a key feature in glioblastoma, particularly in the context of treatment response and hypermutator phenotypes [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).
