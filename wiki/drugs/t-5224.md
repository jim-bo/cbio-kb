# T-5224

## Overview

T-5224 is an investigational small-molecule inhibitor that blocks c-Fos/AP-1 DNA-binding activity. It has been studied preclinically as a tool compound targeting the AP-1 transcription factor complex. T-5224 has not entered clinical trials for prostate cancer and has no regulatory approval. Canonical drug ontology source: corpus (unverified — see `schema/ontology/_observed.md`).

## Evidence in the corpus

- T-5224 (c-Fos/AP-1 DNA-binding inhibitor) preferentially inhibited proliferation of CRPC-SCL organoid models (MSKPCa3, DU145) versus CRPC-AR models (MSKPCa2, 22Rv1) in vitro, nominating the AP-1/YAP-TAZ axis as a therapeutic vulnerability in the CRPC-SCL subtype (~28% of 366 CRPC patients in the SU2C/WCM cohort); T-5224 has not yet been tested in prostate cancer clinical trials [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)
