# ctDx Lung

## Overview

Resolution Bioscience ctDx Lung targeted next-generation sequencing panel for plasma cell-free DNA in non-small-cell lung cancer [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md).

## Used by

- [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md) — used to profile plasma ctDNA from 1,127 advanced [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) patients across MSK and GenesisCare (Sydney); ctDNA detection rate was 64% (722/1,127), and ctDNA detection was independently associated with shorter [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) (HR 2.05, 95% CI 1.74–2.42, P<0.001) [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md).
- [PMID:39147831](../papers/39147831.md) — ctDx Lung (21-gene panel) used in an international generalizability cohort of 463 advanced [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) patients from MSK and GenesisCare (Sydney), demonstrating transferability of the random survival forest ctDNA-VTE model (c-index 0.67) across assay platforms [PMID:39147831](../papers/39147831.md).

## Notes

- Operationally, only 37/1,919 (2%) ctDx Lung or MSK-ACCESS samples failed vs 13% of tissue sequencing attempts; turnaround was 11 days (IQR 9–14) for ctDx Lung/MSK-ACCESS vs 33 days for MSK-IMPACT [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md).
- 25% of patients had genomic alterations detected only in ctDNA (not in time-matched tissue), enriched for subclonal resistance drivers such as [RICTOR](../genes/RICTOR.md) and [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md) [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md).
- ctDx Lung has a small genomic footprint, which limits TMB correlation analyses [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md).
