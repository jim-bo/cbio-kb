# TRIM54

## Overview

TRIM54 (Tripartite Motif Containing 54) is a muscle-expressed E3 ubiquitin ligase. In glioblastoma, TRIM54 has been identified as a novel fusion partner for [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md), generating the FGFR3-TRIM54 fusion. This fusion was discovered in a large [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) PDX panel and represents a structural variant of potential therapeutic relevance, analogous to the canonical FGFR3-TACC3 fusion, though its functional characterization in GBM remains limited.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel FGFR3-TRIM54 fusion identified in a GBM PDX model within a panel of 84 glioblastoma PDX; discovered alongside the canonical FGFR3-TACC3 fusion (n=3) and another novel FGFR3-CALCOCO1 fusion [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md): FGFR3-TRIM54 fusion in glioblastoma PDX; potentially targetable with FGFR inhibitors [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Found in the same GBM PDX cohort as FGFR3-TACC3 (n=3) and FGFR3-CALCOCO1 fusions, and PTPRZ1-MET and CHTOP-NTRK1 fusions [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- As an FGFR3 fusion partner, TRIM54 involvement in FGFR3-TRIM54 may confer sensitivity to FGFR inhibitors, by analogy with the canonical FGFR3-TACC3 fusion [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Open questions

- Functional oncogenicity of FGFR3-TRIM54 fusion not yet demonstrated; FGFR kinase-domain integrity and transformation capacity relative to FGFR3-TACC3 are unknown.
- Prevalence in primary patient GBM tumors is not established.
