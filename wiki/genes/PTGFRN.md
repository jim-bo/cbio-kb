# PTGFRN

## Overview

PTGFRN (prostaglandin F2 receptor inhibitor) encodes a membrane protein involved in prostaglandin signaling and tetraspanin-enriched membrane domains. In cancer, PTGFRN has been identified as carrying clustered/damaging mutations in small cell lung cancer, with conservation of this alteration across human and murine SCLC models.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Clustered/damaging mutations in small cell lung cancer (SCLC); also mutated in murine SCLC models, suggesting biological relevance across species. [PMID:26168399](../papers/26168399.md)
