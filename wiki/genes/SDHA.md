# SDHA

## Overview

SDHA encodes the catalytic flavoprotein subunit of the succinate dehydrogenase complex; germline and somatic loss-of-function mutations cause SDH-deficient tumors including a subset of [GIST](../cancer_types/GIST.md) and paraganglioma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SDHA was assessed for germline and somatic mutation in the SDH-deficient GIST subset (n=26) of the MSK [gist_msk_2023](../datasets/gist_msk_2023.md) cohort [PMID:37477937](../papers/37477937.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [GIST](../cancer_types/GIST.md) — SDHA mutation contributes to the SDH-deficient GIST subset [PMID:37477937](../papers/37477937.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Not specifically reported in the corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- Not directly therapeutically targeted in the corpus.

## Open questions
