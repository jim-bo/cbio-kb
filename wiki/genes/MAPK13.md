# MAPK13

## Overview

MAPK13 (Mitogen-Activated Protein Kinase 13; also known as p38delta or SAPK4) is a member of the p38 MAPK family involved in stress response, inflammation, and cell differentiation. It is activated by cellular stressors and pro-inflammatory cytokines. p38delta has been reported to have both pro- and anti-tumor functions depending on cellular context.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Elevated kinase activity by KSEA (kinase-substrate enrichment analysis) in TP53-mutant HCCs in a European proteogenomic cohort (n=122); classified as a kinase with increased activity in TP53-mutant or overall [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) context; referred to as p38delta [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)**: MAPK13 (p38delta) activity is elevated by KSEA in TP53-mutant HCCs, part of a broader kinase activation landscape in aggressive [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) subtypes [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-elevated with [TTK](../genes/TTK.md), [NEK2](../genes/NEK2.md), [PRKD2](../genes/PRKD2.md), [GSK3B](../genes/GSK3B.md), CDK1/2/5, [AURKA](../genes/AURKA.md), and [EIF2AK2](../genes/EIF2AK2.md) in TP53-mutant or overall [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) by KSEA [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic agent targeting MAPK13 is reported in this study. Its elevated activity in TP53-mutant HCC is documented as part of the broader kinase landscape [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md).

## Open questions

- The functional role of MAPK13 (p38delta) specifically in TP53-mutant HCC pathogenesis is not established; whether it represents a therapeutic vulnerability in this context requires further study [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md).
