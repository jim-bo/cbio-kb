# SRGAP2

## Overview

SRGAP2 (Slit-Robo Rho GTPase-Activating Protein 2) is a GTPase-activating protein that couples ROBO receptor signaling to actin cytoskeleton remodeling, regulating neuronal migration and dendritic spine morphogenesis. SRGAP2 acts downstream of SLIT ligands in the canonical SLIT-ROBO-SRGAP pathway. In adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC), SRGAP2 mutation is part of a recurrently disrupted axon-guidance pathway.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SRGAP2 single-tumor somatic mutation detected in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC) of the salivary gland as part of a recurrently disrupted axon-guidance pathway (56% of tumors in the cohort) [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC): SRGAP2 mutation contributes to axon-guidance pathway disruption; the same pathway is disrupted in pancreatic adenocarcinoma, another perineural-invasion-prone malignancy [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- SRGAP2 mutation co-occurs with other axon-guidance pathway alterations ([SLIT1](../genes/SLIT1.md), [SRGAP3](../genes/SRGAP3.md), [SEMA3G](../genes/SEMA3G.md), [SEMA5A](../genes/SEMA5A.md), [PLXNA1](../genes/PLXNA1.md), [PLXNB1](../genes/PLXNB1.md), [PRKD1](../genes/PRKD1.md)) in ACC [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Rho-pathway and axon-guidance pathway inhibition flagged as candidate therapeutic strategies for ACC; no functional or clinical data specific to SRGAP2 [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Open questions

- Functional consequence of SRGAP2 mutation in ACC and its mechanistic link to perineural invasion are uncharacterized [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)
