# FUT3

## Overview

FUT3 (Fucosyltransferase 3, Lewis enzyme) encodes a Golgi enzyme responsible for the synthesis of Lewis blood-group antigens via alpha-1,3/4-fucosylation of glycan chains. In pancreatic cancer, FUT3 is part of a broader glycosylation enzyme dysregulation landscape that is upregulated at the protein level and validated as a candidate therapeutic target.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Elevated in PDAC tumors vs normal adjacent tissue (NAT) at the protein level; upregulation validated by DIA mass spectrometry; protein-level elevation not seen at the transcript level, supporting post-transcriptional/post-translational regulation of sialyl/fucosyltransferases [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)
- FUT3 upregulation enriched in the C1 (classical) proteogenomic subtype of PDAC [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)**: Protein-level upregulation in pancreatic tumors; enriched in the C1 (classical) subtype, which also shows better prognosis and enrichment for [SMAD4](../genes/SMAD4.md) mutations [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-elevated with [FUT11](../genes/FUT11.md), [ST6GAL1](../genes/ST6GAL1.md), [ST3GAL1](../genes/ST3GAL1.md), [B4GALT1](../genes/B4GALT1.md), [B4GALT4](../genes/B4GALT4.md), ST6GALNAC1 as part of a glycosylation-enzyme upregulation cluster in PDAC [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Fucosyltransferase inhibition (targeting FUT3 and related enzymes) proposed as a therapeutic strategy in PDAC; subtype-tailored glycan-capping blockade in C1 tumors is a specific hypothesis. Authors caution that systemic sialylation/fucosylation blockade (e.g., 3F-NeuAc) caused liver/kidney toxicity in mouse models, requiring selective inhibitor development [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Open questions

- Selective FUT3 inhibitors are not yet developed; on-target systemic toxicity is a major hurdle based on prior mouse model work.
