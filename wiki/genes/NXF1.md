# NXF1

## Overview

NXF1 (Nuclear RNA Export Factor 1) encodes the major mRNA export receptor in metazoans, mediating bulk poly(A)+ mRNA transport from the nucleus to the cytoplasm. In cancer, NXF1 has been identified as a recurrently mutated driver in chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL), belonging to a broader class of RNA processing and export genes disrupted in that disease.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a novel CLL driver in the RNA processing and export category in a 538-sample WES cohort (CLL8 trial); recurrently mutated among RNA processing/export drivers [PMID:26466571](../papers/26466571.md)
