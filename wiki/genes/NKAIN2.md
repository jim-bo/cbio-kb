# NKAIN2

## Overview

NKAIN2 (Na+/K+ Transporting ATPase Interacting 2) encodes a membrane protein involved in ion transport regulation. In adenoid cystic carcinoma, NKAIN2 has been identified as a novel 5'-NFIB fusion partner in a subset of tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel 5'-NFIB fusion partner in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC); identified alongside [XRCC4](../genes/XRCC4.md), [PTPRD](../genes/PTPRD.md), and [AIG1](../genes/AIG1.md) as alternative [NFIB](../genes/NFIB.md) partners in t(6;9)-positive/MYB-NFIB-negative tumors [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Adenoid cystic carcinoma**: 5'-NFIB fusion partner in a subset of t(6;9)-positive tumors lacking canonical MYB-NFIB fusion [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)
