# CopywriteR

## Overview

CopywriteR is an R package for DNA copy-number inference from off-target reads in capture-based sequencing data (e.g., whole-exome sequencing). It exploits the shallow, near-uniform coverage generated by off-target reads to produce genome-wide copy-number profiles without requiring a dedicated low-pass WGS experiment. Output is typically processed with GISTIC2 for identification of significantly amplified or deleted regions.

## Used by

- Applied to off-target reads from 105 CPTAC colon cancer tumor/normal-blood WXS pairs ([coad_cptac_2019](../datasets/coad_cptac_2019.md)) for somatic copy-number alteration analysis; outputs fed to GISTIC2; arm-level SCNAs recapitulated TCGA colorectal findings; 59% of focal driver candidates were confirmed by protein-level evidence [PMID:31031003](../papers/31031003.md)

## Notes

- In the CPTAC colon study, CopywriteR was preferred over Illumina SNP array data, which were dropped due to low dynamic range.
- Commonly paired with GISTIC2 for significance testing of recurrent SCNAs.
- Not in cBioPortal gene-panels ontology; corpus-grown slug.
