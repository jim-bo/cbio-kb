# TAK-500

## Overview

TAK-500 is an investigational agonist antibody targeting 4-1BB ([TNFRSF9](../genes/TNFRSF9.md)), a T-cell co-stimulatory receptor. It is evaluated in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)) and other solid tumors.

## Evidence in the corpus

- TAK-500 anti-4-1BB agonist listed among T-cell co-stimulatory receptor agonists (alongside BAT6026 anti-OX40, ADG106 anti-4-1BB) evaluated in phase I in NPC; no significant NPC activity reported to date [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
