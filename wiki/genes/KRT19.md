# KRT19

## Overview

KRT19 (Keratin 19) encodes a type I intermediate filament protein expressed in epithelial cells and serving as a stem/progenitor cell marker. In [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md), KRT19 functions as both a GWAS susceptibility locus and a component of a prognostic gene expression score.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- KRT19 is both a GWAS susceptibility locus for [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) and a component of a 5-gene tumor score ([TAF9](../genes/TAF9.md), [RAN](../genes/RAN.md), [RAMP3](../genes/RAMP3.md), KRT19, HN1) predicting [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) recurrence and survival after resection [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)
- Biliary marker elevated in the P1 (combined-type cHCC-ICC) cluster, consistent with ICC-like molecular phenotype. [PMID:31130341](../papers/31130341.md)
- Referenced in the context of an integrative proteogenomic characterization of [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) (n=122, European cohort); detected as part of the multi-omic molecular profiling data [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md).
