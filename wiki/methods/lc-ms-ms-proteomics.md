# LC-MS/MS proteomics

## Overview

Liquid chromatography tandem mass spectrometry (LC-MS/MS) whole-cell proteomics enables deep quantitative profiling of the tumor proteome. Single-shot LC-MS/MS can quantify thousands of proteins from a single tumor sample without extensive sample fractionation [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md).

## Used by

- [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md) — single-shot LC-MS/MS whole-cell proteomics was applied to 96 meningioma tumors from the University Health Network Brain Tumor BioBank, quantifying 6,568 proteins; integrated with WES, EPIC methylation, and mRNA-seq data to characterize four stable molecular groups (MG1–MG4) and identify MG4-specific proteomic markers [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md).

## Notes

- Proteomics data complemented mRNA-seq to reveal post-transcriptional regulation differences across meningioma molecular groups [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md).
