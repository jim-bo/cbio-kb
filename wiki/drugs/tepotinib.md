# tepotinib

## Overview

Tepotinib is a selective [MET](../genes/MET.md) tyrosine kinase inhibitor approved for the treatment of [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) harboring MET exon 14 skipping mutations. MET exon 14 alterations are age-skewed, occurring at higher frequency in older patients. Tepotinib's pivotal VISION trial enrolled patients across a broad age range.

## Evidence in the corpus

- MET exon 14 skipping inhibitor; pivotal trial enrolled patients up to 74 years old; MET exon 14 skipping is rare in young-onset NSCLC (0.72% in patients ≤50 years vs 3.25% in patients >70 years), making tepotinib less relevant to the young-onset NSCLC population [PMID:27346245](../papers/27346245.md)
