# THSD7B

## Overview

THSD7B (Thrombospondin Type 1 Domain Containing 7B) encodes a protein with thrombospondin-type 1 repeat domains involved in angiogenesis regulation. In cancer genomics, it was identified as a novel somatically mutated gene in prostate cancer, not previously reported in this context.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel somatically mutated gene identified in prostate cancer WES (112 tumors); not previously known to be somatically altered in prostate cancer [PMID:22610119](../papers/22610119.md)
