# Sarcoma, NOS (SARCNOS)

## Overview

Sarcoma, NOS (not otherwise specified) is a diagnostic category for sarcomas that cannot be further classified into a specific histologic subtype, classified under Soft Tissue Sarcoma in OncoTree (parent: [SOFT_TISSUE](../cancer_types/SOFT_TISSUE.md)). This category encompasses undifferentiated and unclassifiable spindle-cell, round-cell, or pleomorphic sarcomas.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [sarcoma_ucla_2024](../datasets/sarcoma_ucla_2024.md): represented in the 24-subtype UCLA sarcoma PDTO biobank. SARC0117, a metastatic undifferentiated spindle cell sarcoma (USS), is classified here and carried a [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md) hotspot mutation (reported as "H1074L", canonical H1047L). (194 specimens from 126 patients, Feb 2018–May 2022). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md): hotspot mutation (H1047L, rendered as "H1074L" in the manuscript) in SARC0117 (metastatic USS/SARCNOS) drove broad PI3K/mTOR sensitivity ([alpelisib](../drugs/alpelisib.md), apitolisib, copanlisib, BGT226, [vistusertib](../drugs/vistusertib.md)). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
- MET500 metastatic cohort: soft-tissue sarcoma (NOS) was the third most common lineage (42/500, 8.4%); a novel GREB1-NR4A3 activating fusion was identified in uterine leiomyosarcoma and a CIC-CITED1 fusion in undifferentiated high-grade sarcoma; [NF1](../genes/NF1.md) was the most commonly fused tumor suppressor (n=18) across the cohort. [PMID:28783718](../papers/28783718.md)
- TCGA Sarcoma working group profiled 206 adult soft tissue sarcomas across 6 histologies ([LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md), DDLPS, [UPS](../cancer_types/UPS.md), [MFS](../cancer_types/MFS.md), [SS](../cancer_types/SS.md), [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md)); pan-sarcoma mutation burden was low (mean 1.06 mut/Mb) with only 3 pan-cohort SMGs ([TP53](../genes/TP53.md), [ATRX](../genes/ATRX.md), [RB1](../genes/RB1.md)), and complex karyotypes were SCNA-driven [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md)
- N=1 sarcoma sample included in pooled MSS ICB WES cohort of 249 tumors across six cancer types; clonal TMB, driver alterations ([PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md), [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md), [PBRM1](../genes/PBRM1.md) enriched in responders; [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md), biallelic [PTEN](../genes/PTEN.md) loss, 11q/12q amplifications enriched in non-responders) assessed pan-cohort [PMID:30150660](../papers/30150660.md)
- Sarcoma NOS (n=9, 11.8% of TRK fusion-positive cases) was the third most common TRK fusion-positive tumor type at MSK; driver-negative sarcomas are recommended for reflex RNA fusion testing given large NTRK2/3 introns causing ~26% of fusions to be missed by DNA-only assays [PMID:31871300](../papers/31871300.md)
- In a 7,494-sarcoma cohort, sarcoma NOS was the most common histology (17.2%); MSI-H was most often observed in this group, uterine [ESS](../cancer_types/ESS.md), and [LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md); [CD274](../genes/CD274.md) amplification was 2.2% of sarcoma NOS; kinase fusions in 3.8% [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).

## Subtypes

- Undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma ([UPS](../cancer_types/UPS.md); overlaps with [MFH](../cancer_types/MFH.md))
- Undifferentiated spindle cell sarcoma (USS)
- Undifferentiated round cell sarcoma (when not meeting criteria for Ewing or other defined entities)

## Therapeutic landscape

- [alpelisib](../drugs/alpelisib.md): SARC0117 (metastatic USS/SARCNOS with [PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md) H1047L) was among the strongest PI3K/mTOR responders in the UCLA PDTO screen; broad sensitivity across [alpelisib](../drugs/alpelisib.md), apitolisib, copanlisib, BGT226, [vistusertib](../drugs/vistusertib.md). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
