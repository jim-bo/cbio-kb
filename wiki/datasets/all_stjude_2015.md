# Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (St Jude, Nat Genet 2015)

## Overview

This dataset, generated as part of the Pediatric Cancer Genome Project (PCGP), provides a comprehensive mutational landscape of infant acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL) with MLL ([KMT2A](../genes/KMT2A.md)) rearrangements.

## Composition

- **Cancer Types:** [BLLKMT2A](../cancer_types/BLLKMT2A.md) (Infant MLL-R ALL) and [TLL](../cancer_types/TLL.md).
- **Cohort Size:** 65 infants (47 MLL-R and 18 non-MLL-R) and 20 older children (MLL-R).

## Papers using this cohort

- **[PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md):** The primary study defining the mutational landscape of infant MLL-rearranged ALL.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- Infant MLL-R ALL has one of the lowest somatic mutation frequencies in human cancer, averaging only 1.3 non-silent mutations per case [PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md).
- Activating mutations in kinase/PI3K/RAS signaling pathways are found in 47% of infant MLL-R cases, but are frequently sub-clonal and lost at relapse [PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md).
