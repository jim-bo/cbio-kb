# CD8B

## Overview

CD8B encodes the beta chain of the CD8 co-receptor, paired with [CD8A](../genes/CD8A.md) on cytotoxic T lymphocytes.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Included in the 20-gene Immunologic Constant of Rejection (ICR) signature applied to the 348-patient AC-ICAM colon cancer cohort [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Colon adenocarcinoma — CD8B contributes to ICR-based clustering that segregates AC-ICAM into prognostic ICR-high, -medium, and -low subtypes [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [CD8A](../genes/CD8A.md) and other Th1/cytotoxic ICR module genes in ICR-high colon tumors [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct CD8B-targeted therapy is evaluated; CD8B contributes to the ICR prognostic score [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Open questions

- Independent contribution of CD8B vs the ICR module is not dissected [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
