# PDE4DIP

## Overview

PDE4DIP (phosphodiesterase 4D interacting protein) encodes a centrosome-associated scaffold protein (also known as myomegalin). It is involved in centrosome function and cAMP signaling. In cancer, PDE4DIP has been identified as part of a cluster of centrosome-associated genes with recurrent damaging mutations in small cell lung cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Clustered mutations in centrosome-associated genes including PDE4DIP, ASPM, and ALMS1 in small cell lung cancer (SCLC). [PMID:26168399](../papers/26168399.md)
