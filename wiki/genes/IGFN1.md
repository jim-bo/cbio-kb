# IGFN1

## Overview

IGFN1 (Immunoglobulin-like and Fibronectin type III domain-containing protein 1) encodes a large structural protein. In cancer genomics, IGFN1 has been identified as part of a metastatic-enrichment gene signature in breast cancer, with mutations occurring more frequently in metastatic than in early-stage disease.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- IGFN1 identified among eight genes significantly more mutated in metastatic breast cancer (mBC) vs early breast cancer (eBC) at FDR<0.01; part of the 8-gene metastatic-enrichment signature associated with a 2-fold higher death hazard (HR=1.97, p=0.001) [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [Breast carcinoma (BRCA)](../cancer_types/BRCA.md): Enriched for IGFN1 mutations in the metastatic setting vs primary tumors [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- IGFN1 mutations co-occur with [ESR1](../genes/ESR1.md), [FSIP2](../genes/FSIP2.md), AGRN, [FRAS1](../genes/FRAS1.md), [EDC4](../genes/EDC4.md), [OSBPL3](../genes/OSBPL3.md), and [PALB2](../genes/PALB2.md) mutations in the 8-gene metastatic-enrichment signature of HR+/HER2- metastatic breast cancer [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic target identified; IGFN1 is a component of a prognostic multi-gene signature rather than a druggable target [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Open questions

- Whether IGFN1 mutations are drivers of metastatic progression or passenger events co-selected under endocrine therapy pressure requires further functional validation [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).
- The 8-gene metastatic-enrichment survival association was not corrected for mutational load or signature contribution, limiting causal inference [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).
