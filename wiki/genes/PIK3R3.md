# PIK3R3

## Overview

PIK3R3 encodes the p55-gamma regulatory subunit of class IA phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase. Class IA PI3K holoenzymes consist of a p110 catalytic subunit ([PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md), [PIK3CB](../genes/PIK3CB.md), or [PIK3CD](../genes/PIK3CD.md)) coupled to a p85/p55 regulatory subunit. PIK3R3 modulates PI3K activity and subcellular localization. Alterations in regulatory subunits can activate PI3K signaling independently of p110 subunit mutations.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- PIK3R3 was profiled as part of the PI3K/mTOR pathway in metastatic pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors (80 patients, MSK-IMPACT panel, [panet_msk_2018](../datasets/panet_msk_2018.md)). mTOR-pathway alterations collectively occurred in 34/80 (43%) of patients across multiple PI3K-pathway genes. Individual PIK3R3 alteration counts are not separately enumerated in the published report. [PMID:30687805](../papers/30687805.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[PANET](../cancer_types/PANET.md) (pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor):** PIK3R3 is part of the PI3K/mTOR pathway, the second most commonly altered signaling axis in metastatic panNET (43% of patients). [PMID:30687805](../papers/30687805.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Part of the mTOR pathway alteration cluster in panNET alongside [TSC2](../genes/TSC2.md), PIK3CA, PIK3CB, PIK3CD, [PIK3CG](../genes/PIK3CG.md), [PIK3C2G](../genes/PIK3C2G.md), [PIK3C3](../genes/PIK3C3.md), [PTEN](../genes/PTEN.md), and [RHEB](../genes/RHEB.md). [PMID:30687805](../papers/30687805.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- mTOR-pathway alteration status (including PI3K regulatory subunit alterations) did not predict response to [everolimus](../drugs/everolimus.md) in the 29 treated metastatic panNET patients in this cohort. The authors caution against using mTOR-pathway mutation status as a single-marker selection criterion. [PMID:30687805](../papers/30687805.md)

## Open questions

- Individual PIK3R3 alteration frequency and type in panNET are not separately enumerated. Whether PIK3R3 alterations are driver vs passenger in neuroendocrine tumor biology is not established. [PMID:30687805](../papers/30687805.md)
