# sintilimab

## Overview

Sintilimab is an anti-PD-1 monoclonal antibody targeting [PDCD1](../genes/PDCD1.md). It is evaluated for locally advanced and recurrent/metastatic nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Phase III sintilimab + concurrent chemoradiation (CCRT) in locally advanced NPC (NCT03700476, n=425): event-free survival HR 0.59; 36-month EFS 86% vs 76%; grade ≥3 TRAEs 74%; sintilimab added to CCRT improved EFS in locally advanced NPC [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
- Anti-CTLA-4 IBI-310 evaluated in combination with sintilimab in R/M NPC to address compensatory CTLA-4 checkpoint upregulation [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
