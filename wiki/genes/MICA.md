# MICA

## Overview

MICA (MHC Class I Polypeptide-Related Sequence A) encodes an NKG2D ligand involved in immune surveillance. In cancer genomics, MICA has been identified as a GWAS susceptibility locus associated with hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) risk, particularly in the context of viral hepatitis (HBV/HCV).
