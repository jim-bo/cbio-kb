# Myeloproliferative Neoplasms, Unclassifiable (MPNU)

## Overview

Myeloproliferative Neoplasms, Unclassifiable (MPNU) is an OncoTree subtype under [MPN](../cancer_types/MPN.md) for MPN cases that do not meet diagnostic criteria for [ET](../cancer_types/ET.md), [PV](../cancer_types/PV.md), or primary myelofibrosis. These cases can carry the same driver mutations ([JAK2](../genes/JAK2.md), [CALR](../genes/CALR.md), [MPL](../genes/MPL.md)) as other MPNs.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [mpn_cimr_2013](../datasets/mpn_cimr_2013.md) — 2 unclassifiable MPN patients included in the 151-patient whole-exome sequencing cohort. [PMID:24325359](../papers/24325359.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- 2 unclassifiable MPN patients were included in the CALR discovery cohort; CALR, JAK2, and MPL mutations were assessed across all 151 patients (48 PV, 62 ET, 39 [MF](../cancer_types/MF.md), 2 MPNU) with 97% carrying one of these three drivers in mutually exclusive fashion. [PMID:24325359](../papers/24325359.md)

## Subtypes

- By definition, cannot be classified as ET, PV, or PMF.

## Therapeutic landscape

- No MPNU-specific drug data in the current corpus.
