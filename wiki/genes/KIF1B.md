# KIF1B

## Overview

KIF1B encodes a kinesin motor protein involved in axonal transport of mitochondria and synaptic vesicles. In cancer genomics, KIF1B has been identified as a GWAS susceptibility locus associated with hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) risk, particularly in the context of viral hepatitis (HBV/HCV).
