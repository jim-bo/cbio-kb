# CXCL1

## Overview

CXCL1 (C-X-C Motif Chemokine Ligand 1) is a pro-inflammatory chemokine secreted by hepatocytes downstream of [TLR4](../genes/TLR4.md) signaling. In cholangiocarcinoma, CXCL1 recruits polymorphonuclear myeloid-derived suppressor cells (PMN-MDSCs) to accelerate tumor progression; this axis can be disrupted by [neomycin](../drugs/neomycin.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- TLR4-driven hepatocyte chemokine that recruits PMN-MDSCs to accelerate cholangiocarcinoma progression; [neomycin](../drugs/neomycin.md) blocks the CXCL1-PMN-MDSC axis in preclinical models [PMID:25608663](../papers/25608663.md)
