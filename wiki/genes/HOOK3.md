# HOOK3

## Overview

HOOK3 (Hook Microtubule Tethering Protein 3) encodes a microtubule-binding protein involved in organelle positioning and intracellular transport. HOOK3 has been reported as a fusion partner in [RET](../genes/RET.md) and other oncogenic rearrangements in various tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Observed as an altered gene in individual synchronous DCIS/IDC-NST cases in a WES study of paired ductal carcinoma in situ and invasive ductal carcinoma (n=25 patients, MSKCC) [PMID:32220886](../papers/32220886.md)
