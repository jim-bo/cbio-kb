# SOD1

## Overview

SOD1 (Superoxide Dismutase 1) encodes an antioxidant enzyme that converts superoxide radicals to hydrogen peroxide. SOD1 was identified as a secondary synthetic lethal candidate in a DDR screen in FBXO7-deficient colorectal cancer cells, related to oxidative stress management.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a secondary synthetic lethal candidate in the DDR screen in FBXO7-deficient CRC cells (oxidative stress management pathway) [PMID:36334560](../papers/36334560.md)
