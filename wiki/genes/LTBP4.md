# LTBP4

## Overview

LTBP4 (Latent TGF-beta Binding Protein 4) encodes a matrix glycoprotein that regulates the bioavailability of TGF-β in the extracellular matrix. In glioblastoma, LTBP4 variants have been reported in recurrent tumors and patient-derived xenograft models.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- LTBP4 variants identified in 14% of 28 recurrent Mayo [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) PDX at frequencies consistent with prior recurrent-glioblastoma reports; some variants also observed in primary PDX suggesting possible germline contribution [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Glioblastoma (GBM): LTBP4 variants identified in recurrent tumor-derived PDX in the Mayo Clinic GBM PDX National Resource ([gbm_mayo_pdx_sarkaria_2019](../datasets/gbm_mayo_pdx_sarkaria_2019.md)); frequency of 14% in recurrent PDX mirrors rates in prior glioblastoma recurrence literature [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- LTBP4 variants co-occur in recurrent GBM context alongside other recurrence-enriched alterations such as [IGF1R](../genes/IGF1R.md) and [PRDM2](../genes/PRDM2.md) variants [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No targeted therapies specifically directed at LTBP4 reported in this corpus; potential indirect relevance through TGF-β pathway modulation in GBM microenvironment.

## Open questions

- Germline vs. somatic origin of LTBP4 variants in GBM PDX is unresolved; some variants are also seen in primary (non-recurrent) PDX, raising the possibility of germline contribution [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)
