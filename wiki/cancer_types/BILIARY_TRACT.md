# Biliary Tract (BILIARY_TRACT)

## Overview

Biliary Tract (OncoTree: BILIARY_TRACT) is a level-1 OncoTree grouping encompassing cancers of the biliary system (gallbladder, intrahepatic and extrahepatic bile ducts, ampulla of Vater). It sits directly under TISSUE and is the parent node for more specific biliary subtypes (e.g., [CHOL](../cancer_types/CHOL.md), [IHCH](../cancer_types/IHCH.md), [EHCH](../cancer_types/EHCH.md)). Main type is Biliary Tract Cancer.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- No dedicated biliary-tract dataset pages have been linked yet.

## Recurrent alterations

- TRK fusion-positive biliary tract cancer was represented in the MSK prospective cohort (n=26,312 prospectively sequenced); DNA-only assays recommended to reflex to RNA fusion testing given large NTRK2/3 introns [PMID:31871300](../papers/31871300.md)

## Subtypes

- More specific biliary subtypes in OncoTree include CHOL (cholangiocarcinoma), IHCH (intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma), EHCH (extrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma), and [GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md) (gallbladder carcinoma).

## Therapeutic landscape

- TRK inhibitors ([larotrectinib](../drugs/larotrectinib.md)) are active in TRK fusion-positive biliary tract cancers as part of the tumor-agnostic TRK-fusion indication [PMID:31871300](../papers/31871300.md)
