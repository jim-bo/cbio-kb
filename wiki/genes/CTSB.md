# CTSB

## Overview

CTSB (Cathepsin B) encodes a lysosomal cysteine protease involved in intracellular protein degradation, extracellular matrix remodeling, and tumor invasion. In lung adenocarcinoma proteogenomic studies, CTSB has been identified as a protein differentially expressed between tumor and normal adjacent tissue.
