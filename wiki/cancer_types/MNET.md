# Miscellaneous Neuroepithelial Tumor (MNET)

## Overview

Miscellaneous Neuroepithelial Tumor (MNET) sits under the BRAIN parent in OncoTree. Note: In the 40328872 corpus reference, MNET is used alongside [HGONEC](../cancer_types/HGONEC.md) to classify an ovarian mixed neuroendocrine-non-neuroendocrine neoplasm (MiNEN); the paper-compiler assigned both MNET and HGONEC codes to that case. The canonical OncoTree MNET code covers miscellaneous neuroepithelial tumors of the brain/CNS.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [pog570_bcgsc_2020](../datasets/pog570_bcgsc_2020.md) — 1 ovarian MiNEN case (PN19) annotated with MNET in the paper-compiler output within 28-patient metastatic NEN WGTA cohort. [PMID:40328872](../papers/40328872.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- The ovarian MiNEN case (PN19) annotated MNET/HGONEC carried [MYC](../genes/MYC.md) amplification, [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) gain-of-function mutation, [SMAD4](../genes/SMAD4.md) loss, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) loss, and [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) loss. Its transcriptome clustered with TCGA colorectal adenocarcinomas. Median TMB 2.19 mut/Mb across the 28-patient NEN cohort. [PMID:40328872](../papers/40328872.md)

## Subtypes

- MiNEN variant with adenocarcinoma and neuroendocrine components.

## Therapeutic landscape

- Immune-checkpoint inhibitors and [DLL3](../genes/DLL3.md) inhibitors flagged as potential options based on expression data (PN19). [PMID:40328872](../papers/40328872.md)
