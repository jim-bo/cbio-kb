# ZNF595

## Overview

ZNF595 (Zinc Finger Protein 595) encodes a Kruppel-type zinc finger transcription factor. It was identified as a novel somatically mutated gene in prostate cancer, representing one of several previously unreported candidate drivers in this malignancy.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel somatically mutated gene identified in prostate cancer WES (112 tumors); not previously known to be somatically altered in prostate cancer [PMID:22610119](../papers/22610119.md)
