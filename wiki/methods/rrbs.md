# Reduced representation bisulfite sequencing (RRBS)

## Overview

Restriction-enzyme-based bisulfite sequencing approach that enriches CpG-dense regions for cost-effective DNA methylation profiling.

## Used by

- [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md) — RRBS performed on n=509 CLL samples (part of the n=999 methylation cohort, complementing 450k array n=490) and used to extend the n-CLL/i-CLL/m-CLL epitype framework and the epiCMIT mitotic clock [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- eRRBS (enhanced Reduced Representation Bisulfite Sequencing) used for genome-wide single-cytosine methylation profiling of 114 metastatic CRPC biopsies; strong epigenetic segregation of CRPC-NE from CRPC-Adeno identified SPDEF promoter hypermethylation and re-classified 3 histologic adenocarcinomas as CRPC-NE [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
- RRBS promoter methylation profiling applied to 843 of the 1,072 CCLE cancer cell lines (17,182 promoter regions); used to identify paralogue synthetic lethalities ([RPP25](../genes/RPP25.md)/[RPP25L](../genes/RPP25L.md), [LDHA](../genes/LDHA.md)/[LDHB](../genes/LDHB.md)) and lineage transcription factor dependencies driven by promoter hypomethylation [PMID:31068700](../papers/31068700.md)
- Reduced representation bisulfite sequencing (Zymo Methyl-MiniSeq) performed on 25 of 64 MSK-IMPACT-profiled Nigerian CRC tumors; only 8% (2/25) were CIMP-H and only 28.6% (2/7) of MSI-H tumors showed MLH1 hypermethylation [PMID:34819518](../papers/34819518.md)

## Notes

- DNA methylome variation in CLL was dominated by cell-of-origin and proliferative history rather than specific driver mutations [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
