# AKR1C3

## Overview

AKR1C3 (aldo-keto reductase family 1, member C3) encodes an enzyme involved in steroid and androgen metabolism. In cancer, elevated AKR1C3 expression has been associated with androgen biosynthesis in prostate cancer and with sensitivity to AKR1C3-activated prodrug strategies in hematologic malignancies, particularly T-cell leukemia.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- RNA-seq identified T-cell leukemia (TLL/T-ALL) as a target histology for an AKR1C3-activated prodrug based on high AKR1C3 expression in T-ALL PDX models in the PPTC cohort of 261 pediatric cancer PDXs [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[TLL](../cancer_types/TLL.md) (T-ALL):** High AKR1C3 expression identified by RNA-seq in T-ALL PDX models; used to nominate T-ALL as a target histology for AKR1C3-activated prodrug basket-trial design [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md)
