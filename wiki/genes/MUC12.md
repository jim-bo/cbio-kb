# MUC12

## Overview

MUC12 (Mucin 12, Cell Surface Associated) is a transmembrane mucin involved in epithelial cell protection and signaling. In adenoid cystic carcinoma, exclusive mutations in the MYB-NFIB fusion molecular subclass have been reported.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Exclusive somatic mutation in the MYB-NFIB fusion group in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC); functional significance requires validation [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Adenoid cystic carcinoma**: exclusive somatic mutation in the MYB-NFIB molecular subclass [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Mutations exclusive to MYB-NFIB fusion group in ACC; not observed in MYBL1-fusion or fusion-negative subclasses [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)
