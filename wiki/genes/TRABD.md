# TRABD

## Overview

TRABD (TraB domain-containing protein) is the 5' fusion partner in TRABD-DDR2 gene fusions identified in head and neck squamous cell carcinoma ([HNSC](../cancer_types/HNSC.md)). This fusion creates a promoter swap that drives [DDR2](../genes/DDR2.md) overexpression, proposing DDR2 as a druggable target with [dasatinib](../drugs/dasatinib.md). TRABD itself has no established oncogenic function.

## Alterations observed in the corpus
