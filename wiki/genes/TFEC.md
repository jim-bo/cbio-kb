# TFEC

## Overview

TFEC (Transcription Factor EC) is a member of the MiTF/TFE family of basic helix-loop-helix leucine zipper transcription factors. It is discussed in the context of MiTF-family biology in non-clear cell renal carcinoma research.

## Alterations observed in the corpus
