# EVC

## Overview

EVC (Ellis-van Creveld syndrome protein) encodes a transmembrane protein that positively regulates the Hedgehog (Hh) signaling pathway by facilitating ciliary localization of the Smoothened-Gli axis. Germline loss-of-function mutations cause Ellis-van Creveld syndrome (chondroectodermal dysplasia). In cancer, EVC has been identified as a recurrent non-coding (3' UTR) driver in metastatic cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma ([CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md)), implicating dysregulated Hedgehog signaling in this UV-driven malignancy.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Recurrent 3' UTR mutation in 48% of metastatic [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) samples (12/25 WGS cases); nominated as a significant non-coding driver by OncodriveFML (Q=0.011); proposed to dysregulate ciliary Hedgehog signaling in metastatic CSCC [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md): 3' UTR alteration in 48% of 25-sample metastatic CSCC WGS cohort; highest-frequency non-coding driver alongside [PPP1R1A](../genes/PPP1R1A.md); potential non-coding biomarker of metastatic disease invisible to WES/targeted-panel platforms [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-occurs with [PPP1R1A](../genes/PPP1R1A.md) 3' UTR alteration (also 48% of cohort) in non-coding driver landscape of metastatic CSCC [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Hedgehog pathway inhibitors (e.g., [vismodegib](../drugs/vismodegib.md), [sonidegib](../drugs/sonidegib.md)) are established in basal cell carcinoma and could in principle be relevant if EVC 3' UTR mutations dysregulate Hh signaling in CSCC; no direct clinical evidence yet [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Open questions

- Functional consequence of 3' UTR mutations in EVC (e.g., altered mRNA stability, microRNA binding) on Hedgehog pathway activity in CSCC has not been experimentally validated; expression data not available in this WGS-only dataset [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)
