# LLGL1

## Overview

LLGL1 (Lethal Giant Larvae Homolog 1) is a tumor suppressor gene involved in cell polarity and asymmetric cell division. It encodes a component of the LLGL/Scribble polarity complex and has been implicated in suppression of tumor invasion and metastasis in several cancer contexts.

## Alterations observed in the corpus
