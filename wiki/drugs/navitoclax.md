# navitoclax

## Overview

Navitoclax (ABT-263) is an orally bioavailable BH3-mimetic that inhibits BCL-2, BCL-XL, and BCL-W anti-apoptotic proteins, promoting apoptosis in cancer cells that depend on these survival signals. It has been investigated as a senolytic agent to clear senescent cells.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Tested as a senolytic agent in KRASG12C-amplified colorectal cancer cells that entered oncogene-induced senescence after [sotorasib](../drugs/sotorasib.md) + [cetuximab](../drugs/cetuximab.md) withdrawal; navitoclax did NOT show selective activity in the senescent (drug-off) condition, in contrast to the mTOR inhibitor AZD8055 which did show selective cytotoxicity [PMID:36355783](../papers/36355783.md)
