# IDO1

## Overview

IDO1 encodes indoleamine 2,3-dioxygenase 1, a tryptophan-catabolizing enzyme involved in immunoregulatory counter-activation in the tumor microenvironment.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Immunoregulatory counter-activation component of the 20-gene ICR signature in colon cancer [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) — ICR component in the AC-ICAM cohort [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [CD274](../genes/CD274.md), [CTLA4](../genes/CTLA4.md), [FOXP3](../genes/FOXP3.md), [PDCD1](../genes/PDCD1.md) in the ICR immunoregulatory arm [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- Contributes to the ICR module; ICR-high tumors had better OS, and the paper notes potential predictive utility for immunotherapy response in other tumor types [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
