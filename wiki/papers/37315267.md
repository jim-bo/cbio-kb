# Extremity Rhabdomyosarcoma: An Integrated Clinicopathologic and Genomic Study to Improve Risk Stratification

**PMID:** 37315267 · **DOI:** 10.1200/PO.22.00705 · **Journal:** JCO Precision Oncology (2023)

## TL;DR

A single-center retrospective study of 61 patients with extremity rhabdomyosarcoma ([RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md)) treated at MSKCC between 2000 and 2021, integrating clinicopathologic features with MSK-IMPACT targeted DNA sequencing (performed in 40% of patients) to refine risk stratification. The authors confirm the dominance of [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion–positive alveolar [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) ([ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md)) in this anatomic subset and identify [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplifications and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions as mutually exclusive, recurrent events associated with poor overall survival [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Cohort & data

- 61 patients with extremity [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) treated at MSKCC, January 2000–December 2021; median age 8 years; equal gender distribution; ~two-thirds lower extremity [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Histology: 85% [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion–positive [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md), 7 fusion-negative [ERMS](../cancer_types/ERMS.md), 2 [MYOD1](../genes/MYOD1.md)-mutant spindle cell/sclerosing [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) ([SCRMS](../cancer_types/SCRMS.md)) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Cohort corresponds to cBioPortal study [rms_msk_2023](../datasets/rms_msk_2023.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Fusion testing by Archer FusionPlex or [FISH](../methods/fish.md) using custom BAC probes flanking [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md), [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md), and [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md); targeted DNA sequencing by MSK-IMPACT (410–505 genes; [IMPACT410](../methods/IMPACT410.md) / [IMPACT505](../methods/IMPACT505.md)) in 40% of patients [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Key findings

- One-third of patients had localized disease; 18% had regional nodal disease; 51% had distant metastases at diagnosis [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Metastatic disease, high-risk group, and age ≥10 years significantly affected OS (HR 2.68, P=.004; HR 2.78, P=.010; HR 2.26, P=.034) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- 5-year EFS/OS for metastatic disease: 19% / 29%; for nodal involvement without distant metastases: 43% / 66% [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) had worse prognosis and affected older children than [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) (HR 3.45, P=.016) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- The most common genomic events in [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) were [MED12](../genes/MED12.md) alterations, [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplifications, and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions (each 8%–17%) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplifications and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions were mutually exclusive, enriched in acral and high-risk lesions, and correlated with poor OS (P=.02) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Genes & alterations

- **[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md)** — fusion partner in 85% of extremity [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) cases; fusion detected by FusionPlex/[FISH](../methods/fish.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- **[PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)** — [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion present in 70% of [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md); associated with worse OS and older age than [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- **[PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)** — [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion; more favorable prognosis relative to [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::FOXO1 [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- **[MYOD1](../genes/MYOD1.md)** — activating mutation defining the 2 spindle cell/sclerosing [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) cases in the cohort [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- **[MED12](../genes/MED12.md)** — recurrent alterations in ARMS (8%–17% frequency bracket) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- **[CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md)** — focal amplification in ARMS, mutually exclusive with [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletion; associated with poor OS [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- **[CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md)** — homozygous deletion in ARMS, mutually exclusive with [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplification; associated with poor OS and enriched in acral/high-risk tumors [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Clinical implications

- The authors argue that [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplifications and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions should be considered for integration into risk stratification for extremity [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md), given their correlation with poor OS [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) vs [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)::FOXO1 fusion subtype carries independent prognostic weight within alveolar RMS and may warrant fusion-partner–aware stratification [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Nodal-only involvement, while adverse, has a substantially less dismal outcome than distant metastatic disease, supporting aggressive local/regional control in node-positive extremity RMS [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Limitations & open questions

- Single-institution retrospective cohort of 61 patients over 20 years; small sample limits multivariable power, especially for rare fusion-negative and [MYOD1](../genes/MYOD1.md)-mutant subtypes [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- MSK-IMPACT sequencing was available for only 40% of patients, biasing the genomic correlatives toward cases with residual material [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Treatment regimens spanned multiple sequential COG/institutional protocols across two decades, complicating outcome comparisons [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Whether therapeutic targeting of [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md)/6 in CDK4-amplified or [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md)-deleted extremity [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) improves outcomes remains untested [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
