# Neuroendocrine Prostate Cancer (Weill Cornell Medicine, Nature Medicine 2016)

## Overview

The nepc_wcm_2016 cohort was generated by Beltran et al. at Weill Cornell Medicine and published in *Nature Medicine* 2016. It profiles 114 metastatic biopsies from 81 men with castration-resistant prostate cancer (CRPC) — 51 patients with adenocarcinoma histology (CRPC-Adeno, 70 samples) and 30 with neuroendocrine histology (CRPC-NE, 44 samples) — using whole-exome sequencing, RNA-seq, and genome-wide DNA methylation (eRRBS). Multi-region and serial biopsies were obtained from 17 patients. BAM files are deposited at dbGaP accession `phs000909.v.p1` and hosted on cBioPortal for Cancer Genomics. [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)

## Composition

- 81 patients / 114 metastatic biopsies of castration-resistant prostate cancer ([PRAD](../cancer_types/PRAD.md)).
- CRPC-Adeno: 51 patients, 70 samples.
- CRPC-NE: 30 patients, 44 samples.
- Matched normals from all patients; multi-region/serial biopsies from 17 patients.
- All samples are metastatic biopsies (not primary tumors). [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)

## Assays / panels (linked)

- [whole-exome-seq](../methods/whole-exome-seq.md): SureSelect v2/v4 or HaloPlex Exome (Agilent), Illumina HiSeq, mean target coverage ~100×.
- [rna-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md): TruSeq, HiSeq 2500, 2×75 bp paired-end, on 49 specimens.
- [rrbs](../methods/rrbs.md): Enhanced RRBS (eRRBS) for genome-wide single-cytosine methylation.
- Custom NanoString assay for samples with limited material.
- [immunohistochemistry](../methods/immunohistochemistry.md): [AR](../genes/AR.md), synaptophysin, chromogranin A, CD56, [EZH2](../genes/EZH2.md), MMR proteins.
- [fish](../methods/fish.md): [CYLD](../genes/CYLD.md) deletion verification.
- Computational: [MuTect](../methods/mutect.md), [Oncotator](../methods/oncotator.md), [MutSig](../methods/mutsig.md), CLONET for tumor purity/ploidy and allele-specific copy-number clonality. [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md) — Beltran et al. 2016, *Nature Medicine*: Primary study defining the genomic, transcriptomic, and epigenomic landscape of CRPC-NE vs CRPC-Adeno; developed a 70-gene NEPC classifier with precision/recall >0.99.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- Concurrent [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) loss + [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) alteration is the genetic hallmark of CRPC-NE: [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) deletion 70% vs 32% (P=0.003), [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation/deletion 66.7% vs 31.4% (P=0.0043), concurrent 53.3% vs 13.7% (P<0.0004). [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
- CRPC-NE lacks [AR](../genes/AR.md) genomic alterations that dominate CRPC-Adeno (focal AR amplification, point mutations, ARv7 enrichment) — P<0.0001. [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
- [CYLD](../genes/CYLD.md) focal deletion enriched in CRPC-NE (51% of samples, FDR<10%, FISH-verified), with concordant mRNA loss and modest AR-signaling attenuation. [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
- Strong epigenetic segregation of CRPC-NE from CRPC-Adeno despite overlapping somatic copy-number landscape; methylation-based clustering re-classified 3 histologic adenocarcinomas with clinical AR-independence as CRPC-NE. [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
- [EZH2](../genes/EZH2.md) mRNA 2× higher in CRPC-NE (P<10⁻⁶); EZH2 inhibitor GSK343 preferentially reduced viability of NCI-H660 (NE) vs non-NE cell lines. [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
- [SPDEF](../genes/SPDEF.md) promoter hypermethylation and mRNA silencing in CRPC-NE (P<10⁻⁹); also induced by long-term [enzalutamide](../drugs/enzalutamide.md) treatment of LNCaP adenocarcinoma cells — supporting divergent clonal evolution model. [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
- 70-gene integrated NEPC classifier (including [AURKA](../genes/AURKA.md) P<10⁻⁵ and [MYCN](../genes/MYCN.md) P<10⁻⁴) achieved precision/recall >0.99 in the discovery cohort; 0% of treatment-naïve adenocarcinomas scored NEPC-high vs up to 8% of metastatic cases across [prad_tcga](../datasets/prad_tcga.md), [prad_mich](../datasets/prad_mich.md), and [prad_su2c_2015](../datasets/prad_su2c_2015.md). [PMID:26855148](../papers/26855148.md)
