# MSK-IMPACT 505 (IMPACT505)

## Overview

Targeted (505 cancer genes) sequencing of various tumor types via MSK-IMPACT on Illumina HiSeq sequencers — canonical cBioPortal gene panel `IMPACT505`. The largest/most-recent generation of MSK-IMPACT used in the corpus.

## Used by

- [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md) — MSK-IMPACT (up to 505 genes) with select targeted RNA sequencing was used in the Make-an-IMPACT direct-to-patient program; 250/333 enrolled patients had tumor DNA of sufficient quality. In histiocytosis (n=84), most commonly mutated actionable genes were [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) (33%), [MAP2K1](../genes/MAP2K1.md) (13%), [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) (7%), and [CSF1R](../genes/CSF1R.md) (2.4%) [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md) — MSK-IMPACT 410–505 was used in 40% of 61 extremity rhabdomyosarcoma patients; identified mutually exclusive [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplifications and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions associated with poor [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md) — one of the panel versions used in the 233-patient [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) brain metastasis cohort [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md).
- [PMID:39289779](../papers/39289779.md) — IMPACT505 was used to profile 733 CSF samples (later cohort, Nov 2018 -- Nov 2022) in a 1,007-sample CSF ctDNA study at MSK; ctDNA positivity was associated with a three-fold increased risk of death (HR 3.23, p < 0.001) and 50.7% of ctDNA-positive samples carried a level 1 OncoKB actionable alteration [PMID:39289779](../papers/39289779.md).
- [PMID:37699004](../papers/37699004.md) — IMPACT505 (up to 505 genes) used to sequence 902/1,123 esophagogastric cancer patients at MSK; germline analysis used a 76–88-gene MSK-IMPACT panel on 466 patients [PMID:37699004](../papers/37699004.md).
- [PMID:37769223](../papers/37769223.md) — IMPACT505 used in 37 (72.5%) of 51 [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) patients for matched tumor tissue profiling, complementing MSK-ACCESS cfDNA profiling; 92.5% of tissue alterations were detected in matched cfDNA [PMID:37769223](../papers/37769223.md).
- [PMID:38630790](../papers/38630790.md) — IMPACT505 used in an additional 105-patient validation cohort for the GNH subset of diffuse pleural mesothelioma; 4 GNH cases identified [PMID:38630790](../papers/38630790.md).

## Notes

- IMPACT505 is the broadest version of MSK-IMPACT, succeeding IMPACT341/410/468 [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
