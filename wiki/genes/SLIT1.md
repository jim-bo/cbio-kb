# SLIT1

## Overview

SLIT1 (Slit Guidance Ligand 1) is a secreted axon-guidance protein that signals through Roundabout (ROBO) receptors to regulate neuronal migration and axon pathfinding. SLIT-ROBO signaling has tumor-suppressive roles in some contexts, and SLIT1 has been reported as silenced or mutated in multiple cancer types. In adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC), SLIT1 mutation is part of a recurrently disrupted axon-guidance pathway hypothesized to facilitate perineural invasion.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SLIT1 single-tumor somatic mutation detected in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC) of the salivary gland as part of a recurrently disrupted axon-guidance pathway (56% of tumors in the cohort) [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC): SLIT1 mutation part of broader axon-guidance pathway disruption present in 56% of tumors; hypothesized to contribute to perineural invasion [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- SLIT1 mutation co-occurs with other axon-guidance pathway alterations ([SEMA3G](../genes/SEMA3G.md), [SEMA5A](../genes/SEMA5A.md), [SRGAP2](../genes/SRGAP2.md), [SRGAP3](../genes/SRGAP3.md), [PLXNA1](../genes/PLXNA1.md), [PLXNB1](../genes/PLXNB1.md), [PRKD1](../genes/PRKD1.md)) in ACC [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Slit/Robo pathway exploration is flagged as a candidate therapeutic strategy for ACC; no clinical data yet [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Open questions

- Whether SLIT1 mutation in ACC is functionally relevant to Robo-mediated tumor invasion or is a passenger event remains unresolved [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)
