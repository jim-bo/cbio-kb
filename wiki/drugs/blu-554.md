# BLU-554

## Overview

BLU-554 is a selective covalent inhibitor of FGFR4 ([FGFR4](../genes/FGFR4.md)), designed to exploit FGF19/FGFR4 signaling in hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC). [FGF19](../genes/FGF19.md) gene amplification (~5–10% of HCCs) and protein overexpression detected by IHC serve as enrollment biomarkers.

## Evidence in the corpus

- In HCC, BLU-554 produced a 16% response rate in FGFR4-driven (≥1% FGF19 IHC) patients vs 0% in FGFR4-negative patients; FGF19 expression by IHC required for enrichment [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md).
