# H3B-6527

## Overview

H3B-6527 is a selective FGFR4 inhibitor. It targets the FGF19–FGFR4 signaling axis, which is activated in a subset of hepatocellular carcinomas ([HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)) harboring [FGF19](../genes/FGF19.md) high-level focal amplification (~6% of HCCs). Enrichment biomarker: FGF19 expression by IHC (typically ≥1% positive cells).

## Evidence in the corpus

- H3B-6527 listed alongside [BLU-554](../drugs/blu-554.md) and FGF401 as selective FGFR4 inhibitors requiring [FGF19](../genes/FGF19.md) IHC expression for patient enrichment in HCC; BLU-554 achieved 16% ORR in FGFR4-driven patients vs 0% in FGFR4-negative [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md).
