# EP300

## Overview

EP300 (E1A Binding Protein P300) is a histone acetyltransferase and transcriptional co-activator. EP300 is a chromatin-modifying gene frequently mutated in urothelial carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Chromatin-modifying gene with high prevalence of non-silent variants in metastatic urothelial carcinoma (UC-GENOME cohort), consistent with TCGA-BLCA [PMID:36333289](../papers/36333289.md)
- EP300 was identified as a recurrently mutated gene in DLBCL and [FL](../cancer_types/FL.md), implicating histone acetyltransferase activity in lymphomagenesis [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- Inactivating mutations in HAT domain in [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) (29 tumors, CLCGP WES/WGS); mutually exclusive with [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) alterations [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
- Hotspot mutations in chromatin-modifying gene in [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) (36 tumors, JHU WES/WGS) [PMID:22941189](../papers/22941189.md)
- Splice-site mutation in the KAT11 acetyltransferase domain (G1429_splice) identified in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC); functions as a [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) co-activator [PMID:23685749](../papers/23685749.md)
- Mutated (along with CREBBP p.R1446C in the KAT11 domain) in adenoid cystic carcinoma; part of a chromatin-remodeling gene cluster mutated in 12/24 cases [PMID:23778141](../papers/23778141.md)
- Mutation in bladder TCC as part of a chromatin-remodeling gene cluster altered in 58% of cases [PMID:24121792](../papers/24121792.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Urothelial carcinoma (UC): recurrently mutated chromatin modifier [PMID:36333289](../papers/36333289.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Frequently co-mutated with other chromatin-modifying genes ([KMT2D](../genes/KMT2D.md), [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md), [KDM6A](../genes/KDM6A.md), [KMT2C](../genes/KMT2C.md), [KMT2A](../genes/KMT2A.md), [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md), [SMARCA4](../genes/SMARCA4.md)) in metastatic UC [PMID:36333289](../papers/36333289.md)
