# OLIG1

## Overview

OLIG1 (oligodendrocyte transcription factor 1) is a bHLH transcription factor required for oligodendrocyte specification and myelination. In oligodendroglioma, OLIG1 marks the differentiated oligodendrocyte-like tumor cell subpopulation, one of two glial lineage programs identified by single-cell RNA-seq.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- OLIG1 is highly expressed in differentiated oligodendrocyte-like tumor cells in grade II oligodendroglioma (IDH-mutant, 1p/19q codeleted), alongside [OLIG2](../genes/OLIG2.md) and [OMG](../genes/OMG.md); these cells coexist with astrocyte-like ([GFAP](../genes/GFAP.md), [APOE](../genes/APOE.md), [SOX9](../genes/SOX9.md)) and stem/progenitor (SOX2/SOX4/SOX11-high) subpopulations within the same tumors [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [ODG](../cancer_types/ODG.md) — OLIG1 marks the differentiated oligodendrocyte-like compartment; used to establish the developmental hierarchy in oligodendroglioma alongside scRNA-seq data [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [OLIG2](../genes/OLIG2.md) and [OMG](../genes/OMG.md) in oligodendrocyte-like cells; mutually exclusive with astrocyte-like ([GFAP](../genes/GFAP.md), [APOE](../genes/APOE.md)) program at single-cell resolution [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- OLIG1-high differentiated cells are non-proliferating (MKI67-low); therapeutic targeting would need to address the [MKI67](../genes/MKI67.md)+ stem/progenitor subpopulation rather than the bulk differentiated population [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).

## Open questions

- Whether OLIG1 expression is causally maintained by the IDH mutation or 1p/19q codeletion in these tumors is not addressed [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).
