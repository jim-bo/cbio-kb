# Affymetrix U133 Plus 2.0 Array

## Overview

The Affymetrix GeneChip Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array is a microarray platform covering approximately 47,000 transcripts and variants including 38,500 well-characterized human genes. It is widely used for genome-wide gene expression profiling in cancer research and clinical studies.

## Used by

- Applied in ICGC PedBrain pilocytic astrocytoma study for gene expression profiling of 118 pilocytic astrocytomas (including 66 from the WGS cohort) versus 158 other astrocytic tumors; revealed significantly elevated [FGF2](../genes/FGF2.md) and [PTPN11](../genes/PTPN11.md) (SHP-2) expression in pilocytic astrocytoma regardless of [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) status [PMID:23817572](../papers/23817572.md)
