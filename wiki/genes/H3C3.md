# H3C3

## Overview

H3C3 (formerly HIST1H3C) encodes histone H3.1, a replication-dependent core histone. Somatic mutations at canonical hotspot residues — particularly K27 and K36 — have been characterized as oncogenic drivers in pediatric gliomas (H3 K27M) and other cancers by altering chromatin methylation state genome-wide. In [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md), H3C3 was detected at low frequency and flagged as a possible false positive requiring additional confirmatory evidence.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a candidate alteration in cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (CSCC) but classified as a 'possible false positive' requiring additional evidence in an 83-tumor CSCC meta-analysis [PMID:34272401](../papers/34272401.md)
