# NAV3

## Overview

NAV3 (Neuron Navigator 3) encodes a member of the neuron navigator protein family, which guides axonal growth cone migration. In dedifferentiated liposarcoma (DDLPS), NAV3 resides within the hallmark 12q13~15 amplicon and is recurrently amplified alongside [MDM2](../genes/MDM2.md), [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md), [HMGA2](../genes/HMGA2.md), and [FRS2](../genes/FRS2.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- NAV3 amplification was present in 60% of DDLPS cases (n=50) in the TCGA sarcoma cohort; NAV3 is located at 12q13~15, the defining amplicon of DDLPS; copy number and mRNA levels were significantly correlated (p≤0.001) [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **DDLPS (Dedifferentiated Liposarcoma):** NAV3 amplification observed in 60% of DDLPS cases as part of the 12q13~15 amplicon; co-amplified with MDM2 (100%), CDK4 (92%), HMGA2 (76%), and FRS2 (96%) [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-amplified with MDM2, CDK4, HMGA2, FRS2, [JUN](../genes/JUN.md), [DDIT3](../genes/DDIT3.md), [PTPRQ](../genes/PTPRQ.md), [YAP1](../genes/YAP1.md), and [CEBPA](../genes/CEBPA.md) in DDLPS at 12q13~15; PTPRQ and JUN amplifications tended to be mutually exclusive (p=0.026) within the broader amplicon [PMID:29100075](../papers/29100075.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- NAV3 amplification has not been directly therapeutically targeted; its co-amplification with MDM2 and CDK4 in DDLPS is relevant to the therapeutic context of MDM2 inhibitors and CDK4/6 inhibitors being evaluated in DDLPS.

## Open questions

- Whether NAV3 amplification has functional consequences independent of MDM2/CDK4 co-amplification, or is a bystander within the 12q13~15 amplicon, has not been established.
