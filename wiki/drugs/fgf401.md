# FGF401

## Overview

FGF401 is a selective, non-covalent FGFR4 ([FGFR4](../genes/FGFR4.md)) inhibitor under investigation in hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) enriched for [FGF19](../genes/FGF19.md) expression. Like BLU-554, FGF401 requires FGF19 protein expression (by IHC) as an enrollment biomarker, exploiting FGF19–FGFR4 axis dependence in a subset of HCCs.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Cited alongside BLU-554 and H3B-6527 as an FGFR4 inhibitor requiring FGF19 IHC expression for biomarker-driven enrichment in HCC; FGF19 amplification occurs in ~5–10% of HCCs [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md).
