# pimurutamab

## Overview

Pimurutamab is an investigational anti-EGFR monoclonal antibody targeting [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md). It is evaluated in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)), where EGFR is overexpressed and drives tumor progression.
