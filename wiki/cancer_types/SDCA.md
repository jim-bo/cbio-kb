# Salivary Duct Carcinoma (SDCA)

## Overview

Salivary Duct Carcinoma is an aggressive salivary gland malignancy sitting under [SACA](../cancer_types/SACA.md) (Salivary Carcinoma) in OncoTree. It morphologically resembles ductal carcinoma of the breast and frequently harbors actionable alterations including HER2 amplification and [AR](../genes/AR.md) expression, enabling targeted therapeutic strategies.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [hnc_mskcc_2016](../datasets/hnc_mskcc_2016.md) — included as part of the 56 salivary carcinomas (20 "other salivary" beyond [ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md)) in 151 recurrent/metastatic head and neck tumors profiled by MSK-IMPACT 410-gene panel [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- **[PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md)** — alterations identified; prompted PI3K-inhibitor trial enrollment in the MSK-IMPACT cohort [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[HRAS](../genes/HRAS.md)** — mutations recurrent in SDCA in the MSK-IMPACT cohort [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[ERBB2](../genes/ERBB2.md)** — actionable alterations (amplification or mutation); one of the most clinically significant findings in SDCA [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[AR](../genes/AR.md)** — androgen receptor expression/alteration; actionable in SDCA, enabling androgen deprivation therapy [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[NF1](../genes/NF1.md)**, **[ROS1](../genes/ROS1.md)** — structural variants identified in SDCA cases; potential therapeutic targets [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).

## Subtypes

- Not further subclassified in the corpus.

## Therapeutic landscape

- **HER2-directed therapy** — [ERBB2](../genes/ERBB2.md) amplification/mutation in SDCA is a major actionable target; parallels breast ductal carcinoma management rationale [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **Androgen deprivation therapy** — AR expression in SDCA motivates androgen receptor-targeted approaches [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **PI3K inhibitors** — for PIK3CA-altered SDCA; patients enrolled on PI3K-inhibitor basket trials in the MSK-IMPACT cohort [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).

## Sources
