# XRCC2

## Overview

XRCC2 (X-Ray Repair Cross Complementing 2) is a classical [RAD51](../genes/RAD51.md) paralog involved in homologous recombination (HR) DNA repair. It forms part of the BCDX2 complex and promotes RAD51-mediated repair of DNA double-strand breaks. Germline loss-of-function variants in XRCC2 have been associated with elevated breast cancer risk.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Classical RAD51 paralog cited as biological context for [RAD51B](../genes/RAD51B.md) loss-of-function analysis in a cohort of 3,422 breast/ovarian cancer patients. [PMID:34635660](../papers/34635660.md)
