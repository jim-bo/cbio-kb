# JTE-013

## Overview

JTE-013 is a selective antagonist of sphingosine-1-phosphate receptor 2 ([S1PR2](../genes/S1PR2.md)). In cholangiocarcinoma ([CHOL](../cancer_types/CHOL.md)), [S1PR2](../genes/S1PR2.md) is activated by taurocholic acid, triggering ERK/AKT/NF-kB cascades and upregulating [PTGS2](../genes/PTGS2.md) (COX-2), which suppresses CD8+ T-cell activity and recruits regulatory T cells. JTE-013 blocks these pro-tumorigenic and immunosuppressive downstream effects.

## Evidence in the corpus

- JTE-013 ([S1PR2](../genes/S1PR2.md) antagonist) suppresses tumor invasiveness in preclinical CCA models by blocking taurocholic-acid–activated [S1PR2](../genes/S1PR2.md)→ERK/AKT/NF-kB→[PTGS2](../genes/PTGS2.md) (COX-2) signaling that drives CD8+ T-cell suppression and regulatory T-cell recruitment [PMID:25608663](../papers/25608663.md)
