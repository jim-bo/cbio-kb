# Kidney Chromophobe / KICH (TCGA)

## Overview

KICH is the TCGA cohort identifier for kidney chromophobe renal cell carcinoma. The closest OncoTree equivalent is [CHRCC](CHRCC.md) (Chromophobe Renal Cell Carcinoma). This page covers findings specifically attributed to the TCGA KICH cohort in the corpus. KICH is characterized by low tumor purity and large sample-to-sample variability in mutation burden.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- TCGA KICH cohort: included as one of 33 cancer types in the MC3 pan-cancer mutation-calling project and the TCGA fusion landscape study; subset of the PanCancer Atlas ([kich_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018](../datasets/kich_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018.md)).

## Recurrent alterations

- MC3 pan-cancer mutation-calling project (10,510 TCGA tumor/normal pairs) identified KICH alongside [THYM](../cancer_types/THYM.md), [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md), and [UVM](../cancer_types/UVM.md) as showing the largest sample-to-sample variability in mutations per sample, attributable to low tumor purity [PMID:29596782](../papers/29596782.md).
- Pan-cancer fusion study (9,624 TCGA samples) found KICH has a median of 0 fusions per sample; fusion neoantigen burden was lowest in KICH (mean 0.33 predicted neoantigens per fusion) [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md).
- Included in TCGA PanCancer Atlas integrative molecular analysis; KICH co-clusters with ACC in iCluster C9 (both hypodiploid) rather than with other kidney cancers [PMID:29625048](../papers/29625048.md)
- Included in pan-cancer pathway analysis of 9,125 TCGA tumors; KICH has low actionable-alteration rates [PMID:29625050](../papers/29625050.md)
- Included in TCGA Pan-Cancer Clinical Data Resource (11,160 patients, 33 cancer types); KICH had longest median follow-up (~48 months); KICH use endpoint with caution in CDR [PMID:29625055](../papers/29625055.md)

## Subtypes

- Chromophobe [RCC](../cancer_types/RCC.md) is histologically and molecularly distinct from clear cell ([CCRCC](../cancer_types/CCRCC.md)) and papillary ([PRCC](../cancer_types/PRCC.md)) subtypes; KICH (TCGA) corresponds approximately to OncoTree [CHRCC](../cancer_types/CHRCC.md).
