# FOSB

## Overview

FOSB ([FOS](../genes/FOS.md) Proto-Oncogene, AP-1 Transcription Factor Subunit) encodes a leucine zipper protein that dimerizes with [JUN](../genes/JUN.md) family members to form the AP-1 transcription factor complex. In cancer biology, AP-1 complexes regulate cell proliferation, differentiation, and survival. FOSB is a member of the AP-1 family implicated in castration-resistant prostate cancer (CRPC) lineage plasticity, particularly in the stem cell-like (SCL) subtype driven by AP-1 pioneer factors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- AP-1 family member including FOSB identified as part of the transcription factor network enriched in CRPC-SCL chromatin-accessible peaks; CRPC-SCL is the second-most-common CRPC subtype (~28%) among 366 patients across SU2C and WCM cohorts, associated with shorter time on next-generation ARSI [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [PRAD](../cancer_types/PRAD.md) / [PRSCC](../cancer_types/PRSCC.md): AP-1 family (including FOSB) upregulated in CRPC-SCL subtype identified by integrative ATAC-seq/RNA-seq profiling [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [FOSL1](../genes/FOSL1.md), [FOS](../genes/FOS.md), [FOSL2](../genes/FOSL2.md), [JUN](../genes/JUN.md), [JUNB](../genes/JUNB.md), [JUND](../genes/JUND.md) as part of the broader AP-1 family TF network in CRPC-SCL [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- The c-Fos/AP-1 inhibitor T-5224 preferentially inhibits proliferation of CRPC-SCL models (MSKPCa3 and DU145) vs CRPC-AR models, nominating AP-1 including FOS/FOSB members as therapeutic targets [PMID:35617398](../papers/35617398.md)
