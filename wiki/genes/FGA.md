# FGA

## Overview

FGA (Fibrinogen Alpha Chain) encodes a component of fibrinogen, a hepatocyte-secreted clotting protein. In [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) genomics, FGA was newly highlighted as part of a hepatic-differentiation pathway recurrently mutated in tumors, suggesting loss of hepatocyte-specific function contributes to [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) pathogenesis.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Hepatocyte-secreted protein gene recurrently mutated in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md); part of the newly highlighted hepatic-differentiation pathway (34% pathway alteration) [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)**: Recurrently mutated alongside [ALB](../genes/ALB.md) and [APOB](../genes/APOB.md) as hepatocyte-secreted protein genes defining the hepatic-differentiation pathway in a comprehensive HCC genomic study [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity
