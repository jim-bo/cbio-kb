# PEAR1

## Overview

PEAR1 (Platelet Endothelial Aggregation Receptor 1, also known as JEDI and MEGF12) encodes a type-I transmembrane receptor with EGF-like and LDL-receptor domains that mediates phagocytic signaling via [SYK](../genes/SYK.md) and SRC-family kinases. Originally characterized in platelet aggregation, PEAR1 was identified as a single-gene prognostic biomarker for overall survival in acute myeloid leukemia ([AML](../cancer_types/AML.md)) through WGCNA module analysis of the BEAT AML 2.0 dataset. It is the focal gene of PMID:35868306.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Single-gene expression hub of WGCNA module 3 in AML (BEAT AML 2.0, n=805 patients); positively correlated with HSC-like cell state; expression elevated in [TP53](../genes/TP53.md)-, [RUNX1](../genes/RUNX1.md)-, [ASXL1](../genes/ASXL1.md)-, [SRSF2](../genes/SRSF2.md)-, [GATA2](../genes/GATA2.md)-, and [MECOM](../genes/MECOM.md)-mutant AML; lower in [NPM1](../genes/NPM1.md)- and [CEBPA](../genes/CEBPA.md)-mutant cases [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)
- Apparent ethnic-expression difference: Non-Hispanic White mean 1.395 (N=359) vs African Heritage mean 2.205 (N=13) — warrants validation in larger cohorts [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)
- Co-nominated with [PYCARD](../genes/PYCARD.md) in a prior TCGA-LAML immune prognostic signature (Dao et al., 2021) [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **AML (acute myeloid leukemia):** PEAR1 expression predicts shortened [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) across age groups; independent of ELN risk in young (<45 y) patients; hazard ratio equivalent to LSC17 signature [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Elevated PEAR1 co-occurs with [TP53](../genes/TP53.md), [RUNX1](../genes/RUNX1.md), [ASXL1](../genes/ASXL1.md), [BCOR](../genes/BCOR.md), [SF3B1](../genes/SF3B1.md), [SRSF2](../genes/SRSF2.md), [GATA2](../genes/GATA2.md), [MECOM](../genes/MECOM.md) mutations (HSC-like state); lower expression with [NPM1](../genes/NPM1.md) and [CEBPA](../genes/CEBPA.md) (favorable-risk markers) [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Proposed as single-gene prognostic biomarker for OS in AML, performing equivalently to LSC17 and carrying prognostic value independent of ELN risk in young (<45 y) patients [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)
- PEAR1 is surface-expressed on AML blasts, raising the possibility of small-molecule and antibody-based targeting; PEAR1-null mice retain platelet function, suggesting a potential therapeutic window [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)

## Open questions

- Prognostic significance established in anthracycline-treated cohorts; PEAR1's prognostic value under [venetoclax](../drugs/venetoclax.md) + HMA combinations or other modern regimens remains untested [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)
- Forced PEAR1 expression in bone-marrow stromal cells was shown to reduce clonogenic myeloid colony formation — apparently counterintuitive to the adverse prognosis association; underlying biology unresolved [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)
- Ethnic differences in PEAR1 expression warrant validation in larger non-Caucasian cohorts [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)
