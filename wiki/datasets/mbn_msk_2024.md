# Mature B-Cell Neoplasms (MSK, Blood Advances 2024)

## Overview

MSK clinical cohort of 396 DLBCL NOS cases sequenced by [MSK-IMPACT Heme](../methods/msk-hemepact.md), used to validate the LymphGen molecular classification algorithm on a 400-gene clinical next-generation sequencing panel. Data deposited in cBioPortal as `mbn_msk_2024`. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)

## Composition

- 396 [DLBCLNOS](../cancer_types/DLBCLNOS.md) cases sequenced by MSK-IMPACT Heme at MSKCC. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)
- 320 of 396 cases had both [BCL2](../genes/BCL2.md) and [BCL6](../genes/BCL6.md) clinical cytogenetic results available. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)

## Assays / panels (linked)

- [MSK-IMPACT Heme](../methods/msk-hemepact.md) — hybridization capture-based 400-gene panel detecting SNVs, small INDELs, and CNAs in hematopoietic malignancies; uses saliva or nail clippings as germline source. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)
- [FISH](../methods/fish.md) translocation data for [BCL2](../genes/BCL2.md) and [BCL6](../genes/BCL6.md) for LymphGen classification. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md) — Validation of LymphGen classification on a 400-gene clinical NGS panel in DLBCL.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- 55% of 396 cases classified by LymphGen into a molecular subtype: MCD 10%, EZB 22%, BN2 10%, N1 3%, ST2 7%, A53 2%, composite 2%. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)
- MSK Core group (high-confidence predictions, n=148, 37.4% of total): MCD 18.2%, EZB 44%, BN2 22%, N1 7%, ST2 7%, A53 2%. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)
- EZB subtypes predominantly GCB; MCD and N1 predominantly ABC/non-GCB, consistent with NCI findings. [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md)
