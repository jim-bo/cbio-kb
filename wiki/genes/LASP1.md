# LASP1

## Overview

LASP1 (LIM and SH3 Protein 1) encodes an actin-binding scaffold protein that links the actin cytoskeleton to signaling networks. It has been implicated in cell migration, invasion, and metastasis in multiple cancer types, including breast cancer, where it is recurrently amplified at chromosome 17q12.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- LASP1 detected as an altered gene in the whole-exome sequencing landscape of synchronous ductal carcinoma in situ ([DCIS](../cancer_types/DCIS.md)) and matched invasive ductal carcinomas of no special type (IDC-NST) from 25 patients at MSKCC; found in the somatic alteration repertoire of this DCIS/IDC-NST cohort [PMID:32220886](../papers/32220886.md)
