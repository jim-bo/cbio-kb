# PDCD6

## Overview

PDCD6 (Programmed Cell Death 6; also ALG-2) encodes a calcium-binding protein of the penta-EF-hand family involved in apoptotic signaling. In cancer genomics, it has been identified as a structural rearrangement partner near the TERT promoter in chromophobe renal cell carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Partner gene in one intrachromosomal TERT-promoter structural rearrangement in chromophobe RCC; rearrangement associated with >500-unit TERT expression upregulation [PMID:25155756](../papers/25155756.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- ChRCC: structural rearrangement partner with TERT promoter region [PMID:25155756](../papers/25155756.md)
