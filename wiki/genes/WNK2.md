# WNK2

## Overview

WNK2 (with-no-lysine kinase 2) is a serine-threonine kinase and member of the WNK kinase family. WNK2 has been identified in recurrent WNK-family gene fusions in the TCGA pan-cancer fusion landscape, where WNK-family fusions are associated with elevated WNK mRNA without corresponding copy-number amplification.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as part of 23 WNK-family fusions across TCGA pan-cancer samples; WNK-family fusions produce higher WNK mRNA without copy-number amplification [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md)
