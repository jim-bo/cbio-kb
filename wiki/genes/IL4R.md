# IL4R

## Overview

IL4R encodes the interleukin-4 receptor alpha chain. In the cbio-kb corpus it appears as a non-canonical AID off-target in classic Hodgkin lymphoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- In classic Hodgkin lymphoma HRS cells, IL4R showed an elevated noncoding:coding mutation ratio consistent with activation-induced cytidine deaminase (AID) off-target activity [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [CHL](../cancer_types/CHL.md) — AID off-target noncoding mutation accumulation in HRS cells [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Grouped with [BCL7A](../genes/BCL7A.md), [SOCS1](../genes/SOCS1.md), [TMSB4X](../genes/TMSB4X.md), and [ITPKB](../genes/ITPKB.md) as genes with elevated noncoding:coding mutation ratios in cHL [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- None reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Functional consequence of noncoding AID-driven IL4R lesions in cHL is not established [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
