# HSPG2

## Overview

HSPG2 (Perlecan) encodes a heparan sulfate proteoglycan component of the extracellular matrix. Frequent alterations in anaplastic thyroid carcinoma implicate it in tumor cell migration and invasion alongside [HMCN1](../genes/HMCN1.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- HSPG2 is a cell-adhesion gene with frequent alterations in anaplastic thyroid carcinoma (ATC) in the GATCI study (n=141 WES/WGS); implicated in tumor cell migration and invasion alongside [HMCN1](../genes/HMCN1.md) [PMID:38412093](../papers/38412093.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [THAP](../cancer_types/THAP.md) — recurrent alterations observed in ATC in the multi-site GATCI consortium (329 thyroid cancer regions across 292 patients, 15 institutions) [PMID:38412093](../papers/38412093.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-altered with [HMCN1](../genes/HMCN1.md) in ATC as part of a cell-adhesion gene alteration cluster [PMID:38412093](../papers/38412093.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported; cell-adhesion alterations may contribute to metastatic potential in ATC [PMID:38412093](../papers/38412093.md).

## Open questions

- Functional studies are needed to determine which specific HSPG2 mutations contribute to ATC invasion [PMID:38412093](../papers/38412093.md).
