# AMACR

## Overview

AMACR (Alpha-Methylacyl-CoA Racemase) encodes an enzyme involved in beta-oxidation of branched-chain fatty acids. Best known as a prostate cancer biomarker, AMACR also shows concordant upregulation at mRNA and protein in CTNNB1-mutant hepatocellular carcinoma, suggesting involvement in Wnt-driven metabolic reprogramming.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- AMACR protein concordantly upregulated at mRNA and protein in CTNNB1-mutant HCCs in a Chinese HCC proteogenomics cohort (n=159) [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC): AMACR upregulation in CTNNB1-mutant subtype; concordant mRNA and protein elevation [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-elevated with GLUL and ACSS3 in CTNNB1-mutant HCC [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)
