# MSK-IMPACT 505 (IMPACT505)

## Overview

Targeted (505 cancer genes) sequencing of various tumor types via MSK-IMPACT on Illumina HiSeq sequencers — canonical cBioPortal gene panel `IMPACT505`. The largest/most-recent generation of MSK-IMPACT used in the corpus.

## Used by

- [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md) — MSK-IMPACT (up to 505 genes) with select targeted RNA sequencing was used in the Make-an-IMPACT direct-to-patient program; 250/333 enrolled patients had tumor DNA of sufficient quality. In histiocytosis (n=84), most commonly mutated actionable genes were [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) (33%), [MAP2K1](../genes/MAP2K1.md) (13%), [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) (7%), and [CSF1R](../genes/CSF1R.md) (2.4%) [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md) — MSK-IMPACT 410–505 was used in 40% of 61 extremity rhabdomyosarcoma patients; identified mutually exclusive [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplifications and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions associated with poor OS [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md) — one of the panel versions used in the 233-patient [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) brain metastasis cohort [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md).

## Notes

- IMPACT505 is the broadest version of MSK-IMPACT, succeeding IMPACT341/410/468 [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
