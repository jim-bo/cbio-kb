# Ganglioglioma (GNG)

## Overview

Ganglioglioma (GNG) is a rare low-grade glioneuronal tumor of the CNS characterized by a mixed population of neoplastic ganglion cells and glial cells. In OncoTree it sits under ENCG within the Glioma main type. Pediatric gangliogliomas are typically WHO grade 1 or 2 and are strongly associated with CDK5/GSK3B kinase network activity and synaptogenesis-related phosphorylation patterns.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [brain_cptac_2020](../datasets/brain_cptac_2020.md) — 18 ganglioglioma samples in a 218-sample pediatric brain tumor proteogenomic cohort from Children's Hospital of Philadelphia / CBTTC. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- [CDK5](../genes/CDK5.md), [GSK3B](../genes/GSK3B.md) — upregulated in ganglioglioma and the Ganglio-rich [LGG](../cancer_types/LGG.md) subgroup; substrate phosphorylation patterns (ADD2 Ser604/Ser693, SYN1 Ser553, STMN1 Ser38) consistent with synaptogenesis and glioma–neuron synapse formation. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- [CAMK2A](../genes/CAMK2A.md) — most abundant in ganglioglioma (highest protein levels among all histologies); in HGG [CAMK2A](../genes/CAMK2A.md) shows highest kinase activity, phosphorylating GJA1 (Ser325/Ser314) and SYN1 (Ser568/Ser605), linking to glioma–neuron synapse-mediated invasion. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)

## Subtypes

- **Ganglio-rich LGG subgroup (proteomic)** — GNG tumors co-cluster with a subset of [LGGNOS](../cancer_types/LGGNOS.md) in a Ganglio-rich proteomic subgroup defined by CDK5/GSK3B activity and synaptogenic substrate phosphorylation. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- **Hot microenvironment cluster** — mixed LGG/HGG/ganglioglioma cluster overexpressing adenosine producers (ENTPD1, [NT5E](../genes/NT5E.md)); adenosine-axis therapies proposed. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)

## Therapeutic landscape

- Adenosine-reducing therapies proposed for the Hot tumor microenvironment cluster, which includes ganglioglioma alongside LGG/HGG. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- [CDK5](../genes/CDK5.md) inhibition is a hypothesis-generating target based on CDK5/GSK3B kinase-activity upregulation in ganglioglioma; not yet tested clinically in this histology. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
