# KDM4C

## Overview

KDM4C (lysine demethylase 4C) is a histone H3K9/H3K36 demethylase involved in transcriptional regulation and chromatin remodeling. It is recurrently mutated or amplified in several cancer types including prostate cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Chromatin-modifier mutation identified in MSK prostate cancer cell lines, consistent with Grasso 2012 CRPC findings [PMID:25201530](../papers/25201530.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Prostate cancer (CRPC): recurrent chromatin modifier mutation.
