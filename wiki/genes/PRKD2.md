# PRKD2

## Overview

PRKD2 encodes protein kinase D2, a member of the calcium/calmodulin-dependent protein kinase superfamily involved in cell survival, proliferation, and motility. It has roles downstream of diacylglycerol and PKC signaling. In hepatocellular carcinoma, PRKD2 activity was identified as elevated by kinase-substrate enrichment analysis (KSEA) in TP53-mutant tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Elevated kinase activity by KSEA in TP53-mutant HCCs in a 122-patient multi-omics cohort (WES + RNA-seq + proteome + phosphoproteome); co-elevated with CDK1/2/5, [AURKA](../genes/AURKA.md), [TTK](../genes/TTK.md), [NEK2](../genes/NEK2.md), [EIF2AK2](../genes/EIF2AK2.md), and p38delta ([MAPK13](../genes/MAPK13.md)) [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)**: PRKD2 kinase activity elevated in [TP53](../genes/TP53.md)-mutant HCCs by KSEA in a European multi-omics cohort; [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation associated with higher tumor grade and HBV association [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Elevated PRKD2 activity co-occurs with [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation and increased [CDK1](../genes/CDK1.md), [CDK2](../genes/CDK2.md), [AURKA](../genes/AURKA.md), and [MAPK13](../genes/MAPK13.md) activity [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- PRKD2 is among the elevated kinases in TP53-mutant [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) noted as candidates for targeted inhibition in this context [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Open questions

- Whether PRKD2 elevation is a direct consequence of TP53 loss or reflects co-occurring oncogenic signaling in aggressive HCC remains to be dissected.
