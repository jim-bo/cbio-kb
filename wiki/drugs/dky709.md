# DKY709

## Overview

DKY709 is an investigational small-molecule degrader targeting IKZF2 (Helios; [IKZF2](../genes/IKZF2.md)), a transcription factor that stabilizes regulatory T cell (Treg) suppressive function. By degrading Helios, DKY709 is designed to reprogram intratumoral Tregs and relieve immunosuppression in the tumor microenvironment.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Cited among IKZF2 degraders (alongside PLX-4545) under clinical investigation to reprogram Tregs in R/M NPC [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
