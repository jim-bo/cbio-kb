# Ovary/Fallopian Tube (OVARY)

## Overview

OVARY is the organ-level OncoTree node encompassing all ovarian and fallopian tube malignancies (parent: TISSUE). Histologic subtypes include high-grade serous ovarian cancer ([HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md)) and other ovarian carcinoma variants. This page aggregates findings reported at the organ-system level; for histotype-specific data see subtype pages.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- PCAWG pan-cancer WGS cohort (n=2,658 tumors, 38 types): ovarian adenocarcinoma was among the 38 tumor types analyzed for somatic driver landscapes and mutational processes [PMID:32025007](../papers/32025007.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- In PCAWG, ovarian adenocarcinoma showed strong SV dominance over point mutations (mean 5.8 ± 2.6 SVs vs 1.9 ± 1.0 point mutations, P < 1×10⁻¹⁶); germline [BRCA1](../genes/BRCA1.md)/[BRCA2](../genes/BRCA2.md) PTVs accounted for 81% of germline+somatic biallelic combinations across PCAWG [PMID:32025007](../papers/32025007.md).
- CDK12 alterations found in 43/1,034 (4.2%) of ovarian cancers sequenced by MSK-IMPACT (pan-cancer cohort of 26,743 tumors); biallelic inactivation present in 26/28 (93%) CDK12-altered ovarian cases, predominantly via LOH (20/26, 77%), and CDK12 was significantly recurrently mutated in ovarian cancer (FDR=0.056) [PMID:32317181](../papers/32317181.md)

## Subtypes

- [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) — High-Grade Serous Ovarian Cancer; the dominant histotype in the PCAWG cohort.
