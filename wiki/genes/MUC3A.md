# MUC3A

## Overview

MUC3A (Mucin 3A, Cell Surface Associated) is an intestinal-type transmembrane mucin. In uveal melanoma, recurrent missense mutations have been observed but lack functional support for driver status.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- 2 missense mutations identified in uveal melanoma WGS cohort (n=28); flagged as recurrent but without functional support [PMID:26683228](../papers/26683228.md)
