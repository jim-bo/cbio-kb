# sirolimus

## Overview

Macrolide mTORC1 inhibitor (rapamycin).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Referenced among matched targeted therapy options in the Make-an-IMPACT rare-cancer program where matched therapy produced clinical benefit in 17/18 histiocytosis patients (mean duration 21.7 months) [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- In the UCLA PDTO sarcoma screen, PDTOs from patients with progressive disease at follow-up were more resistant to sirolimus/rapamycin (p=0.014) compared to non-progressors. This inverse correlation — reduced mTOR sensitivity in the most aggressive sarcomas — contrasts with the sorafenib/ruxolitinib pattern seen in the same progressive subset. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)

- Not reported in corpus.
