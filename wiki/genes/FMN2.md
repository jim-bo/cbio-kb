# FMN2

## Overview

FMN2 (Formin 2) is an actin-nucleation factor involved in cytoskeletal organization. It was identified as having clustered/damaging mutations in small cell lung cancer ([SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md)), though its functional role in cancer is not yet established.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Clustered/damaging mutations in [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) (110-tumour WGS cohort); also mutated in murine [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) model (along with [PTGFRN](../genes/PTGFRN.md)) [PMID:26168399](../papers/26168399.md)

## Cancer types (linked)
