# Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma (MSK/Sanger/MDACC, 2019)

## Overview

The largest adenoid cystic carcinoma ([ACC](../cancer_types/ACC.md)) cohort assembled to date, comprising 1,045 tumors (177 primary, 868 recurrent/metastatic [R/M]) from patients treated at MSKCC, the Sanger Institute, and MD Anderson Cancer Center. Genomic data were collected via whole-exome sequencing (WES), whole-genome sequencing (WGS), targeted NGS with [MSK-IMPACT](../methods/msk-impact-panel.md) (n=94 MSKCC R/M cases), and [FoundationOne](../methods/foundationone.md) (n=730 R/M cases). The dataset enables the first statistically powered comparison of primary vs. R/M molecular landscapes in [adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACYC)](../cancer_types/ACYC.md), defining four mutually exclusive molecular subgroups with distinct survival outcomes [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).

## Composition

- **Total cases:** 1,045 — 177 primary, 868 recurrent/metastatic [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- **Cancer type:** [Adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACYC)](../cancer_types/ACYC.md); 89.8% salivary/head-and-neck origin, 6.8% lung, 3.4% breast [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- **Clinical follow-up:** 84 MSKCC R/M ACC patients, median follow-up 8.1 years.
- **Germline panel:** 90 R/M MSKCC cases analyzed against a 76-gene cancer-predisposition panel.
- **Clonality / ITH:** Allele-specific copy number by [FACETS](../methods/facets.md); subclonal structure by [PyClone](../methods/pyclone.md); multiregion WES + ultra-deep targeted sequencing (mean ×5,406 coverage) on one salivary ACC with 6 primary subregions + 8 lung metastases.

## Assays / panels (linked)

- [Whole-exome sequencing](../methods/whole-exome-seq.md) and [whole-genome sequencing](../methods/whole-genome-seq.md) — n=193 combined; mean TMB 0.34 mut/Mb [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- [MSK-IMPACT](../methods/msk-impact-panel.md) (410- or 468-gene hybridization capture; median exon coverage >×600) — 94 MSKCC R/M cases [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- [FoundationOne](../methods/foundationone.md) (median exon coverage >×500) — 730 R/M cases [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- Custom Ion AmpliSeq panel (165 amplicons; mean coverage ×5,406) for multiregion clonal validation [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- [FISH](../methods/fish.md) for [MYB](../genes/MYB.md) and [MYBL1](../genes/MYBL1.md) rearrangements; MYB-NFIB clonality confirmed with 3-color BAC probe mix [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- Microsatellite status by [MSIsensor](../methods/msisensor.md) and [MiMSI](../methods/mimsi.md) [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- Variant annotation by [OncoKB](../methods/oncokb-annotation.md) [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md) — Ho, Ochoa et al. Integrated genomic analysis of adenoid cystic carcinomas; defines four molecular subgroups and survival impact of [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md), [KDM6A](../genes/KDM6A.md), and [TERT](../genes/TERT.md) alterations.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- R/M ACCs are significantly enriched for [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) activating mutations (26.3% R/M vs. 8.5% primary; OR 3.86, P<0.0001) and chromatin-remodeling gene alterations ([KDM6A](../genes/KDM6A.md) 15.2%, [KMT2C](../genes/KMT2C.md) 14.3%, [ARID1B](../genes/ARID1B.md) 14.1%, [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md) 13.7%, [BCOR](../genes/BCOR.md) 13.3%) vs. primary tumors [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- [TERT](../genes/TERT.md) promoter mutations (13.1% of R/M) are mutually exclusive with NOTCH1 mutations (q=3.3×10⁻⁴) and [MYB](../genes/MYB.md)/[MYBL1](../genes/MYBL1.md) fusions (q=5.6×10⁻³), defining four mutually exclusive molecular subgroups with distinct overall survival (P<0.0001) [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- NOTCH1-mutant R/M ACC has median [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) 55.1 vs. 204.5 months (P=1.10×10⁻⁴); activating mutations confer worse OS than non-activating (31.1 vs. 73.8 months, P=0.042) [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- 36.7% of MSKCC R/M cases carry ≥1 pathogenic germline variant; 22.2% involve DNA-repair pathways; 16.7% carry [MLH1](../genes/MLH1.md) or [MSH6](../genes/MSH6.md) germline variants but all cases were microsatellite-stable [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
- Intratumor heterogeneity analysis of one salivary ACC with 6 primary subregions + 8 lung metastases identified 36 unique mutations; only 7/36 were present in all regions; 4 mutations were absent from any primary subregion [PMID:31483290](../papers/31483290.md).
