# RYBP

## Overview

RYBP (RING1 and YY1 Binding Protein) encodes a component of the Polycomb repressive complex 1 (PRC1), involved in epigenetic transcriptional repression. Located at 3p14, RYBP is a candidate tumor suppressor in prostate cancer where deletion of this locus is recurrently observed.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Deletion at 3p14 identified as a candidate tumor suppressor in integrative genomic profiling of 218 prostate cancer tumors [PMID:20579941](../papers/20579941.md)
- Co-deleted with [FOXP1](../genes/FOXP1.md) and [SHQ1](../genes/SHQ1.md) in a focal 3p13 deletion identified by aCGH and low-pass WGS of FFPE prostatectomy needle biopsies ([prad_mskcc_2014](../datasets/prad_mskcc_2014.md) proof-of-principle). [PMID:25024180](../papers/25024180.md)
- Part of 3p21 focal heterozygous deletion (co-deleted with [SHQ1](../genes/SHQ1.md) and [FOXP1](../genes/FOXP1.md)) in MSK-PCa1 and MSK-PCa7 prostate cancer cell lines [PMID:25201530](../papers/25201530.md)
- Part of the complex 3p13 deletion locus involving FOXP1/RYBP/SHQ1 in primary prostate cancer [PMID:26544944](../papers/26544944.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Prostate adenocarcinoma ([PRAD](../cancer_types/PRAD.md)): recurrent deletion at 3p14, co-deleted with [FOXP1](../genes/FOXP1.md) and [SHQ1](../genes/SHQ1.md) [PMID:20579941](../papers/20579941.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-deleted with FOXP1 and SHQ1 at the 3p14 locus in prostate cancer [PMID:20579941](../papers/20579941.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting currently described; candidate tumor suppressor status warrants further investigation.

## Open questions

- Whether RYBP deletion is a driver or passenger event at the 3p14 locus has not been functionally validated.
