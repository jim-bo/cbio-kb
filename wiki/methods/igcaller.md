# IgCaller

## Overview

Computational tool for calling structural variants and rearrangements at immunoglobulin loci from short-read sequencing.

## Used by

- [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md) — used in the CLL-map SV analysis to identify Ig-locus rearrangements; in 177 WGS cases, [BCL2](../genes/BCL2.md) translocations were identified predominantly in M-CLL (5/88 WGS, 5.7%) via aberrant V(D)J. SV analysis across the full cohort was extrapolated from WES + IgCaller [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Notes

- The SV landscape of the full CLL cohort relied on only 177 WGS samples plus WES/IgCaller-based extrapolation, a noted limitation of the study [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
