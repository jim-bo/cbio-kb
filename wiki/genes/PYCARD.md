# PYCARD

## Overview

PYCARD (PYD And CARD Domain Containing, also known as ASC or TMS1) encodes the apoptosis-associated speck-like protein containing a CARD domain, a key adapter in inflammasome assembly linking PYD-domain proteins (e.g., NLRP3) to CARD-domain effectors (Caspase-1). In [AML](../cancer_types/AML.md), PYCARD was co-nominated with [PEAR1](../genes/PEAR1.md) in a prior TCGA-LAML immune prognostic signature study.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Co-nominated with [PEAR1](../genes/PEAR1.md) in a prior TCGA-LAML immune prognostic signature (Dao et al., 2021); [MAPK8](../genes/MAPK8.md) (JNK) is part of the PEAR1-downstream phagocytic signaling axis with [SYK](../genes/SYK.md) and SRC-family kinases — mechanistic background in AML drug-sensitivity study [PMID:35868306](../papers/35868306.md)
