# YEATS4

## Overview

YEATS4 (also known as GAS41) is a chromatin reader and transcriptional regulator that is recurrently co-amplified with [MDM2](../genes/MDM2.md) and [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) on chromosome 12q in dedifferentiated liposarcoma. Functional studies suggest it cooperates with MDM2 to repress p53 network activity, making it a candidate therapeutic target in [DDLS](../cancer_types/DDLS.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Co-amplified with [MDM2](../genes/MDM2.md) on chromosome 12q in ~90% of dedifferentiated liposarcomas ([DDLS](../cancer_types/DDLS.md)); YEATS4 was upregulated in amplified vs copy-neutral tumors and in tumors vs normal adipose tissue; YEATS4 knockdown phenocopied MDM2 knockdown in anti-proliferative effect in a 385-gene shRNA screen across three DDLS cell lines (LPS141, DDLS8817, FU-DDLS-1); proposed to cooperatively repress the p53 network alongside MDM2, providing rationale for MDM2 antagonists ([nutlin-3a](../drugs/nutlin-3a.md)) in DDLS ([sarc_mskcc](../datasets/sarc_mskcc.md), n=207) [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [DDLS](../cancer_types/DDLS.md) — co-amplified on 12q with MDM2; functional dependency confirmed by shRNA; upregulated in amplified vs copy-neutral tumors [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-amplified with [MDM2](../genes/MDM2.md) and [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) on chromosome 12q in DDLS [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- YEATS4 knockdown anti-proliferative in DDLS; co-amplification with MDM2 supports evaluation of p53–MDM2 interaction antagonists (nutlin-3a) as a therapeutic strategy [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).

## Open questions

- Mechanistic basis for YEATS4-mediated p53 repression (chromatin reading vs. direct transcriptional regulation) was not established in this study; further functional characterization needed.
