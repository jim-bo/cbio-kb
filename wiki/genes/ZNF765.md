# ZNF765

## Overview

ZNF765 (Zinc Finger Protein 765) is a KRAB-domain zinc finger protein. It was identified as significantly mutated in chromophobe renal cell carcinoma (chRCC) in a comprehensive non-clear cell RCC genomic study, though its functional role in cancer remains uncharacterized.
