# FABP4

## Overview

FABP4 (fatty acid binding protein 4, adipocyte type) is expressed in adipocytes and macrophages and can be upregulated in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md). It is part of the lipid-binding protein axis involved in fatty acid trafficking in the tumor microenvironment.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- FABP4 upregulated in HCC; part of the lipid-binding axis contributing to fatty acid redistribution and metabolic reprogramming in the tumor microenvironment [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) — FABP4 upregulation contributes to lipid metabolic reprogramming in HCC [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Part of the FABP1/FABP4/FABP5 upregulation axis in HCC [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- None individually highlighted in the corpus.

## Open questions

- FABP4's specific role relative to [FABP1](../genes/FABP1.md) and [FABP5](../genes/FABP5.md) in HCC immune evasion is not well-differentiated in the corpus.
