# MAP10

## Overview

MAP10 (Microtubule-Associated Protein 10) is a microtubule-binding protein. Its role in cancer biology is largely uncharacterized; it has been identified as a recurrently mutated gene in adenoid cystic carcinoma, where mutations are restricted to a specific molecular subclass.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Exclusive somatic mutation in the MYBL1-fusion group in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC); functional significance requires validation in larger cohorts [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Adenoid cystic carcinoma**: exclusive somatic mutation in MYBL1-fusion molecular subclass; significance unconfirmed [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Exclusive to the MYBL1-fusion subclass of ACC; not observed in MYB-NFIB fusion or fusion-negative groups [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)
