# HUWE1

## Overview

HUWE1 (HECT, UBA and WWE Domain Containing E3 Ubiquitin Protein Ligase 1) is a large HECT-domain E3 ubiquitin ligase involved in the ubiquitination and proteasomal degradation of key substrates including [MYC](../genes/MYC.md), [MCL1](../genes/MCL1.md), and p53. It plays complex roles in cell proliferation, apoptosis, and DNA damage response. In lung adenocarcinoma from lifelong never/light smokers (LCINS [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md)), HUWE1 is identified as a somatic driver or timing-relevant gene.
