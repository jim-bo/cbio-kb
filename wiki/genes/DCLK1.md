# DCLK1

## Overview

DCLK1 (Doublecortin-like kinase 1) encodes a serine/threonine kinase with roles in neuronal migration and stem cell maintenance. It has been flagged as a candidate driver in cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (cSCC) via MutSig analysis, though low allelic fractions raise uncertainty about its driver versus passenger status.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a MutSig hit (q<0.1) in whole-exome sequencing of 39 aggressive cSCC tumors; however, low allelic fractions suggest it may be a passenger rather than a true driver [PMID:25303977](../papers/25303977.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- **CSCC**: flagged by MutSig in aggressive, UV-driven cutaneous SCC; driver status uncertain due to low allelic fractions [PMID:25303977](../papers/25303977.md).

## Open questions
