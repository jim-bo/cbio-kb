# Malignant Rhabdoid Tumor of the Liver (MRTL)

## Overview

Malignant rhabdoid tumor of the liver (MRTL) is a rare pediatric malignancy, the liver-specific variant of extra-cranial malignant rhabdoid tumor. In the OncoTree hierarchy it is a child of LIVER and carries its own main type (Malignant Rhabdoid Tumor of the Liver), distinct from the kidney-primary code [MRT](../cancer_types/MRT.md). Like [MRT](../cancer_types/MRT.md), it is driven by biallelic inactivation of [SMARCB1](../genes/SMARCB1.md). Only 2 of 67 primary extra-cranial MRT cases in the largest published multi-omic reference cohort were liver-site tumors, limiting site-specific biology for MRTL.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [mrt_bcgsc_2016](../datasets/mrt_bcgsc_2016.md): 2 liver-site MRTs out of 67 primary extra-cranial cases banked by the Children's Oncology Group; both included in the multi-omic dataset alongside 58 kidney and 7 soft-tissue cases [PMID:26977886](../papers/26977886.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- [SMARCB1](../genes/SMARCB1.md) — biallelic inactivation is the near-universal driver of extra-cranial MRT including the liver variant; all 39/40 WGS-profiled cases (including the two liver cases) are SMARCB1-deficient [PMID:26977886](../papers/26977886.md)
- Somatic genome is otherwise quiet (median 0.231 mutations/Mb, median 5 non-synonymous substitutions/case) [PMID:26977886](../papers/26977886.md)
- Liver-site cases were enriched in mRNA sub-group 1 (AT/RT-like; all 6 extra-renal cases were in this sub-group; Fisher's exact P=0.04), though sample size is very small [PMID:26977886](../papers/26977886.md)
- PIPseq cohort: SMARCA4 R1192C identified in hepatic rhabdoid tumor as EZH2-inhibitor target; SMARCB1 homozygous deletion chr22q11.23 with biallelic loss of expression identified in a second hepatic rhabdoid tumor — also EZH2-inhibitor target [PMID:28007021](../papers/28007021.md)

## Subtypes

- See [MRT](../cancer_types/MRT.md) for multi-omic sub-group definitions (miRNA, mRNA, methylation); liver-site tumors fall predominantly in mRNA sub-group 1 (AT/RT-like) [PMID:26977886](../papers/26977886.md)

## Therapeutic landscape

- No prospective treatment data reported in the corpus; therapeutic inferences mirror those for [MRT](../cancer_types/MRT.md) [PMID:26977886](../papers/26977886.md)
