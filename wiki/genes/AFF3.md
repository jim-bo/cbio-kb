# AFF3

## Overview

AFF3 (AF4/FMR2 Family Member 3) encodes a transcriptional activator involved in RNA polymerase II elongation. It has been identified as a fusion partner in rare pediatric sarcomas via comprehensive whole-genome and transcriptome sequencing.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- EPC2–AFF3 fusion detected by cWGTS in an undifferentiated sarcoma patient; undescribed fusion not identified by panel sequencing alone [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)

## Cancer types (linked)
