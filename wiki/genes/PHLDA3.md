# PHLDA3

## Overview

PHLDA3 (Pleckstrin Homology Like Domain Family A Member 3) encodes a p53-regulated repressor of Akt signaling. It functions as a tumor suppressor by competing with Akt for PIP3 binding. In glioblastoma, PHLDA3 protein is upregulated in EGFR-altered tumors, suggesting a context-dependent role as a downstream RTK effector.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Protein upregulated in EGFR-altered glioblastoma tumors without concordant RNA change, identified in a multi-omic profiling study of [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **GBM**: Protein upregulation associated with [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md) alteration (amplification/mutation/SV) in glioblastoma multi-omic profiling [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-upregulated with [PHLDA1](../genes/PHLDA1.md) in EGFR-altered GBM tumors; both elevated at protein level without RNA change [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported in corpus; protein upregulation in EGFR-altered GBM may reflect downstream signaling responses [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Open questions

- Whether PHLDA3 upregulation in EGFR-altered GBM represents a compensatory tumor-suppressive response or a pro-oncogenic function in this context is unresolved.
