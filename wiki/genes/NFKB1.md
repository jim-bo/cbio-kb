# NFKB1

## Overview

NFKB1 encodes a subunit of the NF-kB transcription factor complex. In the cbio-kb corpus it is nominated as a novel U-CLL driver linked to NF-kB signaling and adverse outcomes.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- U-CLL-specific novel driver in the 1,148-patient CLL map; recurrent somatic mutations linked to NF-kB signaling and adverse FFS/OS [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Chronic lymphocytic leukemia ([CLLSLL](../cancer_types/CLLSLL.md)) — novel U-CLL driver; candidate addition to CLL risk models [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Enriched in IGHV-unmutated (U-CLL) subtype relative to M-CLL [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic link reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Functional validation as a CLL driver is deferred to future work [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
