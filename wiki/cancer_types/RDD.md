# Rosai-Dorfman Disease (RDD)

## Overview

Rosai-Dorfman Disease (RDD) is a rare histiocytic disorder characterized by massive painless cervical lymphadenopathy, fever, and elevated erythrocyte sedimentation rate. It sits within the histiocytic and dendritic cell neoplasm (HDCN) lineage in OncoTree, alongside [ECD](./ECD.md) and [LCH](./LCH.md). Like other histiocytic neoplasms, RDD is driven by somatic MAPK-pathway alterations and is clinically distinct from lymphoma, though lymph node involvement may mimic it. Extranodal RDD occurs in skin, soft tissue, CNS, and bone.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [histiocytosis_cobi_msk_2019](../datasets/histiocytosis_cobi_msk_2019.md): 18-patient single-arm phase 2 trial (NCT02649972) of [cobimetinib](../drugs/cobimetinib.md) in histiocytic neoplasms; 2/18 patients (11%) had RDD [PMID:30867592](../papers/30867592.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- MAPK-pathway mutations detected in 83% (15/18) of all histiocytic neoplasm patients in the cobimetinib trial; RDD patients included, with responses observed in both RDD patients (1 SD among 1 evaluable; the other was a responder); altered genes across the cohort included [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) V600E/N486_T491delinsK, [MAP2K1](../genes/MAP2K1.md) (MEK1), [MAP2K2](../genes/MAP2K2.md) (MEK2), [ARAF](../genes/ARAF.md), [RAF1](../genes/RAF1.md), [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md), and [NRAS](../genes/NRAS.md) [PMID:30867592](../papers/30867592.md).
- Two of 18 patients enrolled in the cobimetinib trial (NCT02649972) had RDD; responses occurred in the broader histiocytic cohort across all MAPK genotypes, including in RDD [PMID:30867592](../papers/30867592.md).

## Subtypes

- RDD, [ECD](./ECD.md), [LCH](./LCH.md), and mixed histiocytosis share MAPK-pathway dependence as a unifying molecular feature, supporting a common targeted-therapy approach despite distinct histologic and clinical presentations [PMID:30867592](../papers/30867592.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- Cobimetinib (MEK1/2 inhibitor), 60 mg daily 21-of-28 days, produced an 89% PET ORR (90% CI 73–100) across the 18-patient histiocytic neoplasm cohort (2 RDD), with 100% of responses ongoing at 1 year and no acquired resistance observed; responses occurred regardless of MAPK genotype [PMID:30867592](../papers/30867592.md).
- Prior standard [vemurafenib](../drugs/vemurafenib.md) is approved only for [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) V600-mutant Erdheim-Chester disease; cobimetinib extends targeted therapy options to the ~50% of histiocytosis patients without BRAF V600, including RDD [PMID:30867592](../papers/30867592.md).
