# GATA6

## Overview

GATA6 is a zinc-finger transcription factor of the GATA family involved in gastrointestinal development and differentiation. In cancer, GATA6 is recurrently focally amplified in chromosomally unstable (CIN) gastric adenocarcinoma, where it may function as a lineage-specific driver.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Focal amplification in CIN gastric adenocarcinoma (STAD) tumours (TCGA cohort) [PMID:25079317](../papers/25079317.md)
