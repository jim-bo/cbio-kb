# ACBD6

## Overview

ACBD6 (Acyl-CoA Binding Domain Containing 6) encodes a member of the acyl-CoA binding protein family. It has been identified as a fusion partner in rare pediatric sarcomas discovered through comprehensive whole-genome and transcriptome sequencing.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- MAN1A2–ACBD6 fusion detected by cWGTS in an undifferentiated sarcoma patient; undescribed fusion not identified by panel sequencing alone [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)
