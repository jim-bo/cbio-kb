# Pediatric Preclinical Testing Consortium PDX (PPTC, 2019)

## Overview

A comprehensive genomic characterization of 261 patient-derived xenograft (PDX) models spanning 37 unique pediatric cancer molecular subtypes (29 unique histologies), generated by the Pediatric Preclinical Testing Consortium (PPTC) — the successor to the Pediatric Preclinical Testing Program (PPTP). Data are deposited as cBioPortal study `pptc_2019` and are queryable via PedcBioPortal as a public resource for pediatric oncology drug development. The dataset demonstrates that PDX models faithfully recapitulate the mutational, copy-number, and fusion landscapes of their histologies of origin, including relapsed disease [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).

## Composition

- **261 PDX models** from **37 unique pediatric cancer molecular subtypes** (29 unique histologies); 26 full models and 3 RNA samples excluded during QC [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- Major histology groups include:
  - B-cell precursor ALL ([BLL](../cancer_types/BLL.md)), Ph-like / Ph+ ALL, MLL-rearranged ALL
  - T-cell / ETP-ALL ([TLL](../cancer_types/TLL.md))
  - Neuroblastoma ([NBL](../cancer_types/NBL.md))
  - Osteosarcoma ([OS](../cancer_types/OS.md))
  - Ewing sarcoma ([ES](../cancer_types/ES.md))
  - Wilms tumor ([WT](../cancer_types/WT.md), n=13)
  - Rhabdomyosarcoma ([RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md)) — [ERMS](../cancer_types/ERMS.md) (Fusion-) and [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) (Fusion+)
  - Medulloblastoma ([MBL](../cancer_types/MBL.md), n=20) across SHH/WNT/Group 3/Group 4 subtypes
  - [ATRT](../cancer_types/ATRT.md) and extracranial rhabdoid ([MRT](../cancer_types/MRT.md))
  - Ependymoma ([EPMT](../cancer_types/EPMT.md))
  - High-grade glioma ([HGGNOS](../cancer_types/HGGNOS.md)) and [DIFG](../cancer_types/DIFG.md)/[DMG](../cancer_types/DMG.md)
  - Alveolar soft part sarcoma ([ASPS](../cancer_types/ASPS.md)) and rare histologies
- **Predicted ancestry:** 71% European, 11.5% South Asian/Hispanic, 9.1% African, 5.5% mixed/unknown, 2.4% East Asian [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- **Phase of disease at collection:** 27% from relapsed disease, 6% from post-neoadjuvant resections [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- **Note on TMB:** No paired normal samples; rare germline variants were retained, so reported TMB values are likely inflated relative to matched-normal cohorts [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).

## Assays / panels (linked)

- [Whole-exome sequencing](../methods/whole-exome-seq.md) — HGSC VCRome 2.1 capture; 240 PDX models [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- [RNA-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) — strand-specific poly-A+; 244 PDX models [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- Illumina HumanOmniExpress-24 [SNP arrays](../methods/affymetrix-snp6.md) — copy-number segmentation and ethnicity inference; 252 PDX models [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- [STR profiling](../methods/str-profiling.md) — identity verification; all 261 models [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- **Fusion calling** combined [deFuse](../methods/defuse.md), [FusionCatcher](../methods/fusioncatcher.md), [STAR-Fusion](../methods/star-fusion.md), and [SOAPfuse](../methods/soapfuse.md); yielded 925 high-confidence and 92 known oncogenic driver fusions [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- Hybrid hg19-mm10 competitive mapping for mouse-read subtraction [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md) — Rokita et al. Comprehensive genomic characterization of 261 PPTC PDXs across 37 pediatric cancer subtypes; demonstrates faithful recapitulation of driver landscapes and utility for preclinical basket-trial design.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- PDX models faithfully recapitulate driver mutation, copy-number, and fusion landscapes across pediatric histologies; 92 known oncogenic driver fusions identified among 925 high-confidence fusions [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- [MYCN](../genes/MYCN.md) amplification is the most frequent alteration across all models (66% of neuroblastoma PDXs), largely mutually exclusive with 11q deletion [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- Relapse-derived PDX models have significantly higher tumor mutation burden than diagnosis-derived models (3.08 vs. 2.57 Mut/Mb; Wilcoxon p=2.2×10⁻⁵), with significant effects in neuroblastoma (p=0.016) and T-ALL (p=0.0081) [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- 97% of osteosarcoma models show high chromosomal breakpoint density consistent with chromothripsis; [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) is altered in 82% of osteosarcoma models [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- Pre-trained [TP53 inactivation classifier](../methods/tp53-inactivation-classifier.md) achieves AUROC=0.89 and [NF1 inactivation classifier](../methods/nf1-inactivation-classifier.md) achieves AUROC=0.77 in this pediatric PDX cohort, supporting pathway-activity scores as candidate biomarkers for response to pathway-targeted agents [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
- 25% of models (64/252) show high breakpoint density on ≥1 chromosome; neuroblastoma chromothripsis concentrated on chromosomes 2, 5, 16, 17, 19 — linking to [MYCN](../genes/MYCN.md) amplification, [TERT](../genes/TERT.md) rearrangement, and 17q gain [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md).
