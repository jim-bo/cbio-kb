# ZC3H18

## Overview

ZC3H18 is a novel M-CLL driver nominated in the CLL map cohort and associated with inferior failure-free survival.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ZC3H18 was identified as a novel M-CLL driver associated with short failure-free survival in the integrated CLL map cohort [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- ZC3H18 is a candidate novel prognostic marker (alongside 5q32 loss and 15q25.2 loss) for addition to CLL risk models [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
