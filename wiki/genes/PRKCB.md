# PRKCB

## Overview

PRKCB (Protein Kinase C Beta) encodes a serine/threonine kinase involved in signal transduction downstream of antigen receptors and growth factors. In cancer genomics, it is a direct transcriptional target of the EWS::FLI1 fusion oncogene in Ewing sarcoma and has been validated as a Ewing sarcoma dependency.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Strongly induced novel direct EWS::FLI1 target in heMSCs (EWS::FLI1 binds intron 7); knockdown via EWS::FLI1 siRNA abolishes induction; reaffirmed as a Ewing sarcoma dependency consistent with Surdez et al. [PMID:25186949](../papers/25186949.md)

## Cancer types (linked)
