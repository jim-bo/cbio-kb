# Agilent 244K

## Overview

A microarray platform (Agilent 244,000-feature) used for high-resolution analysis of gene expression or comparative genomic hybridization.

## Used by

- Utilized for gene expression profiling in 206 glioblastoma samples to characterize core pathway alterations and molecular subtypes [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).

## Notes

- Contributed to the definition of the RTK/RAS/PI3K, p53, and RB signaling pathways as being nearly universally altered in GBM [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).
