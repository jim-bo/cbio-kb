# ANK1

## Overview

ANK1 (Ankyrin-1) encodes a member of the ankyrin family of proteins that link the spectrin-based cytoskeleton to the plasma membrane. In cancer genomics, ANK1 has been identified as a target of methylation-driven cis-regulatory effects in lung adenocarcinoma, with coordinated changes across RNA, protein, and phosphoprotein layers.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a methylation cis-effect target in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) with coordinated RNA, protein, and phosphoprotein regulation in a proteogenomic study of 110 treatment-naïve tumors (85/120 methylation-driven cis-effects supported by promoter-region probes) [PMID:32649874](../papers/32649874.md)
