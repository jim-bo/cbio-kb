# Illumina EPIC methylation array

## Overview

The Illumina Infinium MethylationEPIC BeadChip (EPIC/850K array) measures DNA methylation at ~850,000 CpG sites genome-wide, covering enhancers, promoters, and gene bodies. It is the successor to the Illumina 450k array [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md).

## Used by

- [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md) — Illumina EPIC methylation array used to profile 121 fresh-frozen meningiomas from the University Health Network Brain Tumor BioBank; six DNA methylation clusters were derived and integrated via COCA with CNA and mRNA clusters to define four stable molecular groups (MG1–MG4) for meningioma classification [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md).

## Notes

- EPIC array data was one of three molecular layers integrated by COCA alongside WES and mRNA-seq in the meningioma classification study [PMID:34433969](../papers/34433969.md).
