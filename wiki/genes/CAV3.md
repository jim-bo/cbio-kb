# CAV3

## Overview

CAV3 (Caveolin-3) is a member of the caveolin gene family, encoding a muscle-specific scaffolding protein of caveolae. It is primarily associated with muscular dystrophies and cardiomyopathies in the germline context. In cancer genomics, CAV3 has been identified as a rare somatic variant in schwannomatosis tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CAV3 harbors a novel deleterious SNV validated by Sanger sequencing in a single SWNTS-SWN schwannomatosis case; non-recurrent in the 29 WES + 22 WGS sample series [PMID:33025139](../papers/33025139.md)
