# CEACAM1

## Overview

CEACAM1 (Carcinoembryonic Antigen Related Cell Adhesion Molecule 1) is a cell adhesion molecule with roles in immune regulation and tumor biology. It has been identified as a prognostic marker of the bile-acid-active subtype in cholangiocarcinoma.
