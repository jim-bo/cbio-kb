# Translocation-Associated Renal Cell Carcinoma (TRCC)

## Overview

Translocation-Associated Renal Cell Carcinoma (TRCC) is defined by chromosomal translocations involving MiTF/TFE family members (TFE3, TFEB, MITF), producing gene fusions that drive oncogenesis. It sits under NCCRCC in OncoTree (tissue: Kidney) and may be diagnostically challenging to distinguish from other nccRCC subtypes.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [nccrcc_genentech_2014](../datasets/nccrcc_genentech_2014.md) — 6 translocation RCC tumors profiled by whole-exome sequencing, RNA-seq, and SNP copy-number arrays; two additional unclassified cases were reclassified as tRCC after genomic analysis [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- In 6 tRCCs, known ASPSCR1-TFE3 and PRCC-TFE3 fusions were confirmed by FISH; a novel CLTC-TFEB in-frame fusion was identified in one unclassified case reclassified as tRCC; a novel MIDN-SBNO2 fusion (chr19p13.3 inversion) was found in a TFE3-fusion-negative case, driving elevated SBNO2 expression [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md).
- Two tRCC cases showed elevated TFE3 expression without detectable TFE3 fusion or amplification, indicating additional mechanisms of TFE3 activation beyond canonical translocation [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md).

## Subtypes

- MiTF-high subtype: tumors with MiTF/TFE3/TFEB fusions or amplifications consistently overexpress BIRC7, an anti-apoptotic MITF target gene proposed as a diagnostic and therapeutic marker [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- BIRC7 overexpression in MiTF-fusion tumors is proposed as a therapeutic vulnerability [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md).
- TFEB amplification (beyond translocation) expands the set of tRCC cases for whom TFEB-targeted strategies may apply [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md).

## Sources

- [PMID:25401301](../papers/25401301.md) — Durinck et al., integrated genomic profiling of 167 non-clear cell RCCs including 6 tRCCs.
