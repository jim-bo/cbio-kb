# SOX9

## Overview

SOX9 (SRY-Box Transcription Factor 9) is a master transcription factor in chondrogenesis, sex determination, and neural crest development. In glioma, SOX9 marks the astrocytic differentiation lineage and is a key component of the astro-like expression program in oligodendroglioma, defining one of the two major differentiated compartments.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SOX9 is a defining marker of the astrocytic (astro-like) program in oligodendroglioma (one of 128 astro-high signature genes), co-expressed with [APOE](../genes/APOE.md), [ALDOC](../genes/ALDOC.md), and [GFAP](../genes/GFAP.md), across 4,347 single cells from 6 untreated grade II IDH-mutant 1p/19q-codeleted oligodendrogliomas (Smart-Seq2). [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [ODG](../cancer_types/ODG.md) — SOX9 marks the differentiated astro-like tumor cells in oligodendroglioma; these cells are predominantly non-cycling and represent one of two glial differentiation endpoints alongside the oligo-like lineage. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [APOE](../genes/APOE.md), [ALDOC](../genes/ALDOC.md), and [GFAP](../genes/GFAP.md) in astro-like differentiated cells; mutually exclusive with the stem/progenitor program ([SOX2](../genes/SOX2.md), [SOX4](../genes/SOX4.md), [SOX11](../genes/SOX11.md)) and with the oligodendrocytic program ([OLIG1](../genes/OLIG1.md), [OLIG2](../genes/OLIG2.md), [OMG](../genes/OMG.md)). [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- SOX9+ astro-like cells are largely non-cycling and are unlikely to drive tumor regrowth; therapies targeting the differentiated compartment may be of limited value. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Open questions

- Whether SOX9 plays a functional role in astro-like differentiation of oligodendroglioma cells or simply marks that state is not addressed. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)
