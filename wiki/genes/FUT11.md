# FUT11

## Overview

FUT11 (Fucosyltransferase 11) is a member of the fucosyltransferase enzyme family involved in glycan biosynthesis. In pancreatic cancer, FUT11 was identified as part of a cluster of glycosylation enzymes elevated in PDAC tumors relative to normal adjacent tissue, with protein-level upregulation validated by DIA mass spectrometry. Note: FUT11 could not be confirmed in the cBioPortal HUGO gene symbol ontology; treat as corpus-derived until verified.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Elevated in PDAC tumors vs normal adjacent tissue (NAT) at the protein level; upregulation validated by DIA mass spectrometry as part of a glycosylation-enzyme upregulation cluster in PDAC [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)**: Protein-level upregulation detected in the CPTAC PDAC proteogenomic cohort (n=140 tumors) [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

## Therapeutic relevance
