# EIF2AK2

## Overview

EIF2AK2 (Eukaryotic Translation Initiation Factor 2 Alpha Kinase 2), also known as PKR (Protein Kinase R), is a double-stranded RNA-activated serine/threonine kinase that phosphorylates EIF2α and plays a central role in innate antiviral immunity, integrated stress response, and apoptosis. In cancer, EIF2AK2/PKR has been linked to viral hepatitis signaling pathways (HCV, HBV) and to TP53-mutant tumor biology.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- PKR (EIF2AK2) S83 activating autophosphorylation is elevated in TP53-mutant HCCs; EIF2AK2 kinase activity is increased by KSEA in TP53-mutant and overall HCC vs normal liver in a European multi-omic cohort (n=122 WES, n=51 phosphoproteome); involved in stress response and HCV/HBV signaling [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)
- Listed among kinases with increased activity in TP53-mutant HCCs alongside CDK1, CDK2, CDK5, AURKA, TTK, NEK2, PRKD2, and MAPK13 by KSEA [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **HCC**: EIF2AK2/PKR shows elevated phosphorylation/activity in TP53-mutant HCCs; may reflect viral (HBV/HCV) co-exposure and stress signaling in hepatocarcinogenesis [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- EIF2AK2 kinase activation is enriched in TP53-mutant (vs CTNNB1-mutant) HCCs; CTNNB1- and TP53-mutation subgroups are mutually exclusive (p=0.012) in the same cohort [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic data in the corpus; elevated PKR activity in TP53-mutant HCC could represent an avenue for stress-kinase-directed intervention but requires further validation [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Open questions

- Whether elevated EIF2AK2/PKR S83 phosphorylation in TP53-mutant HCC is a consequence of TP53 loss of function, a compensatory stress response, or is driven by viral co-infection biology remains to be determined [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)
