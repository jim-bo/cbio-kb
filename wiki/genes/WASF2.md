# WASF2

## Overview

WASF2 (WAS protein family member 2, also known as WAVE2) encodes an actin nucleation-promoting factor that regulates the ARP2/3 complex. In the pan-cancer TCGA fusion landscape, WASF2 was identified as the 5' partner in FGR-WASF2 fusions, where the WASF2 5' UTR promoter drives [FGR](../genes/FGR.md) kinase overexpression in the majority of fusion-positive samples.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as the 5' UTR-donor partner in FGR-WASF2 fusions across seven pan-cancer samples; the WASF2 5' UTR promoter drives FGR overexpression in five of seven fusion-positive samples [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md)
