# tanespimycin

## Overview

Tanespimycin (17-AAG) is a geldanamycin-derived HSP90 inhibitor. [NQO1](../genes/NQO1.md) bioactivates 17-AAG to the more potent form 17-DMAG ([alvespimycin](../drugs/alvespimycin.md)). GI50 for tanespimycin is inversely correlated with [NQO1](../genes/NQO1.md) expression in hepatocellular carcinoma cell lines. Tumors with [KEAP1](../genes/KEAP1.md) or [NFE2L2](../genes/NFE2L2.md) mutations have elevated [NQO1](../genes/NQO1.md) and may be selectively sensitive, except in carriers of the [NQO1](../genes/NQO1.md) P187S ([NQO1](../genes/NQO1.md)*2) loss-of-function polymorphism.

## Evidence in the corpus

- HSP90 inhibitors tanespimycin (17-AAG) and [alvespimycin](../drugs/alvespimycin.md) (17-DMAG) nominated as candidate strategies for KEAP1/NFE2L2-mutated [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md): GI50 for 17-AAG/17-DMAG was inversely correlated with NQO1 expression (Pearson r = −0.56, P = 0.0015) across 29 liver cancer cell lines; two of three KEAP1-mutant lines were highly sensitive; the third (MHCC97H) was resistant due to homozygous NQO1 P187S (rs1800566) variant that destabilizes NQO1 protein [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)

## Resistance mechanisms

- NQO1 P187S (rs1800566) polymorphism destabilizes NQO1 protein and confers resistance to tanespimycin/alvespimycin in KEAP1-mutated [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) cell line MHCC97H [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)
