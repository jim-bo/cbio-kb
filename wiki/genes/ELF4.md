# ELF4

## Overview

ELF4 (E74 Like ETS Transcription Factor 4) is an ETS family transcription factor involved in immune cell development and DNA damage response signaling. It has been identified as a novel driver gene in chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL), where it is classified among DNA damage response pathway drivers.

## Alterations observed in the corpus
