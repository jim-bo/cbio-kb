# Lung (LUNG)

## Overview

LUNG is the OncoTree top-level tissue node for all lung cancers. It encompasses non-small cell lung carcinoma ([NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md)), small cell lung cancer ([SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md)), and other rare lung malignancies. Subtypes are organized under more specific OncoTree codes (e.g., [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md), [LUSC](../cancer_types/LUSC.md), NSCLC, SCLC).

## Cohorts in the corpus

- No primary cohort studies at the LUNG level in the current corpus; subtype-specific studies (LUAD, LUSC, NSCLC) are linked from those pages.

## Recurrent alterations

- LUNG was cited as an illustrative cancer type in a review of 3D tumor model platforms (scaffold-free spheroids, scaffold-based hydrogels, bioprinting, and microfluidic systems). [PMID:30643250](../papers/30643250.md)
- Lung cancer anatomical site represented as a treatment-planning target in a systematic review of 111 deep learning image-synthesis studies; models applied for MR-to-CT synthesis, low-dose CT restoration, and CBCT-to-CT adaptive radiotherapy — no genomic data [PMID:33305538](../papers/33305538.md)
