# ALB

## Overview

ALB encodes serum albumin, the major plasma protein produced by the liver. Somatic mutations in ALB are recurrently observed in hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC), likely reflecting clonal hepatocyte evolution in the context of chronic liver disease rather than a direct oncogenic driver role.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Somatic mutation reported in the HCC molecular landscape (WES, n=1,289 patients); classified as a non-actionable alteration in the comprehensive review of HCC molecular therapies [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md)
