# Soft Tissue Sarcoma (STS)

> **Note:** STS is a corpus-level umbrella tag used in Wu et al. (2022) to aggregate sarcoma cases. It is not a canonical OncoTree code. The nearest OncoTree parent node is `SOFT_TISSUE`. Specific histologic subtypes ([LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md), [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md), [SYNS](../cancer_types/SYNS.md), [LIPO](../cancer_types/LIPO.md), etc.) have dedicated OncoTree codes.

## Overview

Soft tissue sarcomas (STS) are a heterogeneous group of malignant tumors arising from mesenchymal tissue. In the pan-cancer OrigiMed cohort (Wu et al., 2022), STS was used as a grouping label covering bone and soft-tissue sarcomas collectively, including osteosarcoma, Ewing sarcoma, liposarcoma, leiomyosarcoma, synovial sarcoma, and undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma, among others.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [pan_origimed_2020](../datasets/pan_origimed_2020.md) — STS comprised ~6% of the 10,194-patient Asian pan-cancer cohort [PMID:35871175](../papers/35871175.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md) amplification in 10.9% of STS cases in the pan-cancer OrigiMed cohort (abemaciclib/palbociclib Level 4 per OncoKB); [EWSR1](../genes/EWSR1.md) fusions restricted to sarcomas; younger bone sarcoma enriched for [VEGFA](../genes/VEGFA.md); [VEGFA](../genes/VEGFA.md) amplification age-associated in bone sarcoma [PMID:35871175](../papers/35871175.md).

## Subtypes

- Histologic subtypes aggregated under this umbrella in the OrigiMed cohort include bone and soft-tissue sarcomas (osteosarcoma, Ewing sarcoma, liposarcoma, leiomyosarcoma, synovial sarcoma, undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma) [PMID:35871175](../papers/35871175.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- CDK4/6 inhibitors ([abemaciclib](../drugs/abemaciclib.md), [palbociclib](../drugs/palbociclib.md)) nominated for CDK4-amplified STS (OncoKB Level 4) [PMID:35871175](../papers/35871175.md).
