# PDSS2

## Overview

PDSS2 (Prenyl Diphosphate Synthase Subunit 2) encodes a component of the coenzyme Q biosynthesis pathway. In uveal melanoma, PDSS2 has been identified as a fusion partner in a structural variant detected by whole-genome sequencing.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- FAM135B-PDSS2 in-frame interchromosomal fusion detected in uveal melanoma sample 550 (chromosomes 6 and 8) by WGS [PMID:26683228](../papers/26683228.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Uveal melanoma**: interchromosomal fusion with FAM135B; single case, functional significance unknown [PMID:26683228](../papers/26683228.md)
