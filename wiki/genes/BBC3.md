# BBC3

## Overview

BBC3 (also known as PUMA, p53 upregulated modulator of apoptosis) encodes a pro-apoptotic BH3-only protein in the BCL-2 family. It is a key mediator of p53-dependent and p53-independent apoptosis. In EBV-associated cancers, viral microRNAs inhibit BBC3/PUMA to evade apoptosis.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Pro-apoptotic PUMA protein is inhibited by EBV BART-cluster microRNAs in NPC, contributing to apoptosis resistance [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **NPC**: BBC3/PUMA suppressed by EBV viral microRNAs; contributes to apoptosis evasion in EBV-positive NPC [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md)
