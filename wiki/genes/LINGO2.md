# LINGO2

## Overview

LINGO2 (Leucine Rich Repeat And Ig Domain Containing 2) is a candidate coding driver gene identified in WGS-based analysis of metastatic cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (mCSCC). It was among a set of genes called by at least 2 out of 3 driver-calling tools (OncodriveFML, dNdScv, MutSig2CV) in a 25-sample mCSCC cohort. LINGO2 is known primarily for roles in neural development; its mechanistic contribution to [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) pathogenesis is not established.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Candidate coding driver called by ≥2 driver-calling tools (OncodriveFML, dNdScv, MutSig2CV) in metastatic cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (mCSCC) WGS analysis (n=25); LINGO2 is part of a group including [KHDRBS2](../genes/KHDRBS2.md), [COLEC12](../genes/COLEC12.md), [SLC22A6](../genes/SLC22A6.md), [CDHR5](../genes/CDHR5.md), [ZNF442](../genes/ZNF442.md), [PRLR](../genes/PRLR.md), and [DHRS4](../genes/DHRS4.md) nominated by this criterion [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)
