# PVT1

## Overview

PVT1 is a long non-coding RNA gene at chromosome 8q24 frequently involved in structural rearrangements in cancer. In medulloblastoma, recurrent rearrangements targeting PVT1 are identified as candidate driver events.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Recurrent structural rearrangements targeting PVT1 identified in a 491-sample WGS/WES pan-medulloblastoma cohort alongside rearrangements targeting [PTEN](../genes/PTEN.md) and [GLI2](../genes/GLI2.md) [PMID:28726821](../papers/28726821.md)
