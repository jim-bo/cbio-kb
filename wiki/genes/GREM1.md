# GREM1

## Overview

GREM1 (Gremlin-1) is a secreted glycoprotein that functions as a BMP (bone morphogenetic protein) antagonist. In cancer, GREM1 has been identified as a candidate tumor biomarker and poor-prognosis marker, with high protein expression in tumor versus normal adjacent tissue across several cancer types including lung adenocarcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Protein expression dramatically upregulated in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) tumors vs. paired normal adjacent tissues (log2 FC >5) in the CPTAC proteogenomics cohort (n=110 treatment-naïve tumors); highlighted as a candidate poor-prognosis marker alongside OCIAD2 (log2 FC >4); among 289 proteins stringently upregulated (log2 FC >2, FDR <0.01, differential in ≥90% of pairs) [PMID:32649874](../papers/32649874.md)
