# TCHH

## Overview

TCHH (Trichohyalin) encodes a structural protein of the inner root sheath of hair follicles. In uveal melanoma, TCHH carries recurrent missense mutations, though without current functional support for a driver role; it is flagged as a candidate recurrent mutation in the context of a deep-sequencing study.
