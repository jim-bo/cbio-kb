# VEGFA

## Overview

VEGFA (Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor A) is the primary driver of angiogenesis and is a key therapeutic target in cancer. In hepatocellular carcinoma, focal gain at 6p21 (the VEGFA locus) has been reported as a predictor of sorafenib benefit.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- VEGFA shows 6p21 focal gain in HCC and has been reported as a predictor of sorafenib benefit [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)
