# NEGR1

## Overview

NEGR1 (Neuronal Growth Regulator 1) encodes an immunoglobulin superfamily cell-adhesion molecule expressed predominantly in the nervous system. In cancer genomics, NEGR1 has been flagged in focal-deletion and pathway analyses in clear cell renal cell carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Highlighted as a downstream pathway and SCNA feature in TieDIE / focal-deletion analyses of ccRCC (TCGA 450-case multi-platform dataset) [PMID:23792563](../papers/23792563.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Clear cell renal cell carcinoma (ccRCC): focal deletion identified in TCGA analysis [PMID:23792563](../papers/23792563.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-listed with [CTNNB1](../genes/CTNNB1.md), [TGFBR2](../genes/TGFBR2.md), [QKI](../genes/QKI.md), [CADM2](../genes/CADM2.md), [PTPRD](../genes/PTPRD.md), and [NRXN3](../genes/NRXN3.md) as focal-deletion features in ccRCC pathway analysis [PMID:23792563](../papers/23792563.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic nominations in the corpus.

## Open questions

- The precise mechanism by which NEGR1 deletion contributes to ccRCC biology remains undefined.
