# MCL1

## Overview

MCL1 is an anti-apoptotic BCL-2 family protein that promotes cell survival by sequestering pro-apoptotic BH3-only proteins. Upregulation of MCL1 is a mechanism of chemoresistance observed across cancer types. In the [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) precursor lesion context, MCL1 upregulation is part of the interferon-related DNA damage resistance signature (IRDS).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Anti-apoptotic BCL-2 family member upregulated as part of the interferon-related DNA damage resistance signature (IRDS) in serous tubal intraepithelial carcinoma stage C (STIC.C) and cancer epithelial cells in high-grade serous ovarian carcinoma ([HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md)) precursors; upregulation appears at the STIC.C stage [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).
- MCL1 identified as a significantly mutated gene in pan-lung cancer TCGA analysis (n=1144) with somatic alterations observed across lung adenocarcinoma and squamous cell carcinoma subtypes [PMID:27158780](../papers/27158780.md)
- Alteration identified as part of the actionable alteration list in cisplatin-resistant germ cell tumors ([GCT](../cancer_types/GCT.md)) [PMID:27646943](../papers/27646943.md)
- MCL1 overexpression detected in osteosarcoma ([OS](../cancer_types/OS.md)) in a pediatric precision-oncology cohort; identified alongside [MYC](../genes/MYC.md) and [CCNE1](../genes/CCNE1.md) as BET- and CDK4/6-inhibitor targets in the same patient [PMID:28007021](../papers/28007021.md)
- 18q gain (including BCL2/MALT1/MCL1 locus at 1q23.3) is a defining feature of C2 DLBCL subtype; MCL1 (1q23.3) gain detected in the C2 genomic-instability cluster of a 304-sample DLBCL WES study [PMID:29713087](../papers/29713087.md)
- MCL1 amplification observed in 4% of cholangiocarcinoma patients (n=195) profiled by MSK-IMPACT targeted NGS [PMID:29848569](../papers/29848569.md)
- MCL1 amplification enriched in CDK12-biallelic (CDK12-Bi) prostate cancer relative to CDK12-WT (FDR < 0.05), part of a co-alteration pattern in CDK12-Bi tumors that also includes [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) and [MYC](../genes/MYC.md) amplifications; MCL1 amplification co-occurs with a tandem duplicator phenotype and shorter overall survival [PMID:32317181](../papers/32317181.md)
- Structural variant gain (MCL1 GAIN) detected by LINX/PURPLE in metastatic cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (mCSCC) WGS analysis (n=25) [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- High-grade serous ovarian carcinoma ([HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md)) precursors (STIC) — MCL1 upregulation as part of the IRDS in STIC.C and cancer; may contribute to chemoresistance observed in >80% of stage III/IV [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-upregulated with [MX1](../genes/MX1.md) and other IFN-stimulated genes as part of the IRDS signature in STIC.C and cancer epithelial cells [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- IRDS-associated MCL1 upregulation may contribute to the chemoresistance (platinum/taxane) observed in advanced HGSOC; MCL1 inhibitors are in clinical development [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Open questions

- Whether MCL1 upregulation in STIC.C is causally linked to clinical chemoresistance or is correlative requires prospective validation.
- Direct MCL1 inhibitor strategies in HGSOC have not been evaluated in this corpus.
