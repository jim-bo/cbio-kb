# EP300

## Overview

EP300 (E1A binding protein p300) is a transcriptional coactivator and histone acetyltransferase (HAT) that is the paralog of CREBBP/CBP. EP300 acetylates histones H3 and H4, as well as non-histone proteins including p53, and serves as a scaffold for transcription complexes. EP300 loss-of-function mutations occur in multiple cancer types. In glioblastoma, EP300 protein and acetylation levels are key drivers of H2B N-terminal hyperacetylation that marks a classical-like immune-low [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) subtype.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- EP300 protein and acetylation levels are strongly associated with H2B-K12/K13/K16/K17/K21 acetylation in [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) ([gbm_cptac_2021](../datasets/gbm_cptac_2021.md), n=99) by Lasso regression; together with [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md), EP300 activity defines the classical-like, immune-low H2B-hyperacetylated [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) subset [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).
- Mutated in 21.7% of [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) tumors; loss-of-function mutations including missense mutations clustered in the histone acetyltransferase (HAT) domain; some functionally validated to abolish HAT activity; nominated as a novel [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) driver [PMID:34272401](../papers/34272401.md)
- EP300 ranks among the high positive Δκ<sub>risk</sub> genes (putative network drivers of ICI-modulated robustness) in a 45-patient [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) ICI cohort analyzed by geometric network curvature on MSK-IMPACT copy-number data. [PMID:34819508](../papers/34819508.md)
- Paralog [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) altered among calcium-signaling enriched metastatic-clonal genes in prostate cancer brain metastases (PCBM); EP300 noted in the context of transcriptional coactivator alterations [PMID:35504881](../papers/35504881.md)
- Germline frameshift variant p.A2259fs*20 identified in an osteosarcoma patient by comprehensive WGS+transcriptome sequencing (cWGTS) in a pediatric/rare cancer cohort (n=29 osteosarcoma) [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Glioblastoma ([GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md)):** EP300 protein and acetylation activity co-drives H2B N-terminal hyperacetylation with [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) in the classical-like (nmf3) immune-low subtype; BRD1/BRD3/BRD4 correlate with additional H2B acetylation sites in this subset [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- EP300 activity co-occurs with [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) activity and BRD1/BRD3/BRD4 in the H2B-hyperacetylated, classical-like GBM subset [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- EP300 and CREBBP inhibitors are nominated as therapeutic candidates for the classical-like, H2B-hyperacetylated GBM subset; hypothesis-generating from proteogenomic data only, not clinically tested in this cohort [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).

## Open questions

- Whether targeting EP300/CREBBP HAT activity is clinically feasible and specifically effective in H2B-hyperacetylated GBM requires preclinical and clinical validation [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).
