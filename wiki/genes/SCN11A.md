# SCN11A

## Overview

SCN11A (Sodium Voltage-Gated Channel Alpha Subunit 11) encodes the Nav1.9 voltage-gated sodium channel subunit, expressed primarily in peripheral sensory neurons. In cancer genomics, SCN11A has been identified as a somatically mutated gene in prostate cancer, though its functional significance as a driver is uncertain.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel somatically mutated gene identified in prostate cancer WES (112 tumors); not previously known to be somatically altered in prostate cancer [PMID:22610119](../papers/22610119.md)
