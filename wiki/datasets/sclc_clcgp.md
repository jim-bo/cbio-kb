# Small Cell Lung Cancer (CLCGP, Nature Genetics 2012)

## Overview

99 surgically resected [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) specimens collected by the Clinical Lung Cancer Genome Project (CLCGP) consortium and analysed by multi-platform genomic profiling. Published in Nature Genetics 2012. The study established universal biallelic inactivation of [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) and [RB1](../genes/RB1.md), identified recurrent histone-modifier mutations, and nominated [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) amplification as a potential therapeutic target. [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)

## Composition

- 99 surgically resected SCLC specimens total.
- Exome sequencing: 27 tumours + 2 cell lines (n = 29).
- Whole-genome sequencing: 2 tumours.
- Transcriptome sequencing (RNA-seq): 15 tumours.
- SNP array (Affymetrix SNP 6.0): 63 tumours.
- Mouse model validation: 20 SCLC tumours from p53/Rb1 conditional knockout mice.
- Validation cohort: 26 additional tumours + 34–45 cell lines (gene-specific).
- Cancer type: [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md). [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- Universal biallelic inactivation of [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) and [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) in all 29 sequenced cases; CNLOH enrichment confirms these as early clonal events. [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
- Mutation rate of 7.4 ± 1 protein-changing mutations per million base pairs; elevated C:G>A:T transversions consistent with tobacco carcinogen exposure. [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
- [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md)/[EP300](../genes/EP300.md) mutations (HAT domain) in 18%; mutually exclusive; functional validation showed reduced H3K18 acetylation. [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
- [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) focal amplification in 6% of cases, validated by FISH in 51 independent tumours (3/51, 6%). [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
- [MYCL](../genes/MYCL.md) amplification in 8%, [MYCN](../genes/MYCN.md) in 6%, [MYC](../genes/MYC.md) in 1/63; all mutually exclusive (total 16%). [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
- [SLIT2](../genes/SLIT2.md) mutated in 10% in extended cohort; [PTEN](../genes/PTEN.md) mutations in 3/29 cases. [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
