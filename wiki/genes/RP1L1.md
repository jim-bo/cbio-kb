# RP1L1

## Overview

RP1L1 (Retinitis Pigmentosa 1-Like 1) encodes a photoreceptor-associated protein. It was identified as a MutSigCV-significant gene in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma by whole-exome sequencing, though its functional role in PDA biology has not been established.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a MutSigCV-significant recurrently mutated gene in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (109-case cohort); functional role in PDA biology remains to be validated. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)
