# PADI2

## Overview

PADI2 (Peptidylarginine Deiminase 2) encodes a calcium-dependent enzyme that converts arginine residues to citrulline via post-translational citrullination. In cancer genomics, it is an established transcriptional target of the EWS::FLI1 fusion oncogene in Ewing sarcoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Established EWS::FLI1 transcriptional target; induced upon EWS::FLI1 expression in heMSCs and validated by RT-qPCR in Ewing sarcoma cell-of-origin study [PMID:25186949](../papers/25186949.md)
