# PPARG

## Overview

PPARG (peroxisome proliferator-activated receptor gamma) is a nuclear receptor involved in adipogenesis, lipid storage, and glucose metabolism. In [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md), PPARγ contributes to lipid metabolic reprogramming.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- PPARG drives DNL/FAO programs in HCC; part of the lipid-metabolic axis alongside PPARα [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) — PPARγ contributes to lipid metabolic reprogramming in HCC [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- PPARα and PPARγ have partially overlapping and partially distinct roles in HCC lipid metabolism [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- PPARγ targeting is part of the broader lipid-metabolic therapeutic hypothesis in HCC [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Open questions

- The distinct roles of PPARα vs. PPARγ in different HCC molecular subtypes are not fully resolved in the corpus.
