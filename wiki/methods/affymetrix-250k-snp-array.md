# Affymetrix 250K SNP Array

## Overview

The Affymetrix 250K SNP Array is a high-density oligonucleotide microarray platform that simultaneously genotypes ~250,000 single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNPs) across the genome. It is used for genome-wide somatic copy-number alteration (SCNA) profiling and loss-of-heterozygosity (LOH) analysis in tumor samples.

## Used by

- Used to profile somatic copy-number alterations (n=207 samples) and LOH (n=200 samples) across seven high-grade soft tissue sarcoma subtypes; data analyzed with GISTIC and RAE to identify significantly altered regions [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).

## Notes

- Raw data from the sarcoma study deposited in NCBI GEO under accession GSE21124 [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).
- Copy-number profiling revealed ~90% frequency of chromosome 12q amplification in dedifferentiated liposarcoma ([DDLS](../cancer_types/DDLS.md)) and complex karyotypes in DDLS, [PLLS](../cancer_types/PLLS.md), [LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md), and [MFS](../cancer_types/MFS.md) [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).
