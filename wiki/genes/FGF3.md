# FGF3

## Overview

FGF3 (Fibroblast Growth Factor 3) is a member of the FGF family involved in cell proliferation and differentiation. In [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md), FGF3 is co-amplified with [FGF4](../genes/FGF4.md), [FGF19](../genes/FGF19.md), and [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) at the 11q13.3 locus, a focal amplification independently associated with poor survival.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Co-amplified at 11q13.3 locus with [FGF4](../genes/FGF4.md), [FGF19](../genes/FGF19.md), and [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) in ~6% of [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md); focal amplification appears at advanced stages and is independently associated with poor survival [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)
- Co-amplified on chromosome 11q13 with CCND1, EMSY, PAK1, RSF1; defines IntClust2 (4.5% of ER+ breast cancer); associated with chronic late-relapse risk to 20 years in ER+/HER2- disease [PMID:30867590](../papers/30867590.md)
- Located within the 11q13.3 region of recurrent copy-number gain in CDK12-biallelic prostate cancer; co-amplified with FGF19 and FGF4 [PMID:32317181](../papers/32317181.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)**: 11q13.3 co-amplification (~6% frequency) appears at advanced stages and is independently associated with poor survival in a large [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) cohort [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-amplified with [FGF4](../genes/FGF4.md), [FGF19](../genes/FGF19.md), and [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) at the 11q13.3 locus in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- FGF3/4/19 co-amplification nominates FGFR inhibitors as therapeutic candidates in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) [PMID:25822088](../papers/25822088.md)

## Open questions

- Which component(s) of the 11q13.3 amplicon are the primary oncogenic drivers.
