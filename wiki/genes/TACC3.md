# TACC3

## Overview

TACC3 is a centrosomal/spindle protein best known in cancer genomics as the canonical 3' fusion partner of [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md), producing oncogenic FGFR3-TACC3 fusions.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- FGFR3-TACC3 fusions were observed among oncogenic [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) alterations in urothelial carcinoma; FGFR3-fusion tumors had lower TMB than FGFR3-mutant tumors (median 5 vs 9 mut/Mb, p=0.0006) [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [BLCA](../cancer_types/BLCA.md) / [UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md) — FGFR3-TACC3 fusions contribute to the actionable FGFR3-altered population [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Obligate partner of [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) in FGFR3-TACC3 fusions [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- FGFR3-TACC3-fusion urothelial tumors are eligible for [erdafitinib](../drugs/erdafitinib.md) [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).

## Open questions

- Whether FGFR3-fusion tumors respond differently to [erdafitinib](../drugs/erdafitinib.md) than FGFR3-mutant tumors is not directly resolved in the corpus [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).
