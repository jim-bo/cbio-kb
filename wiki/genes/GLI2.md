# GLI2

## Overview

GLI2 is a transcription factor and effector of Hedgehog (Hh) signaling. In PDAC, GLI2 alterations contribute to Hedgehog-pathway activation, which plays a role in tumor stroma and resistance to therapy.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Hedgehog-pathway alteration in PDAC (alongside [GLI3](../genes/GLI3.md), [SMO](../genes/SMO.md), [PTCH1](../genes/PTCH1.md), [LRP2](../genes/LRP2.md)) [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)
- Overexpressed in MAML3 fusion-positive pheochromocytoma/paraganglioma ([PHC](../cancer_types/PHC.md)/[PGNG](../cancer_types/PGNG.md)) as part of the Wnt/Hedgehog pathway gene overexpression signature in the Wnt-altered mRNA subtype (TCGA PCPG study, n=173) [PMID:28162975](../papers/28162975.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)**: Hedgehog pathway alteration contributing to the landscape of actionable genomic lesions in PDAC [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-altered with [SMO](../genes/SMO.md), [GLI3](../genes/GLI3.md), [PTCH1](../genes/PTCH1.md), and [LRP2](../genes/LRP2.md) as part of Hedgehog-pathway lesions in PDAC [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Hedgehog-pathway alteration nominates Smoothened ([SMO](../genes/SMO.md)) inhibitors and downstream Hh pathway inhibitors as therapeutic candidates in PDAC [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Open questions

- Specific alteration type (amplification/mutation) in GLI2 and whether Hh-pathway alterations predict response to [SMO](../genes/SMO.md) inhibitors in PDAC.
