# EXT1

## Overview

EXT1 (Exostosin Glycosyltransferase 1) encodes a glycosyltransferase involved in heparan sulfate biosynthesis. Germline loss-of-function mutations cause hereditary multiple exostoses (osteochondromas), and somatic alterations have been observed across multiple cancer types.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Observed as a gene altered in individual synchronous DCIS/IDC-NST cases in a WES study of paired ductal carcinoma in situ and invasive ductal carcinoma (n=25 patients, MSKCC) [PMID:32220886](../papers/32220886.md)

## Cancer types (linked)
