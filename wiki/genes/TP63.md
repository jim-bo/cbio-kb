# TP63

## Overview

TP63 is a p53 family transcription factor recurrently altered in peripheral T-cell lymphoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- TP63 was among recurrently altered genes profiled in subset analyses of nodal peripheral T-cell lymphoma treated with CHOP-based chemotherapy [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- TP63 is a defining marker gene of the novel postnatal human adrenal progenitor cluster hC1 (FDR <0.01, Welch's t-test) and is significantly over-expressed in the undifferentiated nC3 cluster enriched in high-risk neuroblastoma (11-tumor single-nuclei RNA-seq, Smart-Seq2); part of the shared gene program between hC1 and nC3. [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) — recurrently altered in subset analyses [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- [NBL](../cancer_types/NBL.md) — progenitor/stem marker over-expressed in both the hC1 normal adrenal progenitor and the undifferentiated nC3 high-risk neuroblastoma cluster; part of the shared signature linking a postnatal cholinergic progenitor to high-risk tumor cells. [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Not reported for PTCL [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- Co-expressed with [BCL11A](../genes/BCL11A.md), [ERBB3](../genes/ERBB3.md), [RTTN](../genes/RTTN.md), [ASXL3](../genes/ASXL3.md), [POU6F2](../genes/POU6F2.md), [SOX6](../genes/SOX6.md), [CLDN11](../genes/CLDN11.md) in the shared hC1/nC3 progenitor gene program in neuroblastoma. [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct targeted therapy reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Independent prognostic effect not established in the [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) cohort [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
