# Neuroendocrine Tumor, NOS (NETNOS)

## Overview

Neuroendocrine Tumor, NOS (NETNOS) is a catch-all OncoTree code under Cancer of Unknown Primary ([CUP](../cancer_types/CUP.md)) for neuroendocrine tumors where the primary anatomical site cannot be determined. In the 24326773 corpus reference, one patient had an unknown primary NEN.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [pog570_bcgsc_2020](../datasets/pog570_bcgsc_2020.md) — 1 NEN of unknown primary within the 28-patient metastatic NEN WGTA cohort at BC Cancer POG. [PMID:24326773](../papers/24326773.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- The NEN of unknown primary in the POG cohort was profiled by whole-genome and transcriptome analysis; the 1,553-gene tumour-type discriminator panel classified 82% of NENs (23/28) with known NENs from external datasets — including the unknown-primary case. Actionable alterations were identified; median TMB 2.19 mut/Mb across the 28-patient NEN cohort. [PMID:24326773](../papers/24326773.md)

## Subtypes

- Unknown primary; histologic grade (well-differentiated vs. poorly differentiated) not specified for this case.

## Therapeutic landscape

- Transcriptome-guided actionable therapy identified for the unknown-primary NEN case via the POG WGTA approach. [PMID:24326773](../papers/24326773.md)
