# PLX-4545

## Overview

PLX-4545 is an investigational [IKZF2](../genes/IKZF2.md) (Helios) degrader designed to reprogram regulatory T cells (Tregs) by eliminating the transcription factor that stabilizes Treg suppressive function. It is evaluated in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)) and other solid tumors.

## Evidence in the corpus

- PLX-4545 IKZF2 degrader listed alongside DKY709 as clinical-stage agents targeting IKZF2 (Helios) to reprogram Tregs in the immunosuppressive NPC tumor microenvironment [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
