# DICER1

## Overview

DICER1 encodes the endoribonuclease essential for miRNA biogenesis; rare somatic and germline mutations generate distinct cancer predispositions and hotspot-driven oncogenic events.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Nominated as a novel CLL driver via 3-D clustering with [CLUMPS](../methods/clumps.md) in the 1,148-patient integrated CLL map [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Also disrupted by the recurrent ~37-Mb chromosome 14 deletion observed in U-CLL (4/87 WGS, 4.6%), which arises via class-switch recombination and simultaneously disrupts [ZFP36L1](../genes/ZFP36L1.md) and [TRAF3](../genes/TRAF3.md) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- CLL — DICER1 is a novel CLUMPS-nominated driver with both point-mutation and structural-variant modes in the U-CLL subtype [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- DICER1 is co-disrupted with [ZFP36L1](../genes/ZFP36L1.md) and [TRAF3](../genes/TRAF3.md) by the recurrent U-CLL chr14 class-switch-recombination deletion [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No DICER1-directed therapy is reported in the corpus [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Open questions

- Functional validation of DICER1 as a CLL driver, one of the 109 novel drivers in the CLL map, is deferred to future work [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
