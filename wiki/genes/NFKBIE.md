# NFKBIE

## Overview

NFKBIE encodes IkB-epsilon, an inhibitor of NF-kB signaling. In the corpus it appears as a recurrent low-frequency driver in CLL and as an NF-kB pathway driver in classic Hodgkin lymphoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Known lower-frequency CLL driver, reconfirmed as prognostic in U-CLL in the 1,148-patient CLL map [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Mutated in 15% of classic Hodgkin lymphoma cases profiled by WGS/WES (n=61); NF-kB pathway driver [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Chronic lymphocytic leukemia ([CLLSLL](../cancer_types/CLLSLL.md)) — prognostic in U-CLL [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Classic Hodgkin lymphoma ([CHL](../cancer_types/CHL.md)) — 15% mutation frequency [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Part of the NF-kB pathway driver set in cHL alongside [TNFAIP3](../genes/TNFAIP3.md) [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic link reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Mechanistic role relative to other NF-kB drivers across B-cell malignancies remains open.
