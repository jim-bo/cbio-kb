# tremelimumab

## Overview

Tremelimumab is an IgG2 monoclonal antibody targeting CTLA-4. It is approved in combination with [durvalumab](../drugs/durvalumab.md) (HIMALAYA regimen) for unresectable [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md).

## Evidence in the corpus

- A narrative review of lipid metabolism in HCC cites durvalumab + tremelimumab (HIMALAYA regimen) as one of the approved ICI combinations for HCC; CD36-mediated [FOXP3](../genes/FOXP3.md)+ Treg recruitment is proposed as a mechanism that may blunt the benefit of CTLA-4 blockade (tremelimumab) by maintaining an immunosuppressive microenvironment. [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)

## Resistance mechanisms

- [CD36](../genes/CD36.md) upregulation in HCC recruits FOXP3+ Tregs and suppresses cytotoxic progenitor-exhausted CD8 T cells, potentially attenuating the benefit of CTLA-4 blockade. [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) — hepatocellular carcinoma; durvalumab + tremelimumab (HIMALAYA regimen); CD36/Treg axis proposed as resistance mechanism.
