# EPCAM

## Overview

EPCAM (Epithelial Cell Adhesion Molecule) is a cell-surface glycoprotein that marks epithelial progenitor and stem-like cells. In hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC), EPCAM expression defines a stem-cell/progenitor subpopulation with distinct biological behavior and adverse prognosis, and is part of a validated prognostic immunohistochemical panel.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- EPCAM, KRT19, and GPC3 form a stem-cell/progenitor-cell signature; expression is prognostic in subsets of HCC [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)
