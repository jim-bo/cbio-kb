# Salivary Carcinoma (SACA)

## Overview

Salivary Carcinoma is the OncoTree umbrella for all malignant salivary gland tumors, sitting under HEAD_NECK. Key subtypes include [ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md) (Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma), [SDCA](../cancer_types/SDCA.md) (Salivary Duct Carcinoma), [ACCC](../cancer_types/ACCC.md) (Acinic Cell Carcinoma), [MUCC](../cancer_types/MUCC.md) (Mucoepidermoid Carcinoma), and [MASC](../cancer_types/MASC.md) (Mammary Analogue Secretory Carcinoma). These tumors are collectively rare and historically managed with surgery ± radiation; systemic options are limited, driving adoption of NGS-guided basket trials.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [hnc_mskcc_2016](../datasets/hnc_mskcc_2016.md) — 56 salivary carcinomas (36 [ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md) + 20 other salivary including [SDCA](../cancer_types/SDCA.md), [ACCC](../cancer_types/ACCC.md), [MUCC](../cancer_types/MUCC.md), [MASC](../cancer_types/MASC.md)) in 151 recurrent/metastatic head and neck tumors profiled by MSK-IMPACT 410-gene panel; dataset publicly available on cBioPortal [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- **[NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md)** — activating mutations or amplifications in 22.2% of recurrent/metastatic [ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md) (8.3-fold enriched vs primary ACYC); recurrent truncating events at serine 2467 [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[TERT](../genes/TERT.md)** — promoter mutations in 14% of ACYC — the first report of [TERT](../genes/TERT.md) promoter mutations in any salivary cancer [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[ETV6](../genes/ETV6.md)-[NTRK3](../genes/NTRK3.md)** — fusion identifying MASC within histologically diagnosed ACCC; enables TRK inhibitor therapy [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[ERBB2](../genes/ERBB2.md)**, **[AR](../genes/AR.md)**, **[HRAS](../genes/HRAS.md)**, **[PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md)** — actionable alterations in SDCA and other salivary subtypes [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[PDGFRA](../genes/PDGFRA.md)**, **[KDR](../genes/KDR.md)**, **[KIT](../genes/KIT.md)** — co-amplification in 4 ACYC patients treated with [regorafenib](../drugs/regorafenib.md) [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- Salivary gland carcinoma showed responses to neratinib in the SUMMIT basket trial for HER2-mutant solid tumors, including kinase domain missense mutations (L755, V777), in the histology-agnostic solid tumor NOS cohort [PMID:29420467](../papers/29420467.md)
- Salivary gland carcinoma including MASC (n=12, 15.8% of TRK fusion-positive cases) had the highest TRK fusion prevalence of any tumor type in the MSK cohort: 5.29% (95% CI 2.76–9.05); ETV6-NTRK3 was present in 11/12 salivary cases; ETV6 break-apart FISH was accepted as inferred TRK fusion evidence [PMID:31871300](../papers/31871300.md)

## Subtypes

- [ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md) — largest salivary subgroup in the corpus (36 recurrent/metastatic cases); defined by [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) enrichment in advanced disease.
- [SDCA](../cancer_types/SDCA.md) — [ERBB2](../genes/ERBB2.md) and [AR](../genes/AR.md) alterations; HER2-directed and androgen-deprivation therapy rationale.
- [ACCC](../cancer_types/ACCC.md) — historically misclassified MASC cases; ETV6-NTRK3 testing critical.
- [MASC](../cancer_types/MASC.md) — defined by ETV6-NTRK3 fusion; TRK inhibitor responsive.
- [MUCC](../cancer_types/MUCC.md) — [HRAS](../genes/HRAS.md) mutations; farnesyl-transferase inhibitor candidates.

## Therapeutic landscape

- **Basket trials** are the dominant vehicle for therapeutic access in salivary carcinoma; 56 salivary patients contributed to the 14% NGS-guided therapy rate in the overall MSK-IMPACT cohort [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **TRK inhibitors** — major/near-complete responses in MASC (ETV6-NTRK3) patients enrolled on basket trial [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **γ-Secretase inhibitors** — candidate for NOTCH1-activating ACYC [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **FGFR inhibitors** — for FGFR3-mutant ACYC [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
- **[Regorafenib](../drugs/regorafenib.md)** — used in 4 ACYC patients with PDGFRA/KDR/KIT co-amplification [PMID:27442865](../papers/27442865.md).
