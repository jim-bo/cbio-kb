# ADH1A

## Overview

ADH1A (Alcohol Dehydrogenase 1A) encodes an enzyme involved in ethanol and retinol metabolism in the liver. In the context of HBV-associated hepatocellular carcinoma, ADH1A has been cited as a prognostic biomarker from prior proteogenomics studies.
