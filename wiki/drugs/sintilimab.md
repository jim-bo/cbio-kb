# sintilimab

## Overview

Sintilimab is an anti-PD-1 monoclonal antibody targeting [PDCD1](../genes/PDCD1.md). It is evaluated for locally advanced and recurrent/metastatic nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Phase III sintilimab + concurrent chemoradiation (CCRT) in locally advanced [NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md) (NCT03700476, n=425): event-free survival HR 0.59; 36-month EFS 86% vs 76%; grade ≥3 TRAEs 74%; sintilimab added to CCRT improved EFS in locally advanced [NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md) [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
- Anti-CTLA-4 IBI-310 evaluated in combination with sintilimab in R/M [NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md) to address compensatory CTLA-4 checkpoint upregulation [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
- Anti-PD-1 agent whose efficacy in biliary tract cancer is reduced by Proteobacteria dominance in gut microbiota composition; microbiota-modulation strategies require careful design to avoid antibiotic-induced immunotherapy resistance [PMID:25608663](../papers/25608663.md)
