# PAX7

## Overview

PAX7 is a paired-box transcription factor. PAX7::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusions define a more favorable prognostic subset of alveolar rhabdomyosarcoma relative to [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- PAX7::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion present in a minority of extremity [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) cases in the 61-patient MSK cohort; detected by Archer FusionPlex or [FISH](../methods/fish.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- PAX7::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) carries more favorable prognosis relative to [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) (HR 3.45 favoring PAX7, P=.016) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- PAX7::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion found in 2/18 FP-RMS patients in a multi-institutional sequential genomic study; associated with FOXO1 amplification in these tumors [PMID:37730754](../papers/37730754.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Alveolar rhabdomyosarcoma ([ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md)) — favorable fusion partner [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Mutually exclusive with [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusions in [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- Supports fusion-partner-aware risk stratification [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Open questions

- Biological basis for prognostic divergence between [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md) and PAX7 fusion subtypes [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
