# NT5C2

## Overview

NT5C2 encodes a cytosolic 5'-nucleotidase implicated in thiopurine resistance in relapsed ALL. In the corpus it appears as a relapse-associated gene in infant ALL [PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Relapse-associated mutations identified in infant ALL [PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [BLLKMT2A](../cancer_types/BLLKMT2A.md): NT5C2 mutations observed at relapse [PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Not reported in this corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- Relevance to thiopurine-based maintenance therapy resistance is implied by relapse enrichment [PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md).

## Open questions

- Whether NT5C2 mutations are selected during maintenance therapy in infant MLL-R ALL specifically [PMID:25730765](../papers/25730765.md).
