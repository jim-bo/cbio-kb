# Liver (LIVER)

## Overview

LIVER is the top-level OncoTree tissue category for liver cancers (parent: TISSUE, level 1). It encompasses hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC/LIHC), cholangiocarcinoma ([CHOL](../cancer_types/CHOL.md)), and other hepatic malignancies. In the corpus this code appears as an anatomical site category in radiation-oncology imaging studies; specific genomic-cohort pages use more specific OncoTree codes (e.g., [LIHC](../cancer_types/LIHC.md) for hepatocellular carcinoma).
