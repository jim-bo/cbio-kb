# linifanib

## Overview

Linifanib is a multi-kinase inhibitor targeting VEGFR and PDGFR. It was evaluated as a frontline therapy for hepatocellular carcinoma ([HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)) but failed to demonstrate superiority over [sorafenib](../drugs/sorafenib.md).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Failed as frontline HCC therapy vs sorafenib (phase III): median OS 8.0–8.8 vs 9.9–10.0 months for sorafenib (HR 1.12–1.15); demonstrated inferior efficacy in first-line setting [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md).
