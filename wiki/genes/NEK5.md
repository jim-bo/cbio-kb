# NEK5

## Overview

NEK5 (NIMA Related Kinase 5) is a serine/threonine kinase of the NEK family involved in mitotic regulation. In cancer genomics, it has been identified as a fusion partner in structural rearrangements affecting the TERT promoter region in chromophobe renal cell carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Partner gene in the one inter-chromosomal TERT-promoter structural rearrangement on chromosome 13 in chromophobe RCC; rearrangement associated with >500-unit TERT expression upregulation [PMID:25155756](../papers/25155756.md)
