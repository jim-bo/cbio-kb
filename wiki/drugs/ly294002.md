# ly294002

## Overview

LY294002 is a chromone-based, non-selective, reversible PI3K inhibitor widely used as a research tool to block class I PI3K activity and downstream [AKT1](../genes/AKT1.md) signalling. It has no clinical development or approved oncology indication. `unverified: true` — not found in OncoKB canonical drug list; slug is corpus-derived.

## Evidence in the corpus

- In gallbladder cancer (GBC) cells, LY294002 blocked [SEMA7A](../genes/SEMA7A.md)-induced phosphorylation of [EP300](../genes/EP300.md) (p300) at S1834 (downstream of [AKT1](../genes/AKT1.md) pS473), establishing the ITGB1/PI3K/AKT/p300 axis as the mechanistic link between stromal SEMA7A signalling and epigenetic activation of EMT drivers ([SNAI1](../genes/SNAI1.md), [ZEB1](../genes/ZEB1.md)) [PMID:24997986](../papers/24997986.md).
