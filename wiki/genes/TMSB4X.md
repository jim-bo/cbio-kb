# TMSB4X

## Overview

TMSB4X is recurrently mutated in classic Hodgkin lymphoma via AID off-target activity.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- TMSB4X showed an elevated noncoding:coding mutation ratio in classic Hodgkin lymphoma HRS cells, consistent with AID off-target activity [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [CHL](../cancer_types/CHL.md) — AID off-target mutation hotspot [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-occurs among AID-target genes ([BCL7A](../genes/BCL7A.md), [SOCS1](../genes/SOCS1.md), [IL4R](../genes/IL4R.md), [ITPKB](../genes/ITPKB.md)) [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct targeted therapy reported in the corpus.

## Open questions
