# CTTN

## Overview

CTTN (Cortactin) is an actin-binding protein encoded on chromosome 11q13 that promotes actin polymerization and is involved in cell motility and invasion. In HNSCC it is co-amplified with [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) and [FADD](../genes/FADD.md) as part of a recurrent 11q13 amplification cassette.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- 11q13 co-amplification cassette with [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) and [FADD](../genes/FADD.md) in 31% of HPV(-) HNSCC vs. 3% of HPV(+) tumors (279-tumor TCGA cohort) [PMID:25631445](../papers/25631445.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Head and neck squamous cell carcinoma ([HNSC](../cancer_types/HNSC.md)): 11q13 co-amplification enriched in HPV(-) subtype [PMID:25631445](../papers/25631445.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-amplified with [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) and [FADD](../genes/FADD.md) at 11q13 in HPV(-) HNSCC [PMID:25631445](../papers/25631445.md)
