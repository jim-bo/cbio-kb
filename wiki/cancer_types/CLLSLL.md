# Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia/Small Lymphocytic Lymphoma (CLLSLL)

## Overview

CLL/SLL is a Mature B-Cell Neoplasm (parent MBN). IGHV-mutated (M-CLL) and IGHV-unmutated (U-CLL) define major molecular subtypes with distinct driver landscapes and prognoses.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [cll_broad_2022](../datasets/cll_broad_2022.md): 1,148 patients (1,095 CLL, 54 [MBL](../cancer_types/MBL.md)); WES/WGS n=1,074 (984 WES, 177 WGS); [RNA-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) n=712; DNA methylation n=999 — the largest integrated CLL cohort to date [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- Cardinal drivers: [SF3B1](../genes/SF3B1.md) 17.5%, [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) 12.3%, [ATM](../genes/ATM.md) 11.2%, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) 9.1% [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- 202 candidate drivers (109 novel); 24.2% of patients carry at least one novel driver mutation [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Non-coding/non-standard drivers: IGLV3-21 R110 9.5%, U1 snRNA g.3A>C 3.8% [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Novel drivers via 3D [CLUMPS](../methods/clumps.md) clustering include [MAP2K2](../genes/MAP2K2.md), [DIS3](../genes/DIS3.md), [DICER1](../genes/DICER1.md), [INO80](../genes/INO80.md) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Recurrent SVs: [BCL2](../genes/BCL2.md) translocations predominantly in M-CLL (5.7% of WGS cases) via aberrant V(D)J; recurrent 37-Mb chr14 deletion disrupting [ZFP36L1](../genes/ZFP36L1.md), [DICER1](../genes/DICER1.md), [TRAF3](../genes/TRAF3.md) in U-CLL (4.6%) via class-switch recombination [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Mutational signatures: canonical AID (SBS84) enriched in U-CLL; non-canonical AID (SBS85) enriched in M-CLL (p=1.6×10^-9) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Subtypes

- IGHV-unmutated CLL (U-CLL, n=459) harbors more drivers than IGHV-mutated CLL (M-CLL, n=512): 54 vs 25 drivers (ratio 2.16, p=0.0015) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- 8 gene-expression clusters identified by unsupervised [RNA-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) clustering (n=603 treatment-naive) are independent prognostic factors beyond IGHV/epitype [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Epitype framework (n-CLL, i-CLL, m-CLL) with epiCMIT mitotic clock [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- In the era of [ibrutinib](../drugs/ibrutinib.md)/[venetoclax](../drugs/venetoclax.md), isolated [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation (without 17p deletion) does not confer adverse prognosis in U-CLL [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- [RRM1](../genes/RRM1.md) is a U-CLL-specific novel driver and a target of [fludarabine](../drugs/fludarabine.md) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
