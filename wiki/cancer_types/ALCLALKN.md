# Anaplastic Large-Cell Lymphoma ALK Negative (ALCLALKN)

## Overview

ALK-negative Anaplastic Large-Cell Lymphoma, a subtype of [ALCL](./ALCL.md) within Mature T and NK Neoplasms.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [mtnn_msk_2022](../datasets/mtnn_msk_2022.md): [ALK](../genes/ALK.md)− [ALCL](../cancer_types/ALCL.md) comprised 11% of the 132-patient MSK nodal [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) cohort [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- Analyzed within the broader [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) cohort where [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations/17p deletions independently predicted inferior PFS and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) deletions predicted inferior [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Subtypes

- See parent [ALCL](./ALCL.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- Curative-intent CHOP-based chemotherapy, including CHOEP/EPOCH and BV-CH(E)P [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
