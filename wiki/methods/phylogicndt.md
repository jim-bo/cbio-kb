# PhylogicNDT

## Overview

Phylogenetic and clonal-dynamics inference toolkit for reconstructing tumor evolution and the relative temporal order of somatic events from bulk sequencing.

## Used by

- [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md) — used in the CLL-map cohort for temporal ordering of driver events: trisomy 12 was placed early; TP53/NOTCH1 intermediate in both IGHV subtypes; [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) early in M-CLL but late in U-CLL (q<0.1); [MYD88](../genes/MYD88.md) early in M-CLL; 20p loss and [FUBP1](../genes/FUBP1.md) potentially initiating in U-CLL [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Applied in [prostate_pcbm_swiss_2019](../datasets/prostate_pcbm_swiss_2019.md) to reconstruct clonal evolution in 20 matched primary-PCBM pairs; phylogenetic trees recovered in 14/20 patients showing subclonal primary clones expanding to clonal level (CCF>0.9) in brain metastases [PMID:35504881](../papers/35504881.md)

## Notes

- Applied to CLL WES/WGS to derive subtype-specific evolutionary trajectories distinguishing M-CLL from U-CLL [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
