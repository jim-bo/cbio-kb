# ARL17A

## Overview

ARL17A (ADP-ribosylation factor-like GTPase 17A) is a small GTPase of the ARF family. It is notable as the 3' partner in the recurrent KANSARL fusion ([KANSL1](../genes/KANSL1.md)–ARL17A), which has been observed in schwannomatosis tumors and is thought to arise from a chromosomal rearrangement at the 17q21 locus.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ARL17A is part of the KANSARL (KANSL1-ARL17A) recurrent gene fusion detected at similar rates in both SWNTS-SWN and NS-SWN schwannomatosis tumor subtypes [PMID:33025139](../papers/33025139.md)
