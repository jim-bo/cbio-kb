# SFTPB

## Overview

SFTPB (Surfactant Protein B) encodes a lipophilic surfactant-associated protein critical for lung function. In lung cancer genomics, somatic SFTPB mutations have been observed in lung adenocarcinoma and represent a timing marker in the clonal evolution of lung adenocarcinoma in never-smokers (LCINS).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SFTPB mutations occur after copy-number gain events in LCINS [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md), in contrast to [RBM10](../genes/RBM10.md) and [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) mutations which precede copy gains; identified as a driver/timing observation in clonal evolution analysis of lung adenocarcinoma in never-smokers [PMID:34493867](../papers/34493867.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Lung adenocarcinoma (LUAD): SFTPB mutations observed in LCINS (lung cancer in never-smokers) with specific timing relative to copy-number gain events [PMID:34493867](../papers/34493867.md)
