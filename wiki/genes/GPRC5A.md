# GPRC5A

## Overview

GPRC5A (G Protein-Coupled Receptor Class C Group 5 Member A) is a retinoic acid-inducible orphan G protein-coupled receptor. Its expression is normally restricted to the lung but is aberrantly elevated in multiple cancers, including colorectal cancer. It has been implicated in cancer cell survival and invasiveness. The strong and uniform upregulation of its protein across colorectal tumors suggests potential utility as a biomarker or therapeutic target.

## Alterations observed in the corpus
