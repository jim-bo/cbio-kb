# GEN1160

## Overview

GEN1160 is an antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) targeting CD70 ([CD70](../genes/CD70.md)), a TNF superfamily ligand that engages CD27 to sustain regulatory T cell (Treg) survival. In NPC, CD70 is expressed on tumor cells and is being exploited as an ADC target.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Cited as an investigational anti-CD70 ADC among agents targeting the immunosuppressive tumor microenvironment in R/M NPC [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
