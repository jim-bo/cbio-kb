# PRKCZ

## Overview

PRKCZ (Protein Kinase C Zeta) encodes an atypical serine/threonine kinase that is part of the PKC family. It plays roles in cell polarity, proliferation, and survival signaling. In cancer genomics, it has been identified as a candidate driver in breast cancer through computational driver network analyses.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Mutation identified in 1 case in a WGS study of 65 triple-negative breast cancers (TNBC); ranked 32nd by driverNet driver scoring [PMID:22495314](../papers/22495314.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Breast cancer (TNBC): candidate driver gene identified by network-based driver analysis [PMID:22495314](../papers/22495314.md)
