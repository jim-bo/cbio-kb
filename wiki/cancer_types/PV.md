# Polycythemia Vera (PV)

## Overview

Polycythemia Vera (PV) is a BCR-ABL1-negative myeloproliferative neoplasm defined by erythrocytosis and near-universal [JAK2](../genes/JAK2.md) V617F mutation. It sits under [MPN](../cancer_types/MPN.md) in OncoTree. PV carries the highest JAK2 mutation prevalence of the classic MPNs.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [mpn_cimr_2013](../datasets/mpn_cimr_2013.md) — 48 PV patients with whole-exome sequencing. [PMID:24325359](../papers/24325359.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- In 48 PV patients, JAK2 V617F was present in 48/48 (100%). [CALR](../genes/CALR.md) mutations were absent in all 48 PV patients, consistent with CALR being restricted to JAK2/MPL-negative [ET](../cancer_types/ET.md) and [MF](../cancer_types/MF.md). Median 6.5 somatic mutations per patient. [PMID:24325359](../papers/24325359.md)

## Subtypes

- No molecular subtypes defined in the current corpus beyond JAK2 driver.

## Therapeutic landscape

- JAK2 inhibition ([ruxolitinib](../drugs/ruxolitinib.md)) is standard-of-care context; no specific drug data in the current corpus. [PMID:24325359](../papers/24325359.md)
