# entrectinib

## Overview

Entrectinib (RXDX-101) is an orally available, CNS-penetrant inhibitor of TRKA/B/C (encoded by NTRK1/2/3), [ROS1](../genes/ROS1.md), and [ALK](../genes/ALK.md) kinases. It is FDA-approved in a tumor-agnostic indication for solid tumors harboring NTRK gene fusions and for ROS1-positive non-small cell lung cancer.

## Evidence in the corpus

- FDA-recognized level 1 (OncoKB) biomarker: [NTRK1](../genes/NTRK1.md) fusions (e.g., LMNA::NTRK1) predict response to entrectinib across solid tumor types including gallbladder carcinoma; 1 patient in the MSK [GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md) cohort (n=233) harbored an actionable NTRK1 fusion [PMID:36228155](../papers/36228155.md)
