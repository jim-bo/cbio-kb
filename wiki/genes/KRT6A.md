# KRT6A

## Overview

KRT6A (Keratin 6A) encodes a type II intermediate filament protein expressed in stratified epithelia, characteristic of squamous differentiation. In bladder cancer, KRT6A is a defining marker of basal/stem identity within the basal-squamous mRNA subtype of muscle-invasive bladder cancer (MIBC), which represents 35% of tumors and is enriched for squamous features and immune infiltration.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Defining basal/stem marker of the **basal-squamous** MIBC subtype (35% of 412 tumors, TCGA [BLCA](../cancer_types/BLCA.md) 2017); high expression co-occurs with [CD44](../genes/CD44.md), [KRT5](../genes/KRT5.md), and [KRT14](../genes/KRT14.md) in squamous-differentiated tumors with strongest immune signature. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)
- Specifically among 37/45 squamous-feature tumors that fell in the basal-squamous subtype (p < 10⁻¹¹). [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[Bladder Urothelial Carcinoma (BLCA)](../cancer_types/BLCA.md):** KRT6A is a key marker of the basal-squamous subtype, which has high [CD274](../genes/CD274.md) and [CTLA4](../genes/CTLA4.md) expression, strong immune infiltrate, and is potentially responsive to both cisplatin-based neoadjuvant chemotherapy and immune checkpoint therapy. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [CD44](../genes/CD44.md), [KRT5](../genes/KRT5.md), and [KRT14](../genes/KRT14.md) as defining basal-squamous markers in MIBC. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)
- Mutually low vs. luminal markers [KRT20](../genes/KRT20.md), [UPK1A](../genes/UPK1A.md), [UPK2](../genes/UPK2.md) in the umbrella-cell luminal subtype. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- The basal-squamous MIBC subtype (defined in part by KRT6A expression) is proposed as an indication for both [cisplatin](../drugs/cisplatin.md)-based neoadjuvant chemotherapy and immune checkpoint therapy (e.g., [atezolizumab](../drugs/atezolizumab.md)); head-to-head trials are warranted. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Open questions

- Whether KRT6A expression level itself (beyond subtype membership) has independent prognostic or predictive value in MIBC has not been demonstrated in this cohort. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)
