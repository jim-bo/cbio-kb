# PHLDA1

## Overview

PHLDA1 (Pleckstrin Homology Like Domain Family A Member 1) encodes a stress-response protein with a pleckstrin-homology domain. It has roles in apoptosis regulation and has been identified as a downstream effector in receptor tyrosine kinase signaling contexts, including in glioblastoma where it is upregulated in EGFR-altered tumors at the protein level.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Protein upregulated in EGFR-altered glioblastoma tumors without concordant RNA change, suggesting post-transcriptional or translational regulation downstream of [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md) phospho-signaling [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md)**: Protein upregulation associated with EGFR alteration (amplification/mutation/SV) in glioblastoma multi-omic profiling [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-upregulated with [PHLDA3](../genes/PHLDA3.md) in EGFR-altered GBM tumors; both elevated at protein level without RNA change [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported in corpus; upregulation as downstream EGFR effector may contribute to oncogenic signaling in GBM [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md)

## Open questions

- Mechanism of protein upregulation without RNA change in EGFR-altered GBM remains uncharacterized.
