# bortezomib

## Overview

Bortezomib is a first-in-class boronic-acid proteasome inhibitor that reversibly inhibits the 26S proteasome, blocking ubiquitin-mediated protein degradation and inducing apoptosis in tumor cells. It is FDA-approved for multiple myeloma and mantle cell lymphoma. In the sarcoma UCLA patient-derived tumor organoid (PDTO) biobank, it was tested as part of a >400-compound screen and showed subtype-specific sensitivity patterns.

## Evidence in the corpus

- In the UCLA sarcoma PDTO biobank ([sarcoma_ucla_2024](../datasets/sarcoma_ucla_2024.md), n=194 specimens, 126 patients across 24 subtypes), [chordoma](../cancer_types/CHDM.md) PDTOs were significantly less sensitive to bortezomib than the pan-sarcoma average (p=1.8×10⁻⁵), identifying chordoma as a subtype with relative bortezomib resistance. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
- In n=5 patients who received a treatment regimen matched to their PDTO screen result, one patient received bortezomib + [panobinostat](../drugs/panobinostat.md); normalized organoid viability correlated with time-to-next-treatment across the matched cohort (R²=0.921, p=0.009). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
- Proteasome inhibitor bortezomib showed broad, highly active anti-tumor effects across all four CTCL cell lines (HH, HUT78, HUT102, SeAX) regardless of genotype, consistent with convergent NFkB activation by [CARD11](../genes/CARD11.md), [TNFRSF1B](../genes/TNFRSF1B.md), and [PRKG1](../genes/PRKG1.md) mutations in Sézary syndrome [PMID:26551667](../papers/26551667.md)

## Resistance mechanisms

- Chordoma ([CHDM](../cancer_types/CHDM.md)) demonstrates constitutive proteasome-pathway insensitivity to bortezomib ex vivo; the mechanism driving this differential resistance was not characterized in this study. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [CHDM](../cancer_types/CHDM.md) — chordoma; significantly less sensitive than pan-sarcoma average (p=1.8×10⁻⁵).
- [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md), [LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md), [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) — sarcoma subtypes included in the pan-sarcoma PDTO screen.
