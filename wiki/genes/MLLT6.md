# MLLT6

## Overview

MLLT6 (MLLT6 Super Elongation Complex Subunit, also known as AF17) encodes a component of the [DOT1L](../genes/DOT1L.md) complex and super elongation complex involved in transcriptional elongation and histone H3K79 methylation. Originally characterized as a translocation partner with [KMT2A](../genes/KMT2A.md) (MLL) in acute leukemia, MLLT6 has been identified as a somatically altered gene in solid tumors including breast cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- MLLT6 detected among the genes altered in whole-exome sequencing of synchronous ductal carcinoma in situ ([DCIS](../cancer_types/DCIS.md)) and invasive ductal carcinomas of no special type (IDC-NST) from 25 patients at MSKCC; found in the somatic alteration repertoire of this cohort [PMID:32220886](../papers/32220886.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [BRCA](../cancer_types/BRCA.md) — identified among the genes altered in synchronous DCIS and IDC-NST WES profiling [PMID:32220886](../papers/32220886.md)

## Open questions
