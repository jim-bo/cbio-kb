# RBX1

## Overview

RBX1 (Ring-Box 1, also known as ROC1) is a RING domain protein and essential component of the SCF (SKP1-CUL1-F-box) E3 ubiquitin ligase complex. Copy number loss of SCF complex genes including RBX1 induces chromosomal instability (CIN) in colorectal cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Invariable core member of the SCF E3 ubiquitin ligase complex; copy number loss of SCF genes ([SKP1](../genes/SKP1.md), [CUL1](../genes/CUL1.md), RBX1) induces CIN in CRC [PMID:36334560](../papers/36334560.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity
