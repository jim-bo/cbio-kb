# POU2AF3

## Overview

POU2AF3 (POU class 2 homeobox associating factor 3) encodes a transcriptional coactivator. It is recognized as an [EWSR1](../genes/EWSR1.md) fusion partner in a rare subtype of undifferentiated small round cell sarcomas within the WHO 2020 "round cell sarcoma with EWSR1-non-ETS fusions" category.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Discussed as a characterised EWSR1-non-ETS fusion partner in the WHO 2020 "round cell sarcoma with EWSR1-non-ETS fusions" category, alongside EWSR1::[BEND2](../genes/BEND2.md), EWSR1::[NFATC2](../genes/NFATC2.md), and EWSR1::[PATZ1](../genes/PATZ1.md); mentioned in the differential diagnostic context of a bladder EWSR1::BEND2 sarcoma case [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Undifferentiated small round cell sarcoma ([SARC](../cancer_types/SARC.md)): EWSR1::POU2AF3 fusions are a WHO-recognized sarcoma subtype distinct from classical Ewing sarcoma [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- EWSR1::POU2AF3 is mutually exclusive with canonical EWSR1::[FLI1](../genes/FLI1.md) Ewing sarcoma fusions; part of the EWSR1-non-ETS group [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No specific targeted therapy reported; Ewing-sarcoma-like chemotherapy protocols are used in the absence of dedicated trials for EWSR1-non-ETS fusion sarcomas [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).

## Open questions

- Classification of EWSR1-non-ETS fusion sarcomas (BEND2, NFATC2, PATZ1, POU2AF3) — whether site/morphology should split or merge these entities within WHO taxonomy remains unresolved [PMID:28199314](../papers/28199314.md).
