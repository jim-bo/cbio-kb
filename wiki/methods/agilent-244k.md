# Agilent 244K

## Overview

A microarray platform (Agilent 244,000-feature) used for high-resolution analysis of gene expression or comparative genomic hybridization.

## Used by

- Utilized for gene expression profiling in 206 glioblastoma samples to characterize core pathway alterations and molecular subtypes [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).
- Used for aCGH (array comparative genomic hybridization) in the MSKCC prostate cancer integrative genomic profiling study of 218 tumors (181 primaries + 37 metastases) [PMID:20579941](../papers/20579941.md)

## Notes

- Contributed to the definition of the RTK/RAS/PI3K, p53, and RB signaling pathways as being nearly universally altered in [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).
