# BRAF Fusions (MSK, Cancer Discovery 2024) — Archer/Fusion Panel

## Overview

MSK cohort of patients with oncogenic [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) fusions detected by RNA-based fusion panel (MSK-Fusion/Archer) at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center between January 2014 and November 2022. This dataset captures fusion-panel confirmed BRAF fusions across 52 histologies and is deposited in cBioPortal as `braf_msk_archer_2024`. A companion tissue-sequenced cohort is available as [braf_msk_impact_2024](../datasets/braf_msk_impact_2024.md). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)

## Composition

- 212 patients with oncogenic BRAF fusions (195 de novo, 17 detected post-targeted therapy; 15 confirmed acquired) across 52 histologies. [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)
- 241 BRAF fusion-positive tumors total.
- Cancer types include pilocytic astrocytoma, ganglioglioma, low-grade neuroepithelial tumors, acinar cell carcinoma of the pancreas, prostate adenocarcinoma, and colorectal cancer, among others. [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)
- 24 patients with de novo BRAF fusions treated with MAPK-pathway directed therapies were evaluable for clinical outcomes (RECIST v1.1). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)

## Assays / panels (linked)

- RNA-based MSK-Fusion panel (Archer) for fusion detection.
- Companion MSK-IMPACT tissue sequencing available in [braf_msk_impact_2024](../datasets/braf_msk_impact_2024.md). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md) — Tumor-agnostic genomic and clinical analysis of BRAF fusions identify actionable targets.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- De novo BRAF fusion prevalence was <1% (195/69,337 total patients screened) across all cancers; highest in pilocytic astrocytomas (56%, N=29). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)
- 82 unique 5' fusion partners identified; 39 (48%) were novel. Most common: [KIAA1549](../genes/KIAA1549.md) (25%), [SND1](../genes/SND1.md) (10%), [AGK](../genes/AGK.md) (6%). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)
- Fusion partner distribution was histology-specific: [SND1](../genes/SND1.md) in prostate adenocarcinoma (21%), [TRIM24](../genes/TRIM24.md) in colorectal cancer (43%). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)
- Of 20 evaluable patients treated with MAPK therapies: PR (N=2), SD (N=11), PD (N=7). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)
- 15 patients acquired BRAF fusions after targeted therapy; 10 had [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md)-mutant [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md). [PMID:38922339](../papers/38922339.md)
