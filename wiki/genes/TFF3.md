# TFF3

## Overview

TFF3 (Trefoil Factor 3) encodes a goblet-cell-expressed secreted peptide involved in mucosal integrity and intestinal differentiation. In colorectal cancer research, TFF3 marks goblet-cell gene expression retained in the serrated polyp lineage even when key differentiation regulators such as ATOH1 are lost.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Goblet-cell gene retained in sessile serrated cells (SSCs) despite loss of ATOH1, co-expressed with [MUC2](../genes/MUC2.md) in the serrated lineage in the COLON MAP/HTAN colorectal polyp atlas [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) — TFF3 expression is maintained in SSCs as part of the goblet-cell differentiation program despite loss of canonical regulators, distinguishing the serrated from the conventional adenoma lineage [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with MUC2 in SSCs; occurs alongside gastric-program genes [TFF2](../genes/TFF2.md), [TACSTD2](../genes/TACSTD2.md), [AQP5](../genes/AQP5.md), and [MSLN](../genes/MSLN.md) in the serrated metaplasia state driven by BRAF-V600E [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- TFF3 contributes to the SSC marker signature (CDX2-low, [MUC5AC](../genes/MUC5AC.md)+, goblet-cell-retained) proposed for diagnostic classification of serrated lesions [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Open questions

- The mechanism by which TFF3 is maintained in SSCs despite ATOH1 loss is unresolved and may involve alternative transcriptional regulators in the serrated lineage [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)
