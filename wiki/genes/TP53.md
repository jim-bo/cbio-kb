# TP53

## Overview

TP53 is the canonical tumor suppressor and the most broadly cited gene in the cbio-kb corpus, recurrently altered across CLL, appendiceal adenocarcinoma, Hodgkin lymphoma, peripheral T-cell lymphoma, and lung adenocarcinoma with disease-specific prognostic implications.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Frequent target of mutation and deletion (35%) in [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) samples; central component of the p53 pathway, which is altered in 78% of cases [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).
- TP53 was mutated in 9.1% of CLL patients and co-occurs with 17p deletion; TP53 mutation *without* 17p deletion was not associated with adverse outcome in U-CLL in the ibrutinib/venetoclax era [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Oncogenic TP53 mutations were observed in a minority of female germ cell tumors profiled via Make-an-IMPACT, mostly clonal by WES [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- Detection of pathogenic TP53 alterations in ctDNA (vs tissue only) was associated with worse prognosis in advanced [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md).
- TP53 was mutated in 8% of classic Hodgkin lymphoma cases [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- TP53 mutations were observed in 16% (21/132) of nodal [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md); 15/21 (71%) lacked concurrent TP53/17p deletion while 6/21 (29%) were co-deleted [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- TP53-mut predominant mucinous appendiceal adenocarcinoma tumors were highly aneuploid, invasive, and carried the worst prognosis; aneuploidy was an independent predictor of poor [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) (P=0.001) [PMID:36493333](../papers/36493333.md).
- Inactivation of TP53 (together with [SMARCA4](../genes/SMARCA4.md) and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md)) correlated with site-specific shorter time to metastasis in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- TP53 alterations were enriched in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) primary tumors from patients who later developed brain metastasis vs non-metastatic primaries; TP53 was commonly shared between BM and PT/EM and often truncal [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md).
- TP53 mutations were observed in 94% of 1p19q intact IDH-mutant astrocytomas in an MSKCC active-surveillance low-grade glioma cohort (MSK-IMPACT subcohort n=73) [PMID:37910594](../papers/37910594.md).
- TP53 was included in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) pathway/metastasis analyses in the MSK-CHORD clinicogenomic dataset (n=24,950) [PMID:39506116](../papers/39506116.md).
- In SDH-deficient GISTs (n=26), TP53 mutations portended worse recurrence-free and disease-free survival [PMID:37477937](../papers/37477937.md).
- TP53 clonal hematopoiesis VAFs consistently expanded at onset of subsequent hematologic malignancy in 26 MSK patients with paired post-diagnosis sequencing, consistent with preleukemic selection under therapy [PMID:38147626](../papers/38147626.md).
- In FGFR3-altered urothelial carcinoma, TP53 is inversely associated with [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) alterations; baseline TP53 co-mutation linked to lower [erdafitinib](../drugs/erdafitinib.md) ORR (22% vs 40%) and enrichment among primary progressors (3/5); acquired TP53 mutations identified in 5/27 serial cfDNA cases as putative [erdafitinib](../drugs/erdafitinib.md) resistance drivers [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).
- In 397 sequenced resected [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) patients (MSK `pancreas_msk_2024`), TP53 was mutated in 71% (284/397); frequency did not differ by [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) allele (G12D / G12V / G12R) [PMID:39214094](../papers/39214094.md).
- TP53 was the most frequently altered gene across all tumor types in CSF ctDNA (n=242, 49% of 489 ctDNA-positive samples) in the [csf_msk_2024](../datasets/csf_msk_2024.md) cohort (1,007 CSF samples, 711 patients) profiled by MSK-IMPACT [PMID:39289779](../papers/39289779.md).
- TP53 LOF mutations/deletions were present in 25-50% of RT-[MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md), RT-[MFH](../cancer_types/MFH.md) (UPS), and RT-[OS](../cancer_types/OS.md), but only 9% of RT-[ANGS](../cancer_types/ANGS.md) (vs 23% sporadic AS); TP53 alterations were depleted in RT-AS relative to sporadic AS in the MSK 82-patient RT-sarcoma cohort [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- TP53 is the defining mutation of [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) precursors (p53 signatures, STICs); mutant p53 protein is elevated by CyCIF in p53.I, STIC.I, STIC.C, and cancer vs normal fallopian tube epithelium; clonal TP53 mutations are shared between STICs and concurrent [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) in a 44-specimen fallopian tube cohort [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).
- In a 2,336-patient MSK PDAC cohort ([pdac_msk_2024](../datasets/pdac_msk_2024.md)), TP53 was altered in 78% of KRAS-mutant tumors, 38% of other-MAPK-mutant (P = 1.4×10⁻⁹), and 45% of MAPK-WT tumors (P = 1.7×10⁻⁵); germline [ATM](../genes/ATM.md) loss-of-function mutations and somatic TP53 mutations were mutually exclusive (P = 4×10⁻¹¹); TP53 co-occurrence with germline [BRCA1](../genes/BRCA1.md) was enriched (P = 4×10⁻⁷) [PMID:39753968](../papers/39753968.md).
- TP53 was mutated in 17% of pleomorphic liposarcoma ([PLLS](../cancer_types/PLLS.md)) — the only soft tissue sarcoma subtype in which TP53 mutations were detected across 207 high-grade sarcomas spanning seven histologic subtypes ([sarc_mskcc](../datasets/sarc_mskcc.md)) [PMID:20601955](../papers/20601955.md).
- Mutated in 66 of 188 [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) tumours (35%); mutation frequency increases with tumour grade (13%, 24%, 52% for grades 1, 2, 3; P=7.8e-6); copy number loss and reduced expression in mutant tumours. [PMID:18948947](../papers/18948947.md)
- Germline P/LP variant identified as incidental finding in a [GIST](../cancer_types/GIST.md) paired tumor-normal sequencing study [PMID:36593350](../papers/36593350.md)
- Most frequently mutated gene (43% of cohort); enriched in metastases (66% vs 33% in primary tumors) in a longitudinal African breast cancer study [PMID:36585450](../papers/36585450.md)
- Mutations (e.g., V157F) more frequent in high-grade invasive and metastatic bladder tumors (q < 0.001) [PMID:36543146](../papers/36543146.md)
- Nearly ubiquitous mutation in [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) (background alteration across all genomic subtypes) [PMID:36517593](../papers/36517593.md)
- Truncal TP53 mutations (R273C, R248W, R248Q) tracked as baseline alterations in CRC patients treated with KRASG12C + [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md) inhibition [PMID:36355783](../papers/36355783.md)
- TP53 expression reduced by shRNA in the A1309 CRC cell model for [FBXO7](../genes/FBXO7.md) synthetic lethality screening [PMID:36334560](../papers/36334560.md)
- TP53 most frequently mutated gene in metastatic UC; E285K hotspot enriched in metastatic cohorts (10.8% in UC-GENOME vs 5.9% in TCGA-BLCA); APOBEC-attributable hotspot mutation [PMID:36333289](../papers/36333289.md)
- TP53 LOF mutations in 63% of [GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md) (most frequently altered gene); TP53 pathway altered in 74% of samples [PMID:36228155](../papers/36228155.md)
- Mutated in prostate cancer identified in integrative genomic profiling of 218 tumors [PMID:20579941](../papers/20579941.md)
- Mutated in pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors (PanNET) identified by exome sequencing [PMID:21252315](../papers/21252315.md)
- Most frequently mutated gene in high-grade serous ovarian carcinoma (HGSOC), altered in ~96% of tumors in TCGA integrated genomic analysis [PMID:21720365](../papers/21720365.md)
- Mutated in diffuse large B-cell lymphoma (DLBCL) and follicular lymphoma identified by whole-genome/exome sequencing [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- TP53 is the most frequently mutated gene in HNSCC (74 tumor-normal pairs, Broad), with truncating and missense mutations across the DNA-binding domain [PMID:21798893](../papers/21798893.md)
- TP53 mutations are the most prevalent driver alterations in HNSCC whole-exome sequencing of 32 primary tumors (Johns Hopkins cohort) [PMID:21798897](../papers/21798897.md)
- TP53 mutations are recurrent in MDS and co-occur with STAG2 and splicing-factor mutations in the Tokyo cohort [PMID:21909114](../papers/21909114.md)
- WES of 103 breast cancers identified TP53 as the most frequently mutated gene (~38% of cases), with mutations concentrated in ER-negative and triple-negative subtypes [PMID:22158541](../papers/22158541.md)
- WES of pancreatic cystic neoplasms identified TP53 mutations in mucinous cystic neoplasms (MCNs) and IPMNs with high-grade dysplasia, consistent with a late-stage role in malignant progression [PMID:22158988](../papers/22158988.md)
- WES of 55 DLBCL tumors identified TP53 mutations in ~8% of cases, associated with chemotherapy resistance and poor clinical outcomes [PMID:22343534](../papers/22343534.md)
- CCLE pharmacogenomic profiling (947 cell lines, 24 drugs) used TP53 mutation status as a key genomic feature in predictive models of drug sensitivity across cancer types [PMID:22460905](../papers/22460905.md)
- Somatic mutations in 62% of basal and 43% of non-basal TNBC; clonally dominant in most tumors [PMID:22495314](../papers/22495314.md)
- Mutational profiling performed across METABRIC subtypes (2,000 tumors); somatic mutations characterised across integrative clusters [PMID:22522925](../papers/22522925.md)
- Recurrently mutated and deleted in prostate cancer; generally absent in SPOP-mutant tumors (mutually exclusive) [PMID:22610119](../papers/22610119.md)
- Referenced in melanoma WGS context; key tumor suppressor altered across melanoma cohort [PMID:22622578](../papers/22622578.md)
- HBx binds p53 directly, inhibiting nucleotide excision repair and transcription-coupled repair; HBx also reduces p53 expression via ASPP1/ASPP2 downregulation and Pin1 interaction in HBV-associated HCC [PMID:22634756](../papers/22634756.md)
- TP53 was among the most frequently mutated genes in breast cancer WES of 100 tumors (Sanger cohort), confirming its role as a major driver [PMID:22722201](../papers/22722201.md)
- TP53 ranked as the most significantly mutated gene in breast cancer WES/WGS of 103 tumors (Broad cohort) [PMID:22722202](../papers/22722202.md)
- TP53 mutations identified in medulloblastoma WGS (PCGP, 37 tumors), particularly in the SHH subgroup [PMID:22722829](../papers/22722829.md)
- TP53 mutations identified in prostate cancer WES of 112 tumors (Michigan cohort), associated with metastatic disease [PMID:22722839](../papers/22722839.md)
- TP53 mutation status in NCI-60 cell lines analyzed as part of CellMiner pharmacogenomics study correlating genomic features with drug sensitivity [PMID:22802077](../papers/22802077.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) — frequent target of mutation and deletion (35%); central to the p53 pathway altered in 78% of samples [PMID:18772890](../papers/18772890.md).
- [CLLSLL](../cancer_types/CLLSLL.md) — 9.1% mutated; 17p co-deletion modifies prognostic impact [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- [APAD](../cancer_types/APAD.md) — defines the aneuploid, worst-prognosis MAAP molecular subtype [PMID:36493333](../papers/36493333.md).
- [CHL](../cancer_types/CHL.md) — 8% mutated [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) — 16% mutated; inferior PFS (HR 3.1, P=0.005); median PFS 4.5 vs 10.5 months [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) / [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) — metastasis-associated driver, ctDNA-detected pathogenic alterations are independently prognostic [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md) [PMID:36357680](../papers/36357680.md) [PMID:37591896](../papers/37591896.md).
- [LCH](../cancer_types/LCH.md) / germ cell tumors — recurrently observed in rare-cancer profiling [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- [BLCA](../cancer_types/BLCA.md) / [UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md) — inversely associated with [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) alterations; co-mutation marks poor [erdafitinib](../drugs/erdafitinib.md) response [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).
- [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) — mutated in 71% of MSK resected PDAC sequenced cohort; not allele-specific across [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) mutants [PMID:39214094](../papers/39214094.md); altered in 78% of KRAS-mutant PDAC in a larger 2,336-patient MSK cohort, with lower rates in other-MAPK-mutant (38%) and MAPK-WT (45%) subtypes [PMID:39753968](../papers/39753968.md).
- [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) — defining mutation of precursor lesions; mutant p53 detectable by CyCIF from p53 signature stage through STIC and invasive cancer; clonal TP53 mutations shared between STICs and concurrent invasive [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).
- [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md) / [MFH](../cancer_types/MFH.md) / [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) — TP53 LOF in 25-50% of RT-sarcomas across these histotypes; infrequent in RT-AS (9%) [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
- [ANGS](../cancer_types/ANGS.md) — TP53 mutations depleted in RT-AS (9%) vs sporadic AS (23%) [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Significantly co-occurs with 17p deletion in CLL [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Co-altered with EGFR/KEAP1/STK11 in ever-metastatic [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) primaries (q<0.001) [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- Mutually exclusive with GNAS-mut and RAS-mut predominant molecular lineages of mucinous appendiceal adenocarcinoma [PMID:36493333](../papers/36493333.md).
- Mutually exclusive with germline [ATM](../genes/ATM.md) loss-of-function mutations in PDAC (P = 4×10⁻¹¹); significantly co-occurs with germline [BRCA1](../genes/BRCA1.md) mutations (P = 4×10⁻⁷) [PMID:39753968](../papers/39753968.md).
- In [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md), TP53 mutations are clonally shared between p53 signatures/STICs and concurrent invasive cancer, consistent with a field-effect or clonal expansion origin [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- In the ibrutinib/venetoclax era, isolated TP53 mutation (without 17p loss) does not confer adverse prognosis in U-CLL [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- Chemoradiotherapy (CRT) upregulates the p53/MDM2 axis in human cervix tumor cells, as observed in ROBIN METEOR Project 1 preliminary data; this upregulation was not accompanied by significant induction of the PDL-1/PD-1 axes [PMID:41941260](../papers/41941260.md).
- TP53 mutation status in [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) identifies patients with markedly inferior PFS on CHOP-based induction and may warrant alternative induction/consolidation strategies [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Open questions

- Discordance between PFS and [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) effect of TP53 in [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) is unexplained (possible salvage-therapy confounding) [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- Mechanistic basis of TP53-mut-associated aneuploidy in appendiceal tumors remains to be determined [PMID:36493333](../papers/36493333.md).
- Allele-specific TP53 inactivation status was not addressed in the RT-sarcoma cohort, leaving bi-allelic vs mono-allelic TP53 impact unclear [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
