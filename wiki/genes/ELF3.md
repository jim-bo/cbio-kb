# ELF3

## Overview

ELF3 (E74-Like ETS Transcription Factor 3) is an ETS family transcription factor. ELF3 LOF mutations are recurrent in gallbladder carcinoma ([GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md)) and represent one of the more frequent driver events in this disease.

## Alterations observed in the corpus
