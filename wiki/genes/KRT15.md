# KRT15

## Overview

KRT15 (Keratin 15) is a type I intermediate filament protein expressed in the basal layer of stratified epithelia and in luminal epithelial lineages. In bladder and upper-tract urothelial biology, KRT15 serves as a marker of basal/stem-like cell identity and is used alongside [KRT5](../genes/KRT5.md) to define basal urothelial subtypes. Differential methylation of KRT15 has been reported in the context of FGFR3-driven urothelial carcinoma subtypes, reflecting epigenetic regulation of luminal-versus-basal cell fate.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Among the top differentially methylated regions (DMRs) distinguishing FGFR3-mutant from FGFR3-WT upper tract urothelial carcinoma ([UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md)); known to be involved in bladder cancer luminal/basal identity alongside KRT5 and [GATA3](../genes/GATA3.md). [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **UTUC / [BLCA](../cancer_types/BLCA.md)**: KRT15 and KRT5 promoter methylation patterns differ between FGFR3-mutant (EpiC-low, luminal) and FGFR3-WT (EpiC-high, basal) UTUC subtypes. [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-regulated with [KRT5](KRT5.md) and [GATA3](GATA3.md) in the luminal/basal identity axis of urothelial carcinoma. [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- KRT15 methylation status is embedded in the EpiC (Epigenetic Cluster) classifier of UTUC; EpiC-low (FGFR3-mutant, hypomethylated) tumors are candidates for [erdafitinib](../drugs/erdafitinib.md)-based FGFR inhibition. [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md)

## Open questions

- Whether KRT15 differential methylation is a driver or a passenger of the FGFR3-mutant luminal-papillary lineage remains unresolved. [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md)
