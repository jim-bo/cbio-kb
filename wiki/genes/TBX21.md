# TBX21

## Overview

TBX21 (T-bet) is a master Th1 transcription factor included in the 20-gene ICR immune signature.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- TBX21 is a component of the 20-gene Immunologic Constant of Rejection (ICR) panel (Th1 signaling genes) used to stratify colon cancer immune subtypes [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) — ICR panel component contributing to ICR-high vs ICR-low prognostic stratification [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with other ICR Th1 genes ([IFNG](../genes/IFNG.md), [CD8A](../genes/CD8A.md), [STAT1](../genes/STAT1.md), [IRF1](../genes/IRF1.md)) as a module [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct targeted therapy reported in the corpus; ICR module may have predictive utility for immunotherapy [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
