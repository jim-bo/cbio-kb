# nimotuzumab

## Overview

Nimotuzumab is a humanized anti-EGFR monoclonal antibody targeting [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md). It is evaluated in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)), where EGFR is overexpressed in a substantial fraction of tumors and drives proliferation, metastasis, and resistance via PI3K/AKT and MAPK pathways.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Nimotuzumab anti-EGFR monoclonal antibody evaluated in NPC where EGFR is overexpressed; listed alongside cetuximab and pimurutamab as EGFR-targeting antibodies, and alongside EGFR ADCs (becotatug vedotin) and bispecific ADCs, as part of the EGFR-targeting therapeutic landscape in R/M NPC [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
