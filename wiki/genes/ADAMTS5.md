# ADAMTS5

## Overview

ADAMTS5 (A Disintegrin And Metalloproteinase with Thrombospondin Motifs 5) encodes a secreted extracellular matrix proteinase involved in aggrecan degradation and tissue remodelling. In the cancer genomics corpus, ADAMTS5 has been identified as a somatically mutated gene in the axon-guidance pathway in adenoid cystic carcinoma ([ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md)), a salivary-gland malignancy characterised by perineural invasion that may be driven in part by disrupted axon-guidance signalling.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Single-tumour somatic mutation in the axon-guidance pathway identified in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACYC); the axon-guidance pathway was disrupted in 56% of tumours in the cohort [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **ACYC (Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma):** ADAMTS5 was among a set of axon-guidance genes somatically mutated in ACYC; the same pathway class is disrupted in pancreatic adenocarcinoma, another cancer with prominent perineural invasion [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-mutated with other axon-guidance pathway genes ([ADAM2](../genes/ADAM2.md), [ADAMTS13](../genes/ADAMTS13.md), [PLXNA1](../genes/PLXNA1.md), [SLIT1](../genes/SLIT1.md), [SRGAP2](../genes/SRGAP2.md), [SRGAP3](../genes/SRGAP3.md), [SEMA3G](../genes/SEMA3G.md)) in ACYC [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting established. Axon-guidance pathway inhibition is proposed as a candidate therapeutic strategy in ACYC [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)

## Open questions

- Whether ADAMTS5 somatic mutations are functionally linked to perineural invasion in ACYC requires functional validation [PMID:26862087](../papers/26862087.md)
