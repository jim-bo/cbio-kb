# CCL13

## Overview

CCL13 (C-C Motif Chemokine Ligand 13; also known as MCP-4) is a monocyte/macrophage chemoattractant chemokine. Together with [CCL2](../genes/CCL2.md), [CCL7](../genes/CCL7.md), and [CCL8](../genes/CCL8.md), it is upregulated in pretreatment melanoma tumors exhibiting innate resistance to anti-PD-1 checkpoint blockade, as part of the IPRES transcriptional resistance signature.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CCL13 was identified as a monocyte/macrophage chemoattractant up-regulated in non-responders to anti-PD-1 therapy in melanoma, part of the IPRES innate resistance transcriptional signature [PMID:26997480](../papers/26997480.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [SKCM](../cancer_types/SKCM.md): CCL13 overexpression in pretreatment melanoma biopsies is enriched in tumors that fail to respond to anti-PD-1 therapy; co-upregulated with CCL2, CCL7, CCL8 [PMID:26997480](../papers/26997480.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with CCL2, CCL7, CCL8, [VEGFA](../genes/VEGFA.md), [VEGFC](../genes/VEGFC.md), [IL10](../genes/IL10.md), and mesenchymal transition transcripts ([AXL](../genes/AXL.md), [ROR2](../genes/ROR2.md), [WNT5A](../genes/WNT5A.md), [LOXL2](../genes/LOXL2.md), [TWIST2](../genes/TWIST2.md)) in IPRES-positive melanoma non-responders [PMID:26997480](../papers/26997480.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- As a component of the IPRES signature, CCL13 overexpression in pretreatment biopsies identifies patients unlikely to respond to anti-PD-1 monotherapy; combination strategies targeting macrophage recruitment are under investigation [PMID:26997480](../papers/26997480.md).

## Open questions

- Prospective validation of CCL13 and the broader IPRES signature in independent cohorts before clinical implementation [PMID:26997480](../papers/26997480.md).
