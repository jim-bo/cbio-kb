# BAIAP2L1

## Overview

BAIAP2L1 (BAI1-Associated Protein 2-Like 1), also known as IRTKS or IRS58, encodes an I-BAR domain-containing adaptor protein involved in membrane curvature sensing and actin remodeling. In cancer, BAIAP2L1 is known as a fusion partner for [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) (FGFR3-BAIAP2L1), an activating rearrangement found in urothelial carcinoma cell lines and tumors that confers constitutive FGFR3 kinase activity and sensitivity to FGFR inhibitors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- FGFR3-BAIAP2L1 fusion present in the SW780 urothelial carcinoma cell line (used for functional validation of FGFR3 inhibitor [erdafitinib](../drugs/erdafitinib.md)); pharmacologic FGFR3 inhibition in this fusion-bearing line upregulates IFNG-response genes ([BST2](../genes/BST2.md)), supporting a cell-autonomous role of FGFR3 signaling in T-cell exclusion [PMID:31278255](../papers/31278255.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md) / urothelial carcinoma: FGFR3-BAIAP2L1 fusion in cell line models; FGFR3 fusions present in the luminal-papillary T-cell-depleted [UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md) subtype [PMID:31278255](../papers/31278255.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- FGFR3-BAIAP2L1 fusion is one of two recurrent FGFR3 fusion types in urothelial models; FGFR3-TACC3 is the other major fusion partner [PMID:31278255](../papers/31278255.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- FGFR3-BAIAP2L1 fusion-bearing SW780 cells respond to erdafitinib (FGFR inhibitor) with upregulation of IFNG-response genes, providing preclinical rationale for FGFR3 inhibition combined with immune checkpoint blockade in FGFR3-rearranged urothelial carcinoma [PMID:31278255](../papers/31278255.md)

## Open questions

- Clinical prevalence of FGFR3-BAIAP2L1 fusions in primary UTUC tumors (as opposed to cell lines) requires prospective confirmation in larger cohorts.
