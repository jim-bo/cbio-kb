# BZW2

## Overview

BZW2 (Basic Leucine Zipper And W2 Domain 2) is a translation regulator. In lung adenocarcinoma, BZW2 was identified as one of five proteins consistently upregulated at the protein level in all tumor samples relative to paired normal adjacent tissues in a large-scale proteogenomic study, nominating it as a potential tumor biomarker.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Protein upregulated in all [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) tumors vs paired normal adjacent tissues (log2 FC >2, FDR <0.01, differential in ≥90% of pairs); among 5 proteins up in all tumors vs NATs in a 110-sample CPTAC proteogenomic cohort [PMID:32649874](../papers/32649874.md)
