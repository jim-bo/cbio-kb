# Liver Hepatocellular Carcinoma / LIHC (TCGA)

## Overview

LIHC is the TCGA cohort identifier for liver hepatocellular carcinoma. The corresponding OncoTree code is [HCC](HCC.md) (Hepatocellular Carcinoma). LIHC encompasses hepatocellular carcinoma broadly, including the rare fibrolamellar carcinoma ([FLC](../cancer_types/FLC.md)) subtype. This page covers findings specifically attributed to the TCGA LIHC cohort.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- TCGA LIHC cohort: included as one of 33 cancer types in the pan-cancer fusion landscape study; subset of the PanCancer Atlas ([lihc_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018](../datasets/lihc_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018.md)).

## Recurrent alterations

- Pan-cancer fusion study (9,624 TCGA samples) identified [DNAJB1](../genes/DNAJB1.md)–[PRKACA](../genes/PRKACA.md) as a liver-only fusion specific to the fibrolamellar carcinoma (FLC) subtype of LIHC, corroborating Dinh et al. 2017; WNK1/WNK2 fusions also detected in LIHC without matching copy-number amplification [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md).

## Subtypes

- **Fibrolamellar carcinoma (FLC):** characterized by the pathognomonic DNAJB1–PRKACA fusion; clinically distinct from conventional [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) (younger patients, lacking cirrhosis). See [FLC](FLC.md).
- **Conventional HCC:** driven by viral hepatitis (HBV/HCV), alcohol, NASH; major drivers include [TP53](../genes/TP53.md), [CTNNB1](../genes/CTNNB1.md), [TERT](../genes/TERT.md).
