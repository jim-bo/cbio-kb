# CPM

## Overview

CPM encodes carboxypeptidase M, a membrane-bound metallocarboxypeptidase. In the context of cancer genomics, CPM has been observed as a focal amplification target in glioblastoma multiforme ([GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md)).
