# CLCN6

## Overview

CLCN6 (Chloride Voltage-Gated Channel 6) encodes a voltage-gated chloride channel. In cancer genomics, it has been identified as a novel 5' fusion partner for [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) in pilocytic astrocytoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel 5' fusion partner for BRAF in pilocytic astrocytoma (WGS cohort n=96); functional dissection of this specific BRAF fusion partner not reported [PMID:23817572](../papers/23817572.md)
