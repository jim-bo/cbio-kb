# EPCAM

## Overview

EPCAM (Epithelial Cell Adhesion Molecule) is a cell-surface glycoprotein that marks epithelial progenitor and stem-like cells. In hepatocellular carcinoma ([HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)), EPCAM expression defines a stem-cell/progenitor subpopulation with distinct biological behavior and adverse prognosis, and is part of a validated prognostic immunohistochemical panel.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- EPCAM, [KRT19](../genes/KRT19.md), and [GPC3](../genes/GPC3.md) form a stem-cell/progenitor-cell signature; expression is prognostic in subsets of [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)
- EPCAM overexpression marks the S2 progenitor-cell proliferation subclass in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) (~50% of tumors), associated with AFP and [IGF2](../genes/IGF2.md) co-expression, HBV aetiology, and poor outcomes. [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md)
- Germline loss of EPCAM (exons 8–9 deletion) confers Lynch syndrome gastric cancer risk; included in the recommended multigene germline panel for familial non-hereditary gastric cancer. [PMID:24816255](../papers/24816255.md)
- EPCAM is an epithelial surface antigen targeted by autologous CAR-T therapy in [NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md) (n=12, ORR 16.7%) in platinum-refractory recurrent/metastatic disease. [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md)
- Upregulated as a stemness marker in [GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md) cells downstream of SEMA7A/ITGB1 signaling; used as a readout of cancer stem-like cell induction by CAF-derived [SEMA7A](../genes/SEMA7A.md) [PMID:24997986](../papers/24997986.md)
- EPCAM is a biliary marker elevated in P1 (combined-type cHCC-ICC, ICC-like) transcriptomic cluster among 133 pan-Asia cHCC-ICC cases [PMID:31130341](../papers/31130341.md)
- Canonical epithelial marker anchoring the proteome-wide EMT-like axis on PC1 in the CCLE pan-cancer proteome (375 cancer cell lines); contrasted with [VIM](../genes/VIM.md) (mesenchymal) [PMID:31978347](../papers/31978347.md)
- EPCAM showed variable/non-uniform expression as part of an EMT marker panel assessed in SCLC-P (POU2F3-driven) PDX models in an MSK [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) biobank study. [PMID:35440124](../papers/35440124.md)
- No pathogenic EPCAM alterations were identified in the MSK endometrial cancer cohort; tested as part of Lynch syndrome germline screening in MMR-D EC [PMID:35849120](../papers/35849120.md)
