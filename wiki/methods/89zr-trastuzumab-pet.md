# 89Zr-trastuzumab PET

## Overview

89Zr-labeled [trastuzumab](../drugs/trastuzumab.md) PET/CT is a non-invasive whole-body imaging modality that measures HER2 receptor expression and heterogeneity in solid tumors. Avid lesions (SUV >= 10) indicate high uniform HER2 expression [PMID:37406106](../papers/37406106.md).

## Used by

- [PMID:37406106](../papers/37406106.md) — 89Zr-trastuzumab PET/CT was performed on 8 patients with HER2-positive metastatic esophagogastric cancer in a phase II trial (NCT02954536); all 15 intensely avid lesions (SUV >= 10) decreased in size after a single dose of [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md) + trastuzumab vs. only 9/32 non-intensely avid lesions (P = 2.4e-6); all 4 patients with >= 1 intensely avid lesion achieved 6-month PFS [PMID:37406106](../papers/37406106.md).

## Notes

- Uniform HER2 IHC 3+ and intense 89Zr-trastuzumab PET avidity (SUV >= 10) are complementary biomarkers for identifying patients most likely to benefit from HER2-directed therapy [PMID:37406106](../papers/37406106.md).
