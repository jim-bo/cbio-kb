# buparlisib

## Overview

Buparlisib (BKM-120) is an oral pan-class I PI3K inhibitor targeting PIK3CA, PIK3CB, PIK3CD, and PIK3CG. It was investigated clinically across multiple solid tumors but did not reach regulatory approval due to toxicity concerns (CNS effects, hepatotoxicity).

## Evidence in the corpus

- AR-amplified MSK-PCa2 CRPC patient-derived organoid (PTEN loss + PIK3R1 mutation) was sensitive to buparlisib (BKM-120) in vitro; results support pan-PI3K inhibition as an approach in CRPC with PI3K-pathway co-activation alongside AR amplification [PMID:25201530](../papers/25201530.md)
