# B4GALT4

## Overview

B4GALT4 (beta-1,4-galactosyltransferase 4) encodes a Golgi-localized enzyme that synthesizes beta-1,4-galactosyl linkages on N-glycan and glycolipid substrates. Like other B4GALT family members, B4GALT4 contributes to cell-surface glycan composition and has been found upregulated in pancreatic cancer, nominating it as a potential therapeutic target via glycosylation-enzyme inhibition.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Elevated at the protein level in PDAC vs paired NAT in CPTAC PDAC proteogenomics (140 treatment-naive tumors); elevation validated by DIA mass spectrometry. Protein-level upregulation was not mirrored at the transcript level. Listed alongside [ST6GAL1](../genes/ST6GAL1.md), [ST3GAL1](../genes/ST3GAL1.md), [FUT3](../genes/FUT3.md), [FUT11](../genes/FUT11.md), [B4GALT1](../genes/B4GALT1.md), B3GALT5, and ST6GALNAC1 as dysregulated glycosylation enzymes. [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **PDAC ([PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)):** Protein upregulation observed in CPTAC cohort (n=140); co-elevated with B4GALT1 and other sialyl-/fucosyltransferases; proposed as part of a glycan-capping blockade therapeutic strategy. [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-elevated with FUT3, FUT11, B4GALT1, ST6GAL1, ST3GAL1 in PDAC tumors; FUT3 upregulation enriched in C1 (classical) subtype. [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- Sialyl-/fucosyltransferase inhibition strategy targeting B4GALT4 (alongside B4GALT1, ST6GAL1, ST3GAL1, FUT3, FUT11) proposed in PDAC; systemic sialylation blockade caused liver/kidney toxicity in mouse models, highlighting selectivity challenges. [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)

## Open questions

- Selective B4GALT4 inhibitors have not been developed; on-target toxicity of broad galactosyltransferase inhibition is a concern.
- Post-transcriptional or post-translational mechanism underlying protein-only elevation in PDAC is unknown.
