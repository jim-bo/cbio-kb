# pictilisib

## Overview

Pictilisib (GDC-0941) is a pan-PI3K inhibitor that targets all class I PI3K isoforms ([PIK3CA](../genes/PIK3CA.md), [PIK3CB](../genes/PIK3CB.md), [PIK3CD](../genes/PIK3CD.md), [PIK3CG](../genes/PIK3CG.md)). It was evaluated in multiple solid-tumor indications including ER+ breast cancer. A page also exists for this compound under its research code at [gdc-0941](gdc-0941.md). The FERGI trial investigated pictilisib in combination with [fulvestrant](fulvestrant.md) in endocrine-refractory ER+ metastatic breast cancer.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Referenced via the FERGI trial (Krop et al. Lancet Oncol 2016) in which pictilisib + [fulvestrant](fulvestrant.md) showed preferential benefit in the progesterone receptor-positive (PR+) / luminal A subset of ER+ metastatic breast cancer, supporting luminal A subtype as a predictive biomarker for pan-PI3K inhibitor + endocrine therapy combinations [PMID:31552290](../papers/31552290.md).

## Resistance mechanisms

- PR-negative (luminal B) tumors appeared less responsive to pictilisib + [fulvestrant](../drugs/fulvestrant.md) in the FERGI trial, suggesting intrinsic subtype-dependent resistance.

## Cancer types (linked)

- ER+/HER2- metastatic breast cancer.
