# CCDC74A

## Overview

CCDC74A (Coiled-Coil Domain Containing 74A), previously reported in the literature under the alias KIAA1551, was identified as a significantly mutated gene in African American colorectal cancers, suggesting an ethnicity-associated role in CRC.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a significantly mutated gene (reported as KIAA1551 in the original paper) in African American colorectal cancers in both discovery and validation cohorts (103 AA vs. 129 Caucasian CRC samples); part of the 20-gene panel preferentially mutated in AA CRCs [PMID:25583493](../papers/25583493.md)
