# RAI14

## Overview

RAI14 (retinoic acid-induced protein 14) encodes a cytoskeletal-associated protein involved in cell adhesion and actin organisation. It has been identified as a gene with alterations in acral melanoma genomic profiling.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Listed among genes with alterations in the acral melanoma (ALM) integrated genomic analysis cohort (34 patients; WGS + RNA-seq + SNP array) [PMID:28373299](../papers/28373299.md).
