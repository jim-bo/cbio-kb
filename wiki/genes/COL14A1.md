# COL14A1

## Overview

COL14A1 encodes Collagen Type XIV Alpha 1 Chain, a fibrillar-associated collagen. It was identified as a MutSigCV-significant gene in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (PDAC), though its functional role in PDA biology requires further validation.
