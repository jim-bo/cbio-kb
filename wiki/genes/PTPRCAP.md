# PTPRCAP

## Overview

PTPRCAP (protein tyrosine phosphatase receptor type C associated protein) is a transmembrane adaptor protein that associates with CD45 (PTPRC) and modulates its phosphatase activity. In cancer genomics, PTPRCAP has been identified as a target of methylation-driven cis-regulatory effects in lung adenocarcinoma, with promoter methylation suppressing its expression in a coordinated fashion across RNA, protein, and phosphoprotein layers.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a methylation cis-effect target with [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) relevance in the CPTAC proteogenomic cohort (n=110 treatment-naïve [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) tumors); PTPRCAP methylation was part of 120 methylation-driven cis-effects coordinated across RNA, protein, and phosphoprotein, with promoter-region probes supporting the silencing [PMID:32649874](../papers/32649874.md)
