# NRXN3

## Overview

NRXN3 (Neurexin 3) encodes a presynaptic cell-adhesion molecule belonging to the neurexin family. In cancer genomics, NRXN3 has been identified as a focal deletion target in clear cell renal cell carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Highlighted as a downstream pathway and SCNA feature in TieDIE / focal-deletion analyses of ccRCC (TCGA 450-case multi-platform dataset) [PMID:23792563](../papers/23792563.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Clear cell renal cell carcinoma (ccRCC): focal deletion identified in TCGA analysis [PMID:23792563](../papers/23792563.md)
