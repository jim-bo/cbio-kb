# RBM46

## Overview

RBM46 (RNA Binding Motif Protein 46) is an RNA-binding protein. It was identified as a MutSig/inactivation-bias hit in aggressive cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma, though with low allelic fractions suggesting it may be a passenger rather than a driver.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- RBM46 identified as a MutSig/inactivation-bias hit in aggressive cSCC (39 patients, whole exome sequencing); notably had low allelic fractions suggesting it may be a passenger rather than a driver [PMID:25303977](../papers/25303977.md)
