# LBL-007

## Overview

LBL-007 is an investigational anti-LAG-3 monoclonal antibody targeting [LAG3](../genes/LAG3.md), an inhibitory receptor on exhausted T cells. It is evaluated in combination with toripalimab in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- LBL-007 + [toripalimab](../drugs/toripalimab.md) in R/M NPC (NCT05102006, n=30): ORR 33.3% in ICI-naïve (n=12) and 11.8% in ICI-refractory (n=17); median PFS 10.8 months; median DOR 15 months [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
