# STAT1

## Overview

STAT1 is a Th1 signaling transcription factor included in the 20-gene Immunologic Constant of Rejection (ICR) immune signature.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- STAT1 is a component of the 20-gene ICR panel (Th1 signaling genes) used to stratify colon cancer immune subtypes [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
- STAT1 is an IFN-stimulated gene (ISG) upregulated from the earliest [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) precursor stage (p53.I incidental fimbriae) onward in fallopian tube epithelium, alongside [IFITM1](../genes/IFITM1.md), [IRF7](../genes/IRF7.md), [IRF9](../genes/IRF9.md), [ISG15](../genes/ISG15.md), [TAP1](../genes/TAP1.md), and [MX1](../genes/MX1.md); upregulation is activated by the cGAS–STING–[TBK1](../genes/TBK1.md) axis triggered by micronuclear rupture in pre-malignant cells. [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md)
- STAT1 is upregulated as part of the JAK-STAT pathway in pan-cancer iClusters C3 (mesenchymal/immune), C20 (stromal/immune), C14 (LUAD), and C10 (pan-SCC), suggesting therapeutic vulnerability to JAK-STAT inhibitors; IDH1-driven gliomas specifically show reduced STAT1 expression and consequently decreased CXCL10 and immune infiltrate [PMID:29625048](../papers/29625048.md)
- IDH1-driven gliomas exhibit lower STAT1 expression, resulting in decreased CXCL10 and reduced immune infiltrate within the immunologically quiet C5 immune subtype (ANOVA P < 2e-8 for macrophage vs CD8 composition differences in ATRX/TP53/IDH1-driven tumors) [PMID:29625049](../papers/29625049.md)
- Truncation mutation observed in a POLE TMB-H/APM-L endometrial carcinoma sample; loss of STAT1 alongside JAK1 frameshift mutations is proposed as a candidate immune-evasion mechanism [PMID:32059776](../papers/32059776.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) — ICR panel component; contributes to ICR-high vs ICR-low prognostic stratification (HR 0.54, P=0.0095) [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
- High-grade serous ovarian carcinoma ([HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md)) precursor lesions — STAT1 is a constituent of the interferon-response gene program activated in p53 signatures, STICs, and invasive [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md); upregulation begins in the earliest pre-malignant fallopian tube lesions (p53.I). [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with other ICR Th1 genes ([IFNG](../genes/IFNG.md), [TBX21](../genes/TBX21.md), [CD8A](../genes/CD8A.md), [IRF1](../genes/IRF1.md)) as a module [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct targeted therapy reported in the corpus; ICR module may have predictive utility for immunotherapy [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Open questions

- None specific to STAT1 in the corpus.
