# dovitinib

## Overview

Dovitinib (TKI258) is a multi-targeted kinase inhibitor active against FGFR1/2/3, VEGFR1/2/3, PDGFR, [KIT](../genes/KIT.md), [FLT3](../genes/FLT3.md), and [CSF1R](../genes/CSF1R.md). It was investigated in clinical trials for renal cell carcinoma and other solid tumors but did not achieve regulatory approval. In the UCLA sarcoma PDTO screen it was tested in the >400-compound panel and showed subtype-specific differential sensitivity.

## Evidence in the corpus

- In the UCLA sarcoma PDTO biobank ([sarcoma_ucla_2024](../datasets/sarcoma_ucla_2024.md), n=194 specimens), [leiomyosarcoma](../cancer_types/LMS.md) PDTOs were more sensitive to dovitinib (p=0.0068) compared to the pan-sarcoma average, consistent with prior negative trials in [LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md) with less selective agents. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
- In patient SARC0133 ([RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md)) who harbored an [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) gain on chromosome 8, the PDTO was a top responder to [infigratinib](../drugs/infigratinib.md) (an FGFR-selective inhibitor) but showed minimal response to dovitinib, demonstrating intra-class drug differentiation within FGFR inhibitors. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
- Three likely activating [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) mutations identified in adenoid cystic carcinoma ([ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md)) prompted nomination of FGFR inhibitors including dovitinib (NCT01524692 trial) as a therapeutic strategy in this disease [PMID:23778141](../papers/23778141.md).

## Resistance mechanisms

- [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) genomic gain (chromosome 8) does not predict sensitivity to dovitinib in sarcoma, despite predicting sensitivity to the more selective FGFR inhibitor [infigratinib](../drugs/infigratinib.md) — illustrating that multi-kinase inhibitor promiscuity may dilute on-target efficacy. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md) — leiomyosarcoma; significantly more sensitive to dovitinib than pan-sarcoma average (p=0.0068).
- [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) — rhabdomyosarcoma; SARC0133 FGFR1-gain specimen resistant to dovitinib despite [infigratinib](../drugs/infigratinib.md) sensitivity.
