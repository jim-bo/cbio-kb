# cyclophosphamide

## Overview

Nitrogen mustard alkylating chemotherapy; backbone of CHOP-based lymphoma regimens and pediatric sarcoma protocols.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Component of curative-intent CHOP/CHOEP/EPOCH/BV-CH(E)P regimens used in 132 nodal [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) patients at MSK; [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation (HR 3.1, P=.005) identified patients with inferior PFS on cyclophosphamide-containing induction [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
- Used in extremity rhabdomyosarcoma chemotherapy backbones across COG/institutional protocols spanning 2000–2021 [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- Cyclophosphamide appears in the UCLA sarcoma PDTO drug library ([sarcoma_ucla_2024](../datasets/sarcoma_ucla_2024.md)) and is listed among NCCN-recommended regimens for [rhabdomyosarcoma](../cancer_types/RMS.md) identified in the functional screen (alongside [doxorubicin](../drugs/doxorubicin.md) and [vinorelbine](../drugs/vinorelbine.md)). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
- Component of the FC/FCR chemoimmunotherapy regimens evaluated in the CLL8 trial (n=538 CLL patients); mutated [TP53](../genes/TP53.md), [SF3B1](../genes/SF3B1.md), and novel driver [RPS15](../genes/RPS15.md) each predicted shorter PFS, and presence of any subclonal driver predicted shorter PFS (HR 1.6) in both FC and FCR arms [PMID:26466571](../papers/26466571.md).
- Referenced as a component of high-dose conditioning regimens for allogeneic SCT — the treatment modality with the largest survival benefit in the WashU AML/MDS [decitabine](../drugs/decitabine.md) trial (N=116); allogeneic SCT vs no SCT was the dominant Cox survival predictor (P<0.001) independent of [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) status [PMID:27959731](../papers/27959731.md)

## Resistance mechanisms

- [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations and 17p deletions associate with markedly inferior PFS on CHOP-based induction in [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
