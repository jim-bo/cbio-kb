# GEN1

## Overview

GEN1 is a Holliday junction resolvase involved in homologous recombination DNA repair. In cancer genomics, GEN1 was identified as a single-case mutation in sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma with suspected germline origin.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- GEN1 frameshift mutation observed in a single sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma case; suspected germline based on allele fraction (not validated against matched normal tissue) [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)

## Cancer types (linked)
