# NDRG1

## Overview

NDRG1 (N-Myc Downstream Regulated 1) is a stress-response protein involved in cell differentiation and tumor suppression. In prostate cancer, NDRG1 serves as an alternative androgen-regulated 5' fusion partner in ETS-family gene fusions, analogous to [TMPRSS2](../genes/TMPRSS2.md) and [SLC45A3](../genes/SLC45A3.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Alternative androgen-regulated 5' fusion partner in prostate cancer ETS-family fusions (alongside [SLC45A3](../genes/SLC45A3.md) and [TMPRSS2](../genes/TMPRSS2.md)) in the TCGA prostate adenocarcinoma cohort (n=333) [PMID:26544944](../papers/26544944.md)
