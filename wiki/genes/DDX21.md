# DDX21

## Overview

DDX21 (DEAD-Box Helicase 21) is an RNA helicase involved in rRNA processing and ribosome biogenesis. It has been identified as one of the most uniformly upregulated proteins in colorectal cancer compared to normal adjacent tissue, making it a candidate biomarker and potential therapeutic target. Its overexpression may reflect enhanced ribosome biogenesis associated with cancer cell proliferation.
