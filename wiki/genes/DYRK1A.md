# DYRK1A

## Overview

DYRK1A (Dual Specificity Tyrosine Phosphorylation Regulated Kinase 1A) is a serine/threonine/tyrosine kinase involved in cell-cycle regulation, DNA damage response, and transcriptional control. It has been identified as a novel driver gene in chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL), where it is implicated in the DNA damage response pathway.
