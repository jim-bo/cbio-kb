# GPR149

## Overview

GPR149 (G Protein-Coupled Receptor 149) is an orphan G protein-coupled receptor. It was identified as a significantly mutated gene in African American colorectal cancers, forming part of the ethnicity-enriched mutational panel in CRC.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a significantly mutated gene in the 20-gene AA-enriched CRC panel (significantly mutated in both discovery and validation cohorts) [PMID:25583493](../papers/25583493.md)
