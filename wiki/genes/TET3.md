# TET3

## Overview

TET3 (Tet Methylcytosine Dioxygenase 3) is a DNA demethylation enzyme that converts 5-methylcytosine to 5-hydroxymethylcytosine. Somatic mutations with recurrent hotspots in TET3 were identified in colorectal cancer, representing one of the first reports of TET family mutations in CRC.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Somatic mutations with recurrent hotspots in colorectal cancer; among the first reports of TET family somatic mutations in CRC [PMID:22895193](../papers/22895193.md)
