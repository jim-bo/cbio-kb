# SMARCE1

## Overview

SMARCE1 is a subunit of the SWI/SNF chromatin remodeling complex that contains an HMG-box DNA-binding domain. In adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC), SMARCE1 harbors a missense mutation (Y73C) in its HMG-box, predicted to impair DNA binding and suggesting a loss-of-function mechanism consistent with its role as a chromatin remodeling complex component.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- HMG-box missense Y73C; frequency 2%; predicted loss of DNA-binding function; SWI/SNF complex subunit in adenoid cystic carcinoma. [PMID:23685749](../papers/23685749.md)
- Putative miR-200a target in extra-cranial MRT, suggesting miRNA-mediated dysregulation of SWI/SNF complex members [PMID:26977886](../papers/26977886.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md) (Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma)**: Missense mutation in HMG-box domain; part of the broader SWI/SNF subunit mutation landscape in ACC alongside [SMARCA2](../genes/SMARCA2.md) and [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md). [PMID:23685749](../papers/23685749.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Mutated in the same tumor type as other SWI/SNF complex members [SMARCA2](../genes/SMARCA2.md) and [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md) in ACC; SWI/SNF complex mutations collectively affect 12/24 cases in the extended ACC cohort. [PMID:23685749](../papers/23685749.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic implication reported; SWI/SNF complex vulnerability (synthetic lethality) is an area of active investigation across cancers. [PMID:23685749](../papers/23685749.md)

## Open questions

- Whether SMARCE1 Y73C is functionally inactivating in an ACC cellular context has not been directly tested. [PMID:23685749](../papers/23685749.md)
