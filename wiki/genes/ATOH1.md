# ATOH1

## Overview

ATOH1 (Atonal BHLH Transcription Factor 1) is a basic helix-loop-helix (bHLH) transcription factor that functions as a master regulator of neuroendocrine differentiation. In the context of small cell lung cancer ([SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md)), ATOH1 is expressed at low levels in the non-neuroendocrine SCLC-P (POU2F3-driven) subtype, serving as a transcriptional marker distinguishing NE from non-NE tumor subtypes.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ATOH1 expression is low in SCLC-P (POU2F3-driven) subtype PDX/CDX models, in contrast to neuroendocrine [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) subtypes (SCLC-A and SCLC-N); identified in multi-omic profiling of 46 [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) PDX/CDX models from 33 MSK patients [PMID:35440124](../papers/35440124.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md):** ATOH1 serves as a low-expression marker in the non-neuroendocrine SCLC-P subtype; expression pattern contrasts with high-ASCL1 and high-NEUROD1 neuroendocrine subtypes [PMID:35440124](../papers/35440124.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- ATOH1 low expression co-occurs with high [REST](../genes/REST.md), high [BACH2](../genes/BACH2.md), and low [DLL3](../genes/DLL3.md) in SCLC-P; contrasts with ASCL1-high (SCLC-A) and NEUROD1-high (SCLC-N) subtypes [PMID:35440124](../papers/35440124.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported in the corpus. ATOH1 expression pattern may help subtype [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) tumors for clinical trial enrollment.

## Open questions

- Whether ATOH1 low expression is a driver or consequence of POU2F3-driven non-NE differentiation in SCLC-P remains to be established.
