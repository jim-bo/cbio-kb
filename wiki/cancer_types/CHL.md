# Classical Hodgkin Lymphoma (CHL)

## Overview

Classical Hodgkin Lymphoma (cHL) is a Hodgkin Lymphoma (parent HL) characterized by Hodgkin and Reed-Sternberg (HRS) cells.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [chl_sccc_2023](../datasets/chl_sccc_2023.md): 25 cHL cases profiled by WGS on FACS-isolated HRS cells plus 36 additional cases by WES, for a combined 61-patient driver-gene cohort [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- Two age peaks represented: ped/AYA (ages 7–27, n=19 WGS) and older adults (ages 55–85, n=6 WGS); 22/25 samples from diagnosis, 3/25 from relapse [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- 26 mutated driver genes/hotspots identified; 8 newly described in cHL [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- Top drivers: [SOCS1](../genes/SOCS1.md) 62%, [TNFAIP3](../genes/TNFAIP3.md) 36%, [B2M](../genes/B2M.md) ~32–33%, [ITPKB](../genes/ITPKB.md) 28%, [GNA13](../genes/GNA13.md) 26%, [STAT6](../genes/STAT6.md) 20%, [BCL7A](../genes/BCL7A.md) 18%, [NFKBIE](../genes/NFKBIE.md) 15%, [PTPN1](../genes/PTPN1.md) 8%, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) 8%, [XPO1](../genes/XPO1.md) 8% [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- APOBEC mutagenesis (SBS2/SBS13) highly prevalent, exceeding non-Hodgkin lymphoma and CLL (P<0.00001) [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- AID off-target activity concentrated on noncoding footprints of [BCL7A](../genes/BCL7A.md), [SOCS1](../genes/SOCS1.md), [TMSB4X](../genes/TMSB4X.md), [IL4R](../genes/IL4R.md), and [ITPKB](../genes/ITPKB.md) [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- Complex SVs including chromothripsis; SV breakpoints enriched for RAG recombination motifs [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
- High ploidy typically achieved via multiple independent chromosomal gains including whole-genome duplication (WGD) rather than a single event [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Subtypes

- Ped/AYA cases carried significantly higher genome-wide SBS+indel burden than older adults (median 6,270 vs 3,723, Wilcoxon P=0.0033) [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- Paper does not evaluate specific drug responses; treatment implications are inferential only [PMID:36723991](../papers/36723991.md).
