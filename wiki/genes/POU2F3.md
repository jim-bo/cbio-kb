# POU2F3

## Overview

POU2F3 is a transcription factor that defines a distinct molecular subtype of small cell lung cancer (SCLC-P subtype). It is expressed in tuft cells and marks a non-neuroendocrine, chemosensory [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) subtype.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Absent or rare in aSCLC (atypical SCLC); aSCLC is uniformly ASCL1-dominant and lacks [NEUROD1](../genes/NEUROD1.md), POU2F3, and [YAP1](../genes/YAP1.md) subtype expression; aSCLC cohort n=20 (MSKCC) [PMID:39185963](../papers/39185963.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- aSCLC (atypical SCLC) — POU2F3 subtype absent [PMID:39185963](../papers/39185963.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Mutually exclusive with [ASCL1](../genes/ASCL1.md) dominance in aSCLC [PMID:39185963](../papers/39185963.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- None flagged in the corpus for aSCLC.

## Open questions

- Whether any aSCLC cases have mixed ASCL1/POU2F3 expression is not addressed in the corpus.
