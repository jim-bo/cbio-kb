# PDGFA

## Overview

PDGFA (Platelet Derived Growth Factor Subunit A) encodes a ligand for the PDGF receptor tyrosine kinases. It was identified as a recurrently amplified locus in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma by GISTIC analysis.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- In pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma ([PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)), PDGFA (7p22.3) was identified as a recurrently amplified locus by GISTIC analysis in the 109-case microdissected whole-exome sequencing cohort. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md): recurrent focal amplification identified by GISTIC. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- No specific co-occurrence or mutual exclusivity patterns reported in the corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- PDGF-receptor pathway inhibitors (e.g., PDGFR family inhibitors) are among FDA-targetable alterations noted in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) (PDGFR family ~3% of cases) in related corpus papers.

## Open questions

- Functional significance of PDGFA amplification in [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) as a driver vs. bystander event remains to be validated.
