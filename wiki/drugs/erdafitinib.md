# erdafitinib

## Overview

Oral pan-FGFR tyrosine kinase inhibitor targeting FGFR1/[FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md)/[FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md)/FGFR4; FDA-approved for FGFR2/3-altered metastatic urothelial carcinoma ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- Real-world MSK cohort of 32 metastatic urothelial carcinoma patients treated 8/2019–7/2022: ORR 40% (12/30 evaluable), median PFS 2.8 months (95% CI 2.6–5.4), median [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) 6.6 months (95% CI 4.6–17.3) — activity modest relative to BLC2001 ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).
- Tolerability-limited: dose reductions in 38% (12/32), interruptions in 50% (16/32); TEAEs included hyperphosphatemia 84%, fatigue 59%, mucositis 47% (grade >=3 16%), PPE 38% (grade >=3 9%), dysgeusia 31% ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).
- Archival primary vs metastatic [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) status discordant in 26% (7/27) of paired cases, arguing for metastatic-site or cfDNA profiling to guide erdafitinib selection ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).
- Baseline [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) co-alteration associated with lower ORR (22%, 2/9) and enriched among primary progressors ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).

## Resistance mechanisms

- Acquired second-site [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) kinase-domain mutations detected in serial cfDNA: V553M and N540S (plus K650M, R669G); in silico modeling supports V553M as a gatekeeper allele allosterically blocking erdafitinib binding ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).
- Acquired [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations in 5/27 on-treatment cfDNA series as putative resistance driver ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).
- Acquired activating [AKT1](../genes/AKT1.md) mutation in 1/27 as putative bypass resistance ([PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md)).
