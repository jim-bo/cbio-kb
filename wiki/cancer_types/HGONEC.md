# High-Grade Neuroendocrine Carcinoma of the Ovary (HGONEC)

## Overview

High-Grade Neuroendocrine Carcinoma of the Ovary (HGONEC) is a rare, aggressive malignancy under Ovarian Cancer (OOVC) in OncoTree. It may present as a pure neuroendocrine tumor or as part of a mixed neuroendocrine-non-neuroendocrine neoplasm (MiNEN).

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [pog570_bcgsc_2020](../datasets/pog570_bcgsc_2020.md) — 1 ovarian MiNEN patient (PN19; 60% adenocarcinoma / 40% neuroendocrine, classified as MNET/HGONEC) within 28-patient metastatic NEN WGTA cohort. [PMID:24326773](../papers/24326773.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- The single ovarian MiNEN (PN19) in the POG cohort carried [MYC](../genes/MYC.md) amplification, [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) gain-of-function mutation, [SMAD4](../genes/SMAD4.md) loss, [CDK8](../genes/CDK8.md) and [FLT1](../genes/FLT1.md) amplifications, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) loss, and [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) loss. It clustered with TCGA colorectal adenocarcinomas on transcriptome analysis — the only case not clustering with other NENs. High PD-L1 ([CD274](../genes/CD274.md)) expression and high [DLL3](../genes/DLL3.md) expression were identified as potential therapeutic targets. [PMID:24326773](../papers/24326773.md)

## Subtypes

- MiNEN (Mixed neuroendocrine-non-neuroendocrine neoplasm) with adenocarcinoma component.

## Therapeutic landscape

- Immune-checkpoint inhibitors (anti-PD-1/PD-L1) supported by high CD274/PDCD1 expression; DLL3 inhibitors supported by high DLL3 expression; no standard systemic therapy; patient died shortly after biopsy. [PMID:24326773](../papers/24326773.md)
