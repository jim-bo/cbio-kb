# CCNB1

## Overview

CCNB1 (Cyclin B1) encodes a regulatory protein that controls cell cycle progression at the G2/M checkpoint. Elevated CCNB1 expression is associated with aggressive tumor phenotypes and is frequently observed in genomically unstable cancers.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- High RPPA protein expression in POLE-ultramutated and cell-cycle-deregulated endometrial carcinoma subgroups [PMID:23636398](../papers/23636398.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Endometrial carcinoma: elevated RPPA expression in POLE-ultramutated subgroup [PMID:23636398](../papers/23636398.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Elevated alongside [AURKA](../genes/AURKA.md) and [ASNS](../genes/ASNS.md) in POLE-ultramutated endometrial subgroup [PMID:23636398](../papers/23636398.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic data in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Whether CCNB1 overexpression is a driver or passenger event in POLE-ultramutated tumors remains unclear.
