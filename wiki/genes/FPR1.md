# FPR1

## Overview

FPR1 (Formyl Peptide Receptor 1) is a G protein-coupled receptor involved in innate immune signaling. It was identified as a significantly mutated gene in small cell lung cancer ([SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md)), though its functional significance in that context remains unclear.
