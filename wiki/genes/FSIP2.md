# FSIP2

## Overview

FSIP2 (fibrous sheath interacting protein 2) is a large scaffold protein normally expressed in sperm flagella. Somatic mutations in FSIP2 are significantly enriched in metastatic breast cancer relative to early breast cancer and are part of an 8-gene prognostic signature associated with a 2-fold increase in death hazard.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Mutations more frequent in metastatic breast cancer ([BRCA](../cancer_types/BRCA.md)) vs early breast cancer (eBC) at FDR<0.01 (one of eight metastasis-enriched genes: [ESR1](../genes/ESR1.md), FSIP2, AGRN, [FRAS1](../genes/FRAS1.md), [IGFN1](../genes/IGFN1.md), [EDC4](../genes/EDC4.md), [OSBPL3](../genes/OSBPL3.md), [PALB2](../genes/PALB2.md)) in 216 mBC whole-exome sequencing samples [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).
- Patients with ≥1 mutation in the 8-gene metastatic-enrichment set had a 2-fold increase in death hazard (HR=1.97, 95% CI 1.34–2.89, p=0.001) on multivariate analysis [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [BRCA](../cancer_types/BRCA.md) — FSIP2 is part of the 8-gene metastasis-enriched mutation signature; prognostic significance requires independent validation [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-enriched with ESR1, AGRN, FRAS1, IGFN1, EDC4, OSBPL3, and PALB2 as part of the metastasis-enriched mutation signature in mBC [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported in the corpus [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).

## Open questions

- Whether FSIP2 mutations are functional drivers of metastasis or reflect passenger accumulation is not established; the 8-gene survival association was not corrected for mutational load [PMID:28027327](../papers/28027327.md).
