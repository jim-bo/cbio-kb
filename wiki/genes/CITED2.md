# CITED2

## Overview

CITED2 (Cbp/p300-Interacting Transactivator With Glu/Asp-Rich Carboxy-Terminal Domain 2) encodes a transcriptional co-activator that interacts with CBP/p300 and modulates HIF-1α and other transcription factors. In the pediatric cancer context, CITED2 has been identified as a fusion partner in rare sarcoma entities.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CITED2–MGA fusion identified by comprehensive WGS+transcriptome sequencing (cWGTS) in a round-cell sarcoma NOS patient; described as an undescribed fusion in a pediatric/rare cancer cohort (n=114, MSK prospective study NCT01775072) [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Round-cell sarcoma NOS**: CITED2–MGA fusion identified as a novel undescribed fusion event [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Fusion partner MGA is a MAX dimerization protein with roles in MYC pathway regulation; the CITED2–MGA fusion joins a transcriptional co-activator with a MYC-network component [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No established therapeutic implications for the CITED2–MGA fusion; discovery context (cWGTS vs panel sequencing) highlights that novel fusions in rare sarcomas require WGS for detection [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)

## Open questions

- Functional consequence and oncogenic mechanism of the CITED2–MGA fusion in round-cell sarcoma remain to be characterized [PMID:35585047](../papers/35585047.md)
