# IL12B

## Overview

IL12B encodes the p40 subunit shared by IL-12 and IL-23, key cytokines driving Th1 differentiation and cytotoxic immunity.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Th1-signaling component of the 20-gene ICR signature used in the AC-ICAM colon cancer atlas [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
