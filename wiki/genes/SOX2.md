# SOX2

## Overview

SOX2 (SRY-Box Transcription Factor 2) is a master transcription factor for neural stem cell self-renewal and pluripotency. In glioma, SOX2 is a key marker of the neural stem/progenitor compartment and is part of the glioblastoma cancer stem cell signature. In oligodendroglioma, SOX2 co-defines the rare proliferative stem/progenitor subpopulation at the apex of the tumor developmental hierarchy.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SOX2 is among the top-ranking genes of the stem/progenitor program (PC2/PC3) across 4,347 single cells from 6 untreated grade II IDH-mutant 1p/19q-codeleted oligodendrogliomas (Smart-Seq2); co-expressed with [SOX4](../genes/SOX4.md) and [SOX11](../genes/SOX11.md). The program matches human NPCs (t-test P = 2×10⁻³⁵) and activated mouse NSCs (P = 3×10⁻⁶) more closely than OPCs, supporting a tri-potent NPC-like cell of origin. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [ODG](../cancer_types/ODG.md) — SOX2 marks the rare stem/progenitor cells (1.5–8% of cells per tumor) in grade II oligodendroglioma that account for nearly all proliferating cells; expression is enriched in prenatal brain (Allen Brain Atlas, P = 8×10⁻¹⁸) and fetal human brain (hypergeometric P = 0.006); the stem/progenitor signature also correlates with the cell-cycle signature in 69 bulk [TCGA LGG](../datasets/lgg_tcga.md) oligodendrogliomas. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [SOX4](../genes/SOX4.md), [SOX11](../genes/SOX11.md), [NFIB](../genes/NFIB.md), [ASCL1](../genes/ASCL1.md), [CHD7](../genes/CHD7.md), [CD24](../genes/CD24.md), [BOC](../genes/BOC.md), [TCF4](../genes/TCF4.md), [CCND2](../genes/CCND2.md) in the stem/progenitor compartment; mutually exclusive with oligodendrocytic ([OLIG1](../genes/OLIG1.md), [OLIG2](../genes/OLIG2.md)) and astrocytic ([SOX9](../genes/SOX9.md), [GFAP](../genes/GFAP.md)) lineage genes. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- SOX2-high stem/progenitor cells represent the proliferative apex of oligodendroglioma; no specific drugs are evaluated in this study. Targeting this compartment is proposed as a therapeutic direction. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)

## Open questions

- Stemness of SOX2-high cells was not validated by xenotransplantation; generality to IDH-wild-type or higher-grade gliomas is not established in this corpus. [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md)
