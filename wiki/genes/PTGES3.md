# PTGES3

## Overview

PTGES3 (Prostaglandin E Synthase 3) encodes a cytosolic prostaglandin E synthase that functions as a co-chaperone of HSP90 and is involved in prostaglandin biosynthesis. In cancer, PTGES3 has been implicated through epigenetic regulation, with promoter methylation affecting downstream expression cascades.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Among 20 genes whose promoter-methylation cis-effects cascaded to RNA, protein, and PTM levels and were differentially expressed between LSCC tumors and NATs in a proteogenomic LSCC study [PMID:34358469](../papers/34358469.md)
