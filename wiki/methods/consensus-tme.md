# ConsensusTME

## Overview

Consensus immune-cell deconvolution method that combines multiple gene-set/score-based tools to estimate the relative abundance of immune and stromal cell types in bulk tumor expression data.

## Used by

- [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md) — used in the AC-ICAM colon cancer atlas to show that NK and T cell subset abundances were highest in ICR-high tumors across all CMS subtypes [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Notes

- Used as a complement to the ICR signature to characterize the immune contexture associated with prognosis [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
