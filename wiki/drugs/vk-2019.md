# VK-2019

## Overview

VK-2019 is an investigational small-molecule inhibitor of EBV EBNA1, the viral nuclear antigen that drives NF-κB/MAPK/PI3K-AKT signaling and is required for EBV episome maintenance. It is evaluated in EBV-positive nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- VK-2019 EBNA1 inhibitor in EBV+ R/M NPC (NCT04925544, n=22): ORR 4.5% — limited single-agent activity observed; EBNA1 inhibition remains a proof-of-concept target [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
