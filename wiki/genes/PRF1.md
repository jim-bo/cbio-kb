# PRF1

## Overview

PRF1 encodes perforin, a cytotoxic effector molecule. In the corpus it is part of the ICR gene expression signature prognostic in colon cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Effector cytotoxic component of the 20-gene ICR signature used to classify 348 primary colon cancers in the AC-ICAM cohort; no PRF1-specific mutation finding reported [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Colon adenocarcinoma ([COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md)) — part of ICR signature that outperforms CMS/MSI for prognosis [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [GNLY](../genes/GNLY.md), [GZMA](../genes/GZMA.md), [GZMB](../genes/GZMB.md), [GZMH](../genes/GZMH.md) in ICR-high colon tumors [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic link reported in the corpus.

## Open questions
