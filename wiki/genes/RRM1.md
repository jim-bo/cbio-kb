# RRM1

## Overview

RRM1 encodes the large subunit of ribonucleotide reductase and is a target of the nucleoside analog [fludarabine](../drugs/fludarabine.md). In the corpus it is nominated as a novel U-CLL driver.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Novel U-CLL-specific driver in the 1,148-patient CLL map; target of [fludarabine](../drugs/fludarabine.md) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Chronic lymphocytic leukemia ([CLLSLL](../cancer_types/CLLSLL.md)) — novel U-CLL driver [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Enriched in IGHV-unmutated (U-CLL) subtype [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- RRM1 is the molecular target of [fludarabine](../drugs/fludarabine.md); mutations potentially relevant to chemoresistance [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Open questions

- Functional validation of RRM1 as a CLL driver and its interaction with [fludarabine](../drugs/fludarabine.md) sensitivity is deferred to future work [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
