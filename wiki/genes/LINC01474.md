# LINC01474

## Overview

LINC01474 is a long non-coding RNA (lncRNA) identified in WGS-based driver analysis of metastatic cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (mCSCC). It was detected as a recurrently mutated non-coding RNA in this context, highlighting the importance of whole-genome approaches over exome-only sequencing for capturing the full spectrum of candidate non-coding drivers in metastatic skin cancer.
