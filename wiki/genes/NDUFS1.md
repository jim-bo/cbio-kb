# NDUFS1

## Overview

NDUFS1 (NADH:Ubiquinone Oxidoreductase Core Subunit S1) encodes the largest subunit of mitochondrial Complex I (NADH dehydrogenase), the entry point of the electron transport chain. In cancer genomics, it is implicated in tumors with altered mitochondrial metabolism, particularly those exhibiting compensatory mitochondrial biogenesis responses.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Elevated mRNA expression among 43 mitochondrial-GO genes in MT-ND5-mutated chromophobe RCC cases, consistent with compensatory mitochondrial biogenesis [PMID:25155756](../papers/25155756.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- ChRCC: upregulated in cases with somatic MT-ND5 mtDNA mutations; part of broader mitochondrial biogenesis response [PMID:25155756](../papers/25155756.md)
