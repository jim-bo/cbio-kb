# DKK2

## Overview

DKK2 (Dickkopf WNT signaling pathway inhibitor 2) is a secreted WNT pathway antagonist. It has been identified as a transcriptional target of the EWS::FLI1 fusion oncogene in Ewing sarcoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Established EWS::FLI1 transcriptional target; induced upon oncogene expression in heMSCs and validated by RT-qPCR in Ewing sarcoma cell-of-origin model [PMID:25186949](../papers/25186949.md)
