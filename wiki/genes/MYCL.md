# MYCL

## Overview

MYCL (L-Myc) is a MYC-family transcription factor with oncogenic activity in neuroendocrine tumors. Focal amplification on chromosome 1p34 has been reported in small cell lung cancer and related neuroendocrine malignancies.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Focal amplification on chromothriptic chr1 (1p34) in 10% of aSCLC cases (reported as MYCL1); chromothripsis confirmed by WGS; aSCLC cohort n=20 (MSKCC) [PMID:39185963](../papers/39185963.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- aSCLC (atypical [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md)) — focal chr1 amplification in 10% of cases [PMID:39185963](../papers/39185963.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- None flagged in the corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- None flagged in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Whether MYCL amplification in aSCLC is functionally equivalent to [MYCN](../genes/MYCN.md) amplification in conventional SCLC is not established.
