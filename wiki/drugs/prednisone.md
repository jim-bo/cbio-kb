# prednisone

## Overview

Synthetic glucocorticoid; the "P" in CHOP/CHOEP/EPOCH/BV-CH(E)P regimens.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Component of curative-intent CHOP-based induction in 132 MSK nodal [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) patients; [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation (HR 3.1, P=.005) and 17p deletion (HR 4.1, P=.03) identified those with inferior PFS on prednisone-containing regimens [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).

## Resistance mechanisms

- [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutations and 17p deletions associate with inferior PFS on CHOP-based induction in [PTCL](../cancer_types/PTCL.md) [PMID:37078708](../papers/37078708.md).
