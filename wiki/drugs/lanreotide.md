# lanreotide

## Overview

Lanreotide is a synthetic somatostatin analog (long-acting depot formulation) targeting [SSTR2](../genes/SSTR2.md) and SSTR5, used for antiproliferative and antisecretory therapy in neuroendocrine tumors. It is approved for unresectable, well- or moderately-differentiated, locally advanced or metastatic gastroenteropancreatic NETs.

## Evidence in the corpus

- High SSTR2 expression identified by WGTA (POG program) supported somatostatin-analog therapy (octreotide/lanreotide) in metastatic NEN patients PN5, PN7, PN10, PN23, PN28; PN23 had a 1972-day duration on octreotide/lanreotide [PMID:40328872](../papers/40328872.md)
