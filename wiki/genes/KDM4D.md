# KDM4D

## Overview

KDM4D (lysine demethylase 4D) is a histone H3K9/H3K36 demethylase in the KDM4 family. It participates in chromatin remodeling and transcriptional regulation, and is recurrently mutated in prostate cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Chromatin-modifier mutation identified in MSK prostate cancer cell lines, consistent with Grasso 2012 CRPC findings [PMID:25201530](../papers/25201530.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Prostate cancer (CRPC): recurrent chromatin modifier mutation.
