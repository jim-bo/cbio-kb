# EML6

## Overview

EML6 (Echinoderm Microtubule Associated Protein Like 6) is a member of the EML family of microtubule-associated proteins. It was identified as a significantly mutated gene in African American colorectal cancers, suggesting a role in ethnicity-associated mutational landscapes in CRC.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a significantly mutated gene in African American (AA) CRCs (significantly mutated in both discovery and validation cohorts); included in the 20-gene AA-enriched panel [PMID:25583493](../papers/25583493.md)
