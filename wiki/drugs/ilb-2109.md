# ILB-2109

## Overview

ILB-2109 is a selective antagonist of the adenosine A2A receptor ([ADORA2A](../genes/ADORA2A.md)), which suppresses T-cell effector function in adenosine-rich tumor microenvironments. It is evaluated in combination with toripalimab (anti-PD-1) in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- ILB-2109 selective A2AR antagonist + [toripalimab](../drugs/toripalimab.md) under investigation in phase Ib/IIa for recurrent/metastatic NPC; targets adenosine-mediated T-cell suppression in the immunosuppressive NPC tumor microenvironment [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
