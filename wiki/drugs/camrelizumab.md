# camrelizumab

## Overview

Camrelizumab (SHR-1210) is a humanized IgG4 monoclonal antibody targeting PD-1. It is approved in China in combination with [rivoceranib](../drugs/rivoceranib.md) (apatinib) for first-line [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) (CARES-310 trial).

## Evidence in the corpus

- A narrative review of lipid metabolism in HCC cites camrelizumab + rivoceranib (CARES-310 regimen) as one of the approved ICI + anti-angiogenic combinations for HCC, representing an alternative first-line option to [atezolizumab](../drugs/atezolizumab.md) + [bevacizumab](../drugs/bevacizumab.md); lipid-metabolic reprogramming including FABP1-mediated T-cell impairment and ACLY/DNL-driven PD-L1 suppression are proposed as mechanisms of ICI resistance relevant to this combination. [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)

## Resistance mechanisms

- FABP1-mediated competition for long-chain fatty acids impairs T-cell activation and may blunt camrelizumab efficacy; ACLY-driven DNL suppresses cGAS–STING and reduces PD-L1 expression in a subset of HCC. [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)
