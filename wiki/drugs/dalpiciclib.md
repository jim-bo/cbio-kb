# dalpiciclib

## Overview

Dalpiciclib (SHR6390) is an oral CDK4/6 ([CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md), [CDK6](../genes/CDK6.md)) inhibitor that induces G1 cell cycle arrest and, in preclinical models, upregulates MHC class I expression and IFN signaling — providing rationale for combination with immune checkpoint inhibitors in immune-excluded tumors.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Dalpiciclib + camrelizumab in anti-PD-1-refractory R/M NPC (NCT05724355, n=34): ORR 32.4%; median DOR 10.4 mo; median PFS 6.7 mo; grade ≥3 TRAE 76.5% [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
