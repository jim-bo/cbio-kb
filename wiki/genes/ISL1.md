# ISL1

## Overview

ISL1 (ISL LIM homeobox 1) is a LIM-homeodomain transcription factor required for the development of the sympathetic nervous system and adrenal medulla. In neuroblastoma, ISL1 serves as a marker of the noradrenergic differentiation program that characterizes low-risk tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ISL1 is part of the noradrenergic/adrenergic panel ([PHOX2B](../genes/PHOX2B.md), [PHOX2A](../genes/PHOX2A.md), [TH](../genes/TH.md), [DBH](../genes/DBH.md), [CHGA](../genes/CHGA.md), [CHGB](../genes/CHGB.md), [PNMT](../genes/PNMT.md), ISL1) marking NOR tumor clusters (nC5/nC7/nC8/nC9) and postnatal chromaffin cells in neuroblastoma snRNA-seq analysis [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).
- NOR clusters over-express ISL1 (FDR <0.01, Welch's t-test) compared to the undifferentiated high-risk nC3 cluster and the postnatal human progenitor hC1, both of which lack the noradrenergic gene program [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [NBL](../cancer_types/NBL.md) — ISL1 marks the noradrenergic lineage of low-risk neuroblastoma; its absence in the undifferentiated nC3 cluster is a feature of high-risk tumor identity [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [TH](../genes/TH.md), [DBH](../genes/DBH.md), [PHOX2A](../genes/PHOX2A.md), [PHOX2B](../genes/PHOX2B.md), [CHGA](../genes/CHGA.md) in noradrenergic NOR clusters; mutually exclusive with [NTRK2](../genes/NTRK2.md)-high undifferentiated nC3 cells [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- ISL1 expression level reflects the degree of noradrenergic differentiation in neuroblastoma; higher ISL1 correlates with low-risk, favorable-outcome tumor identity [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Open questions

- The transcriptional mechanisms by which ISL1 is silenced in high-risk neuroblastoma progenitor-like cells are not addressed in the corpus [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).
