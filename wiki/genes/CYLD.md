# CYLD

## Overview

CYLD (CYLD Lysine 63 Deubiquitinase) is a tumor suppressor gene encoding a deubiquitinase that negatively regulates NF-kB signaling. Germline loss-of-function mutations cause cylindromatosis; somatic truncating mutations have been observed across multiple cancer types including adenoid cystic carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Truncating mutation in ACC exome-sequencing cohort (n=24); classified as known cancer/tumor-suppressor gene [PMID:23778141](../papers/23778141.md)
- NF-kB pathway regulator recurrently mutated in multiple myeloma (MM): 5 patients with point mutations plus inclusion in a significant homozygous-deletion peak by GISTIC (n=153 patients); one of the 11 significantly mutated genes (q<0.1) in MM [PMID:24434212](../papers/24434212.md)

## Cancer types (linked)
