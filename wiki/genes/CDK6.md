# CDK6

## Overview

CDK6 (Cyclin-Dependent Kinase 6) partners with Cyclin D proteins to phosphorylate and inactivate [RB1](../genes/RB1.md), driving cell cycle progression through G1 into S phase. CDK6 amplification is a recurrent oncogenic event in multiple cancer types. In glioblastoma, CDK6 is upregulated specifically in EGFR-altered tumors, while in esophageal adenocarcinoma it is among the amplification-driven recurrent drivers.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CDK6 upregulation is observed in EGFR-altered [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) tumors in the CPTAC proteogenomic cohort ([gbm_cptac_2021](../datasets/gbm_cptac_2021.md), n=99); CDK6 protein tracks with [UBE2I](../genes/UBE2I.md) (Pearson r = 0.413), a known SUMOylation target [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).
- CDK6 amplification is a recurrent driver at ≥5% frequency in esophageal/GEJ adenocarcinoma ([egc_mskcc_2020](../datasets/egc_mskcc_2020.md), n=487); grouped among amplification-driven recurrent drivers without independently demonstrated [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) association [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).
- No significant association of CDK6 amplification with CDKN2A/p16 or Rb protein levels in CPTAC LSCC proteogenomics (108 tumors); downstream [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) protein expression + phospho-Rb functional assessment proposed over copy-number status for CDK4/6-inhibitor patient selection. [PMID:34358469](../papers/34358469.md)
- CDK6 upregulation (amplification 0.4%; or transcriptional induction downstream of FAT1/PTEN/ARID1A loss) drives CDK4/6i resistance in HR+/HER2− breast cancer; INK4 binding deforms the CDK6 ATP pocket reducing [abemaciclib](../drugs/abemaciclib.md) affinity ~4× (Kd 20.2→86.4 nmol/L); PROTAC degraders BSJ-05-017/BSJ-03-096 overcome this resistance with IC50 7.37 nmol/L in CDK6-high cells vs [palbociclib](../drugs/palbociclib.md) IC50 252.0 nmol/L [PMID:34544752](../papers/34544752.md)
- CDK6 amplifications recurrent in [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) and other sarcomas in MAPPYACTS pediatric trial; 105 CDK4/6 inhibitor recommendations generated; CDKN2A/CDKN2B loss (157 inactivating events) identified as the matched predictive biomarker [PMID:35292802](../papers/35292802.md)
- Amplified in 0.7% of sarcomas (10.6% for [CDK4](../genes/CDK4.md)); acts as a target for CDK4/6 inhibitors; co-occurs with CCND1/CCND2/CCND3 and CDKN2A/B in an unusual within-pathway co-occurrence pattern in mesenchymal tumors [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Glioblastoma ([GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md)):** CDK6 protein upregulated in EGFR-altered tumors; correlates with [UBE2I](../genes/UBE2I.md) SUMOylation activity [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).
- **Esophageal/gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma ([EAC](../cancer_types/EAC.md)):** CDK6 amplification at ≥5% frequency; no independent [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) association in this cohort [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-occurs with [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md) structural variants and amplification in [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md); also elevated alongside [CTNND2](../genes/CTNND2.md) and [CDKN2C](../genes/CDKN2C.md) in EGFR-altered tumors [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).
- Co-occurs with [MYC](../genes/MYC.md), [VEGFA](../genes/VEGFA.md), [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md), and [CCND3](../genes/CCND3.md) amplifications in [EAC](../cancer_types/EAC.md) [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- CDK6 upregulation in EGFR-altered [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) may be relevant to CDK4/6 inhibitor strategies; not directly tested in the corpus [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).

## Open questions

- Whether CDK6 amplification independently drives resistance or contributes to prognosis in [EAC](../cancer_types/EAC.md) beyond its association with chromosomal instability is not established [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).
