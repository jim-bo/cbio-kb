# ATM

## Overview

ATM is a core DNA damage response kinase and one of the cardinal drivers of chronic lymphocytic leukemia.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Mutated in 11.2% of patients in the 1,148-patient CLL map, making it one of the top four cardinal CLL drivers alongside [SF3B1](../genes/SF3B1.md), [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md), and [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
