# STAT4

## Overview

STAT4 (Signal Transducer and Activator of Transcription 4) is a transcription factor in the JAK-STAT signaling pathway, important for immune response regulation. A GWAS susceptibility locus at STAT4 has been associated with HBV- or HCV-related hepatocellular carcinoma risk.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- STAT4 is a GWAS susceptibility locus/SNP associated with HBV- or HCV-related HCC risk [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)

## Cancer types (linked)
