# COX6C

## Overview

COX6C (Cytochrome C Oxidase Subunit 6C) encodes a subunit of cytochrome c oxidase, the terminal enzyme of the mitochondrial respiratory chain. In breast cancer, COX6C is located near 8p22–23 and has been reported as a fusion partner in some chromosomal rearrangements. It appears in breast cancer genomic studies as part of chromosomal copy-number alterations.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Listed among the somatic genes identified in WES/MSK-IMPACT profiling of synchronous [DCIS](../cancer_types/DCIS.md) and IDC-NST pairs (n=25 patients) in a breast cancer clonal evolution study [PMID:32220886](../papers/32220886.md)
