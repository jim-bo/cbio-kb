# SPOPL

## Overview

SPOPL (Speckle-type POZ Protein-Like) is a paralog of [SPOP](../genes/SPOP.md), encoding a BTB-domain-containing substrate adaptor for the [CUL3](../genes/CUL3.md) ubiquitin ligase complex. In prostate cancer, SPOPL undergoes recurrent focal deletion, suggesting a tumor-suppressive function analogous to [SPOP](../genes/SPOP.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Recurrent focal deletion at 2q22.1 in primary prostate cancer ([PRAD](../cancer_types/PRAD.md)); identified in the TCGA molecular taxonomy as a significantly deleted locus [PMID:26544944](../papers/26544944.md)
