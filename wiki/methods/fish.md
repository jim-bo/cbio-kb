# Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH)

## Overview

Cytogenetic technique using fluorescent DNA probes to detect specific genomic rearrangements, fusions, and copy-number changes on interphase or metaphase nuclei.

## Used by

- [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md) — used with custom BAC probes flanking [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md), [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md), and [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md) to detect [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusions in extremity rhabdomyosarcoma; complemented Archer FusionPlex testing. 85% of cases were [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion-positive [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md), 70% of which carried [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md) — FISH translocation data for [BCL2](../genes/BCL2.md) and [BCL6](../genes/BCL6.md) used as input to the [LymphGen](../methods/lymphgen.md) probabilistic algorithm in 320 of 396 DLBCL cases sequenced by MSK-IMPACT Heme [PMID:38497151](../papers/38497151.md).
- [PMID:38758238](../papers/38758238.md) — FISH on five pituitary neuroendocrine tumor patients to validate chromosomal losses identified by [FACETS](../methods/facets.md) and [MSK-IMPACT](../methods/msk-impact-panel.md) [PMID:38758238](../papers/38758238.md).
- [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md) — FISH with probes on 1p36/1q25 and 19q13/19p13 to confirm 1p/19q co-deletion in 6 IDH-mutant oligodendrogliomas; complemented bulk WES and Smart-Seq2 scRNA-seq in defining the genetic composition of each tumor's subclones [PMID:27806376](../papers/27806376.md).
- [PMID:30325352](../papers/30325352.md) — FISH used to detect [EML4](../genes/EML4.md)-[ALK](../genes/ALK.md) translocation status in 196/211 NSCLC subjects in the Stanford radiogenomics dataset; complement to SNaPshot PCR for EGFR/KRAS single-gene testing [PMID:30325352](../papers/30325352.md).

## Notes

- FISH and Archer FusionPlex were used together for fusion confirmation in the extremity [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) cohort [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- In the NSCLC radiogenomics dataset, FISH was the sole assay for ALK/EML4 translocation detection (n=196/211); SNaPshot PCR covered EGFR and KRAS [PMID:30325352](../papers/30325352.md).
