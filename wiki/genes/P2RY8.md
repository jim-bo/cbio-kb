# P2RY8

## Overview

P2RY8 encodes a G protein-coupled purinergic receptor expressed on lymphocytes. Somatic mutations in P2RY8 were identified as recurrent events in diffuse large B-cell lymphoma, suggesting a role in modulating lymphocyte signaling or homing in the tumor microenvironment.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- P2RY8 harbored 8 somatic mutations in 6 of 55 DLBCL patients by whole-exome sequencing; allelic fractions >0.5 in 3 patients suggested loss of heterozygosity or amplification of the mutant allele [PMID:22343534](../papers/22343534.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- DLBCL: recurrent somatic mutations, detected in 6/55 (11%) patients in the Broad WES cohort [PMID:22343534](../papers/22343534.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-mutated in DLBCL samples alongside other recurrent drivers including [MYD88](../genes/MYD88.md), [CD79B](../genes/CD79B.md), [CARD11](../genes/CARD11.md), [MEF2B](../genes/MEF2B.md), and [EZH2](../genes/EZH2.md) [PMID:22343534](../papers/22343534.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No drugs directly targeting P2RY8 were reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- The functional consequence of P2RY8 mutations in DLBCL (gain vs. loss of function, downstream signaling impact) is not established [PMID:22343534](../papers/22343534.md)
