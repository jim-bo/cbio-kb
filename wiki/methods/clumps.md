# CLUMPS

## Overview

3-D protein-structure mutation clustering method used to nominate driver genes whose somatic mutations cluster in protein space rather than along the linear sequence.

## Used by

- [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md) — used in the CLL-map driver-discovery pipeline alongside recurrent sSNV/indel testing; nominated six additional CLL drivers (including [MAP2K2](../genes/MAP2K2.md), [DIS3](../genes/DIS3.md), [DICER1](../genes/DICER1.md)) on top of the 82 from recurrent SNV/indels [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).

## Notes

- Specific CLUMPS-nominated driver examples in CLL: [MAP2K2](../genes/MAP2K2.md) (3 mutations in the kinase domain, activating ERK signaling analogous to [MAP2K1](../genes/MAP2K1.md)), [DIS3](../genes/DIS3.md) (2/4 sites at cancer hotspots D479/D488 in the catalytic domain), [DICER1](../genes/DICER1.md), and [INO80](../genes/INO80.md) (mutations clustered in the DNA-binding domain) [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
