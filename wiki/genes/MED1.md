# MED1

## Overview

MED1 (Mediator Complex Subunit 1; also TRAP220, DRIP205) encodes a subunit of the Mediator transcriptional coactivator complex that bridges enhancers and RNA Pol II. It is a known AR coactivator amplified or overexpressed in castration-resistant prostate cancer, and also observed as a chromatin-modifier mutation in CRPC genomics studies.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Chromatin-modifier mutation observed in patient-derived CRPC organoids consistent with Grasso 2012 CRPC genomic findings [PMID:25201530](../papers/25201530.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- CRPC: chromatin-modifier mutation; co-occurs with AR amplification and PTEN loss in patient-derived organoid models [PMID:25201530](../papers/25201530.md)
