# H3-3A

## Overview

H3-3A encodes the replication-independent histone variant H3.3. Recurrent missense mutations at K27M (p.K28M in the mature protein) define diffuse midline glioma, H3 K27M-mutant, a WHO-recognized CNS tumor entity with poor prognosis.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- H3-3A p.K28M (H3 K27M) was detected in CSF ctDNA, supporting the diagnosis of diffuse midline glioma in the [csf_msk_2024](../datasets/csf_msk_2024.md) cohort (1,007 CSF samples, 711 patients) profiled by MSK-IMPACT [PMID:39289779](../papers/39289779.md).
- K27M mutation co-occurs with [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) activation and [NF1](../genes/NF1.md) alteration in pilocytic astrocytoma/glioblastoma; same K27M found in 3/48 pediatric [GB](../cancer_types/GB.md) samples in a screening cohort, often with [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) mutation and [NF1](../genes/NF1.md) loss [PMID:23817572](../papers/23817572.md)
- H3-3A (histone H3.3) mutations were absent in the TCGA 2013 primary [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) cohort (n=291 WES), consistent with their previously reported occurrence in pediatric rather than adult GBM [PMID:24120142](../papers/24120142.md)
- Only 2 samples mutated in a WGS pan-glioma dataset (n=1122), despite frequent involvement in pediatric gliomas; H3-3A (H3F3A) mutant status largely absent in adult diffuse glioma, supporting a distinct ALT mechanism (DAXX/H3.3-independent) in this population [PMID:26824661](../papers/26824661.md)
- H3-3A (H3F3A) K28M (K27M) mutation identified in glioma and glioblastoma multiforme as an HDAC-inhibitor target; also co-occurred with [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) N577K in one pediatric glioma patient [PMID:28007021](../papers/28007021.md).
- Both DIPG PDX models (IBs-P1215DIPG, IBs-W0128DIPG) in the PPTC dataset express histone H3.3 (H3-3A) at high levels (FPKM > 50) but lack canonical H3.1/H3.3 K27M/G34 mutations [PMID:31693904](../papers/31693904.md)
- H3-3A (H3F3A) p.K27M mutation found in GBM36, a cerebellar diffuse midline glioma PDX, co-occurring with ATRX and TP53 mutations — recapitulates the canonical H3 K27M-mutant diffuse midline glioma driver combination [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)
- H3-3A (H3F3A) K27M point mutation used as key prognostic stratifier for IDH analysis in pediatric HGG; included as confounder in OS Cox models; H3-mutant vs H3-WT HGG show divergent IDH protein-OS effects [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [DIFG](../cancer_types/DIFG.md) — H3-3A K27M mutation is the defining molecular alteration of diffuse midline glioma; detected in CSF ctDNA as a non-invasive diagnostic tool [PMID:39289779](../papers/39289779.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Not reported in the corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- H3 K27M mutation identifies patients eligible for emerging epigenetic-targeted therapies; CSF ctDNA detection provides a non-invasive route to molecular diagnosis in surgically inaccessible midline tumors [PMID:39289779](../papers/39289779.md).
