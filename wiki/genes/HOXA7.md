# HOXA7

## Overview

HOXA7 is a homeobox transcription factor involved in developmental patterning. In IDH-mutant glioma, HOXA7 is an experimentally confirmed target of [HOXD13](../genes/HOXD13.md), itself a master regulator of IDH-mutant astrocytoma epigenetic progression.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- HOXA7 is an experimentally confirmed HOXD13 target gene in IDH-mutant glioma; upregulated as part of the HOXD13-driven transcriptional program in recurrent IDH-mutant non-codeleted astrocytomas and associated with tumor growth and metastasis promotion [PMID:38117484](../papers/38117484.md).
- HOXA7 upregulation is associated with the epigenetic shift from GCIMP-high to GCIMP-low (i.e., DNA hypomethylation at recurrence) in treated IDH-mutant gliomas from the GLASS consortium (n=132 matched initial and recurrent gliomas) [PMID:38117484](../papers/38117484.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [DIFG](../cancer_types/DIFG.md) / [ASTR](../cancer_types/ASTR.md) — HOXA7 is a downstream effector of HOXD13 in IDH-mutant astrocytoma progression; associated with more aggressive recurrent disease [PMID:38117484](../papers/38117484.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Epigenetically co-activated with [CENPF](../genes/CENPF.md) and [PCNA](../genes/PCNA.md) as HOXD13 target genes in recurrent IDH-mutant astrocytomas [PMID:38117484](../papers/38117484.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- HOXD13 and its downstream targets including HOXA7 represent candidate therapeutic targets in recurrent IDH-mutant glioma [PMID:38117484](../papers/38117484.md).

## Open questions

- The precise contribution of HOXA7 vs other HOXD13 targets ([CENPF](../genes/CENPF.md), [PCNA](../genes/PCNA.md)) to IDH-mutant glioma progression remains to be delineated [PMID:38117484](../papers/38117484.md).
