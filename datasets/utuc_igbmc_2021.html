Upper Tract Urothelial Carcinoma (IGBMC, Genome Biology 2021)

Overview

The utuc_igbmc_2021 cohort was generated by Su et al. (Genome Biology 2021) from fresh-frozen surgical resections sourced from the Pitié-Salpêtrière Hospital biobank (Paris, France). It is the first comprehensive integrative multi-omic profiling of upper-tract urothelial carcinoma (UTUC), combining whole-exome sequencing, RNA-seq, and Infinium MethylationEPIC array data from 40 microsatellite-stable patients (20 non-muscle-invasive [NMI] + 20 muscle-invasive [MI]). Median follow-up 53 months (range 2–98); 18 patients recurred, 12 died of disease. The cBioPortal study reports WES data from the 30 tumor–normal paired cases (hg19 reference).

Composition

  • Total patients: 40 (20 NMI + 20 MI UTUC).
  • Cancer type: UTUC — all microsatellite-stable; one FGFR3-TACC3 fusion detected (UTUC-11).
  • WES: 30 paired tumor–normal (Agilent SureSelect 50 Mb, Illumina HiSeq 2000; tumor 79–158×, germline 43–80×): 15 NMI + 15 MI.
  • RNA-seq: 20 cases (15 MI + 5 NMI; NuGen Ovation V2, 75 bp PE, aligned GRCh37/hg19).
  • Methylation: Infinium MethylationEPIC array on 35 UTUC + 8 normal adjacent tissues; 836,691 probes after filtering.
  • Tumor mutation burden: Mean 2.9 ± 3.5 mut/Mb; median 1.5 (range 0.3–15.7).

Assays / panels (linked)

  • whole-exome-seq — Agilent SureSelect 50 Mb capture; MutSigCV for SMGs; GISTIC for focal SCNAs; deconstructSigs + nmf-clustering for mutational signatures.
  • rna-seq — consensusMIBC and BASE47 subtype assignment; MCP-counter and ESTIMATE for immune/stromal deconvolution.
  • epic-methylation-array — ChAMP for DMP/DMR analysis; iClusterBayes for multi-omics integration; MeTIL score for methylation-derived TIL inference.
  • sanger-sequencing — Targeted validation of FGFR3 hotspots (S249C, Y373C) in 35 cases.

Papers using this cohort

  • PMID:33397444 — Su et al. (Genome Biology 2021): Comprehensive integrative profiling of upper tract urothelial carcinomas.

Notable findings derived from this cohort

  • Three significantly mutated genes by MutSigCV (FDR<0.05): FGFR3 50%, KDM6A 27%, and novel ZFP36L1 20% PMID:33397444.
  • DNA methylation analysis defined two epigenetic clusters: EpiC-high (hypermethylated, immune-infiltrated, MI-enriched, SWI/SNF-mutant) and EpiC-low (hypomethylated, immune-depleted, NMI-enriched, FGFR3-mutant) PMID:33397444.
  • EpiC-high independently predicted shorter OS (HR=6.57; 95% CI 1.87–23.04; P=0.038) and shorter distant-metastasis-free survival (HR=6.55; P=0.036) — the only independent prognostic variable in the cohort PMID:33397444.
  • FGFR3-mutant UTUC is globally hypomethylated (82,991/84,717 differential probes hypomethylated vs FGFR3-WT) and immune-cold (lower MeTIL score, P=7.8×10⁻⁵) PMID:33397444.
  • ZFP36L1 siRNA knockdown in TCCSUP bladder cells caused EMT-like morphology and increased migration without affecting proliferation, supporting a tumor-suppressor role for ZFP36 family members in urothelial cancer PMID:33397444.
  • The EpiC methylation signature projected onto TCGA MI-BLCA (blca_tcga_pub_2017) recovered analogous BLCA-C1 vs BLCA-C2 subgroups with matching OS differences (HR=1.38; 95% CI 1.03–1.85; P=0.035) PMID:33397444.
  • FGFR3–KMT2C co-occurrence (log odds ratio=3.1, P=0.04) validated in utuc_mskcc_2015 (LOR=2.9, P<0.001) PMID:33397444.
