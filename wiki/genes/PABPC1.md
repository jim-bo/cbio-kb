# PABPC1

## Overview

PABPC1 (Poly(A) Binding Protein Cytoplasmic 1) encodes an RNA-binding protein involved in mRNA stability and translation. It was identified as a significantly mutated gene (MutSigCV) in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma; its oncogenic driver status in PDA is unvalidated.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- In pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma ([PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)), PABPC1 was among 24 significantly mutated genes (MutSigCV q<0.1) in a 109-case whole-exome sequencing study; mutated in ~4% of cases. Functional role in PDA biology remains to be validated. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md): recurrently mutated at ~4% frequency by MutSigCV; driver vs. passenger status unresolved. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)
