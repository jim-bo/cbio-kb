# eribulin

## Overview

Eribulin mesylate (E7389) is a non-taxane microtubule dynamics inhibitor derived from halichondrin B. It binds to the plus ends of microtubules and suppresses dynamic instability without shortening microtubule plus-end growth, leading to mitotic arrest and apoptosis. Eribulin is FDA-approved for metastatic breast cancer (after ≥2 prior chemotherapy regimens) and liposarcoma. It is used in the salvage setting for refractory metastatic triple-negative breast cancer.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Eribulin is listed as a reference treatment option available within the FUTURE umbrella trial for refractory metastatic TNBC (NCT03805399); it provides historical-control context for the 29.0% ITT ORR achieved by biomarker-guided arms in this heavily pretreated population [PMID:32719455](../papers/32719455.md).
