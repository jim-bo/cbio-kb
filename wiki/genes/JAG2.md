# JAG2

## Overview

JAG2 (Jagged canonical Notch ligand 2) encodes a Notch pathway ligand. In oral squamous cell carcinoma (OSCC), copy gains of JAG2 are observed alongside [JAG1](../genes/JAG1.md) gains; these may act through cis-inhibition of [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) or via Notch pathway activation, in a context where [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) acts as a tumor suppressor.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Copy gain observed in OSCC alongside [JAG1](../genes/JAG1.md); Notch ligand gains may inhibit [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) via cis-inhibition or activate it, consistent with the tumor-suppressive role inferred for [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) in OSCC [PMID:23619168](../papers/23619168.md)
- JAG2 part of NOTCH-pathway alterations ([NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md)–4, JAG1/JAG2, [MAML1](../genes/MAML1.md)–3) totalling 31% of cases in a 109-case PDA WES cohort; amplification/mutation patterns nominate γ-secretase inhibitor MK0752 as a candidate therapy [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **OSCC (Oral Squamous Cell Carcinoma):** copy number gain; co-occurs with [JAG1](../genes/JAG1.md) gains; mechanistic interpretation is uncertain [PMID:23619168](../papers/23619168.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-gained with [JAG1](../genes/JAG1.md) in OSCC; both Notch ligands observed in context of [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) tumor-suppressor mutations (9% of OSCC) and NUMB/MAML1 Notch pathway modulator alterations [PMID:23619168](../papers/23619168.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported; Notch pathway context in OSCC under investigation [PMID:23619168](../papers/23619168.md)

## Open questions

- Whether JAG2 copy gain drives cis-inhibition of [NOTCH1](../genes/NOTCH1.md) (reinforcing tumor suppression) or activates Notch signaling (oncogenic) is unresolved [PMID:23619168](../papers/23619168.md)
