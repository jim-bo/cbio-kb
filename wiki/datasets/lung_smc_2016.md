# Lung Cancer (Samsung Medical Center, 2016)

## Overview

Lung cancer cohort from Samsung Medical Center (South Korea, 2016), used as a validation dataset in multi-cancer RNA-based tumor classification studies. Included as one of 41 non-TCGA datasets in the ATLAS classifier validation set. [PMID:27634761](../papers/27634761.md)

## Composition

- Lung cancer patients from Samsung Medical Center; included as part of the non-TCGA validation cohort (N=6,820 across 41 datasets) in the ATLAS classifier study. [PMID:27634761](../papers/27634761.md)
- Cancer types include [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) (lung adenocarcinoma [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) and squamous cell carcinoma [LUSC](../cancer_types/LUSC.md)). [PMID:27634761](../papers/27634761.md)

## Assays / panels (linked)

- [RNA-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) used for tumor lineage and site classification. [PMID:27634761](../papers/27634761.md)

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:27634761](../papers/27634761.md) — A platform-independent AI tumor lineage and site (ATLAS) classifier.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- Contributed to multi-dataset validation of the ATLAS classifier, which achieved 91.4% overall accuracy for cancer site of origin (22 classes) and 97.1% for cancer lineage (8 classes) across 10,376 validation samples. [PMID:27634761](../papers/27634761.md)
