# CLDN11

## Overview

CLDN11 (claudin-11, also known as oligodendrocyte-specific protein/OSP) is a tight junction component expressed in oligodendrocytes and other neural cell types. In neuroblastoma, CLDN11 is part of the progenitor/migratory gene program shared between the postnatal human adrenal progenitor cluster and high-risk undifferentiated tumor cells.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CLDN11 is part of the progenitor/migratory gene program shared between the normal postnatal human adrenal progenitor cluster (hC1) and the undifferentiated high-risk neuroblastoma cluster (nC3), alongside [BCL11A](../genes/BCL11A.md), [ASXL3](../genes/ASXL3.md), [ERBB3](../genes/ERBB3.md), [RTTN](../genes/RTTN.md), [TP63](../genes/TP63.md), [POU6F2](../genes/POU6F2.md), [SOX6](../genes/SOX6.md), and [DOCK7](../genes/DOCK7.md); identified by single-nuclei RNA-seq of 11 neuroblastoma tumors [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).
- The authors specifically note that the TRKB+/CLDN11+ cholinergic progenitor signature could represent the cell of origin for high-risk neuroblastoma in older children [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Neuroblastoma (NBL) — CLDN11 marks the high-risk undifferentiated nC3 cluster enriched for MYCN-amplified and/or 11q-deleted tumors; the TRKB+/CLDN11+ signature stratifies survival in 498 SEQC patients (Kaplan-Meier, Bonferroni-corrected p < 0.01) [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [BCL11A](../genes/BCL11A.md), [ASXL3](../genes/ASXL3.md), [NTRK2](../genes/NTRK2.md), [ALK](../genes/ALK.md), and [CHRNA7](../genes/CHRNA7.md) in the hC1/nC3 high-risk progenitor program; mutually exclusive with the noradrenergic [NTRK1](../genes/NTRK1.md)-high low-risk clusters [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- Not directly therapeutically targeted in the corpus; identified as a prognostic signature component with potential utility as a transcriptomic biomarker for high-risk neuroblastoma [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Open questions

- Whether CLDN11 is functionally required for the undifferentiated state of nC3 cells, or serves purely as a marker of a shared progenitor identity, is not tested in the corpus.
