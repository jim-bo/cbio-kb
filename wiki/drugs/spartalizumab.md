# spartalizumab

## Overview

Spartalizumab is an anti-PD-1 monoclonal antibody targeting [PDCD1](../genes/PDCD1.md). It was evaluated as an immune checkpoint inhibitor in hepatocellular carcinoma ([HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md)).
