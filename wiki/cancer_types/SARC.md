# Sarcoma / SARC (TCGA)

## Overview

SARC is the TCGA cohort identifier for sarcoma (predominantly soft tissue and bone sarcomas). The closest OncoTree parent is [SARCNOS](SARCNOS.md) (Sarcoma, NOS). The TCGA SARC cohort is a heterogeneous collection of multiple sarcoma subtypes including leiomyosarcoma, liposarcoma, synovial sarcoma, and dedifferentiated liposarcoma.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- TCGA SARC cohort: included as one of 33 cancer types in the pan-cancer fusion landscape study; subset of the PanCancer Atlas ([sarc_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018](../datasets/sarc_tcga_pan_can_atlas_2018.md)).

## Recurrent alterations

- Pan-cancer fusion study (9,624 TCGA samples) identified SARC as unique in harboring both [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) fusions and TP53 point mutations (unlike most cancer types where one mechanism predominates); druggable fusions annotated across 29 cancer types including SARC [PMID:29617662](../papers/29617662.md).

## Subtypes

- Leiomyosarcoma ([LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md)), dedifferentiated liposarcoma ([DDLS](../cancer_types/DDLS.md)), liposarcoma ([LIPO](../cancer_types/LIPO.md)), synovial sarcoma ([SS](../cancer_types/SS.md)), malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumor ([MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md)), undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma (UPS), and others in the TCGA SARC cohort.
