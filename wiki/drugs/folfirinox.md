# folfirinox

## Overview

FOLFIRINOX is a four-drug chemotherapy regimen comprising [oxaliplatin](../drugs/oxaliplatin.md) (platinum-based DNA crosslinker), [irinotecan](../drugs/irinotecan.md) (topoisomerase I inhibitor), [leucovorin](../drugs/leucovorin.md) (folinic acid modulator), and [fluorouracil](../drugs/fluorouracil.md) (pyrimidine antimetabolite). It is a standard first-line regimen for metastatic and locally advanced pancreatic adenocarcinoma in patients with adequate performance status, and is also used in the neoadjuvant setting for borderline-resectable pancreatic cancer.

## Evidence in the corpus

- [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md)^G12R^-mutant [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) patients were enriched among those receiving neoadjuvant FOLFIRINOX followed by resection (32.7% vs 17.9% for [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md)^G12D^, p=0.094) in a 1,360-patient MSK PDAC cohort; the authors suggest this may reflect greater KRAS-allele-specific chemosensitivity rather than purely borderline-resectable case selection [PMID:39214094](../papers/39214094.md).
- FOLFIRINOX was administered to 38% of the curated 1,480-patient MSK PDAC clinicogenomic cohort as first-line systemic therapy; gemcitabine/nab-paclitaxel was used in 37%. Chemotherapy backbone choice between these two regimens was not significantly associated with overall survival in the n=304 metastatic chemotherapy-treated subset [PMID:39753968](../papers/39753968.md).
- FOLFIRINOX combined with SBRT is under evaluation in two prospective trials cited in this 2016 review (NCT01992705, NCT01446458) for locally advanced [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md); no efficacy data were available at time of publication, but the combination is highlighted as a priority investigation given FOLFIRINOX's established OS benefit in metastatic disease [PMID:27826200](../papers/27826200.md)

## Resistance mechanisms

- No specific FOLFIRINOX resistance mechanisms reported in the corpus at this time. KRAS mutant-allele dosage gains were prognostic for shorter [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) independent of first-line chemotherapy backbone [PMID:39753968](../papers/39753968.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) — pancreatic adenocarcinoma; dominant first-line regimen (38% of curated MSK cohort), with possible KRAS allele-specific chemosensitivity in KRAS^G12R^ tumors.
