# BRD2

## Overview

BRD2 (Bromodomain Containing 2) is a BET family bromodomain protein that reads acetylated lysines on histones and facilitates transcriptional regulation. It is part of the chromatin-remodeling machinery altered in various cancers including adenoid cystic carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- In-frame deletion in chromatin-remodeling gene cluster in ACC exome-sequencing cohort (n=24); part of cluster collectively mutated in 12/24 cases [PMID:23778141](../papers/23778141.md)
