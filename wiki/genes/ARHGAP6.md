# ARHGAP6

## Overview

ARHGAP6 is a Rho GTPase-activating protein. In gastric cancer, in-frame fusions involving CLDN18 and ARHGAP6 (related to ARHGAP26) are observed in the genomically stable (GS) subtype and are mutually exclusive with RHOA mutation.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CLDN18-ARHGAP6 in-frame fusion present in the GS subtype of gastric adenocarcinoma; part of the broader class of CLDN18-ARHGAP rearrangements (~15% of GS tumours); mutually exclusive with RHOA mutation [PMID:25079317](../papers/25079317.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [STAD](../cancer_types/STAD.md): CLDN18-ARHGAP6 fusion in GS subtype; mutually exclusive with RHOA hotspot mutations [PMID:25079317](../papers/25079317.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Mutually exclusive with RHOA mutation in GS gastric tumours [PMID:25079317](../papers/25079317.md)
