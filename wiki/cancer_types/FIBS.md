# Fibrosarcoma (FIBS)

## Overview

Fibrosarcoma is a malignant tumor of fibroblasts, classified under Soft Tissue Sarcoma in OncoTree (parent: SOFT_TISSUE). Adult fibrosarcoma is a diagnosis of exclusion after other spindle-cell sarcomas have been ruled out. Infantile fibrosarcoma ([IFS](../cancer_types/IFS.md)) is a distinct entity driven by [ETV6](../genes/ETV6.md)–[NTRK3](../genes/NTRK3.md) fusion.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [sarcoma_ucla_2024](../datasets/sarcoma_ucla_2024.md): represented in the 24-subtype UCLA sarcoma PDTO biobank (194 specimens from 126 patients, Feb 2018–May 2022). [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- No specific alterations directly genotyped in the UCLA PDTO study for adult fibrosarcoma.

## Subtypes

- Adult fibrosarcoma (FIBS)
- Infantile fibrosarcoma ([IFS](../cancer_types/IFS.md)) — distinct entity with [ETV6](../genes/ETV6.md)–[NTRK3](../genes/NTRK3.md) fusion

## Therapeutic landscape

- Patient-derived tumor organoid (PDTO) drug screening was applied across all 24 sarcoma subtypes in the UCLA cohort, including FIBS. Across the full cohort, 80.4% of specimens screened had at least one significant drug response, and 59% had at least one FDA-approved or NCCN-recommended top-five regimen. [PMID:39305899](../papers/39305899.md)
