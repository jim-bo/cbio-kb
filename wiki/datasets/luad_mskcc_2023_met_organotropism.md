# Lung Adenocarcinoma Met Organotropism (MSK, Cancer Cell 2023)

## Overview

Large MSK cohort of lung adenocarcinoma primaries and metastases profiled with MSK-IMPACT (and a WES subcohort) to identify clinicopathologic and genomic features linked to metastasis, metastatic burden, organotropism, and metastasis-free survival [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).

## Composition

- 2,532 primary and metastatic [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) specimens from 2,309 patients [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- Metastatic sites profiled: adrenal gland, bone, CNS, liver/biliary, lung, lymph node, and pleura [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).

## Assays / panels (linked)

- Targeted sequencing via the [MSK-IMPACT panel](../methods/msk-impact-panel.md), with an additional [whole-exome sequencing](../methods/whole-exome-seq.md) subcohort for mutational-signature analysis [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).

## Papers using this cohort

- [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md) — Lengel et al., Genomic Mapping of Metastatic Organotropism in Lung Adenocarcinoma.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- Ever-metastatic primaries had higher TMB, chromosomal instability, and fraction of genome doubled than never-metastatic primaries; APOBEC SBS2/SBS13 more prevalent in metastases (18% vs 8%, p=0.012), particularly in liver lesions [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- Inactivation of [TP53](../genes/TP53.md), [SMARCA4](../genes/SMARCA4.md), and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) correlated with site-specific shorter time to metastasis; [SMARCA4](../genes/SMARCA4.md) strongly enriched in bone metastases (OR=6.47; time to bone met OR=2.79, p<0.001) [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- [EGFR](../genes/EGFR.md) activating mutations enriched at adrenal metastases (133/202, 66%); [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) G12C elevated in primaries that metastasized to liver (21%) vs matched liver metastases (6%, p<0.001) [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- Only ~4% of metastases harbored therapeutically actionable alterations undetected in their matched primaries [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
