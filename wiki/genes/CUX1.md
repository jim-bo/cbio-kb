# CUX1

## Overview

CUX1 (Cut Homeobox 1) is a haploinsufficient tumor suppressor located on chromosome 7q22.1. It encodes a homeodomain transcription factor implicated in cell cycle regulation and differentiation. Deletions of 7q, which commonly encompass CUX1, are recurrent in myeloid malignancies including [AML](../cancer_types/AML.md) and [MDS](../cancer_types/MDS.md), where monoallelic loss is sufficient to promote clonal expansion.
