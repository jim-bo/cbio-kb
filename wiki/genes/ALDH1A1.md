# ALDH1A1

## Overview

ALDH1A1 (aldehyde dehydrogenase 1 family member A1) is a widely used marker of cancer stem-like cells (CSCs). Its expression is associated with EMT, tumor initiating capacity, and poor prognosis in multiple cancer types.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- EMT/stemness marker upregulated in GBC cells (GBC-SD, NOZ) in response to paracrine SEMA7A from stiff-matrix-activated cancer-associated fibroblasts; downregulated by SEMA7A knockdown in co-injection xenografts [PMID:24997986](../papers/24997986.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md): ALDH1A1 upregulation downstream of SEMA7A/ITGB1/AKT1/EP300 signaling axis reflects acquisition of stem-like traits in GBC cells exposed to CAF-conditioned medium [PMID:24997986](../papers/24997986.md)
