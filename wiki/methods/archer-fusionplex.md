# Archer FusionPlex

## Overview

Targeted RNA-based anchored multiplex PCR sequencing assay used to detect gene fusions, including partner-agnostic fusion calling.

## Used by

- [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md) — used to detect [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusions ([PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md), [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md)) in extremity rhabdomyosarcoma, complementing [FISH](../methods/fish.md) with custom BAC probes flanking [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md), [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md), and [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
- [PMID:37730754](../papers/37730754.md) — Archer FusionPlex used for [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion evaluation in the MSK FP-RMS cohort of relapsed/metastatic rhabdomyosarcoma (n=20); confirmed [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) and [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) status in fusion-positive cases [PMID:37730754](../papers/37730754.md).

## Notes

- 85% of extremity [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md) cases in the cohort were [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion-positive; [PAX3](../genes/PAX3.md)::[FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) had worse prognosis than [PAX7](../genes/PAX7.md)::FOXO1 (HR 3.45, P=.016) [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
