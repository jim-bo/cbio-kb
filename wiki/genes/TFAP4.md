# TFAP4

## Overview

TFAP4 (Transcription Factor AP-4) is a basic helix-loop-helix leucine zipper transcription factor that activates or represses transcription in response to [MYC](../genes/MYC.md) signaling. In salivary gland adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC), TFAP4 carries exclusive somatic mutations in the MYB-NFIB fusion-positive subtype, though its role requires further validation.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Exclusive somatic mutations in the MYB-NFIB fusion-positive ACC subtype (t(6;9) tumors); functional significance requires validation in larger cohorts [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[ACYC](../cancer_types/ACYC.md)**: Exclusive mutations in the MYB-NFIB fusion-positive subgroup of salivary adenoid cystic carcinoma [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Mutations exclusive to the MYB-NFIB fusion subtype of ACC (mutually exclusive with MYBL1-NFIB and fusion-negative subgroups) [PMID:26631609](../papers/26631609.md)
