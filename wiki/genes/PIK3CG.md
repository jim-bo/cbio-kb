# PIK3CG

## Overview

PIK3CG (Phosphatidylinositol-4,5-Bisphosphate 3-Kinase Catalytic Subunit Gamma) encodes the class I PI3K catalytic subunit p110γ, primarily expressed in leukocytes and involved in immune cell signaling. In cancer genomics, it appears as a low-frequency PI3K-pathway event in colorectal cancer.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Private PI3K-pathway event of unknown significance in paired primary/metastasis CRC cohort; detected in spatially separate tumor regions indicating subclonality [PMID:25164765](../papers/25164765.md)
