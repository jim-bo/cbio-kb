# Immunologic Constant of Rejection (ICR) signature

## Overview

A 20-gene transcriptional signature capturing T-helper-1 / cytotoxic immune activation, used to stratify tumors into ICR-high (hot), ICR-medium, and ICR-low (cold) phenotypes [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Used by

- [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md) — applied to AC-ICAM (n=348 colon cancer); ICR was prognostic for OS (ICR-high vs ICR-low HR 0.54, 95% CI 0.34–0.86, P=0.0095; ICR-medium vs ICR-low HR 0.63, 95% CI 0.43–0.91, P=0.014) and outperformed CMS and MSI classifications, retaining prognostic value within the CMS4/mesenchymal subtype [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Notes

- The 20-gene panel includes T-helper-1 signaling genes ([IFNG](../genes/IFNG.md), [TBX21](../genes/TBX21.md), [CD8A](../genes/CD8A.md), [CD8B](../genes/CD8B.md), [IL12B](../genes/IL12B.md), [STAT1](../genes/STAT1.md), [IRF1](../genes/IRF1.md)), CXCR3/CCR5 chemokine ligands ([CCL5](../genes/CCL5.md), [CXCL9](../genes/CXCL9.md), [CXCL10](../genes/CXCL10.md)), effector cytotoxic genes ([GNLY](../genes/GNLY.md), [PRF1](../genes/PRF1.md), [GZMA](../genes/GZMA.md), [GZMB](../genes/GZMB.md), [GZMH](../genes/GZMH.md)), and immunoregulatory counter-activation genes ([CD274](../genes/CD274.md), [CTLA4](../genes/CTLA4.md), [FOXP3](../genes/FOXP3.md), [IDO1](../genes/IDO1.md), [PDCD1](../genes/PDCD1.md)) [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
- Combined with a *Ruminococcus bromii*-driven microbiome signature into the composite mICRoScore, which identified a colon cancer subgroup with excellent survival [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
