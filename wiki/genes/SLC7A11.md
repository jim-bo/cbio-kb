# SLC7A11

## Overview

SLC7A11 (xCT) is the catalytic subunit of the cystine/glutamate antiporter system Xc-, which imports cystine for glutathione biosynthesis and suppresses lipid peroxidation. In [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md), high SLC7A11 expression is associated with ferroptosis resistance and resistance to sorafenib/regorafenib.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- SLC7A11 suppresses lipid peroxidation in HCC; targeted by sorafenib/regorafenib-induced ferroptosis; high expression confers ferroptosis resistance [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) — SLC7A11 upregulation is a ferroptosis-resistance mechanism and [sorafenib](../drugs/sorafenib.md) resistance driver [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Part of the SLC7A11/GPX4 ferroptosis-suppression axis in HCC [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- SLC7A11 inhibitors (e.g., erastin, sulfasalazine) are proposed to resensitize HCC to sorafenib/regorafenib by inducing ferroptosis [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Open questions

- Clinical-grade SLC7A11 inhibitors in HCC are at early stages; combination with TKIs requires pharmacological validation.
