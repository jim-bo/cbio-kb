# LSAMP

## Overview

LSAMP (Limbic System-Associated Membrane Protein) is a GPI-anchored cell-adhesion molecule expressed in the limbic system. In cancer genomics, LSAMP maps to 3q13.3 and has been identified as a recurrently focally deleted locus in glioblastoma, consistent with a tumor-suppressor role.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Single-gene focal deletion target in [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md); identified as a focal deletion locus in the TCGA multi-platform GBM analysis (n=543) [PMID:24120142](../papers/24120142.md)
