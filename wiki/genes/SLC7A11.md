# SLC7A11

## Overview

SLC7A11, also known as xCT, is the cystine/glutamate antiporter that imports extracellular cystine in exchange for glutamate, providing the cysteine required for glutathione synthesis. It is a central negative regulator of ferroptosis. In hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC), SLC7A11 is stabilized by FASN-driven HIF1alpha and a MYC-ZNF706 transcriptional circuit, and its expression suppresses ferroptotic cell death to confer resistance to sorafenib and radiotherapy. SOCS2-mediated ubiquitination and degradation of SLC7A11 restores radiosensitivity in preclinical HCC models.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Ferroptosis suppressor in HCC; stabilized by FASN-driven HIF1alpha and a MYC-ZNF706 circuit; SOCS2-mediated ubiquitination of SLC7A11 enhances radiosensitivity; elevated expression confers sorafenib and radiotherapy resistance [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md):** SLC7A11 upregulation suppresses ferroptotic cell death via glutathione synthesis; linked to sorafenib resistance and radioresistance; part of a broader lipid-peroxidation-suppression program alongside SCD and GPX4 [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Acts in concert with [SCD](../genes/SCD.md) and [GPX4](../genes/GPX4.md) to suppress ferroptosis in HCC; all three are upregulated as part of the lipid-peroxidation-suppression program in therapy-resistant HCC [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- SOCS2-mediated SLC7A11 ubiquitination enhances radiosensitivity in HCC preclinical models; SLC7A11 inhibition is a candidate ferroptosis-induction strategy to overcome sorafenib/radiotherapy resistance [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)

## Open questions

- Whether SLC7A11 expression level predicts clinical ferroptosis sensitivity and sorafenib response in HCC patients remains to be validated prospectively [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md)
