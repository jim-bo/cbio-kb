# Spindle Cell Rhabdomyosarcoma (SCRMS)

## Overview

Spindle Cell Rhabdomyosarcoma is a Soft Tissue Sarcoma subtype of [RMS](./RMS.md), including spindle cell/sclerosing variants often defined by [MYOD1](../genes/MYOD1.md) mutations.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [rms_msk_2023](../datasets/rms_msk_2023.md): 2 [MYOD1](../genes/MYOD1.md)-mutant spindle cell/sclerosing [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) cases within the 61-patient extremity [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) cohort [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- [MYOD1](../genes/MYOD1.md) activating mutation defines the 2 spindle cell/sclerosing [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) cases in the cohort [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Subtypes

- Defined by [MYOD1](../genes/MYOD1.md) mutation rather than [FOXO1](../genes/FOXO1.md) fusion status [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- Treated under sequential COG/institutional [RMS](../cancer_types/RMS.md) protocols at MSKCC; small sample limits subtype-specific inference [PMID:37315267](../papers/37315267.md).
