# MSI PCR Pentaplex (Promega)

## Overview

PCR-based microsatellite instability (MSI) testing using the Promega MSI Analysis System, a five-mononucleotide pentaplex panel comprising the quasi-monomorphic markers BAT-25, BAT-26, NR-21, NR-24, and MONO-27. Fluorescently labeled PCR products are resolved by capillary electrophoresis and sized against matched normal DNA. Samples are classified as MSI-High (MSI-H, ≥2 unstable markers), MSI-Low (MSI-L, 1 unstable marker), or microsatellite stable (MSS, 0 unstable markers). Supplementary markers (BAT-40, NR-22, NR-27, CAT-25) may be added to resolve equivocal cases.

## Used by

- Applied to 23 pancreatic neoplasms with acinar differentiation ([paac_jhu_2014](../datasets/paac_jhu_2014.md)) to detect MSI outliers; identified two MSI-H tumors (ACINAR01: 701 non-synonymous mutations; ACINAR03: 404) and one borderline case (ACINAR28) reclassified as MSI-Low after supplementary markers were added [PMID:24293293](../papers/24293293.md).
- MSI-PCR (pentaplex panel) recommended alongside MMR IHC and EBV ISH for confirming MSI / EBV status in familial gastric cancer before clinical decision-making [PMID:24816255](../papers/24816255.md)
- Used to confirm microsatellite-stable (MSS) status in 69 matched CRC trios (BAT25, BAT26, D17S250, D2S123, D5S346 markers); study restricted to MSS tumors only. [PMID:25164765](../papers/25164765.md)
- Five-mononucleotide-repeat PCR genotyping used to assess microsatellite instability in 63 clear cell endometrial carcinomas ([uccc_nih_2017](../datasets/uccc_nih_2017.md)); MSI-high (>=2 unstable markers) found in 11.3% [PMID:28485815](../papers/28485815.md)
- PCR-based MSI testing (Promega MSI Analysis System; 5 mononucleotide + 2 pentanucleotide markers) applied to 85 CPTAC colon cancer samples; 100% concordance with WXS-based MSI calls; identified 24 MSI-H and 82 MSS tumors [PMID:31031003](../papers/31031003.md)
- MSI-PCR pentaplex panel referenced for MSI-H classification in 4,561-patient MSK CRC cohort; MSI-H enriched 27% in dual-driver vs 8.7% in single-driver CRC (P=.0004) [PMID:35235413](../papers/35235413.md).

## Notes

- Standard five-marker panel (BAT-25, BAT-26, NR-21, NR-24, MONO-27) is the Promega MSI Analysis System; extended panel adds BAT-40, NR-22, NR-27, CAT-25 for ambiguous cases.
- Requires matched tumor and normal DNA; applicable to both fresh-frozen and FFPE material.
- MSI-H status in this corpus has been associated with hypermutator phenotypes and biallelic MMR gene loss (e.g. [MSH2](../genes/MSH2.md) in pancreatic acinar cell carcinoma).
