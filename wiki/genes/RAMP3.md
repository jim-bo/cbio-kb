# RAMP3

## Overview

RAMP3 (Receptor Activity-Modifying Protein 3) is a single-pass transmembrane protein involved in modifying the pharmacology and trafficking of G protein-coupled receptors. In cancer genomics, it has been identified as a component of a prognostic gene expression signature in hepatocellular carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- RAMP3 is part of a 5-gene tumor score (TAF9, RAN, RAMP3, KRT19, HN1) that predicts HCC recurrence and survival after resection [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)
