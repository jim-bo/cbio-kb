# PTPRT

## Overview

PTPRT (protein tyrosine phosphatase receptor type T) encodes a receptor-type phosphatase implicated as a tumor suppressor. PTPRT missense mutations have been observed in radiation-associated osteosarcoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Missense mutations in 17% of RT-OS vs 7% of sporadic [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md), indicating enrichment in radiation-associated osteosarcoma [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) -- PTPRT missense mutations enriched in RT-OS (17%) compared to sporadic OS (7%) [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Not specifically reported in the corpus for PTPRT partners [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- No PTPRT-directed therapy is reported in this corpus [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).

## Open questions

- The functional significance of PTPRT missense mutations in RT-OS and their potential interaction with other RT-OS alterations (e.g., [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md) amplification) are not established [PMID:37350195](../papers/37350195.md).
