# IL7R

## Overview

IL7R (Interleukin 7 Receptor) encodes the alpha chain of the IL-7 receptor, which signals through JAK1/JAK3 and STAT5 to promote lymphocyte survival and proliferation. In esophageal squamous cell carcinoma (ESCC), focal amplification of IL7R activates JAK-STAT3 signaling, contributing to the oncogenic STAT3 activation observed in this cancer type.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Amplification activating JAK-STAT3 signaling in ESCC (139 paired tumor/germline samples by whole-exome/targeted sequencing) [PMID:24686850](../papers/24686850.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-occurs with JAK1 mutations and SOX2 amplification as part of the JAK-STAT3 activation axis in ESCC [PMID:24686850](../papers/24686850.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- JAK-STAT3 pathway activation via IL7R amplification suggests potential sensitivity to JAK inhibitors in ESCC [PMID:24686850](../papers/24686850.md)
