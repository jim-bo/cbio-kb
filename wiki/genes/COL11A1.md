# COL11A1

## Overview

COL11A1 encodes the alpha-1 chain of type XI collagen, an extracellular matrix structural protein. In [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) genomics, COL11A1 appeared as a borderline hit in statistical driver-discovery analyses but was ultimately classified as a likely false positive due to its unusual gene structure (many small exons), low expression in keratinocytes, and a borderline q-value that did not survive scrutiny.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Appeared as a borderline hit in CSCC driver-discovery analyses across 88 QC-passed tumors; classified as a "likely false positive / requiring more evidence" by the authors due to borderline q-value, unusual gene structure with many small exons, and low expression in keratinocytes — not nominated as a bona fide CSCC driver [PMID:34272401](../papers/34272401.md)
