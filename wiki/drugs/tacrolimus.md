# tacrolimus

## Overview

Tacrolimus (FK506) is a macrolide calcineurin inhibitor immunosuppressant widely used to prevent organ rejection in solid-organ transplantation. It inhibits T-cell activation by blocking calcineurin-mediated NFAT dephosphorylation. Chronic immunosuppression with tacrolimus increases susceptibility to opportunistic infections and UV-driven skin cancers including cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma ([CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus
