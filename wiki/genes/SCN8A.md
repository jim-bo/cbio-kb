# SCN8A

## Overview

SCN8A (Sodium Voltage-Gated Channel Alpha Subunit 8) encodes a voltage-gated sodium channel expressed primarily in neurons. Gain-of-function mutations cause epileptic encephalopathy. Its role in cancer is not well characterized; it has been identified as preferentially mutated in [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) tumors with durable clinical benefit from PD-1 blockade, likely reflecting passenger mutations in hypermutated tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Harbored deleterious mutations in ≥4 DCB patients and 0 NDB patients in a [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md) [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) cohort; association not corrected for overall mutation burden and requires independent validation [PMID:25765070](../papers/25765070.md)
