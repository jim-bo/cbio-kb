# ALDH3A1

## Overview

ALDH3A1 (Aldehyde Dehydrogenase 3 Family Member A1) encodes an enzyme involved in aldehyde oxidation. It functions as a Wnt/beta-catenin target gene and shows differential expression in CTNNB1-mutant hepatocellular carcinoma, with mRNA-protein discordance suggesting post-transcriptional regulation.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ALDH3A1 is a Wnt target gene differentially expressed at mRNA but not at protein level in CTNNB1-mutant HCCs (n=159), implying post-transcriptional regulation [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC): ALDH3A1 mRNA elevated but protein unchanged in CTNNB1-mutant HCC; mRNA-protein discordance implicates post-transcriptional control [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-regulated with AXIN2, NKD1, RNF43 as Wnt target genes in CTNNB1-mutant HCC [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)
