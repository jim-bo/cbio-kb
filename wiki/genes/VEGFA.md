# VEGFA

## Overview

VEGFA encodes vascular endothelial growth factor A, the principal driver of tumor angiogenesis. VEGFA focal amplification occurs in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md), and VEGFA signaling is the central mechanism of action of multiple approved HCC systemic therapies. Anti-VEGF strategies are also investigated in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- High-level focal amplification in 5% of HCCs (range 1-8%; SNP-array, n=704 patients). [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md)
- Anti-VEGF antibody [bevacizumab](../drugs/bevacizumab.md) + [sintilimab](../drugs/sintilimab.md) achieved ORR 54.5% in platinum-refractory ICI-naive recurrent/metastatic NPC. [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md)
- Recurrent focal amplification in 7% of gastric cancers (TCGA, CIN subtype); rationale for VEGFR-pathway inhibition (e.g., [ramucirumab](../drugs/ramucirumab.md)). [PMID:25079317](../papers/25079317.md)
- Cited in the context of mouse lymphatic-metastasis models of cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (cSCC); not directly assayed in the 29-tumour targeted-sequencing cohort [PMID:25589618](../papers/25589618.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **HCC** — VEGFA amplification in 5%; angiogenic signaling prominent across all HCC subclasses; mechanistic target of [sorafenib](../drugs/sorafenib.md), [lenvatinib](../drugs/lenvatinib.md), [regorafenib](../drugs/regorafenib.md), [cabozantinib](../drugs/cabozantinib.md), ramucirumab, and bevacizumab. [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md)
- **NPC** — VEGFA pathway targeted via bevacizumab (ORR 54.5% combined with sintilimab) and VEGFR2 TKI [apatinib](../drugs/apatinib.md) + [camrelizumab](../drugs/camrelizumab.md) (ORR 65.5%) in R/M disease. [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- No co-occurrence or mutual exclusivity data with specific partner genes reported in the current corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- **HCC:** Sorafenib, lenvatinib, regorafenib, cabozantinib (multi-kinase VEGFR inhibitors); ramucirumab (anti-VEGFR2); bevacizumab + [atezolizumab](../drugs/atezolizumab.md) (FDA breakthrough designation). [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md)
- **NPC:** Bevacizumab + sintilimab (ORR 54.5%); apatinib + camrelizumab (ORR 65.5%). [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md)

## Open questions

- No predictive biomarkers for VEGFA-targeting therapies have been validated in HCC; VEGFA amplification has not been prospectively validated as a predictive biomarker for anti-angiogenic agents.
