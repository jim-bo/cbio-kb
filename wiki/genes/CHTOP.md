# CHTOP

## Overview

CHTOP (Chromatin Target Of PRMT1) encodes a chromatin-associated protein involved in transcriptional regulation via PRMT1-mediated arginine methylation. In cancer genomics, CHTOP has been identified as an upstream fusion partner of [NTRK1](../genes/NTRK1.md) in glioblastoma, producing a potentially targetable oncogenic kinase fusion.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Upstream fusion partner of NTRK1 in the CHTOP–NTRK1 fusion identified in a glioblastoma PDX panel (83 IDH-wildtype [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md)); flagged as potentially targetable with TRK inhibitors alongside [PTPRZ1](../genes/PTPRZ1.md)–[MET](../genes/MET.md) and [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md)–[TACC3](../genes/TACC3.md) fusions [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Glioblastoma: CHTOP–NTRK1 fusion detected in GBM PDX; TRK inhibitors are candidates for NTRK1 fusion-positive tumors [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- CHTOP–NTRK1 identified in the same GBM PDX panel carrying other targetable fusions (PTPRZ1–MET, FGFR3–TACC3, FGFR3–[TRIM54](../genes/TRIM54.md), FGFR3–[CALCOCO1](../genes/CALCOCO1.md)) [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- CHTOP–NTRK1 fusion may confer sensitivity to TRK inhibitors (e.g., [larotrectinib](../drugs/larotrectinib.md), [entrectinib](../drugs/entrectinib.md)); no direct drug-sensitivity data reported for this specific PDX in the study [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Open questions

- Functional validation of CHTOP–NTRK1 as an oncogenic driver and its sensitivity profile to first-generation and brain-penetrant TRK inhibitors has not been reported [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)
