# FAM47C

## Overview

FAM47C (Family with Sequence Similarity 47, Member C) is a gene of largely unknown function. In metastatic triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC), it has been observed at low frequency, enriched in metastatic vs. primary samples. Its role in cancer biology remains to be functionally characterized.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- FAM47C was observed at low frequency, enriched in metastatic vs. primary TNBC samples in a biomarker-guided umbrella trial; functional significance in the metastatic TNBC context is not yet established. [PMID:32719455](../papers/32719455.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC):** Low-frequency somatic mutation enriched in metastatic vs. primary TNBC; identified in the context of a biomarker-stratified umbrella trial for heavily pretreated metastatic TNBC. [PMID:32719455](../papers/32719455.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-listed with [PTPRD](../genes/PTPRD.md), [PLCG1](../genes/PLCG1.md), [ARID1B](../genes/ARID1B.md), and [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) as low-frequency genes enriched in metastatic vs. primary TNBC samples. [PMID:32719455](../papers/32719455.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No established therapeutic relevance; functional impact of FAM47C mutation in metastatic TNBC is largely unknown and was not directly tested in the cited trial. [PMID:32719455](../papers/32719455.md)

## Open questions

- Functional impact of FAM47C somatic mutation in metastatic TNBC has not been characterized. Whether enrichment in metastatic vs. primary samples reflects a driver or passenger role is unknown. [PMID:32719455](../papers/32719455.md)
