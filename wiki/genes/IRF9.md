# IRF9

## Overview

IRF9 (Interferon Regulatory Factor 9) is a transcription factor that forms the ISGF3 complex with [STAT1](../genes/STAT1.md) and STAT2 to mediate type I IFN signaling. In [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md), IRF9 is part of the early IFN pathway activation in precursor lesions.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- IRF9 is an IFN-stimulated gene upregulated early in HGSOC development beginning at the p53 signature stage; part of an IFN-alpha and IFN-gamma pathway gene upregulation program also including STAT1, [IFITM1](../genes/IFITM1.md), [IRF7](../genes/IRF7.md), [ISG15](../genes/ISG15.md), TAP1, and [HLA-A](../genes/HLA-A.md) [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) — upregulated in fallopian tube precursor lesions (p53 signatures, STICs) and invasive cancer; studied by GeoMx spatial transcriptomics on 44 specimens from 43 individuals [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-upregulated with [HLA-A](../genes/HLA-A.md), [HLA-E](../genes/HLA-E.md), [IFITM1](../genes/IFITM1.md), [IRF7](../genes/IRF7.md), [ISG15](../genes/ISG15.md), [STAT1](../genes/STAT1.md), [MX1](../genes/MX1.md), TAP1 in the IFN response program of HGSOC precursors [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- Early IFN pathway activation including IRF9 in HGSOC precursors could serve as a biomarker panel for risk stratification of high-risk patients [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Open questions

- The relationship between chronic IFN activation (including IRF9 upregulation), IRDS gene signature emergence, and clinical chemoresistance in HGSOC is correlative and requires prospective investigation [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).
