# PPARGC1A

## Overview

PPARGC1A (PPARG Coactivator 1 Alpha; also PGC-1α) encodes a master transcriptional coactivator of mitochondrial biogenesis and oxidative metabolism. In cancer genomics, elevated PPARGC1A expression marks tumors with a non-Warburg, oxidative metabolic phenotype, particularly chromophobe renal cell carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Significantly elevated mRNA in chromophobe RCC vs normal kidney (p<1E-5), consistent with increased mitochondrial biogenesis and the non-Warburg metabolic phenotype that distinguishes ChRCC from ccRCC [PMID:25155756](../papers/25155756.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- ChRCC: overexpressed; marker of increased mitochondrial biogenesis; distinguishes ChRCC from ccRCC at the metabolic level [PMID:25155756](../papers/25155756.md)
