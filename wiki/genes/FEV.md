# FEV

## Overview

FEV (FEV Transcription Factor, ETS Family Member) encodes an ETS family transcription factor. In Ewing sarcoma, FEV is an ETS-family fusion partner of EWSR1, though its specific frequency in large cohorts is not well documented.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Listed as an ETS-family fusion partner of EWSR1 in Ewing sarcoma alongside FLI1, ERG, and ETV1; specific case count in the 112-tumor WGS cohort not separately reported [PMID:25223734](../papers/25223734.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- **ES (Ewing sarcoma)**: ETS-family fusion partner of EWSR1 [PMID:25223734](../papers/25223734.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- No co-occurrence or mutual exclusivity data specific to FEV reported in the corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic associations reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Frequency and clinical significance of EWSR1-FEV fusions in Ewing sarcoma relative to other ETS-family partners is not resolved in this cohort [PMID:25223734](../papers/25223734.md).
