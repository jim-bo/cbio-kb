**PMID:** 32561749 · **DOI:** 10.1038/s41597-020-0508-5 · **Journal:** Scientific Data (2020)

## TL;DR

Pollard et al. describe the Johns Hopkins Comprehensive Neurofibromatosis Center (JHCNC) [NF1](../genes/NF1.md) biospecimen repository — a clinically and genomically annotated collection of nerve sheath tumors and patient-derived models from patients with neurofibromatosis type 1 (NF1). The first data release comprises whole-exome and/or RNA sequencing of 55 tumor samples from 23 unique patients, including matched germline DNA, derived cell lines, and patient-derived xenografts (PDX). Tumor histologies span cutaneous neurofibromas (cNF), plexiform neurofibromas (pNF), atypical neurofibromatous neoplasms of uncertain biologic potential (ANNUBP), and malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumors ([MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md)). The cohort is the cBioPortal study [nst_nfosi_ntap](../datasets/nst_nfosi_ntap.md) and all primary data are released via the NF Data Portal / Synapse (syn4939902).

## Cohort & data

- **23 NF1 patients**, **55 tumor samples** total — sequencing matrix in the paper's Table 3 covers normal/germline DNA plus tumor, derived cell line, and PDX channels for each patient where available [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- Histologies: cutaneous neurofibroma (cNF), superficial diffuse infiltrating neurofibroma, plexiform neurofibroma (pNF) ([NFIB](../cancer_types/NFIB.md)), ANNUBP, and [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Assays:**
  - [whole-exome-seq](../methods/whole-exome-seq.md) — Agilent SureSelectXT HumanAllExon V6 capture, Illumina HiSeq2500, 125-bp paired-end; SNV calling with BWA-MEM (0.7.15) → GATK v4.0.1.1 VQSR → Annovar annotation, plus DeepVariant 0.8 cross-check; CNA via the GATK copy-ratio pipeline. Reference: 1000 Genomes phase 2 (GRCh37) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
  - [rna-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) — paired-end on flash-frozen tumor; Salmon 0.11.3 quantification against gencode v29; raw counts harmonized with prior pNF Schwann-cell RNA-seq resources. One sample (2-025 Neurofibroma) was dropped for >90% duplication, leaving 28 RNA-seq samples [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
  - [patient-derived-xenograft](../methods/patient-derived-xenograft.md) — tumor fragments dipped in Matrigel and implanted subcutaneously / pre-tibial / pre-sacral in NOD SCID gamma (NSG) mice; **PDX take rate 50% for [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md)** [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Dataset:** released as cBioPortal study [nst_nfosi_ntap](../datasets/nst_nfosi_ntap.md); primary data hosted on Synapse at syn4939902 (project URL `http://synapse.org/jhubiobank`); code at `github.com/Sage-Bionetworks/JHU-biobank` with a Zenodo-archived release (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.3726380) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Reference genome:** GRCh37 (hg19) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- A second cohort (51 additional WES + 28 additional RNA-seq samples) was described as being processed at the time of publication [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).

## Key findings

- This is a **data descriptor**, not a hypothesis-testing study; primary deliverable is a paired clinical/genomic resource for the NF research community covering cNF → pNF → ANNUBP → [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md) progression specimens, with derived cell-line and PDX models for ex vivo and in vivo work [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Genetic fidelity of derived models.** Mutational profiles of commonly mutated genes in plexiform neurofibroma and [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md) — including [NF1](../genes/NF1.md), [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md), [TP53](../genes/TP53.md), [EED](../genes/EED.md)/[SUZ12](../genes/SUZ12.md), and [ATRX](../genes/ATRX.md) — were preserved between the original tumor, the patient-derived cell line, and the PDX (Fig. 3a) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **PRC2 inactivation tracks histology.** Mutations in PRC2-complex genes ([EED](../genes/EED.md), [SUZ12](../genes/SUZ12.md)) were "notably absent, other than in the single [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md) sample" in this first-release cohort — consistent with prior reports that PRC2 loss is restricted to malignant transformation rather than benign neurofibromas [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Copy-ratio recapitulation in models.** For patient 2-031, the [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md) tumor showed a chromosome 17 copy-ratio reduction at the [NF1](../genes/NF1.md) locus (CR of blood = 1, CR of tumor = 0.5), and this 50% copy loss was preserved in both the derived cell line and the xenograft (Fig. 3c–d) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **RNA-seq quality.** Per-sample z-scored gene counts were similarly distributed across all 28 retained RNA-seq samples; PCA grouped samples by tumor type with consistent clustering across tumor tissue, cell line, and xenograft sample shapes (Fig. 4) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).

## Genes & alterations

- [NF1](../genes/NF1.md) — germline driver of the disease; somatic biallelic inactivation (e.g. 50% copy ratio at the chromosome 17 NF1 locus in patient 2-031 [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md)) is the signature alteration tracked across tumor → cell line → PDX [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) — included in the panel of commonly mutated genes plotted across the WES cohort; loss in malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumors (vs neurofibromas) is the historical prior the authors anchor their plot to [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) — among the commonly mutated genes profiled in the cohort's MPNST/pNF gene set [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- [EED](../genes/EED.md) and [SUZ12](../genes/SUZ12.md) — PRC2-complex tumor suppressors; in this first-release cohort, only the single [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md) sample carried a PRC2 alteration, consistent with PRC2 loss being a marker of malignant transformation [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- [ATRX](../genes/ATRX.md) — included in the panel of commonly mutated genes used to evaluate fidelity of derived models [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).

## Clinical implications

- This paper does not claim a biomarker or therapy. Its clinical implication is **resource-level**: it removes a long-standing bottleneck in NF1 nerve-sheath-tumor research by releasing matched tumor / cell line / PDX models with paired germline + somatic sequencing, enabling downstream biomarker and preclinical-drug studies (especially for plexiform neurofibroma, where surgery has been the only therapeutic option) [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- 50% MPNST PDX take rate provides a concrete planning number for future preclinical experiments [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).

## Limitations & open questions

- **Small cohort.** 23 patients / 55 samples; only a **single [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md)** sample contributes to the malignant arm, so PRC2-loss frequency estimates from this dataset alone are not interpretable — the finding is consistent with prior literature but not powered to refine it [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Sample dropout.** One Neurofibroma RNA-seq sample (2-025) was excluded for >90% read duplication, dropping the RNA-seq cohort to 28 samples [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Heterogeneous germline source.** When patient blood was unavailable, normal adjacent tissue was used as the germline reference, which may carry occult somatic alterations [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **No treatment / outcome arm.** As a biospecimen-and-sequencing descriptor, the paper does not link genomic features to therapy response or survival; that work is deferred to downstream users of the resource [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
- **Access model.** Data are released via Synapse under a request-for-access workflow rather than open download, which is appropriate for the consent posture but adds friction for re-analysis [PMID:32561749](../papers/32561749.md).
