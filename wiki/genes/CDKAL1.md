# CDKAL1

## Overview

CDKAL1 (CDK5 regulatory subunit associated protein 1-like 1) encodes a tRNA methylthiotransferase involved in translational fidelity. Germline variants in CDKAL1 are associated with type 2 diabetes risk. In [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md), CDKAL1 harbors clustered chromosomal breakpoints on chromosome 6, identifying it as a recurrently rearranged locus in this cancer type.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Clustered chromosomal breakpoints on chromosome 6 in [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) (110-tumor WGS cohort); identified alongside [TTC28](../genes/TTC28.md) (chromosome 22) as genes with recurrent structural variation in [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) [PMID:26168399](../papers/26168399.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md): recurrent chromosomal breakpoints at the CDKAL1 locus; functional consequences for [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) biology not characterized [PMID:26168399](../papers/26168399.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-occurs with [TTC28](../genes/TTC28.md) (chromosome 22 breakpoints) as recurrently rearranged chromosomal loci in SCLC [PMID:26168399](../papers/26168399.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic associations identified in this corpus.

## Open questions

- Whether CDKAL1 chromosomal breakpoints disrupt gene function or activate regulatory elements in SCLC remains unknown [PMID:26168399](../papers/26168399.md)
