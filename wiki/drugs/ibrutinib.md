# ibrutinib

## Overview

Covalent BTK inhibitor; standard-of-care in CLL and other B-cell malignancies.

## Evidence in the corpus

- In the 1,148-patient CLL map, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation in the absence of 17p deletion was not associated with adverse outcomes in U-CLL, consistent with the modern ibrutinib/venetoclax era reducing the prognostic impact of isolated [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mutation [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
- In the Beat AML program ex vivo screen (409 specimens / 363 AML patients), [FLT3](../genes/FLT3.md)-ITD alone or any [NPM1](../genes/NPM1.md) mutation (including triple FLT3-ITD/NPM1/DNMT3A) conferred significantly greater ex vivo ibrutinib sensitivity than wild-type; DNMT3A alone was insufficient, consistent with BTK/SYK pathway dependence driven by the FLT3-ITD/NPM1 co-mutation context [PMID:30333627](../papers/30333627.md).

## Resistance mechanisms

- Not directly characterized in this corpus; the paper cites the ibrutinib era as attenuating TP53-alone adverse prognosis [PMID:35927489](../papers/35927489.md).
