# SMARCC2

## Overview

SMARCC2 (SWI/SNF Related Matrix Associated Actin Dependent Regulator of Chromatin Subfamily C Member 2), also known as BAF170, is a core structural subunit of the SWI/SNF chromatin-remodelling complex. It is recurrently altered in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma as part of the broader SWI/SNF disruption landscape affecting over 42% of cases.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Part of SWI/SNF chromatin-remodelling lesions present in >42% of pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma cases (along with [SMARCA2](../genes/SMARCA2.md), [SMARCA4](../genes/SMARCA4.md), [SMARCC1](../genes/SMARCC1.md), [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md), [ARID1B](../genes/ARID1B.md), [PBRM1](../genes/PBRM1.md)); specific individual frequency not reported separately. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)**: SWI/SNF complex member recurrently altered; aggregate pathway disruption in >42% of cases. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-occurs with other SWI/SNF complex members ([SMARCA2](../genes/SMARCA2.md), [SMARCA4](../genes/SMARCA4.md), [SMARCC1](../genes/SMARCC1.md), [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md), [ARID1B](../genes/ARID1B.md), [PBRM1](../genes/PBRM1.md)) in PDA. [PMID:25855536](../papers/25855536.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported; SWI/SNF-deficient tumours may have vulnerabilities to targeted agents.

## Open questions

- Individual mutation frequency and functional role of SMARCC2 alterations specifically in PDA await detailed characterisation.
