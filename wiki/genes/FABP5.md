# FABP5

## Overview

FABP5 (fatty acid binding protein 5, epidermal type) suppresses fatty acid oxidation to block ferroptosis and contributes to immune evasion in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md). Anti-FABP5 (SBFI-103) is a preclinical lead with T-cell-activating and ICI-sensitizing effects.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- FABP5 upregulated in HCC; suppresses FAO to block ferroptosis; anti-FABP5 antibody (SBFI-103) activates T cells and sensitizes HCC to ICIs in preclinical models [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) — FABP5 upregulation blocks ferroptosis and drives immune evasion; preclinical target [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Part of the FABP1/FABP4/FABP5 upregulation axis in HCC [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- SBFI-103 (anti-FABP5, preclinical) activates T cells and sensitizes HCC to ICIs [PMID:38355797](../papers/38355797.md).

## Open questions

- SBFI-103 is preclinical; clinical translation (toxicity, selectivity) is open.
