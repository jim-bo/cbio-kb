# HEY1

## Overview

HEY1 (Hes Related Family BHLH Transcription Factor With YRPW Motif 1) is a basic helix-loop-helix transcription factor downstream of Notch signaling. It acts as a transcriptional repressor involved in development and differentiation. HEY1 has been implicated in mesenchymal chondrosarcoma via the HEY1-NCOA2 fusion.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Observed as an altered gene in individual synchronous DCIS/IDC-NST cases in a WES study of paired ductal carcinoma in situ and invasive ductal carcinoma (n=25 patients, MSKCC) [PMID:32220886](../papers/32220886.md)
