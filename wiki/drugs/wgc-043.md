# WGc-043

## Overview

WGc-043 is an investigational mRNA therapeutic vaccine encoding EBV antigens (LMP1/LMP2/EBNA1). It is evaluated in EBV-positive recurrent/metastatic nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- mRNA EBV vaccine WGc-043 in EBV+ R/M NPC (NCT05714748, n=12): ORR 16.7%; no grade ≥3 treatment-related adverse events [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
