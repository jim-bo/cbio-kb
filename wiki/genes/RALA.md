# RALA

## Overview

RALA (RAS like proto-oncogene A) is a small GTPase of the RAS superfamily involved in vesicular trafficking, cell migration, and proliferation signaling. It is an effector of RAS family GTPases operating through the RALGDS pathway. In lung adenocarcinoma proteogenomics, RALA has been nominated alongside other small GTPase regulators as a component of signaling networks perturbed by driver mutations.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified in the context of RAS-effector and small GTPase signaling in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) proteogenomics (CPTAC cohort, n=110 treatment-naïve tumors); implicated via pathway-level analysis downstream of [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) mutations [PMID:32649874](../papers/32649874.md)
