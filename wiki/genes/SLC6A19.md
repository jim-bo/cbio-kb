# SLC6A19

## Overview

SLC6A19 encodes the B(0)AT1 neutral amino acid transporter (solute carrier family 6 member 19), located at chromosomal position 5p15.33 near [TERT](../genes/TERT.md). In neuroblastoma, SLC6A19 was identified as a bystander gene markedly upregulated specifically in TERT-rearranged tumors, reflecting directional spread of the hijacked enhancer chromatin landscape caused by structural rearrangements upstream of [TERT](../genes/TERT.md).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Distal neighbour of [TERT](../genes/TERT.md) at 5p15.33 that becomes markedly upregulated only in TERT-rearranged neuroblastoma tumors, reflecting directional spread of the hijacked enhancer chromatin landscape; not upregulated in MYCN-amplified or ATRX-mutant tumors [PMID:26466568](../papers/26466568.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[NBL](../cancer_types/NBL.md) (neuroblastoma)** — Expression upregulation is a consequence of TERT-rearrangement-driven enhancer hijacking; co-upregulated with [SLC6A18](../genes/SLC6A18.md) as a marker of the TERT-rearranged molecular subtype [PMID:26466568](../papers/26466568.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-upregulated with [SLC6A18](../genes/SLC6A18.md) in TERT-rearranged neuroblastoma; [CLPTM1L](../genes/CLPTM1L.md) (proximal TERT neighbour) is notably not upregulated [PMID:26466568](../papers/26466568.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic target; upregulation reflects the enhancer hijacking mechanism of TERT activation [PMID:26466568](../papers/26466568.md)

## Open questions

- Whether SLC6A19 upregulation is purely a bystander effect or contributes functionally to neuroblastoma biology is unknown [PMID:26466568](../papers/26466568.md)
