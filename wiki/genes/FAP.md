# FAP

## Overview

FAP (Fibroblast Activation Protein Alpha) encodes a serine protease expressed on activated stromal fibroblasts and mesenchymal cells. In cancer, FAP is a marker of cancer-associated fibroblasts and epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition (EMT), where its expression is associated with immunosuppressive tumor microenvironments and resistance to immune checkpoint blockade.
