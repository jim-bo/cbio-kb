# HERC1

## Overview

HERC1 (HECT And RLD Domain Containing E3 Ubiquitin Protein Ligase 1) is a large E3 ubiquitin ligase. A HERC1 missense mutation (P3278S) was identified as the source of an immunogenic neoantigen that drove a validated CD8+ T cell response in a [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md) exceptional responder with [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md), establishing HERC1 as a neoantigen-generating locus of clinical significance.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- P3278S missense mutation generated the validated HLA-A-restricted neoantigen ASNASSAAK that drove a measurable, treatment-induced CD8+ T cell response in an exceptional responder to [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md) in [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) [PMID:25765070](../papers/25765070.md)
