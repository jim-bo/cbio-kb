# PRDM2

## Overview

PRDM2 (PR/SET domain 2) encodes a histone methyltransferase and transcriptional repressor belonging to the PRDM family. It has been implicated as a candidate tumor suppressor, particularly in glioma, where variants have been identified in recurrent tumors.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Variants identified in recurrent [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) PDX at frequency of ~7% of 28 recurrent PDX in the Mayo PDX panel; some also seen in primary PDX (possible germline origin) [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- GBM: recurrent-tumor variants observed in PDX models [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-occurs with other recurrent GBM alterations ([LTBP4](../genes/LTBP4.md), [IGF1R](../genes/IGF1R.md) variants) at similar frequencies in the Mayo PDX panel [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No targeted therapies reported in this corpus.

## Open questions

- Germline vs somatic origin unclear for variants seen in both primary and recurrent PDX [PMID:31852831](../papers/31852831.md)
