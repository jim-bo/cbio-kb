# SHR-1701

## Overview

SHR-1701 is an investigational bispecific antibody fusion targeting [CD274](../genes/CD274.md) (PD-L1) and TGF-β signaling. It is evaluated in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)) and other solid tumors.
