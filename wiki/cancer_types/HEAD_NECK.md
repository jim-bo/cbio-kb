# Head and Neck (HEAD_NECK)

## Overview

HEAD_NECK is the top-level OncoTree tissue category for head and neck cancers (parent: TISSUE, level 1). It encompasses all head and neck tumor types including squamous cell carcinomas, salivary gland tumors, thyroid cancers, and others. In the corpus this code appears as an anatomical site category in radiation-oncology imaging studies; specific genomic-cohort pages use more specific OncoTree codes (e.g., [HNSC](../cancer_types/HNSC.md) for head and neck squamous cell carcinoma).

## Cohorts in the corpus

- Head and neck represented as an anatomical treatment site in a systematic review of 111 deep learning image-synthesis studies, particularly for MR-to-CT synthesis and deformable registration: CycleGAN-based approach reduced average landmark error from 9.8±3.1 mm to 6.0±2.1 mm in head/neck (McKenzie et al., 2019). [PMID:33305538](../papers/33305538.md)
