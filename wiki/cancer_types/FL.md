# Follicular Lymphoma (FL)

## Overview

Follicular lymphoma (FL) is an indolent B-cell non-Hodgkin lymphoma (NHL) of germinal-center B-cell origin. It sits at OncoTree level 5 under the MBN (Mature B-Cell Neoplasms) lineage. The t(14;18) BCL2/IGH translocation is a hallmark, but somatic mutations in histone-modifying genes — particularly [KMT2D](../genes/KMT2D.md) — are now recognized as equally prevalent founding events.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- 35 FL tumors analyzed as part of a 127-sample B-cell NHL cohort (plus 10 DLBCL cell lines); 14 cases with matched tumor/normal whole-genome or whole-exome sequencing; validation of KMT2D in 35 FL and 37 DLBCL via Illumina amplicon resequencing; validation of [MEF2B](../genes/MEF2B.md) in 261 FL cases via Sanger sequencing; dataset [nhl_bcgsc_2011](../datasets/nhl_bcgsc_2011.md). [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- [KMT2D](../genes/KMT2D.md) (MLL2): mutated in 89% (31/35) of FL; 91% of mutations inactivating (frameshift indels, nonsense, splice-site); bi-allelic inactivation confirmed in 8 FL cases; as prevalent as t(14;18), suggesting a foundational role in FL pathogenesis. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- [MEF2B](../genes/MEF2B.md): recurrent non-synonymous mutations in 15.3% of FL; 59.4% of variants cluster at four residues (K4, Y69, N81, D83); restricted to GCB subtype; cooperates with CREBBP/EP300 in histone acetylation. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- [EZH2](../genes/EZH2.md): Y641 gain-of-function hotspot with allelic imbalance favouring the mutant allele; GCB-enriched; enhances H3K27 trimethylation. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md): inactivating mutations (nonsense, missense); HAT activity disruption. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- [BCL2](../genes/BCL2.md): missense mutations via somatic hypermutation; allelic imbalance favouring the translocated allele. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- [TNFRSF14](../genes/TNFRSF14.md): nonsense mutations; tumor suppressor; GCB-restricted. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)

## Subtypes

- GCB subtype dominates FL; characterized by KMT2D, MEF2B, [EZH2](../genes/EZH2.md), and [TNFRSF14](../genes/TNFRSF14.md) mutations; distinct from ABC-enriched mutational profile seen in DLBCL. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)

## Therapeutic landscape

- KMT2D inactivation (89%) and EZH2 gain-of-function mutations implicate disrupted histone methylation as a therapeutic target; EZH2 inhibitors (e.g., tazemetostat) have clinical relevance in EZH2-mutant FL. [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
