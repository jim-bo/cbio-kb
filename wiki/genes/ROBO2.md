# ROBO2

## Overview

ROBO2 encodes roundabout guidance receptor 2, a SLIT ligand receptor involved in axon repulsion and cell migration. In pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma, ROBO2 is mutated in approximately 5% of tumors and low ROBO2 expression associates with poor prognosis, implicating it as a candidate tumor suppressor within the SLIT-ROBO pathway.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Mutations in approximately 5% of pancreatic cancer tumors in WES of 142 ICGC tumors; low ROBO2 expression associates with poor prognosis in PDAC [PMID:23103869](../papers/23103869.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma ([PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md)): mutated in ~5% of tumors; low expression is a poor prognostic biomarker [PMID:23103869](../papers/23103869.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-altered with [SLIT2](../genes/SLIT2.md), [ROBO1](../genes/ROBO1.md), and [ROBO3](../genes/ROBO3.md) as part of the SLIT-ROBO axon guidance pathway in pancreatic cancer [PMID:23103869](../papers/23103869.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- ROBO2 expression level may serve as a prognostic biomarker in PDAC; low ROBO2 expression may identify patients with poor prognosis [PMID:23103869](../papers/23103869.md)

## Open questions

- Functional validation of ROBO2 mutations as true drivers in PDAC is incomplete; the prognostic association of low expression requires prospective validation [PMID:23103869](../papers/23103869.md)
