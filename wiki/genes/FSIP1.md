# FSIP1

## Overview

FSIP1 (Fibrous Sheath Interacting Protein 1) is a structural protein primarily associated with sperm flagella. It was identified as a loss-of-function burden candidate tumor suppressor in desmoplastic melanoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Loss-of-function burden candidate tumor suppressor in desmoplastic melanoma (WGS/WES cohort); identified alongside [CDH2](../genes/CDH2.md), [IL36A](../genes/IL36A.md), and [PAK3](../genes/PAK3.md) as LOF candidates [PMID:26343386](../papers/26343386.md)
