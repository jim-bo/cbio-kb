# Renal Non-Clear Cell Carcinoma (NCCRCC)

## Overview

Renal Non-Clear Cell Carcinoma (NCCRCC) is an OncoTree grouping node under [Renal Cell Carcinoma (RCC)](RCC.md) that encompasses all [RCC](../cancer_types/RCC.md) subtypes lacking clear-cell histology. Its OncoTree children include [Papillary Renal Cell Carcinoma (PRCC)](PRCC.md), [Chromophobe Renal Cell Carcinoma (CHRCC)](CHRCC.md), [Translocation-Associated Renal Cell Carcinoma (TRCC)](TRCC.md), and [Unclassified Renal Cell Carcinoma (URCC)](URCC.md). High-grade primary tumors that cannot be assigned to any established subtype after rigorous pathologic review are classified as [URCC](../cancer_types/URCC.md); URCC comprises ~4–5% of all RCC [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [urcc_mskcc_2016](../datasets/urcc_mskcc_2016.md) — 62 high-grade primary unclassified RCCs (URCC subtype) from MSKCC; the cohort explicitly excluded MiTF-family translocation tumors (TFE3/TFEB IHC/FISH negative), confirming their NCCRCC classification [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).

See also subtype-specific cohorts under [PRCC](PRCC.md), [CHRCC](CHRCC.md), [TRCC](TRCC.md).

## Recurrent alterations

Alterations vary by subtype; see individual subtype pages. Cross-NCCRCC observations:

- [NF2](../genes/NF2.md) — recurrent in URCC (18%); also previously reported in small numbers of pRCC and collecting-duct RCC [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).
- [SETD2](../genes/SETD2.md) — recurrent in both [PRCC](../cancer_types/PRCC.md) and URCC (18% overall in URCC, 44% in NF2-loss URCC) [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).
- mTOR pathway ([MTOR](../genes/MTOR.md), [TSC1](../genes/TSC1.md), [TSC2](../genes/TSC2.md), [PTEN](../genes/PTEN.md)) — hyperactivating mutations define 21% of URCC and 23% of [CHRCC](../cancer_types/CHRCC.md), making mTOR inhibition a cross-NCCRCC candidate strategy [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).
- [FH](../genes/FH.md) — FH-deficient subset in URCC (6%); germline [FH](../genes/FH.md) mutations underlie HLRCC [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).
- [ALK](../genes/ALK.md) fusions — [TPM3](../genes/TPM3.md)–[ALK](../genes/ALK.md) fusion in 1 URCC case; TFE3/TFEB fusions define [TRCC](../cancer_types/TRCC.md) [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).

## Subtypes

- [Papillary Renal Cell Carcinoma (PRCC)](PRCC.md)
- [Chromophobe Renal Cell Carcinoma (CHRCC)](CHRCC.md)
- [Translocation-Associated Renal Cell Carcinoma (TRCC)](TRCC.md)
- [Unclassified Renal Cell Carcinoma (URCC)](URCC.md) — high-grade tumors not assignable to established subtypes; ~4–5% of all RCC; four molecularly distinct subsets defined [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- mTOR-pathway alterations across multiple NCCRCC subtypes (URCC 21%, CHRCC 23%) support subset-stratified mTOR-inhibitor trials [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).
- MET-targeted therapy is supported in PRCC ([MET](../genes/MET.md) mutations 15%, chr7/MET amplification ~70%).
- NF2-loss URCC: candidate for YAP-pathway inhibitors and WEE1 synthetic-lethality (concurrent [SETD2](../genes/SETD2.md) mutation) [PMID:27713405](../papers/27713405.md).
