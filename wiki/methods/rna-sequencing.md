# RNA sequencing (study-specific)

## Overview

Bulk RNA sequencing for transcriptomic profiling of tumor samples; see also the broader [RNA-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) page. This page tracks corpus studies that specifically reference "RNA-sequencing" in their methods without linking to the canonical [rna-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) slug.

## Used by

- [PMID:36611031](../papers/36611031.md) — RNA-seq performed on 5 CAC and 6 CRC patients for Wnt pathway ssGSEA scoring; showed lower Wnt ssGSEA score in CAC vs. CRC (p=0.13); also applied to nine patient-derived xenografts (PDXs) for pathway analysis; identified functional Wnt independence of CAC including APC-mutant tumors [PMID:36611031](../papers/36611031.md).

## Notes

- The Wnt RNA-seq cohort was small (5 CAC vs. 6 CRC; nine PDXs); the difference was suggestive (p=0.13) but not formally significant.
