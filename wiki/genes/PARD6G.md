# PARD6G

## Overview

PARD6G (Par-6 Family Cell Polarity Regulator Gamma) encodes a scaffolding protein involved in cell polarity establishment via interactions with CDC42, [RHOA](../genes/RHOA.md), and atypical PKC complexes. In cancer genomics it has been identified as a structural variant target in cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Structural variant (LINX/PURPLE) detected in metastatic [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) WGS cohort (n=25); involved in copy-number rearrangement landscape [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) (cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma):** structural variant detected in metastatic nodal disease WGS cohort [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Detected alongside [PTPRD](../genes/PTPRD.md), [RAF1](../genes/RAF1.md), and [PTPN13](../genes/PTPN13.md) in the LINX/PURPLE structural variant calls of the same metastatic CSCC cohort [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic targeting reported in corpus.

## Open questions

- Functional significance of the PARD6G structural variant in CSCC metastasis is unresolved; experimental validation is lacking [PMID:35982973](../papers/35982973.md)
