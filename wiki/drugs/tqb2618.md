# TQB2618

## Overview

TQB2618 is an investigational anti-TIM-3 monoclonal antibody targeting [HAVCR2](../genes/HAVCR2.md) (TIM-3), a compensatory checkpoint receptor on exhausted T cells. It is evaluated alone and in combination with penpulimab in nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)).

## Evidence in the corpus

- TQB2618 TIM-3 inhibitor ± penpulimab in R/M NPC (NCT05563480, n=17): ORR 0%; median PFS 1.6 months — limited single-agent and combination efficacy observed [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
