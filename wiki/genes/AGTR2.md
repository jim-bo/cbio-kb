# AGTR2

## Overview

AGTR2 (Angiotensin II Receptor Type 2) encodes a G-protein-coupled receptor that mediates the effects of angiotensin II. In cancer genomics, recurrent missense mutations in AGTR2 have been observed in ER+ breast cancer, flagging it as a possible therapeutic target. Functional consequences of these mutations in the tumor context remain to be established.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Recurrent P271L missense mutations observed in 6 ER+ breast cancer tumors in the METABRIC 2,433-sample cohort; flagged as a possible therapeutic target [PMID:27161491](../papers/27161491.md)
