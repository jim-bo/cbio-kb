# Breast (BREAST)

## Overview

BREAST is the top-level OncoTree tissue category for breast cancers (parent: TISSUE, level 1). It encompasses all breast tumor types. Specific histologic subtypes in the corpus include invasive ductal carcinoma ([IDC](../cancer_types/IDC.md)), invasive lobular carcinoma ([ILC](../cancer_types/ILC.md)), ductal carcinoma in situ ([DCIS](../cancer_types/DCIS.md)), and the broader invasive breast carcinoma code [BRCA](../cancer_types/BRCA.md). In some corpus papers BREAST appears as an anatomical site category in imaging/radiation-oncology contexts.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [brca_cptac_2020](../datasets/brca_cptac_2020.md) — 122 primary invasive breast tumors profiled by integrated multi-omics (WES, RNA-seq, TMT proteomics, phospho/acetyl-proteomics). [PMID:33212010](../papers/33212010.md)
- [brca_jup_msk_2020](../datasets/brca_jup_msk_2020.md) — 3 juvenile papillomatosis lesions including one with coexisting [DCIS](../cancer_types/DCIS.md) and IDC-NST. [PMID:33263939](../papers/33263939.md)
- Breast represented as anatomical treatment site in a systematic review of 111 deep learning image-synthesis studies. [PMID:33305538](../papers/33305538.md)
