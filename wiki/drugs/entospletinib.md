# entospletinib

## Overview

Entospletinib is a selective inhibitor of spleen tyrosine kinase ([SYK](../genes/SYK.md)), which has been investigated in [AML](../cancer_types/AML.md) and B-cell malignancies. [SYK](../genes/SYK.md) is a downstream interacting target of FLT3-ITD, providing a mechanistic rationale for entospletinib activity in FLT3-ITD/NPM1-co-mutant [AML](../cancer_types/AML.md).

## Evidence in the corpus

- In the Beat AML program, entospletinib showed the same [FLT3](../genes/FLT3.md)-ITD/[NPM1](../genes/NPM1.md) co-mutation sensitivity pattern as [ibrutinib](../drugs/ibrutinib.md) in ex vivo profiling of 409 specimens from 363 AML patients; cases with FLT3-ITD alone or any [NPM1](../genes/NPM1.md) mutation were significantly more sensitive than wild-type (FDR <0.1), consistent with SYK being an FLT3-ITD interacting target [PMID:30333627](../papers/30333627.md).
- [DNMT3A](../genes/DNMT3A.md) alone or DNMT3A/FLT3 double-mutation did not confer entospletinib sensitivity, whereas the triple FLT3-ITD/NPM1/DNMT3A combination did, implying a NPM1-driven rather than DNMT3A-driven mechanism [PMID:30333627](../papers/30333627.md).

## Resistance mechanisms

- [DNMT3A](../genes/DNMT3A.md) mutation alone was not sufficient for entospletinib sensitivity; NPM1 mutation (in combination with FLT3-ITD) was the critical co-mutational determinant [PMID:30333627](../papers/30333627.md).
