# capecitabine

## Overview

Oral 5-FU prodrug used in colorectal and other GI adenocarcinomas.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Listed among standard colorectal-style first-line regimens (alongside FOLFOX/FOLFIRI/FOLFIRINOX) offered to appendiceal adenocarcinoma patients; GNAS-mut predominant MAAP patients appear unlikely to benefit from such regimens given a 6% first-line response rate vs 50% in RAS-mut predominant MAAP (P=.03) [PMID:36493333](../papers/36493333.md).

## Resistance mechanisms

- GNAS-mut predominant molecular subtype of mucinous appendiceal adenocarcinoma associates with chemotherapy resistance to fluoropyrimidine-based regimens [PMID:36493333](../papers/36493333.md).
