# Extrahepatic Cholangiocarcinoma (EHCH)

## Overview

Extrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma arising from the extrahepatic biliary tree.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [hcc_msk_2024](../datasets/hcc_msk_2024.md) — EHC cases used as a training reference class (together with [GBC](GBC.md)) for the hidden-genome classifier of intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma, within a broader MSK biliary/HCC MSK-IMPACT cohort [PMID:38864854](../papers/38864854.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- EHC/GBC genetic class characterized by [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md), [SMAD4](../genes/SMAD4.md), and [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) loss; serves as the "biliary-class" reference for [IHCH](IHCH.md) classification [PMID:38864854](../papers/38864854.md).
- Narrative review of etiology-driven genomic landscape of eCCA: [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) in 37-46%, [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) in 35-68%, [SMAD4](../genes/SMAD4.md) in ~25% (vs. ~4% in iCCA; loss associated with poor prognosis), [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md) in 14%, CDKN2A/B in 19%, [ERBB2](../genes/ERBB2.md) in 3-20%; PSC-associated eCCA shows [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) (35.5%), [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) (28.5%), [CDKN2A](../genes/CDKN2A.md) (14.5%), [SMAD4](../genes/SMAD4.md) (11.3%), [ERBB2](../genes/ERBB2.md) overexpression [PMID:25526346](../papers/25526346.md).
- Review encompasses extrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma (eCCA/dCCA) as part of the CCA spectrum where gut microbiota dysbiosis, bile acid disturbances ([NR1H4](../genes/NR1H4.md)/FXR downregulation, [S1PR2](../genes/S1PR2.md)/taurocholic-acid-driven ERK/AKT/NF-kB), and LPS/TLR4 signaling converge to drive tumor progression. [PMID:25608663](../papers/25608663.md)
- One of three periampullary tumor cohorts (44 distal bile-duct/cholangiocarcinoma cases); TP53, KRAS, SMAD4, and CDKN2A were the four MutSig-CV-significant genes in this subset; IDH1/IDH2 and BAP1 were essentially absent, distinguishing extrahepatic from intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma [PMID:26804919](../papers/26804919.md)
- In the ICGC 489-CCA cohort, extrahepatic (distal) tumors are enriched in Clusters 1 and 2 (fluke-positive), characterized by ERBB2 amplifications (10.4% fluke-pos), TP53 mutations, and CpG-island hypermethylation; anatomical classification alone was insufficient to predict molecular subtype or prognosis. [PMID:28667006](../papers/28667006.md)
- MSK-IMPACT profiling (n=37 extrahepatic cases): enriched for KRAS (38% vs 7% IHCH, q<0.001), SMAD4 (q=0.001), TP53 (q=0.007), and STK11 (q=0.031) vs intrahepatic; KRAS associated with shorter OS in advanced disease (p=0.026) [PMID:29848569](../papers/29848569.md)
