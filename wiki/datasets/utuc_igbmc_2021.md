# Upper Tract Urothelial Carcinoma (IGBMC, Genome Biology 2021)

## Overview

The utuc_igbmc_2021 cohort was generated by Su et al. (Genome Biology 2021) from fresh-frozen surgical resections sourced from the Pitié-Salpêtrière Hospital biobank (Paris, France). It is the first comprehensive integrative multi-omic profiling of upper-tract urothelial carcinoma ([UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md)), combining whole-exome sequencing, RNA-seq, and Infinium MethylationEPIC array data from 40 microsatellite-stable patients (20 non-muscle-invasive [NMI] + 20 muscle-invasive [MI]). Median follow-up 53 months (range 2–98); 18 patients recurred, 12 died of disease. The cBioPortal study reports WES data from the 30 tumor–normal paired cases (hg19 reference).

## Composition

- **Total patients:** 40 (20 NMI + 20 MI UTUC).
- **Cancer type:** [UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md) — all microsatellite-stable; one FGFR3-TACC3 fusion detected (UTUC-11).
- **WES:** 30 paired tumor–normal (Agilent SureSelect 50 Mb, Illumina HiSeq 2000; tumor 79–158×, germline 43–80×): 15 NMI + 15 MI.
- **RNA-seq:** 20 cases (15 MI + 5 NMI; NuGen Ovation V2, 75 bp PE, aligned GRCh37/hg19).
- **Methylation:** Infinium MethylationEPIC array on 35 UTUC + 8 normal adjacent tissues; 836,691 probes after filtering.
- **Tumor mutation burden:** Mean 2.9 ± 3.5 mut/Mb; median 1.5 (range 0.3–15.7).

## Assays / panels (linked)

- [whole-exome-seq](../methods/whole-exome-seq.md) — Agilent SureSelect 50 Mb capture; [MutSigCV](../methods/mutsigcv.md) for SMGs; [GISTIC](../methods/gistic.md) for focal SCNAs; [deconstructSigs](../methods/deconstructsigs.md) + [nmf-clustering](../methods/nmf-clustering.md) for mutational signatures.
- [rna-seq](../methods/rna-seq.md) — [consensusMIBC](../methods/cms-classifier.md) and BASE47 subtype assignment; [MCP-counter](../methods/mcp-counter.md) and [ESTIMATE](../methods/estimate.md) for immune/stromal deconvolution.
- [epic-methylation-array](../methods/epic-methylation-array.md) — [ChAMP](../methods/champ.md) for DMP/DMR analysis; [iClusterBayes](../methods/icluster.md) for multi-omics integration; MeTIL score for methylation-derived TIL inference.
- [sanger-sequencing](../methods/sanger-sequencing.md) — Targeted validation of [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) hotspots (S249C, Y373C) in 35 cases.

## Notable findings derived from this cohort

- Three significantly mutated genes by MutSigCV (FDR<0.05): [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) 50%, [KDM6A](../genes/KDM6A.md) 27%, and novel [ZFP36L1](../genes/ZFP36L1.md) 20% [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md).
- DNA methylation analysis defined two epigenetic clusters: EpiC-high (hypermethylated, immune-infiltrated, MI-enriched, SWI/SNF-mutant) and EpiC-low (hypomethylated, immune-depleted, NMI-enriched, FGFR3-mutant) [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md).
- EpiC-high independently predicted shorter [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) (HR=6.57; 95% CI 1.87–23.04; P=0.038) and shorter distant-metastasis-free survival (HR=6.55; P=0.036) — the only independent prognostic variable in the cohort [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md).
- FGFR3-mutant UTUC is globally hypomethylated (82,991/84,717 differential probes hypomethylated vs FGFR3-WT) and immune-cold (lower MeTIL score, P=7.8×10⁻⁵) [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md).
- [ZFP36L1](../genes/ZFP36L1.md) siRNA knockdown in TCCSUP bladder cells caused EMT-like morphology and increased migration without affecting proliferation, supporting a tumor-suppressor role for [ZFP36](../genes/ZFP36.md) family members in urothelial cancer [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md).
- The EpiC methylation signature projected onto TCGA MI-BLCA ([blca_tcga_pub_2017](../datasets/blca_tcga_pub_2017.md)) recovered analogous BLCA-C1 vs BLCA-C2 subgroups with matching OS differences (HR=1.38; 95% CI 1.03–1.85; P=0.035) [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md).
- FGFR3–[KMT2C](../genes/KMT2C.md) co-occurrence (log odds ratio=3.1, P=0.04) validated in [utuc_mskcc_2015](../datasets/utuc_mskcc_2015.md) (LOR=2.9, P<0.001) [PMID:33397444](../papers/33397444.md).
