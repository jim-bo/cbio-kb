# SLC16A4

## Overview

SLC16A4 encodes monocarboxylate transporter 5 (MCT5), a member of the solute carrier family 16 involved in transmembrane transport of monocarboxylates such as lactate and pyruvate. It was identified as a significantly mutated gene in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma, potentially linking metabolic transport dysregulation to PDAC pathogenesis.
