# CHGB

## Overview

CHGB (chromogranin B, also known as secretogranin I) is a neuroendocrine secretory protein of the granin family. In neuroblastoma single-cell transcriptomics, CHGB is part of the noradrenergic/adrenergic gene panel marking noradrenergic tumor clusters and postnatal chromaffin cells in normal adrenal gland.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CHGB is part of the noradrenergic/adrenergic panel alongside [PHOX2B](../genes/PHOX2B.md), [PHOX2A](../genes/PHOX2A.md), [TH](../genes/TH.md), [DBH](../genes/DBH.md), [CHGA](../genes/CHGA.md), [PNMT](../genes/PNMT.md), and [ISL1](../genes/ISL1.md); these genes mark the NOR (noradrenergic) tumor clusters and postnatal chromaffin cells identified by single-nuclei RNA-seq of 11 neuroblastoma tumors [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Neuroblastoma (NBL) — CHGB marks the noradrenergic/adrenergic low-risk tumor clusters (nC5/nC7/nC8/nC9) that are enriched in low-risk disease; expression is characteristic of adrenergic/chromaffin differentiation [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [CHGA](../genes/CHGA.md), [PHOX2B](../genes/PHOX2B.md), [TH](../genes/TH.md), [DBH](../genes/DBH.md), and [NTRK1](../genes/NTRK1.md) in the noradrenergic low-risk clusters; mutually exclusive with the [NTRK2](../genes/NTRK2.md)/[ALK](../genes/ALK.md)/[MYCN](../genes/MYCN.md)-high undifferentiated nC3 high-risk cluster [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- CHGB-positive noradrenergic tumor cells characterize low-risk neuroblastoma; their expression profile is associated with favorable prognosis through adrenergic differentiation [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md).

## Open questions

- The role of CHGB protein secretion in neuroblastoma tumor microenvironment biology is not addressed in the corpus.
