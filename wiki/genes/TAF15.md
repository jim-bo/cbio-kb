# TAF15

## Overview

TAF15 (TATA-Box Binding Protein Associated Factor 15) is a TET-family RNA-binding protein. In Ewing sarcoma, TAF15 serves as an alternative N-terminal fusion partner for EWSR1 in rare cases where the canonical EWSR1-FLI1/ERG fusions are absent.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- TAF15 identified as an alternative TET-family N-terminal fusion partner with EWSR1 in rare Ewing sarcoma cases (alongside FUS); context is translocation variant tumors where the canonical EWSR1-ETS fusions are absent [PMID:25223734](../papers/25223734.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- ES (Ewing sarcoma) — rare alternative EWSR1 fusion partner; the canonical EWSR1-FLI1 fusion accounts for ~90% of cases [PMID:25223734](../papers/25223734.md)
