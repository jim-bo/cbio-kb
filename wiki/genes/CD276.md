# CD276

## Overview

CD276 (also known as B7-H3) is an immune checkpoint molecule overexpressed on tumor cells and tumor vasculature across many solid tumor types. Unlike other B7-family members, its receptor on T cells remains incompletely characterized. B7-H3 overexpression is associated with poor prognosis and immune evasion; it is a therapeutic target for antibody-drug conjugates (ADCs).
