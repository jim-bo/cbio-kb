# KRT20

## Overview

KRT20 (Keratin 20) encodes a type I intermediate filament protein expressed in mature gastrointestinal epithelial cells and umbrella cells of the urothelium. In bladder cancer, KRT20 is a defining marker of umbrella-cell-like differentiation within the luminal mRNA subtype of muscle-invasive bladder cancer (MIBC).

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Highest expression in the new **luminal** MIBC subtype (6% of 412 tumors, TCGA [BLCA](../cancer_types/BLCA.md) 2017), alongside [UPK1A](../genes/UPK1A.md), [UPK2](../genes/UPK2.md), and [SNX31](../genes/SNX31.md), suggesting umbrella-cell-like differentiation. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **[Bladder Urothelial Carcinoma (BLCA)](../cancer_types/BLCA.md):** KRT20 is among the umbrella-cell differentiation markers most highly expressed in the luminal MIBC subtype; useful for subtype assignment in future clinical assays. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with [UPK1A](../genes/UPK1A.md), [UPK2](../genes/UPK2.md), and [SNX31](../genes/SNX31.md) in the luminal MIBC subtype; mutually low vs. basal markers [KRT5](../genes/KRT5.md), [KRT6A](../genes/KRT6A.md), [KRT14](../genes/KRT14.md), and [CD44](../genes/CD44.md) in the basal-squamous subtype. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct targeted therapy against KRT20 is reported in this corpus. As a luminal subtype marker, KRT20 expression patterns may inform subtype-stratified therapeutic decisions (e.g., FGFR3-targeted therapy for luminal-papillary tumors). [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)

## Open questions

- Optimal therapeutic strategies for the luminal MIBC subtype (defined in part by KRT20 expression) are not yet established; comparison of neoadjuvant chemotherapy vs mutation-targeted therapy is proposed for future trials. [PMID:28988769](../papers/28988769.md)
