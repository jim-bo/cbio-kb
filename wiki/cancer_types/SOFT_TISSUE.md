# Soft Tissue (SOFT_TISSUE)

## Overview

SOFT_TISSUE is the OncoTree umbrella category for all cancers arising in soft tissue, encompassing leiomyosarcoma, liposarcoma, synovial sarcoma, rhabdomyosarcoma, undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma, angiosarcoma, [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md), [GIST](../cancer_types/GIST.md), and many other mesenchymal malignancies. In cBioPortal cohorts this tag is used as a grouping for soft tissue sarcomas distinct from bone sarcomas.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [sarcoma_msk_2022](../datasets/sarcoma_msk_2022.md): 6,067 soft tissue sarcomas (81.0% of 7,494) profiled by FoundationOne Heme across 44 histologies [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).
- [sarcoma_mskcc_2022](../datasets/sarcoma_mskcc_2022.md): bone and soft tissue sarcomas combined (2,138 total) profiled by MSK-IMPACT across 45 OncoTree entities [PMID:35705560](../papers/35705560.md).

## Recurrent alterations

- In a 7,494-sarcoma cohort, soft tissue sarcomas (81.0%) were stratified into translocation-associated (23.0%, n=1,724) vs genomically complex/other (77.0%, n=5,770); complex sarcomas had significantly higher rates of p53 pathway (58.2% vs 12.9%), Rb pathway (56% vs 15.8%), and TMB (2.4 vs 1.6 mut/Mb; p<4×10⁻⁶¹) [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).
- In a 2,138-sarcoma MSK-IMPACT cohort, 31.7% of all sarcomas had ≥1 OncoKB-actionable alteration; 2.6% had kinase-gene fusions ([ALK](../genes/ALK.md), [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md), [FGFR1](../genes/FGFR1.md)–4, [NTRK1](../genes/NTRK1.md)–3, [RET](../genes/RET.md), [ROS1](../genes/ROS1.md)); TMB ≥10 mut/Mb in 3.9% [PMID:35705560](../papers/35705560.md).

## Subtypes

Key soft tissue sarcoma subtypes in the corpus: [ANGS](../cancer_types/ANGS.md), [DSRCT](../cancer_types/DSRCT.md), [EPIS](../cancer_types/EPIS.md), [ERMS](../cancer_types/ERMS.md), [ARMS](../cancer_types/ARMS.md), [ES](../cancer_types/ES.md), [GIST](../cancer_types/GIST.md), [IMT](../cancer_types/IMT.md), [LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md), [MFH](../cancer_types/MFH.md), [MPNST](../cancer_types/MPNST.md), [PECOMA](../cancer_types/PECOMA.md), [SARCNOS](../cancer_types/SARCNOS.md), [SYNS](../cancer_types/SYNS.md), [ULMS](../cancer_types/ULMS.md).

## Therapeutic landscape

- Translocation-associated sarcomas ([SYNS](../cancer_types/SYNS.md), [ES](../cancer_types/ES.md), [IMT](../cancer_types/IMT.md)): targeted by fusion-specific TKIs ([larotrectinib](../drugs/larotrectinib.md) for NTRK fusions) [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).
- Genomically complex sarcomas ([LMS](../cancer_types/LMS.md), [MFH](../cancer_types/MFH.md), MPNST): more targetable alterations overall; ICI most relevant in TMB-high subset (3.9–10.9%) [PMID:35705558](../papers/35705558.md).
