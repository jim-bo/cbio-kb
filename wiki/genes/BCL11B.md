# BCL11B

## Overview

BCL11B (B-cell CLL/lymphoma 11B) is a transcription factor with roles in T-cell development and neural differentiation. In cancer genomics it has emerged as a candidate marker in Ewing sarcoma, where its expression is linked to the neural/endothelial features of the disease.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Newly nominated Ewing sarcoma-specific marker from spatial transcriptomics of experimental EWS::FLI1-driven tumors; previously linked to neural/endothelial features of Ewing; proposed as a discriminating biomarker (DepMap overexpression, positive IHC in experimental tumors, negative in normal thymus, spleen, and liver) [PMID:25186949](../papers/25186949.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Ewing sarcoma: candidate marker induced in the EWS::FLI1 cell-of-origin model [PMID:25186949](../papers/25186949.md)
