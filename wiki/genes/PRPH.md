# PRPH

## Overview

PRPH (Peripherin) encodes an intermediate filament protein expressed in neurons of the peripheral nervous system. It is used as a marker of peripheral neuronal differentiation and is relevant to neuroblastoma biology as part of the sympathoadrenal lineage gene expression programs.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- PRPH is among the genes characterizing neuroblastoma tumor populations in a single-nuclei RNA-seq study of 11 neuroblastoma tumors spanning different INRG risk groups and INSS stages (Smart-Seq2, 3,212 high-quality nuclei). [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [NBL](../cancer_types/NBL.md) — detected in neuroblastoma single-nuclei transcriptomic dataset as part of the neuronal/sympathoadrenal gene expression landscape. [PMID:34493726](../papers/34493726.md)
