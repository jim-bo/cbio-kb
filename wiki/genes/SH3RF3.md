# SH3RF3

## Overview

SH3RF3 is a scaffold protein involved in MAPK signaling. In aSCLC, it has been observed as a translocation partner that mediates enhancer-hijacking of [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) on chromosome 11.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Translocation involving SH3RF3 on chromothriptic chr11 mediates enhancer-hijacking overexpression of [CCND1](../genes/CCND1.md) in aSCLC case A17 (the mechanism of CCND1 overexpression in two cases lacking CCND1 amplification); aSCLC cohort n=20 (MSKCC) [PMID:39185963](../papers/39185963.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- aSCLC (atypical [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md)) — SH3RF3 translocation drives CCND1 enhancer hijacking in a subset of cases [PMID:39185963](../papers/39185963.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- None flagged in the corpus beyond the CCND1 enhancer-hijack mechanism.

## Therapeutic relevance

- None flagged in the corpus.

## Open questions

- The frequency of SH3RF3 translocation as an enhancer-hijack mechanism for CCND1 overexpression outside aSCLC is unknown.
