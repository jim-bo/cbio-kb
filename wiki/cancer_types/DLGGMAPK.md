# Diffuse Low-Grade Glioma, MAPK Pathway-Altered (DLGGMAPK)

## Overview

Diffuse low-grade glioma, MAPK pathway-altered (DLGGMAPK) is a CNS/Brain tumor classified under the Gliomas, Glioneuronal Tumors, and Neuronal Tumors main type. It sits under PDIFLG in the OncoTree hierarchy. This code captures diffuse [LGG](../cancer_types/LGG.md) defined by activating MAPK pathway alterations (most frequently [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) fusions or BRAFV600E), reflecting the 2021 WHO CNS tumor classification emphasis on molecular subclassification. Many pediatric LGG cases previously designated as [LGGNOS](../cancer_types/LGGNOS.md) carry MAPK alterations and belong to this category.

## Cohorts in the corpus

- [brain_cptac_2020](../datasets/brain_cptac_2020.md) — a subset of the 93 LGG samples (218-sample pediatric brain tumor proteogenomic cohort) carry BRAF-fusion or BRAFV600E alterations defining this molecular subtype. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)

## Recurrent alterations

- [BRAF](../genes/BRAF.md) — BRAFV600E and BRAF-fusion are the defining molecular events. BRAFV600E LGG showed upregulation of MAPK pathway substrates and AKT1/AKT2 at protein level (validated by immuno-MRM); BRAFFusion LGG showed elevated [PDGFRA](../genes/PDGFRA.md) protein/RNA and a PDGFRA-enriched phosphosite module. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- [AKT1](../genes/AKT1.md), [AKT2](../genes/AKT2.md) — upregulated at RNA and protein in BRAFV600E LGG; an AKT2-substrate phosphosite module (including HNRNPUL1/HNRNPUL2) is specific to BRAFV600E vs BRAFFusion. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- [PDGFRA](../genes/PDGFRA.md) — top druggable kinase nominated from a BRAFFusion-specific phosphosite module; protein/RNA levels in BRAFFusion LGG comparable to HGG levels. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- [MAPK1](../genes/MAPK1.md) — kinase activity elevated in HGG vs LGG, confirming MEK/ERK pathway as shared signaling axis. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)

## Subtypes

- **BRAFV600E LGG** — MEK/ERK and AKT/mTOR activated at protein level; a subset of pediatric craniopharyngioma ([ACPP](../cancer_types/ACPP.md)) without BRAFV600E shares this proteomic signature. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- **BRAFFusion LGG** — PDGFRA pathway phosphorylation; M2 microglia polarization in tumor microenvironment. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)

## Therapeutic landscape

- Combined MEK + mTOR inhibition for BRAFV600E LGG supported by AKT1/AKT2 upregulation and the AKT2-substrate phosphosite co-expression module. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- PDGFRA-targeted therapy evaluation proposed for BRAFFusion LGG by analogy to pediatric HGG. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- MEK inhibition also nominated for a subset of [ACPP](../cancer_types/ACPP.md) (craniopharyngioma) tumors that share the BRAFV600E LGG proteomic signature despite lacking BRAFV600E mutation. [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
