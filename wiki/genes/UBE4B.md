# UBE4B

## Overview

UBE4B (Ubiquitination Factor E4B) is an E3/E4 ubiquitin ligase involved in protein ubiquitination and degradation. A GWAS susceptibility locus at UBE4B has been associated with HBV- or HCV-related hepatocellular carcinoma risk.

## Alterations observed in the corpus
