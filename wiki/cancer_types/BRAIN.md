# CNS/Brain (BRAIN)

## Overview

BRAIN is the top-level OncoTree tissue category for CNS/Brain cancers (parent: TISSUE, level 1). It encompasses all CNS and brain tumor types including gliomas, ependymomas, medulloblastomas, and other CNS neoplasms. In the corpus this code appears as an anatomical site category in imaging and radiation-oncology contexts, not as a specific histologic subtype. Specific subtypes include [LGGNOS](../cancer_types/LGGNOS.md), [DLGGMAPK](../cancer_types/DLGGMAPK.md), [HGGNOS](../cancer_types/HGGNOS.md), [EPM](../cancer_types/EPM.md), [MBL](../cancer_types/MBL.md), [GNG](../cancer_types/GNG.md), [ACPP](../cancer_types/ACPP.md), and [ATRT](../cancer_types/ATRT.md).

## Cohorts in the corpus

- Multiple cBioPortal pediatric brain tumor cohorts: see [brain_cptac_2020](../datasets/brain_cptac_2020.md) (218 samples, 7 histologies). [PMID:33242424](../papers/33242424.md)
- Brain is represented as an anatomical treatment site in a systematic review of 111 deep learning image-synthesis studies for MR-to-CT synthesis and PET attenuation correction in radiation oncology. [PMID:33305538](../papers/33305538.md)
