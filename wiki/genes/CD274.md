# CD274

## Overview

CD274 (PD-L1) is an immune checkpoint ligand whose expression marks counter-regulation of adaptive anti-tumor immunity and is a target of PD-(L)1 blockade.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Included in the 20-gene Immunologic Constant of Rejection (ICR) signature as one of the immunoregulatory counter-activation genes, alongside [CTLA4](../genes/CTLA4.md), [FOXP3](../genes/FOXP3.md), [IDO1](../genes/IDO1.md), and [PDCD1](../genes/PDCD1.md), applied to the 348-patient AC-ICAM colon cancer cohort [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
- Tumor PD-L1 protein expression (CD274) measured by IHC with the 22C3 pharmDx assay (CPS scoring) in 51 recurrent/second-primary HNSCC patients treated with IMRT reirradiation + [nivolumab](../drugs/nivolumab.md); CPS <20 vs ≥20 did not predict PFS (68.8% vs 59.2% 1-year PFS, P = .86) or [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) benefit, unlike the established predictive role of PD-L1 in R/M HNSCC with single-agent PD-1 blockade [PMID:38780927](../papers/38780927.md).
- CD274 (PDL1) protein expression used as a treatment-decision biomarker extracted by NLP pipelines for [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) patients in the MSK-CHORD real-world cohort (n=42,655 patients); PDL1+ vs PDL1− patients on immunotherapy showed a preserved [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) benefit (HR ≈ 0.6) at scale [PMID:39506116](../papers/39506116.md).
- PD-L1; upregulated expression in HRD tumors (Cancer.cell.3 cluster) and adnexal HRD-Dup [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) tumors (P=2.8e-3) [PMID:36517593](../papers/36517593.md)
- CD274 (PD-L1) expression levels identified as an immunotherapy biomarker in ccRCC molecular subtyping, correlating with immune infiltration patterns in TCGA KIRC cohort [PMID:22138691](../papers/22138691.md)
- High expression (PD-L1) flagged as immune-checkpoint-inhibitor rationale in neuroendocrine neoplasm patients PN5, PN18, PN19 in WGTA-guided therapy study [PMID:24326773](../papers/24326773.md)
- PD-L1 expression defines the immune-active class in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) (~30% of tumors); IHC does not predict response to [nivolumab](../drugs/nivolumab.md) or [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md) in [HCC](../cancer_types/HCC.md) trials [PMID:24798001](../papers/24798001.md)
- PD-L1 upregulated in non-keratinizing [NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md) due to chronic EBV antigen exposure; multiple anti-PD-1/PD-L1 mAbs approved or investigational [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md)
- Co-amplified with [JAK2](../genes/JAK2.md) and [PDCD1LG2](../genes/PDCD1LG2.md) at 9p24.1 in 15% of EBV-positive gastric tumours; elevated mRNA expression provides mechanistic rationale for PD-L1/L2 immune-checkpoint blockade in EBV-positive gastric cancer [PMID:25079317](../papers/25079317.md)
- No mutations or copy-number alterations in CD274 (PD-L1) or antigen-presentation pathway genes were associated with response or resistance to [pembrolizumab](../drugs/pembrolizumab.md) in advanced [NSCLC](../cancer_types/NSCLC.md) (34-patient cohort) [PMID:25765070](../papers/25765070.md)
- Focal amplifications in BRAF-mutant melanoma subtype (PD-L1); relevant to pembrolizumab/nivolumab biomarker hypothesis [PMID:26091043](../papers/26091043.md)
- CD274 (PD-L1) was not significantly differentially expressed between anti-PD-1 responders and non-responders in whole-tumor transcriptome of melanoma (n=28 RNA-seq), underscoring that checkpoint-molecule expression alone does not predict anti-PD-1 benefit in this cohort [PMID:26997480](../papers/26997480.md).
- CD274 (PD-L1) context: 47% of lung ADC and 53% of lung SqCC tumors had ≥5 predicted neoepitopes, supporting broad checkpoint immunotherapy applicability across NSCLC histologies regardless of PD-L1 expression [PMID:27158780](../papers/27158780.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- Colon adenocarcinoma — CD274 contributes to ICR clustering that prognostically stratifies AC-ICAM patients independent of CMS and MSI classifications [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
- [HNSC](../cancer_types/HNSC.md) — PD-L1 CPS measured in recurrent/second-primary HNSCC (n=51) treated with IMRT reirradiation + [nivolumab](../drugs/nivolumab.md); CPS did not stratify benefit in this reirradiation context [PMID:38780927](../papers/38780927.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with Th1/cytotoxic effector genes in ICR-high colon tumors, reflecting the paradoxical coupling of inflammation and checkpoint counter-regulation [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- The paper references that ICR correlates with immunotherapy response in other tumor types but does not directly test PD-(L)1 blockade response in colon cancer [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
- In the ROBIN METEOR center's cervix cancer Project 1, CRT does not significantly upregulate the PDL-1 (CD274)/PD-1 axis in cervix tumor cells, in contrast to the p53/MDM2 axis which is upregulated; the ImmunoRad MCT further demonstrated that intensity-modulated re-irradiation combined with PD-1 inhibition ([nivolumab](../drugs/nivolumab.md)) is tolerable and effective in recurrent/second primary HNSCC [PMID:41941260](../papers/41941260.md).

## Open questions

- Whether CD274 expression drives or merely reports the ICR-high phenotype, and whether the mICRoScore predicts immune checkpoint blockade benefit, is not tested in this cohort [PMID:37202560](../papers/37202560.md).
- The failure of PD-L1 CPS to predict benefit with reirradiation + [nivolumab](../drugs/nivolumab.md) suggests tumor PD-L1 expression may be a less reliable biomarker when PD-1 blockade is combined with reirradiation in a previously irradiated field [PMID:38780927](../papers/38780927.md).
