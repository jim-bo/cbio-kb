# TFF2

## Overview

TFF2 (Trefoil Factor 2) encodes a small secreted peptide of the trefoil factor family expressed in gastric mucosa and plays a role in mucosal protection and repair. In colorectal cancer research, TFF2 marks a gastric-type metaplasia program activated in serrated polyp lineages.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Co-expressed as part of the gastric/endodermal program (alongside [AQP5](../genes/AQP5.md), TACSTD2/TROP2, [MSLN](../genes/MSLN.md)) in sessile serrated cells (SSCs) in the COLON MAP/HTAN colorectal polyp atlas [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- [COAD](../cancer_types/COAD.md) — TFF2 expression marks the serrated/gastric-metaplasia lineage in colorectal pre-malignant polyps; co-expressed in SSCs as part of the metaplastic gastric program that distinguishes serrated from conventional adenoma pathways [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-expressed with AQP5, [TACSTD2](../genes/TACSTD2.md) (TROP2), MSLN, [TFF3](../genes/TFF3.md), and [MUC2](../genes/MUC2.md) in SSCs as part of a gastric/endodermal metaplasia program driven by BRAF-V600E [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- The gastric-program markers including TFF2 are proposed as diagnostic readouts for serrated lesion classification alongside CDX2-low/MUC5AC+ status [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)

## Open questions

- Whether TFF2 expression in SSCs confers distinct immune microenvironment properties beyond the cytotoxic CD8+ infiltrate observed in serrated polyps is not yet resolved [PMID:34910928](../papers/34910928.md)
