# KRT19

## Overview

KRT19 (Keratin 19) encodes a type I intermediate filament protein expressed in epithelial cells and serving as a stem/progenitor cell marker. In HCC, KRT19 functions as both a GWAS susceptibility locus and a component of a prognostic gene expression score.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- KRT19 is both a GWAS susceptibility locus for HCC and a component of a 5-gene tumor score (TAF9, RAN, RAMP3, KRT19, HN1) predicting HCC recurrence and survival after resection [PMID:24735922](../papers/24735922.md)
