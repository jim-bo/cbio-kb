# TCF25

## Overview

TCF25 (Transcription Factor 25) is a co-complex partner of the RQC E3 ubiquitin ligase [LTN1](../genes/LTN1.md) within the ribosome quality control (RQC) surveillance complex. It has been observed to be upregulated in microsatellite-instable (MSI) cancer cell lines, in contrast to LTN1 which is downregulated, suggesting a regulatory role in proteostasis under MSI conditions.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Upregulated in MSI cancer cell lines as a co-complex partner of LTN1 (RQC E3 ubiquitin ligase); expression association with the RQC surveillance complex [PMID:31978347](../papers/31978347.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Observed in MSI cancer cell lines across multiple cancer types in the CCLE proteomics dataset [PMID:31978347](../papers/31978347.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Co-complex partner of LTN1; inversely regulated relative to LTN1 in MSI lines (LTN1 downregulated, TCF25 upregulated) [PMID:31978347](../papers/31978347.md)

## Therapeutic relevance

- No direct therapeutic relevance reported in the corpus.

## Open questions

- Mechanistic basis for the opposing regulation of TCF25 and LTN1 in MSI lines remains unclear [PMID:31978347](../papers/31978347.md)
