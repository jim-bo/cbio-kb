# CDKN2A

## Overview

CDKN2A encodes two distinct tumor suppressors via alternative reading frames: p16/INK4A (inhibits CDK4/6-Cyclin D complexes, blocking [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) phosphorylation) and p14/ARF (stabilizes p53 by inhibiting [MDM2](../genes/MDM2.md)). Homozygous deletion of CDKN2A is one of the most common alterations across human cancers and is a major driver of cell-cycle dysregulation. CDKN2A loss is independently prognostic in multiple cancer types including intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma and esophageal adenocarcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- CDKN2A alterations associated with worse [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) in esophageal/GEJ adenocarcinoma: KM p<0.001; adjusted HR 1.65; multivariable HR 1.50 (95% CI 1.14–1.97, p=0.004) in the [egc_mskcc_2020](../datasets/egc_mskcc_2020.md) cohort (n=487). CDKN2A deletions alone were not prognostic on univariable analysis [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).
- CDKN2A focal alterations in 15% of intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma (iCCA); homozygous deletions in 13%; strongest independent prognostic factor (HR 3.40 in resected cohort multivariate; HR 2.5 in whole cohort). Only deep (homozygous) deletions are prognostic; monoallelic alterations are not. Mutually exclusive with IDH1/2 mutations and [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) fusions [PMID:33765338](../papers/33765338.md).
- CDKN2A/CDKN2B show differential alteration patterns between IDH-WT and IDH-mutant [GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md) tumors in the CPTAC proteogenomic cohort ([gbm_cptac_2021](../datasets/gbm_cptac_2021.md), n=99) [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).
- Detected at varying frequencies across TERT-altered and TERT-WT metaplastic breast cancer groups in a 60-case MSKCC cohort [PMID:33863915](../papers/33863915.md)
- Deletion (HR 3.3, 95% CI 1.1–9.5, P=0.030) independently predicts worse [OS](../cancer_types/OS.md) in node-positive (N1) intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma; combined CDKN2A/B deletion (HR 5.0) part of the poor-prognosis composite ([TP53](../genes/TP53.md) mut / [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) mut / CDKN2A/B del): median OS 12.1 vs 30.9 months (P=0.002) [PMID:33963001](../papers/33963001.md)
- Mutated/deleted in 34.9% of [CSCC](../cancer_types/CSCC.md) tumors including focal homozygous deletions; drives cell-cycle-checkpoint (Rb pathway) loss present in 38.6% of tumors [PMID:34272401](../papers/34272401.md)
- Copy number alterations enriched in pN-positive [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) tumors (9% vs 4%, p=0.039) in a 426-patient MSK-IMPACT cohort; CDKN2A was among gene-level CNAs assessed at ≥1% frequency [PMID:34290393](../papers/34290393.md)
- Homozygous deletion universally present in LSCC (along with [RB1](../genes/RB1.md) loss); 28 CDKN2A-WT tumors had p16-promoter hypermethylation; mutations paradoxically yielded increased CDKN2A RNA; loss of CDKN2A or RB1 was found in every one of 108 LSCC samples [PMID:34358469](../papers/34358469.md)
- Among the long-latency driver groups in LCINS (MRCA > a decade pre-diagnosis); identified among 25 recurrently mutated IntOGen driver genes in the 232-patient Sherlock-Lung WGS cohort of never smokers [PMID:34493867](../papers/34493867.md)
- Altered in 48% of high-cellularity PDAC tumors (CNV-driven focal deletions > intragenic mutations); deletions raise phospho-NPM1 S70/S214 as a trans-proteomic effect [PMID:34534465](../papers/34534465.md)
- CDKN2A (p16INK4A) is absent endogenously in MCF7 parental cells; ectopic p16 overexpression in T47D reduces [abemaciclib](../drugs/abemaciclib.md) and [palbociclib](../drugs/palbociclib.md) potency; high CDKN2A mRNA correlates with poor [palbociclib](../drugs/palbociclib.md) response in PRISM screen, consistent with the CDK6-INK4 resistance mechanism [PMID:34544752](../papers/34544752.md)
- Deletion enriched in metastases in 5 tumor types in the MSK-MET cohort; in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) more frequent in skin (16% vs 60%, q=0.03) and liver (16% vs 27%, q=0.03) metastases but less frequent in lymph node mets (19% vs 12%, q=0.002); deletion frequency correlated with metastatic burden in bladder urothelial, LUAD, and papillary thyroid; mutations less frequent in primary [PAAD](../cancer_types/PAAD.md) of metastatic patients (17% vs 7%, q=0.02) [PMID:35120664](../papers/35120664.md)
- CDKN2A — 157 deletions/inactivating events across sarcomas, CNS tumors, and leukemia in MAPPYACTS pediatric cohort (n=624); matched to CDK4/6 inhibitors (105 recommendations) [PMID:35292802](../papers/35292802.md)
- CDKN2A altered in 22% of MSI-stable locally advanced esophageal/GEJ adenocarcinoma (n=237); high prevalence but not significantly associated with pathologic response to neoadjuvant chemoradiotherapy [PMID:35377946](../papers/35377946.md)
- CDKN2A among the genes listed in the genomic landscape of [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) PDX/CDX cohort (n=46 models from 33 MSK patients); canonical [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) genomic landscape dominated by [TP53](../genes/TP53.md) and RB1 loss [PMID:35440124](../papers/35440124.md)
- Homozygous deletion in 8% (9/119) of low-grade serous ovarian carcinomas (LGSC) sequenced by MSK-IMPACT; acquired CDKN2A/B deletion observed at recurrence in 1/5 patients initially without detectable driver alteration [PMID:35443055](../papers/35443055.md)
- Non-SMG cancer gene mutated in ≥3 HCCs in a European multi-omic cohort (n=122); associated with poor OS independent of BCLC staging in multivariate Cox analysis [PMID:35508466](../papers/35508466.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- **Esophageal/gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma ([EAC](../cancer_types/EAC.md)):** CDKN2A alterations independently prognostic of worse OS (multivariable HR 1.50, p=0.004); deletions alone not significant on univariable; Cell Cycle pathway enrichment [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).
- **Intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma ([IHCH](../cancer_types/IHCH.md)):** homozygous deletion in 13%; strongest independent prognostic factor (HR 3.40 resected, HR 2.5 whole cohort); defines a subgroup with no benefit from resection in clinical high-risk patients; mutually exclusive with IDH1/2 and [FGFR2](../genes/FGFR2.md) [PMID:33765338](../papers/33765338.md).
- **Glioblastoma ([GBM](../cancer_types/GBM.md)):** differential CDKN2A/CDKN2B alteration patterns between IDH-WT and IDH-mutant tumors [PMID:33577785](../papers/33577785.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Mutually exclusive with IDH1/2 mutations and FGFR2 fusions in intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma [PMID:33765338](../papers/33765338.md).
- CDKN2A alterations associated with multifocal disease in iCCA [PMID:33765338](../papers/33765338.md).
- Co-occurs with TP53 and [KRAS](../genes/KRAS.md) alterations as part of the high-risk genomic profile in iCCA [PMID:33765338](../papers/33765338.md).

## Therapeutic relevance

- In iCCA, CDKN2A homozygous deletion (with TP53 and KRAS) should be routinely reported as part of pretreatment targeted sequencing to stratify OS independently of stage and treatment; patients with CDKN2A deep deletion + clinical high-risk features may be better served by upfront systemic therapy ± HAIC than resection [PMID:33765338](../papers/33765338.md).

## Open questions

- In [EAC](../cancer_types/EAC.md), why CDKN2A deletions alone are not prognostic on univariable analysis while the broader alteration category is, requires further investigation; may reflect reduced sensitivity of panel-based sequencing for deletions at low tumor purity [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).
- Panel-based sequencing has reduced sensitivity for deletions at low tumor purity, potentially dampening the apparent prognostic effect of CDKN2A deletions [PMID:33795256](../papers/33795256.md).
