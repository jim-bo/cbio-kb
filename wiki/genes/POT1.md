# POT1

## Overview

POT1 (protection of telomeres protein 1) encodes a single-stranded telomeric DNA-binding protein that is a core component of the shelterin complex. POT1 mutations disrupt telomere length regulation and genome stability. It was identified as a newly confirmed CLL driver gene, with mutations validated across multiple independent studies.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Identified as a newly confirmed CLL driver gene in WES of 160 CLL tumors (Broad Institute cohort); POT1 mutations were validated by confirmation in other studies [PMID:23415222](../papers/23415222.md)
