# NR0B1

## Overview

NR0B1 (Nuclear Receptor Subfamily 0 Group B Member 1; also DAX1) encodes an atypical nuclear receptor that acts as a transcriptional repressor. In cancer genomics, it is an established transcriptional target of the EWS::FLI1 fusion oncogene in Ewing sarcoma, marking cells with oncogene activity.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Established EWS::FLI1 transcriptional target; induced upon EWS::FLI1 expression in heMSCs and validated by RT-qPCR as a Ewing sarcoma marker [PMID:25186949](../papers/25186949.md)
