# GSE1

## Overview

GSE1 (Gse1 Coiled-Coil Protein) encodes a component of the LSD1-containing CoREST/NuRD histone-deacetylase repressor complexes. In cancer genomics, GSE1 is identified as a recurrently altered candidate driver in medulloblastoma across molecular subgroups.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Listed among additional recurrently altered candidate driver genes stratified across subgroups in medulloblastoma in the ICGC/MAGIC 491-tumor whole-genome sequencing cohort, identified in Figs 2-3 oncoprints [PMID:28726821](../papers/28726821.md).
