SMARCA4

Overview

SMARCA4 encodes the ATPase subunit of the SWI/SNF chromatin remodeling complex. In the cbio-kb corpus it appears as a metastasis-associated driver in lung adenocarcinoma.

Alterations observed in the corpus

  • SMARCA4 alterations were strongly enriched in LUAD patients with bone metastasis (OR=6.47) and shortened time to bone metastasis (OR=2.79, 95% CI 1.71–4.56, p<0.001) PMID:37084736.
  • SMARCA4, STK11, KEAP1, and KRAS alterations were mutually exclusive with EGFR alterations in LUAD PMID:37084736.
  • Inactivation of SMARCA4 (together with TP53 and CDKN2A) correlated with site-specific shorter time to metastasis in LUAD PMID:37084736.
  • SMARCA4 alterations were enriched in primary LUAD tumors from patients who later developed brain metastasis compared with non-metastatic primaries PMID:37591896.
  • SMARCA4 was included in LUAD pathway/metastasis analyses in MSK-CHORD (n=24,950) PMID:39506116.
  • SMARCA4 is a chromatin-modifying gene with high prevalence of non-silent variants in metastatic UC (UC-GENOME cohort) PMID:36333289
  • SMARCA4 assessed in gallbladder carcinoma (GBC) genomic landscape study PMID:36228155
  • Identified as recurrently mutated in medulloblastoma WGS (PCGP, 37 tumors), implicating chromatin remodeling complexes in medulloblastoma pathogenesis PMID:22722829
  • Significantly mutated in melanoma WES cohort (121 tumors, Broad); SMARCA4 identified among chromatin-remodeling genes recurrently altered in melanoma PMID:22817889
  • Recurrently mutated in medulloblastoma WES cohort (92 tumors, Broad); SMARCA4 SWI/SNF complex mutations enriched in specific molecular subgroups PMID:22820256
  • Identified as significantly mutated in medulloblastoma WGS/WES cohort (76 tumors, ICGC); SMARCA4 alterations associated with SHH-subgroup medulloblastoma PMID:22832583
  • Identified as significantly mutated in TCGA LUSC (178 tumors) via COSMIC-restricted analysis; epigenetic regulator PMID:22960745
  • Inactivating mutations identified as significantly mutated in LUAD (Broad WES cohort, 183 tumors); epigenetic regulator that could not be mapped to any classic oncogenic hallmark PMID:22980975
  • SMARCA4 is part of recurrent SWI/SNF chromatin-remodeling mutations in 24% of esophageal adenocarcinomas; also identified as a putative partner in a SMARCA4-DNM2 fusion PMID:23525077
  • SWI/SNF complex member; co-occurs in the HotNet PBAF subnetwork with PBRM1 and ARID1A in clear cell renal cell carcinoma (ccRCC); significantly mutated gene in ccRCC. PMID:23792563
  • Recurrently mutated chromatin-remodeling gene in TCC; part of the 58% of bladder cancer cases with chromatin-remodeling gene mutations PMID:24121792
  • Mutations private to the initial or recurrent tumor in 6 of 7 affected low-grade glioma patients; appear to confer selective advantage given preexisting early driver events (IDH1 R132H) rather than being founding events PMID:24336570
  • Mutated in 2/21 sequenced sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinomas (AdCC; 9%); missense p.Gly1194Trp and p.Glu882Lys; SWI/SNF chromatin-remodeling complex component PMID:24418857
  • SMARCA4 was mutated in RMH004 ccRCC tumor (p.Thr910Met) alongside PBRM1 and ARID1A hits, implicating SWI/SNF complex disruption in this tumor PMID:24487277
  • SMARCA4 (BRG1) harbors biallelic inactivating mutations in 12/12 SCCOHT tumors (nonsense, frameshift, splice-site, intragenic exonic deletions) affecting helicase catalytic domains; established as the defining tumor suppressor of SCCOHT. Truncating mutations correlate with reduced SMARCA4 expression in TCGA, consistent with nonsense-mediated decay PMID:24658004
  • Mutated in 6% of LUAD (TCGA, n=230); chromatin modifier (SWI/SNF complex); mutually exclusive with ARID1A in bladder cancer. PMID:25079552
  • Alterations mutually exclusive with ARID1A truncating mutations (28%) in high-grade urothelial carcinoma of the bladder (MSK-IMPACT, n=109). PMID:25092538
  • Chromatin remodeler truncated or hit at COSMIC sites in metastatic cSCC; chromatin-remodeling inactivation (including SMARCA4/SMARCB1/KMT2D/CREBBP/EP300/ARID family) present in 48% of cohort PMID:25589618
  • Part of SWI/SNF chromatin-remodelling lesions in >42% of pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma cases (along with SMARCA2, SMARCC1, SMARCC2, ARID1A, ARID1B, PBRM1). PMID:25855536
  • Somatic mutation detected in both low-grade and high-grade UTUC (upper tract urothelial carcinoma); listed among chromatin-modifying genes (alongside KDM6A, KMT2D, KMT2A, KMT2C, CREBBP, EP300, ARID1A) that are frequently altered and concordant across spatial tumour components, suggesting early/clonal events PMID:26278805
  • p.Ser1238Tyr (PolyPhen2 = 0.99) in Sézary syndrome; p.Arg251Lys in mycosis fungoides; SWI/SNF chromatin remodeling component PMID:26551667
  • Sole driver of SMARCB1-intact extra-cranial malignant rhabdoid tumor (MRT) via somatic LOH plus a germline one-base deletion, demonstrating SWI/SNF complex redundancy in MRT pathogenesis PMID:26977886

Cancer types (linked)

  • LUAD — metastasis-associated driver with site-specific bone tropism PMID:37084736; enriched in BM+ primaries PMID:37591896.

Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

  • Mutually exclusive with EGFR alterations in LUAD PMID:37084736.

Therapeutic relevance

  • No direct targeted therapy reported in the corpus.

Open questions

  • Some site-specific analyses (e.g., liver) for SMARCA4 were underpowered PMID:37084736.
