# CyCIF (Cyclic Immunofluorescence)

## Overview

CyCIF is a multiplexed cyclic immunofluorescence imaging method that enables detection of dozens of protein markers in situ using iterative rounds of staining, imaging, and signal bleaching on FFPE tissue sections [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Used by

- [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md) — CyCIF with a 31-antibody panel was applied to 44 fallopian tube specimens (including [HGSOC](../cancer_types/HGSOC.md) precursors: normal FTE, SCOUT, STIC, invasive HGSOC) to characterize the tumor immune microenvironment; revealed progressive immune suppression from FTE through STIC to invasive HGSOC, including increased PD-L1 expression and reduced CD8+ T cell infiltration [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).

## Notes

- Combined with [GeoMx WTA](../methods/geomx-wta.md) spatial transcriptomics to provide complementary protein and transcriptomic spatial profiling of HGSOC precursor lesions [PMID:39386723](../papers/39386723.md).
