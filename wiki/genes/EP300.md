# EP300

## Overview

EP300 (E1A Binding Protein P300) is a histone acetyltransferase and transcriptional co-activator. EP300 is a chromatin-modifying gene frequently mutated in urothelial carcinoma.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- Chromatin-modifying gene with high prevalence of non-silent variants in metastatic urothelial carcinoma (UC-GENOME cohort), consistent with TCGA-BLCA [PMID:36333289](../papers/36333289.md)
- EP300 was identified as a recurrently mutated gene in DLBCL and [FL](../cancer_types/FL.md), implicating histone acetyltransferase activity in lymphomagenesis [PMID:21796119](../papers/21796119.md)
- Inactivating mutations in HAT domain in [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) (29 tumors, CLCGP WES/WGS); mutually exclusive with [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) alterations [PMID:22941188](../papers/22941188.md)
- Hotspot mutations in chromatin-modifying gene in [SCLC](../cancer_types/SCLC.md) (36 tumors, JHU WES/WGS) [PMID:22941189](../papers/22941189.md)
- Splice-site mutation in the KAT11 acetyltransferase domain (G1429_splice) identified in adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC); functions as a [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) co-activator [PMID:23685749](../papers/23685749.md)
- Mutated (along with [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) p.R1446C in the KAT11 domain) in adenoid cystic carcinoma; part of a chromatin-remodeling gene cluster mutated in 12/24 cases [PMID:23778141](../papers/23778141.md)
- Mutation in bladder TCC as part of a chromatin-remodeling gene cluster altered in 58% of cases [PMID:24121792](../papers/24121792.md)
- EP300 mutated in 3/21 (14%) of sinonasal adenoid cystic carcinoma cases, with frameshift (c.3355del p.Asp1119MetfsTer38), missense (c.1811G>A p.Arg604Gln), and nonsense (c.1927G>T p.Glu643Ter) variants [PMID:24418857](../papers/24418857.md)
- EP300 identified as a significantly mutated histone acetyltransferase in 15% of muscle-invasive bladder cancers; chromatin-targeting therapies proposed for the 76% of tumors with chromatin regulator mutations including EP300 [PMID:24476821](../papers/24476821.md)
- Phosphorylated at S1834 downstream of SEMA7A/ITGB1/AKT signaling in [GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md) cells; p300-S1834 phosphorylation drives H3K27ac at [SNAI1](../genes/SNAI1.md) and [ZEB1](../genes/ZEB1.md) promoters promoting EMT; S1834A mutant is null; the AKT→EP300 axis proposed as a therapeutic target in [GBC](../cancer_types/GBC.md) [PMID:24997986](../papers/24997986.md)
- Truncating mutations in 15% of muscle-invasive bladder cancer (MSKCC cohort); mutually exclusive with [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md) truncating mutations (13%) [PMID:25092538](../papers/25092538.md)
- Truncating EP300 mutations in cSCC; EP300 truncating events are mutually exclusive with NOTCH truncating events; EP300 is a chromatin remodeler truncated or hit at COSMIC sites in 48% of the cSCC cohort [PMID:25589618](../papers/25589618.md)

## Cancer types (linked)

- Urothelial carcinoma (UC): recurrently mutated chromatin modifier [PMID:36333289](../papers/36333289.md)

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Frequently co-mutated with other chromatin-modifying genes ([KMT2D](../genes/KMT2D.md), [ARID1A](../genes/ARID1A.md), [KDM6A](../genes/KDM6A.md), [KMT2C](../genes/KMT2C.md), [KMT2A](../genes/KMT2A.md), [CREBBP](../genes/CREBBP.md), [SMARCA4](../genes/SMARCA4.md)) in metastatic UC [PMID:36333289](../papers/36333289.md)
