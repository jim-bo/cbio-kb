# PTPN3

## Overview

PTPN3 encodes a non-receptor protein tyrosine phosphatase. In hepatocellular carcinoma, PTPN3 was identified as a target of recurrent homozygous deletion, suggesting a tumor-suppressor role in liver cancer.
