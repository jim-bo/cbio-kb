# ERBB2

## Overview

ERBB2 (HER2) is a receptor tyrosine kinase whose amplification and activating mutations drive multiple solid tumors and define HER2-directed therapy eligibility.

## Alterations observed in the corpus

- ERBB2 was among the genes altered *less* often in [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) metastases than in matched primaries [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- ERBB2 amplification was identified in one female ovarian germ cell tumor treated with [trastuzumab](../drugs/trastuzumab.md), without durable response [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- In urothelial carcinoma, ERBB2 was inversely associated with [FGFR3](../genes/FGFR3.md) alterations overall but co-altered in 5% (19/414) of FGFR3-altered tumors, ranging from 2.5% in NMIBC to 11% in MIBC [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).

## Cancer types (linked)

- [LUAD](../cancer_types/LUAD.md) — depleted in metastases vs primaries [PMID:37084736](../papers/37084736.md).
- [OGCT](../cancer_types/OGCT.md) — amplification in one patient in the Make-an-IMPACT rare-cancer cohort [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
- [BLCA](../cancer_types/BLCA.md) / [UTUC](../cancer_types/UTUC.md) — co-altered in 11% of FGFR3-altered MIBC [PMID:37682528](../papers/37682528.md).

## Co-occurrence and mutual exclusivity

- Not reported in the corpus.

## Therapeutic relevance

- [trastuzumab](../drugs/trastuzumab.md) treatment of an ERBB2-amplified ovarian GCT did not produce a durable response [PMID:36862133](../papers/36862133.md).
