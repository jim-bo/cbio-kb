# tabelecleucel

## Overview

Tabelecleucel is an allogeneic EBV-specific cytotoxic T lymphocyte (CTL) cell therapy. It was evaluated in EBV-positive recurrent/metastatic nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([NPC](../cancer_types/NPC.md)), but development in NPC has been terminated.

## Evidence in the corpus

- Tabelecleucel (allogeneic EBV-CTL) in EBV+ R/M NPC (NCT03769467, n=12): stable disease in 50%, no objective responses; development in NPC has been terminated due to lack of objective responses [PMID:24952746](../papers/24952746.md).
