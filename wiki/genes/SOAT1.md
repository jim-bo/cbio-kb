# SOAT1

## Overview

SOAT1 (Sterol O-Acyltransferase 1, also known as ACAT1) encodes an enzyme that converts cholesterol to cholesterol esters for storage in lipid droplets. SOAT1 is overexpressed in various cancers and promotes tumor growth by facilitating cholesterol esterification and membrane biosynthesis. In hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC), SOAT1 inhibition via avasimibe has been shown to reduce tumor size in patient-derived xenograft (PDX) models, establishing it as a metabolic therapeutic target.
